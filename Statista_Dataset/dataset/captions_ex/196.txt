In 2020 , the Walt Disney Company held assets worth a total of over 201.55 billion U.S. dollars . In the same year , the American media company generated global revenue of 65.39 billion U.S. dollars . Of this revenue generated in 2020 , 28.39 billion U.S. dollars was in the media networks segment and over 16.5 billion was generated through the company 's parks and resorts .
