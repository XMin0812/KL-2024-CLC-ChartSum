In 2019 the United Kingdom 's economy grew by approximately 1.3 percent , the same growth rate as 2018 and also the slowest annual growth rate since the great recession of the late 2010s , where the economy shrank by 4.2 percent . Prior to the economic crash of 2008 , the British economy expanded at a relatively healthy rate , particularly in 1998 when gross-domestic product grew by 3.7 percent .
