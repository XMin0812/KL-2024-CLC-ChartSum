On June 17 , 2016 , Korean education brand Pinkfong released their video `` Baby Shark Dance '' , and the rest is history . On November 2 , 2020 , the video became the most-viewed YouTube video of all time , surpassing the former record holder `` Despacito '' by Luis Fonsi . `` Baby Shark Dance '' currently has more than 7.05 billion lifetime views on YouTube .
