Hogs and pigs are one of the main sources of meat in the United States , and there is a thriving domestic pork market . As of March 2020 , Iowa had an inventory of about 24.6 million hogs and pigs , making it the top producer of swine in the country by a large margin . In that same year , the second largest producer of hogs and pigs was Minnesota , with about 9.3 million head .
