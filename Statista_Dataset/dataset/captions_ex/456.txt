As of January 5 , 2021 , the total number of tests for coronavirus infections conducted in Italy surpassed 27.1 million . The region that conducted the most tests was Lombardy , followed by Veneto , and Lazio . As of January 5 , 2021 , the total number of cases reported by the authorities surpassed 2.1 million . The north of the country was mostly hit , and the region with the highest number of cases was Lombardy , which registered more than 487 thousand of them . The neighboring regions of Veneto and Piedmont followed in the list . For a global overview visit Statista 's webpage exclusively dedicated to coronavirus , its development , and its impact .
