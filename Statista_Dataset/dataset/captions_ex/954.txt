Which is leading company in Denmark ? With a revenue of over 231 billion Danish kroner , the digital postbox provider , e-Boks A/S ranked first as of December 2020 . The prepress company , Glunz & Jensen Holding A/S , ranked second with around 196 billion kroner . Ranked third was the shipping company , A.P . Møller - Mærsk A/S , with a revenue of over 192 billion Danish kroner .
