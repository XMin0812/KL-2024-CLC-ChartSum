As of December 20 , 2020 , the Belgian health authorities reported that over 625 thousand people have been tested positive for the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) . From a regional perspective , with over 271 thousand positive test results , most confirmed cases were reported in Wallonia . Flanders faced the second-highest amount of positive cases with 263 thousand confirmed cases , while the Brussels-Capital Region registered roughly 80 thousand positive tests .
