During the 2019/2020 football season , Manchester City paid the highest average annual player salary out of all teams in the English Premier League ( EPL ) , with an average of roughly 8.73 million U.S. dollars . Manchester United was the highest paying club in the EPL for the previous two seasons , while Sheffield United , which moved up into the EPL from the Football League Championship this season , paid the lowest annual average of approximately 910 thousand U.S. dollars per player in 2019/20 .
