How many people play PUBG ? PlayerUnknown 's Battlegrounds ( PUBG ) , which is available on console and PC , crossed the 70 million player base mark in July 2020 - an impressive figure considering it was only fully released at the end of 2017 . The Battle Royale game developed by Bluehole was made available as an early access beta program in March 2017 before its full release in December 2017 .
