PayPal 's growth of active registered accounts continued in 2020 , growing by nearly 70 million year-on-year - or 22 percent y-o-y - in the third quarter of 2020 . Daily active users ( DAU ) can vary per country , however . PayPal use in the UK , for instance , was somewhat higher than in Germany but significantly higher than the DAU found in 19 other European countries .
