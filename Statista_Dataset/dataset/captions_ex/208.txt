As of January 06 , 2021 , overall coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) cases in South Africa reached its highest at 1,149,591 infections . It was also the largest volume of confirmed cases compared to other African countries . Regionally , Gauteng ( Johannesburg ) was hit hardest and registered 310,201 cases , whereas KwaZulu-Natal ( Durban ) and Western Cape ( Cape Town ) counted 230,283 and 227,654 coronavirus cases , respectively . In total 6,898,207 tests were conducted in the country . Total recoveries amounted to 929,239 . On December 31 , 2020 , the highest daily increase in cases was recorded in South Africa .
