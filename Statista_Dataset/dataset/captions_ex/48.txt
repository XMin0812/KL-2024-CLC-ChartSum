As of January 5 , 2021 , an average of around 1,039 people per day have died from COVID-19 in the U.S. since the first case was confirmed in the country on January 20th . On an average day , nearly 8,000 people die from all causes in the United States , based on data from 2019 . Based on the latest information , one in nine deaths each day can be attributed to COVID-19 since January 20th . The daily death toll from seasonal flu , using preliminary maximum estimates from the 2019 - 2020 influenza season , stood at an average of almost 332 people .
