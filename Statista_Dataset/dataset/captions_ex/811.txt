As of May 17 , 2020 , there have been 320 total infections of coronavirus in Vietnam . At the moment , 260 patients have recovered . Vietnam has gone 31 days without new infection cases of COVID-19 as of 6:05 PM on May 17 , 2020 . As of April 23 , 2020 , Hanoi has had the highest number of COVID-19 infections with 123 cases .
