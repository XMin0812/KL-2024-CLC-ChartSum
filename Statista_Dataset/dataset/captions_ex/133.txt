As of January 6 , 2021 , the Malaysian state of Sabah had around 39 thousand coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) confirmed cases , the highest in the country . Sabah is currently experiencing the worst outbreak of COVID-19 in Malaysia . Malaysia is currently one out of more than 200 countries and territories battling with the novel coronavirus .
