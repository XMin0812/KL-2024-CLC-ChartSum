The south Indian state of Kerala had cumulatively conducted around 25 thousand tests to detect the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) as of April 29 , 2020 . Kerala had reported the first case of the virus in India in early February . It was also one of the only states reporting daily updates on the number of samples being tested . Overall , India had over 31 thousand confirmed cases of the coronavirus as of April 29 , 2020 .
