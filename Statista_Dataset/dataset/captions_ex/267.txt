When was the euro at its highest ? This statistic displays the annual exchange rate ( average or standardized measure ) of the euro to the British pound sterling ( EUR GBP ) according to data provided by the European Central Bank from the introduction of the euro in 1999 up until 2019 . The average ( standardized ) measure is based on many observations throughout the period in question . This is different than an annual measure which presents concrete values as of the end of the year .
