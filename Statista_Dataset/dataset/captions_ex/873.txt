During the 2019/2020 football season , Juventus paid the highest average annual salary to its ' clubs players out of all teams in the Serie A with an average of roughly 10.1 million U.S. dollars . The team has been holding the first position as a highest paying club for the past four years . In 2019/2020 , it was followed by Roma and Internazionale which payed its players an average of 4.49 million U.S. dollars and 4.08 million U.S. dollars respectively .
