In 2019 , Ford Fiesta was the best selling car model at 77,833 units . Currently in its seventh generation , it was first sold in 1979 and had been popular with UK consumers from the beginning . Two years after its introduction on the market , the Ford Fiesta Mk1 had already reached the peak of the most sold supermini cars . As seen in this statistic , three of the leading ten car models were produced by Ford , with the car manufacturer pushing ahead of Volkswagen , the only other manufacturer with more than one entry . In terms of car sales in the UK , by far the most profitable month for Ford was March , when the it had sold 40,755 units .
