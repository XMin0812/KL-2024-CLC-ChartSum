As of August 28 , 2020 , over 2.29 million people who were diagnosed with the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Europe have recovered from the disease . There have been 796,498 confirmed recoveries in Russia . There have been a further 240,792 official recoveries in Turkey , while in Germany 213,725 people have been confirmed as recovered from the virus . Across Europe , there has been 3,815,209 confirmed cases of coronavirus , with 975,576 confirmed cases in Russia as of August 28 .
