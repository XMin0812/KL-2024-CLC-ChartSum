Facebook was the social network with the largest market share in Sweden , as of October 2020 . The market share of Facebook made up nearly 81 percent of the total market in Sweden . Pinterest reached over seven percent , ranking second in the list . Twitter , third in this list , had roughly five percent of all Swedish page views . The share of Internet users in Sweden who used Facebook increased over the last years . From 63 percent in 2011 , the percentage increased to 76 percent in 2018 and slightly decreased to 74 percent as of 2019 .
