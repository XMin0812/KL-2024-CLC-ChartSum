In 2019 , global sales of the Italian fashion company Tod 's experienced an overall contraction . In particular , the sales of the brands Tod 's , Fay , and Hogan experienced contraction of 7.4 , eight and 4.7 percent , respectively . By contrast , the sales of articles branded Roger Vivier experienced a positive growth of 15.5 percent . In terms of value , Tod 's Group registered total sales amounting to 916 million euros in 2019 .
