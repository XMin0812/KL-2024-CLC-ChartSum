This statistic illustrates the sales value of anti-cellulite care products in Italy in the second quarter of 2017 , broken down by sales channel . According to data , the sales value of anti-cellulite care products via hypermarkets and supermarkets combined amounted to about 8.3 million euros . By contrast , in the same period , the sales value of anti-cellulite care products in traditional grocery stores was over 1.2 million euros .
