This statistic shows the total annual number of albuterol prescriptions in the U.S. from 2004 to 2017 , in millions . In 2004 , albuterol was prescribed over 37 million times . As of 2017 , the number of prescriptions for this drug has increased to over 50 million . Albuterol is primarily used to treat asthma including asthma attacks , exercise-induced bronchoconstriction , and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease ( COPD ) .
