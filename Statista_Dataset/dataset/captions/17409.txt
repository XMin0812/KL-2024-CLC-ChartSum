How many upper secondary schools are there in Sweden ? From the schoolyear 2012/13 , the number of upper secondary schools rose significantly and peaked in 2013/14 , when there were 1,346 upper secondary schools in Sweden . As of 2019/20 , the number had declined to 1,307 upper secondary schools . Upper secondary school ( gymnasieskola in Swedish ) is a voluntary upper secondary education for children between age 16 and 19 .
