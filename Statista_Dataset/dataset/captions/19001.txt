This statistic shows the amount of mephedrone-related deaths reported to NPSAD in England in 2012 , by age group . Statistically , people aged between 25 and 34 years were the most likely to have had a mephedrone-related death . Mephedrone is known as a 'legal high ' and also referred to in slang as 'Meow Meow ' _ , 'drone ' and 'MCAT ' _ . It is important to note that these deaths may also have involved other implicated psychoactive substances that are not detailed here .
