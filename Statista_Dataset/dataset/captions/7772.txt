Between 2008 and 2019 , Curaçao 's Gross Domestic Product increased from nearly 5.1 billion ANG to 5.5 billion ANG ( or roughly 3.1 billion U.S. dollars ) . GDP was highest in 2014 , at over 5.6 billion ANG . After 2014 , Curaçao 's GDP fell annually , until 2018 . Only in the most recent year , Curaçao 's GDP saw a modest recovery . As a consequence , Curaçao 's real GDP growth was negative nearly every year between 2014 and 2018 .
