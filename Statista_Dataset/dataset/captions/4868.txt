The spread of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Italy has hit mostly people over 50 years of age . Also , the virus claimed approximately 35.6 thousand lives since it entered the country between the end of January and the beginning of February 2020 . As the chart shows , nearly 85 percent of the victims were older people , aged 70 years and older . Since the first case was detected at the end of January in Italy , coronavirus has been spreading fast . As of September 24 , 2020 , the authorities reported 304 thousand cases in the country . The area mostly hit by the virus is the North , in particular the region of Lombardy .
