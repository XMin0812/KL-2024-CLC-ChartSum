When it comes to Poles communicating with their friends and family through mobile apps , the Facebook messaging app was the most frequently chosen mobile app . In 2019 , 86.4 percent of Polish people used the Messenger application to communicate with others . WhatsApp was ranked second , with almost 44 percent of respondents using the app . Polish instant messaging client Gadu-Gadu came fifth with eight percent of respondents communicating through the app .
