This statistic shows the monthly trend in the volume of textiles , clothing , footwear and leather goods sales ( quantity bought ) in Great Britain from January 2015 to June 2020 . The figures are seasonally adjusted estimates , measured using the Retail Sales Index ( RSI ) and published in index form with a reference year of 2016 equal to 100 . Sales increased to an index level of 71.4 in June 2020 from a low of 42 in May 2020 .
