The population density of Spain maintained steadily at a rate of over 92 inhabitants per square kilometers in the last decade , with the latest figures revealing a density of 93.67 people per square kilometer in 2018 . Spain 's degree of urbanization is rather high , with levels reaching almost 81 percent of urbanization in the country . Andalusia , with a total number of 8.4 million inhabitants , ranked first on the list of most populous autonomous communities in Spain as of 1st July 2018 .
