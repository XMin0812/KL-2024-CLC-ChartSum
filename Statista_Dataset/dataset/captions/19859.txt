This statistic shows the share of Banco Santander worldwide customers who accessed banking services digitally in 2013 and 2014 , as well as an estimation for 2017 . This estimated figure is the bank 's goal . In 2013 , approximately 23 percent of Santander customers used digital channels to conduct banking transactions and get information . This share rose to 28 percent in 2014 . Santander hoped that by 2017 active users would rise to approximately 45 percent .
