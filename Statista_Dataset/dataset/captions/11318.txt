In 2018 , Belgium 's town-halls have registered over a thousand same-sex marriages . Marriage between same-sex couples was legalized in the country on the first of June 2003 . It is the second European country to do so after the Netherlands in 2001 , and therefore , the second worldwide . Spain was the third European country to legalize these unions in 2005 . Since then , many countries have legalized same-sex marriage . In 2019 , same-sex marriage is available in 28 countries .
