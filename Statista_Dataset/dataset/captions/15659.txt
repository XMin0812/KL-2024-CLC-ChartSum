The statistic displays the number of registered passenger cars in Curaçao from 2009 to 2018 . Within the nine year period considered , the highest number of registered passenger cars in Curaçao was seen in 2010 , when just under 81,000 passenger cars were registered . Following a sudden decrease in the number of registered passenger cars in 2011 , the number of cars in Curaçao increased again from 2013 onwards , and as of 2018 there were around 77,300 registered passenger cars in Curaçao .
