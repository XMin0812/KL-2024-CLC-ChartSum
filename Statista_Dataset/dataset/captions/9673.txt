The population , including the primary school-age population in Sweden grew annually from 2009 to 2019 . In 2019 , there were roughly 1.2 million children in Sweden between 5 and 14 years old . The number of pupils in primary school was the lowest in the schoolyear 2010/11 when around 887 thousand pupils attended primary school . After the schoolyear 2010/11 , the number of pupils that attended primary school increased annually , especially in the later years of the period , reaching around 1.1 million pupils in the schoolyear 2019/20 .
