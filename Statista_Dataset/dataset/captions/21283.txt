The statistic illustrates the value of ten British pound value banknotes removed from circulation and destroyed by the Bank of England in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2013/2014 to 2018/2019 . It can be seen that the value of ten British pound banknotes removed from circulation fluctuated during the period under observation , reaching a total of approximately 4.9 billion British pounds of notes as of 2018/2019 . The highest value of ten British pound banknotes removed from circulation and destroyed in one year was in 2017/2018 , at a total of over 7.7 billion British pounds .
