In the second week of April 2020 , online sales of sterilized milk in Russia increased by nearly 200 percent compared to the same period of the previous year . The increase in fast-moving consumer goods sales over the internet was observed due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , as more cases of the infection were reported in Russia and around the world . During the self-isolation regime , Russians used online grocery delivery services , which led to an increase in sales .
