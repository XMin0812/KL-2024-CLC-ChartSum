Konfío , a fintech trademark owned by the Mexican company Red Amigo DAL , was found to be the fastest growing business in Latin America . Based on its compound annual growth rate ( CAGR ) , Konfío 's revenue grew 185.7 percent per year between 2015 and 2018 . Coderhouse , an edutech platform based in Argentina , ranked second with a CAGR of 155.4 percent . Another Mexican company , the financial management platform ComparaGuru.com , made it third with a CAGR of 109 percent .
