In early 2017 , more than half of Dutch consumers used contactless or Near Field Communication ( NFC ) payments at least once . The total number of registered NFC payments at retail points of sales ( POS ) increased in almost every quarter since 2016 . Contactless payments were introduced in the Netherlands in 2014 and mainly consist of methods supported and developed by Dutch banks , as services as Apple Pay or Android Pay are not available . Apple Pay was likely to arrive in the country in the middle of 2019 .
