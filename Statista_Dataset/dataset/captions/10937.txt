This statistic shows the dividend per share of chemical and gas company Air Liquide from 2002 to 2019 . In 2019 , the company paid a dividend of 2.70 euros per share . Air Liquide S.A. is a leading global chemical company , specializing in industrial gases for various industries . The company is headquartered in Paris , France . In 2015 , Air Liquide held the ninth place among the ten largest chemical companies worldwide based on revenue .
