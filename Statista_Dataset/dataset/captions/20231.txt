In the second quarter of 2020 , the unemployment rate in Italy stood at 8.3 percent , the lowest rate registered between 2019 and 2020 . In the first quarter of 2019 , 10.4 percent of people were unemployed , the highest figure depicted in the chart . In 2020 , the share of unemployed individuals aged between 15 and 24 years was over 30 percent .
