From 2012 to 2018 , the value of gross written premiums by life insurance companies on the domestic market in Poland has seen an almost year-on-year decrease . 2012 was the year with the highest amount of life insurance premiums at almost nine billion euros . In 2018 , the value reached a little over five billion euros , which was a four billion less than the height of 2012 . The United Kingdom had the highest value of life insurance premiums among all European counties in 2018 , followed by France and Italy . Together , the top three countries accounted for more than 60 percent of the European market .
