On December 3 , 2020 , 2,507 cases of coronavirus disease ( COVID-19 ) were newly confirmed in Japan . The number peaked on November 28 at 2,674 . New cases have been reported every day since February 13 with a growth acceleration observed from the last week of March , from the end of June , and again in November . The health ministry of the country announced that the total number of infections amounted to around 155.2 thousand cases as of December 4 , 2020 .
