As of December 2019 , the UK headquartered Royal Dutch Shell plc was the leading company listed on the London Stock Exchange with a market capital of over 176 billion British pounds . this made them the largest of all 2,055 companies trading shares on the LSE in December 2019 from more than 70 countries globally with a combined market capital of over 3.9 trillion British pounds ( GBP ) . Seen as the heart of the global financial community , the London Stock Exchange is the second largest market in Europe and ranks seventh globally .
