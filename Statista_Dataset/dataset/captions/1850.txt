High run chases in the fourth and final innings of a cricket test match are notoriously difficult given the time constraints and deteriorating pitch . The highest successful fourth innings run chase of all time in test cricket belongs to the West Indies - in 2003 , the team chased down Australia 's target of 418 runs for the loss of seven wickets thanks to centuries from Ramnaresh Sarwan and Shivnarine Chanderpaul .
