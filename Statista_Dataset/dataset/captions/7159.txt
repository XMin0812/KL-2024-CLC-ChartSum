Between 2008 and 2018 , the number of stores that specialize in the sales of meat has been shrinking In the United Kingdom . During this period , the number of meat specialty stores has seen a decrease of 945 . Of these stores , approximately 1.75 thousand generated a turnover between one hundred thousand and 250 thousand British pounds in 2018 . Only slightly above a hundred stores generated revenue of two million or more British pounds .
