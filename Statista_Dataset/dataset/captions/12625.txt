In a survey , conducted annually from 2015 to 2018 , the share of people participating in any form of watersport in the United Kingdom ( UK ) was established . According to the survey , overall tendencies towards watersports increased gradually from a quarter of the population in 2005 to roughly 31.8 percent in 2018 with some up and downs in between . Overall the most popular activities are spending leisure time at the beach , coastal walking , swimming and recreational boating .
