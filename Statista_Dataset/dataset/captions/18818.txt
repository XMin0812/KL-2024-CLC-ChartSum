This statistic shows the Sierra Club 's ranking of the greenest universities in the United States as of 2020 . To calculate the ranking Sierra Club looked at the range of sustainable practices , research and programs offered by universities as well as total emissions and energy consumption and also a number of other factors including vegan catering options , water initiatives , bike facilities etc . The University of California , Irvine was ranked first as the greenest university of the U.S. for 2020 with a total score of 89.95 .
