This statistic illustrates the net sales revenue of the telecommunications provider Ipko in Kosovo from 2014 to 2016 . The net sales revenue of Ipko increased from 67,603,000 euros in 2015 to 69,462,000 euros in 2016 . Ipko operators under the larger telecommunications operator Telekom Slovenije , which is one of the largest in the region . They provide services such as fixed and mobile communication services as well as television and other multimedia services .
