According to a survey conducted to understand the opinion among Indians on the coronavirus COVID-19 , about 72 percent of respondents stated that they are staying alert and taking precautions . On the contrary , 16 percent said that they did not believe that the virus can impact them or Indians at large . The country reported over a thousand cases on March 28 , 2020 . A lockdown was enforced on March 25 , 2020 for 21 days , the largest in the world , restricting 1.3 billion people .
