Due to the crisis of the coronavirus COVID-19 , in March 2020 there was a large drop in hotel reservations in Spain for the Easter holidays that year . In the Balearic Islands , the most affected region , the drop in the first 17 days of March with respect to the entire month of February 2020 exceeded 70 % . Across the country , the decrease in hotel reservations compared to the previous month was practically 60 % .
