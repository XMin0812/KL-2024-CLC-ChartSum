In Chad and Sierra Leone , the maternal mortality rate was above one thousand . Chad recorded in 2017 the highest number of mothers ' deaths per 100,000 live births . That year , every 100,000 children , 1,140 mothers died from any cause related to or aggravated by pregnancy or its management . The maternal death rate in Sierra Leone equaled to 1,120 . Nigeria followed with 917 deaths per 100,000 live births .
