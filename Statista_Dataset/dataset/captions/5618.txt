This graph depicts the franchise value of the New Orleans Saints from the National Football League from 2002 to 2020 . In 2020 , the franchise value amounted to about 2.5 billion U.S. dollars . Since 1985 , the New Orleans Saints have been owned by Gayle Benson , wife of the late Thomas Benson , who bought the franchise for 70.2 million U.S. dollars in 1985 .
