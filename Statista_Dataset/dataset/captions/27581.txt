This statistic displays the number of specialist surgeons employed in the health sector in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2000 to 2019 . During this period the number of specialist surgeons employed in the health sector in the United Kingdom increased . In 2000 the number of specialist surgeons amounted to approximately 30.1 thousand , by 2019 the number of specialists surgeons came to almost 57.5 thousand , this was the highest number recorded during the twenty-year period .
