As of January 3 , 2021 , there have been 26,399,030 confirmed cases of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) across the whole of Europe since the first confirmed cases in France on January 25 . There were approximately two million new cases reported in the week ending November 8 , 2020 , the highest number of daily cases in a single week . There has been a distinct increase in the number of new cases in Europe since the end of August . Russia has had the highest amount of confirmed cases in Europe with 3,260,138 , followed by France with 2,655,728 cases . A full breakdown of the confirmed cases in Europe can be found here .
