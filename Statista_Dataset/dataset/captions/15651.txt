This statistic displays the sales of products for coughs and colds in the self-medication industry in Europe in 2017 . In this year Germany was the market leader in sales of cough and cold medications at approximately 1.5 billion euro . This was followed by Italy at 717.8 million euro in cough and cold medication sales . The country with the lowest sales of products for cough and colds was Denmark at 4.8 million euro .
