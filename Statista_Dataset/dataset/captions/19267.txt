This statistic shows Scotland 's budget balance , excluding revenue generated by the North Sea , as a share of the United Kingdom ( UK ) 's gross domestic product ( GDP ) from 2008/09 to 2018/19 . The deficit increased between 2008/09 and 2009/10 but has slowly been decreasing from 2009/10 onwards . However , Scotland 's deficit as a share of the UK 's GDP is still higher than pre-recession levels .
