This statistic depicts the percentage of workers covered by self-funded health insurance plans in the U.S. from 1999 to 2020 . According to the data , among all firms the percentage of employees covered by self-funded plans had increased from 44 percent in 1999 to a record high of 67 percent in 2020 . Self-funded plans are those in which companies choose to pay for some or all of the health services of their workers directly rather than purchasing health insurance for them .
