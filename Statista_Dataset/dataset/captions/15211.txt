Face masks ' price inflation in the view of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak in Russia was monitored by the Federal Antimonopoly Service ( FAS ) . As a result , ceiling prices of the named product fell in most regions of the country . The most significant fall of over 95 percent during the second week of March relative to the previous one was measured in the Belgorod region .
