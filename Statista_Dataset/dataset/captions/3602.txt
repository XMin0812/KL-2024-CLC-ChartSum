The statistic shows gross domestic product ( GDP ) per capita in Pakistan from 1984 to 2019 . GDP is the total value of all goods and services produced in a country in a year . It is considered to be a very important indicator of the economic strength of a country and a positive change is an indicator of economic growth . In 2019 , the GDP per capita in Pakistan amounted to around 1,348.68 U.S. dollars .
