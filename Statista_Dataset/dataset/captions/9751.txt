Despite coming from a very low base , the number of electric cars in Italy continued its positive trend to exceed 10,500 new registrations in 2019 . The same positive trend can be noticed with other alternative types , such as hybrid vehicles , specifically petrol-electric and diesel-electric vehicles . In total , new registrations of hybrid electric vehicles , plug-in hybrid electric vehicles , and range extenders reached 116,327 units in 2019 .
