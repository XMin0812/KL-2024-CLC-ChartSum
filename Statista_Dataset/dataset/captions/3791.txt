As of January 3 , 2021 , there have been 584,454 deaths across Europe due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) since the first recorded European death on February 15 . The number of deaths first started to increase significantly during March , and again in November . The week ending November 29 , was the deadliest week so far with 37,042 deaths recorded . Italy currently has the highest amount of deaths of any country in Europe at 75,332 . The worldwide number of confirmed cases of coronavirus was over 86 million as of January 3 , 2021 .
