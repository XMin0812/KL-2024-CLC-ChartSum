As of 2020 , Demba Savage , an active player of FC Honka , lead the score board of the Finnish Football League ( Veikkausliiga ) with 75 goals . He was followed by the FC Inter player Timo Furuholm with 68 goals , and the RoPS striker Aleksandr Kokko with 63 goals . Football is a beloved hobby sport in Finland , but it falls behind ice hockey in popularity as professional spectator sport .
