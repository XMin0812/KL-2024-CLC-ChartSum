The statistic represents the U.S. corporate income tax revenues from 2000 to 2019 with an additional forecast from 2020 to 2030 as a percentage of the Gross Domestic Product . Corporate income tax revenues amounted to 230 billion U.S. dollars in 2019 , which was about 1.1 percent of the U.S. GDP . The forecast predicts an increase in corporate income tax revenues up to 406 billion U.S. dollars in 2030 , which would be about 1.3 percent of the projected U.S. GDP .
