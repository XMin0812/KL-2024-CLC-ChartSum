The results of a survey showed most concern for unavailability of essential items such as groceries and medicine as a result of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic in India in April 2020 . The leading concerns were related to contracting the virus and being able to access healthcare , followed by the impact on mental health . A lockdown was enforced on March 25 , 2020 for 21 days , the largest in the world , restricting 1.3 billion people , now extended until May 3 , 2020 .
