Mercadona was the leading supermarket chain in Spain in 2019 , with over one quarter of the country 's market share . Carrefour held 8.7 percent of this share , ranking the French multinational retailer second on the list . The Spanish DIA Group followed third , taking up 8.7 percent of the market share in the Mediterranean country . Other important supermarkets include Eroski , Lidl and Grupo Auchan , also known as Alcampo in Spain .
