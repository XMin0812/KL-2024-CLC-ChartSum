This statistic shows the American customer satisfaction index scores of Wendy 's restaurants in the United States from 2000 to 2020 . Wendy 's had an ACSI score of 76 in 2020.The limited-service restaurant industry was not measured in 2004 . In 2019 , there were nearly five and a half thousand franchised and 357 company-owned Wendy 's restaurants in the U.S .
