The statistic shows the U.S. budget balance from 2000 to 2019 with an additional forecast from 2020 to 2030 , as a percentage of the Gross Domestic Product . State deficit amounted to 984 billion U.S. dollars in 2019 , which was about 4.6 percent of the U.S. GDP . The forecast predicts an increase in state deficit up to 1.74 trillion U.S. dollars in 2030 , which would be about 5.4 percent of the U.S. GDP .
