This statistic displays the growth in the United States and European market for sleep disorder diagnostic devices from 2013 to 2017 in million U.S. dollars . In 2013 , the industry earned a revenue of 95.6 million U.S. dollars . Clinical polysymnogram and ambulatory polysymnogram systems are included in this prediction . Devices that can be used in a home and clinical setting are growing in demand . This may be due to limited number of technicians or specialized physicians and long wait times .
