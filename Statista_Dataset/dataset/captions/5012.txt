The highest position of Sweden 's national football team in the FIFA World Ranking ever reached was in 1994 , when they came at fourth place . In this year , Sweden was in the play-off for the third place in the 1994 FIFA World Cup . The teams lowest rank was 44 , which was reached in 2014 . The women 's national team 's highest position was at third place in 2007 , when Sweden was part of the FIFA Women 's World Cup and made the tenth position together with Japan .
