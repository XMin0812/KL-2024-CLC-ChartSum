This statistic shows the number of cars sold by Mazda in the Republic of Ireland between 2009 and 2019 . In the period of consideration , Irish sales of Mazda cars oscillated . From 2009 to 2016 , Mazda sales grew to its peak , at approximately three thousand units as of 2016 . From 2017 onwards , Irish Mazda sales declined and has fluctuated . In 2019 , Mazda sales amounted to 1.9 thousand units , a decrease of roughly 15 percent in comparison with the year prior .
