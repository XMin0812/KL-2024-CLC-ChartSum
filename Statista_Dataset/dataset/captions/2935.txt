In May 2020 , up to six percent of all online news and posts related to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) and released in Italy were false or not accurate . The percentage was calculated on the average volume of posts and articles published by the Italian media outlets , including posts on social media . The peak in the release of fake news was registered in the early stage of the pandemic at the end of January 2020 , with 7.3 percent of the coronavirus-related information .
