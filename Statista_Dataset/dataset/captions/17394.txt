Data on the number of persons arrested in England and Wales ( UK ) for violence against the person from 2015/16 to 2019/20 shows that at the outset of this period there was a high number of persons arrested in England and Wales for violence against the person , but since then the number of persons arrested had mostly been dropping year on year . In the year of 2019/29 it amounted to 261,747 cases of arrest .
