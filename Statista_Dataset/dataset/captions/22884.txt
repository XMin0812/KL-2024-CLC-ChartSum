This statistic illustrates the Index of Private Housing Rental Prices ( IPHRP ) in England from January 2016 to July 2020 . The Index of Private Housing Rental Prices measures the change in price of renting residential property from private landlords , based on an index value of 100 in January 2015 . It can be seen that the IPHRP increased gradually during the time period under observation , reaching a value of 109.8 index points as of July 2020 . This was an increase of 1.6 index points in comparison to July 2020 , when the IPHRP for England stood at a value of 108.2 index points .
