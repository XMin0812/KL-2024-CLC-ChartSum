This statistic shows the average annual expenditure on slipcovers and decorative pillows per consumer unit in the United States from 2007 to 2019 . In 2019 , the country 's average expenditure on slipcovers and decorative pillows amounted to 5.59 U.S. dollars per consumer unit . Reflecting the growing complexity of mattress designs , the pillow segment continues to evolve with new constructions , styles and features designed to deliver a better sleep experience . Today 's producers are offering a wide range of options to consumers , from basic fiber and shredded foam models at the lower end to sophisticated latex , memory foam , down-fill , gel and even innerspring and air models at the medium to higher ends .
