This statistic ranks the leading films by weekend box office revenue in France in May 2018 . The movie `` Avengers : Infinity War '' was the highest grossing film that month , at roughly 15.8 million U.S. dollars . `` Deadpool 2 '' and `` Rampage ( 2018 ) '' ranked second and third highest , at 12.5 million U.S. dollars and 4.7 million U.S. dollars , respectively .
