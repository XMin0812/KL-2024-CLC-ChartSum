This statistic shows the national debt of Botswana from 2015 to 2019 , with projections up until 2025 , in relation to the gross domestic product ( GDP ) . The figures refer to the whole country and include the debts of the state , the communities , the municipalities and the social insurances . In 2019 , the national debt of Botswana amounted to approximately 15.1 percent of the GDP .
