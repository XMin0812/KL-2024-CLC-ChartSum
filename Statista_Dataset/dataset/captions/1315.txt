Average household electricity prices in Sweden were 18.26 euro cents per kilowatt-hour ( kWh ) in the first half of 2020 . This was the lowest electricity price since the first half of 2010 , when prices averaged 18.39 euro cents per kWh . Households with an annual consumption of 2,500 to 5,000 kWh paid less than those with a consumption of up to 2,499 kWh .
