Denso 's revenue contracted by almost four percent between 2019 and 2020 , not unlike other companies in the Japanese auto sector . The automotive supplier generated around five trillion Japanese yen or 47.8 billion U.S. dollars in revenue in 2020 . Denso 's business was hampered by a slowing Japanese economy , trade tensions between China and the United States , as well as the outbreak of Covid-19 .
