The budget limit for Real Madrid was set at approximately 470 million euros during the 2020/2021 season . Spain 's two most popular football squads – Real Madrid and FC Barcelona – are also well known around the world both for their success and the hefty salaries of their members . To pay for those salaries , teams have budgets for which they need to set certain limitations . FC Barcelona 's budget was the second with the most flexibility , with up to 383 million euros .
