In 2019 , U.S. book store sales revenue dropped to ten billion U.S. dollars , down from 10.6 billion in the previous year . Previous trends suggest that the figure will continue to fall after a small increase between 2017 and 2018 , and the COVID-19 outbreak will not make things any easier for the book market . Whilst some book fans in the U.S. reported reading more as a result of the pandemic , only time will tell whether or not increased reading will lead to more sales .
