The travel and tourism sector contributed directly to roughly 10.8 percent to the gross domestic product ( GDP ) of Saint Kitts and Nevis in 2019 . This was the highest contribution registered in the past decade and the first above 10 percent during the same period . The two-island country is a former British colony located in the Caribbean West Indies . In 2019 , the internal travel and tourism consumption of St. Kitts and Nevis also reached its highest level since 2010 , with more than 456 million U.S. dollars .
