This statistic shows the most popular global events on Twitter , measured in amount of tweets per second generated between January 2011 and February 2012 . On March 11 , 2011 , the earthquake and tsunami in Japan generated 5,530 tweets per second . In December 2011 , a television airing of the 1986 animated Studio Ghibli film Castle in the Sky set the record of being the most-tweeted-about event with 25,088 tweets per second . Furthermore , during the 2012 UEFA European Football Championship finale between Spain and Italy , messages spiked at 13,358 tweets per second during the fourth goal for Spain .
