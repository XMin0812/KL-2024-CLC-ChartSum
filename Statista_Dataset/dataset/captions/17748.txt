This statistic shows the total number of passengers uplifted by BA CityFlyer Ltd in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2008 to 2018 . BA CityFlyer is a subsidiary of British Airways based at London City Airport and Edinburgh Airpot . BA CityFlyer was founded in 2007 and has shown strong growth since then . BA CityFlyer 's passengers rose from 713,670 in 2008 to over 2.8 million passengers in 2019 . The company has not seen a decline in passenger numbers since 2009 .
