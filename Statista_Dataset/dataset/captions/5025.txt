This statistic shows the popular opinion on extreme quarantine measures potentially taken by the government in order to contain the spread of the novel coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in France in 2020 as of February 29 , 2020 . According to Ipsos , 70 percent of respondents would accept total quarantine measures to manage the risk .
