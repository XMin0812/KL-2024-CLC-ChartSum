This statistic depicts the opinion of Italians on the basic income guarantee , in Italy as of May 2018 . A basic income guarantee ( known also as basic income , citizen 's income , unconditional basic income , universal basic income etc . ) is a new kind of welfare program in which all citizens / permanent residents of a country receive from the government a regular , livable and unconditional amount of money . According to data , roughly 40 percent of the interviewed Italians were contrary to the introduction of citizen 's income in their country .
