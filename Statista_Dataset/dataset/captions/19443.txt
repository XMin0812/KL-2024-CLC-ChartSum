This statistic illustrates the share of municipalities covered by 4G/4G Plus in the Italian region of Sicily as of 2018 , broken down by province . In 2018 , Caltanissetta was the province with the lowest mobile internet penetration rate , roughly 91 percent . By comparison , all the municipalities in the provinces of Enna , Ragusa , Trapani , and Catania were covered by the 4G network .
