The number of new patients hospitalized due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Norway peaked on March 26 , 2020 , when there was a total of 56 new patients . After a period of relatively low admissions , a second increase of hospitalizations took place at the beginning of November . For instance , 19 patients were admitted to the hospital on November 3 . To this day , 1,643 people have been hospitalized in Norway due to the coronavirus . The number of people who were or had been confirmed infected by the virus in the country had reached a total of 34,747 as of November 26 , 2020 .
