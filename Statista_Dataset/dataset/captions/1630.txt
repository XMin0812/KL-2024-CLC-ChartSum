In the third quarter of 2020 , Dell held a 15.2 percent share of global PC unit shipments , a fall from the 16.4 percent share the company held in the second quarter of 2020 . Dell 's market share of PC shipments worldwide remained above 16 percent for the entire year of 2019 , making Dell the number three PC vendor in the world following Lenovo and Hewlett Packard . Of the total global personal computer unit shipments of around 261 million in 2018 , Dell was responsible for almost 44 million .
