This statistic shows the amount of stop and searches carried out by the police in England and Wales under section 44/47A of the Terrorism Act ( 2000 ) from fiscal year 2001/2003 to fiscal year 2010/2011 . There was a huge increase in stop and searches of this type in 2007/2008 before increasing again the following year . This was , however , the peak . Over the next four years , stop and searches under this legislation reduced to zero .
