This graph shows the real value added to the Gross Domestic Product ( GDP ) of New York in 2019 , by industry . In 2019 , the mining industry added 1.14 billion chained 2012 U.S. dollars of value to the state GDP . Total real GDP amounted to about 1.49 trillion chained 2012 U.S. dollars . In 2019 , the per capita personal income in New York was 71,717 U.S. dollars , an increase from 68,657 dollars in 2018 .
