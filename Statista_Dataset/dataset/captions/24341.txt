This statistic represents the percent change of the PNC Christmas Price Index from 1992 to 2019 . According to the source , the index reflects the current cost for one set of each of the gifts given in the song `` The Twelve Days of Christmas '' . With a decrease of 21.7 percent over previous year , 1995 saw the greatest decline in cost for these gifts . The cost for the seven swans-a-swimming - the most expensive item in the index - was at 13,125 U.S. dollars in 2019 .
