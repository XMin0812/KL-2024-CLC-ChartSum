The statistic shows the total volume of merger and acquisition ( M & A ) transactions in Central and Eastern Europe from 2011 to 2019 . The number of deals decreased throughout the period , falling from 4,175 deals in 2011 to 1,985 deals in 2016 . 2017 and 2018 saw a slight increase from the previous year , later dropping again to 1,958 deals in 2019 .
