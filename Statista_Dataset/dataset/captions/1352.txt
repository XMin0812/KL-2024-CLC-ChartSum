As of January 3 , 2021 , the United Kingdom had the highest number of reported coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) cases in Europe in the preceding two weeks at 614,632 . Additionally , Russia has recorded 497,470 cases in the last two weeks . Russia has also been the worst affected country in Europe overall since the pandemic began . There have been more than 26.3 million cases of coronavirus in Europe . Furthermore , the coronavirus has so far been responsible for 584,454 confirmed deaths in Europe as of January 3 , with 75,332 of the deaths occurring in Italy .
