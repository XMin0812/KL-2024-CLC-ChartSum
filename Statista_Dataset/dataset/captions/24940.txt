As of 2019 , the 151-meter-tall MGM Cotai in Macau was the most expensive building in China , with a cost of 3.4 billion U.S. dollars . It was the winner of Emporis Skyscraper Award in 2018 with its innovative design . Shanghai Tower and The Venetian Macao ranked second place , both cost 2.4 billion U.S. dollars .
