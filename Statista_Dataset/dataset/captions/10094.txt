This statistic shows the number of Nissan Leaf units sold every month in the United Kingdom ( UK ) between July 2013 and June 2015 . Total sales over this period came to 8,269 . The Nissan Leaf was the most popular electric car in the United Kingdom and is 100 percent electric , thus produces zero road emissions . March 2015 was the most successful month for the Nissan Leaf with 1,254 units sold .
