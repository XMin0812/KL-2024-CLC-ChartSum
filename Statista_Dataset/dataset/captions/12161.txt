2020 has seen a slight decrease in the penetration of devices able to connect to the internet i.e . PCs , laptops or tablet computers . In the UK , 81 percent of adults owned at least one connected device in 2020 , compared to 82 percent in 2019 . The use of connected devices in households is becoming a trend , and the number of connected devices per person was high among respondents from the UK . In addition , there were many smart home devices such as smart thermostats or smartphones .
