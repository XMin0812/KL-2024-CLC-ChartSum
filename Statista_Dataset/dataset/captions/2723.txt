The number of Swedes with higher education , a post-secondary education of three years or more , increased since 2000 , while the number without decreased . In 2019 , around 37 percent of the population had at least some level of post-secondary education . Among them , 21.7 percent had an post-secondary education of three years or more . During the fall semester 2018 , 349,300 students were registered at universities or higher education institutions in Sweden , which was the highest number in five years .
