The annual spending on pets and pet related products in the UK from 2005 to 2019 has had ups and downs . Generally , however , spending has increased during this time period . From 2005 to 2009 and from 2011 to 2012 , spending in this category was on a downward trend . However , from 2015 onwards , annual spending on pets and related products has been on an upward trend , reaching 6.5 billion British pounds in 2019 .
