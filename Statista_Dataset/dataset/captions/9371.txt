Topping the ranking of the world 's highest earning authors in 2019 was J.K. Rowling , with an income of 92 million U.S. dollars . Rowling is best known for her 'Harry Potter ' series , but came under fire in May 2020 for comments on Twitter which users felt were transphobic . The author quickly became embroiled in a social media row with fans and LGBQTIA+ rights advocates , as well as other renowned authors including Stephen King .
