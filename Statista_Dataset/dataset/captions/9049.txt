This statistic outlines AngloGold Ashanti 's number of employees from 2009 to 2019 . AngloGold Ashanti Limited is a gold mining company from Johannesburg , South Africa . The company was formed in 2004 by the fusion of the South African AngloGold and the Ghanaian Ashanti Goldfields Corporation . In 2019 , AngloGold Ashanti had an average number of 34,263 employees ( including contractors ) .
