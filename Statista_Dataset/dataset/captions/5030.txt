The statistic shows the market capitalization of the leading companies listed on the Milan Stock Exchange in Italy as of August 2020 . As of the survey period , the leading company listed on the Milan Stock Exchange was Enel , operating in the energy sector , with a market capitalization of over 77 billion euros . Second on the list came ENI , also operating in the energy sector , with a market capitalization of nearly 29 billion euros . Third came Ferrari with a market cap of 31.8 billion euros .
