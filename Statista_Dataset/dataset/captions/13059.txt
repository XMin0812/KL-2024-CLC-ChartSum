In Italy , in September 2020 the highest audience share of the TV channel Rai 1 on a daily average was reported between from 6 p.m. to 8.30 p.m. , with a rate of 20.67 percent . By contrast , the least favorite time range to watch Rai 1 by Italians was from 3 p.m. to 6 p.m . Indeed , the corresponding audience share was 12.55 percent .
