This statistic displays the numbers of seats obtained by each party in the parliamentary elections of April 14 , 2019 in Finland . Suomen Sosialidemokraattinen Puolue ( Social Democratic Party ) obtained a slight majority in the parliament with 40 seats , followed closely behind by Perussuomalaiset ( Finns Party ) with 39 seats . Kansallinen Kokoomus ( National Coalition Party ) came third with 38 seats . The official parliamentary election day took place on April 14 , 2019 .
