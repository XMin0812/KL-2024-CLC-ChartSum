According to a forecast from November 2020 , the unemployment rate in Italy could reach 9.9 percent of the total labor force in 2020 , due to the impact of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak . The rate is then expected to increase to 11.6 percent in 2021 . Italy 's unemployment rate stood at 9.9 percent already in 2019 , one of the lowest rates of the last years .
