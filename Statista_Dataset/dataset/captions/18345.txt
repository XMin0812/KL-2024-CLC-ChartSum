As of May 2020 , 40.2 percent of TikTok users in the United States had a household income of 100,000 USD and over . In contrast , only 7.2 percent of the social app 's U.S. audience had an income of $ 60k to $ 75k . TikTok is a video-sharing social networking service founded in 2012 and owned by ByteDance .
