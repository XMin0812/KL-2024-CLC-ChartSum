This statistic represents Ford 's revenue in the fiscal years of 2009 to 2018 in the United Kingdom ( UK ) , in million U.S. dollars . In the period under consideration , Ford 's revenue fluctuated . Between 2009 and 2013 , this value rose from 8.6 billion U.S. dollars to 10 billion U.S. dollars . In 2014 , Ford 's revenue in the UK reached its peak , at 11.7 billion U.S. dollars . From 2015 onwards , this value has continuously decreased . Ford 's revenue amounted to 9.6 billion U.S. dollars in 2018 .
