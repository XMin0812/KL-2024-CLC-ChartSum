The statistic shows the result of the Survey Stili d'Italia conducted by Italiani.Coop on the polyamorous community from May to June 2018 . When Italians where asked to state what their relationship the community is , two percent claimed to belong to it . While another nine percent declared to have friends in the community or to be interested in it , 60 percent of the people interviewed did n't know what they were asked about .
