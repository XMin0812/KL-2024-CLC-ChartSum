The COVID-19 pandemic at the beginning of 2020 hit the sports industry hard . Many professional leagues across the globe suspended their seasons and events have been cancelled . The Olympic Games were due to take place in Tokyo at the end of July 2020 but , on March 24 , the International Olympic Committee ( IOC ) took the decision to delay the Games until 2021 . Given the commitment and training involved in the lead up to an Olympic year , this postponement has had a significant impact on the 11,000 Olympic athletes and 4,400 Paralympic athletes who were due to compete in the event .
