In 2018 , Colombia was the deadliest Latin American country for land activists and environmental defenders . Up to 24 such activists were murdered in Colombia in that year . Meanwhile , in Brazil , at least 20 environmentalists were killed . Guatemala and Mexico were also among the countries with the highest number of this type of politically-motivated homicides in Latin America . In 2018 , Chile made it into this infamous list , with two murders of environmentalists or activists , after recording no such cases one year earlier .
