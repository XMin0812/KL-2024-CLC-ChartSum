Between 2000 and 2019 , the number of foreign prisoners in Italy reached its peak in 2010 at 24,954 inmates . As of June 2019 , the population of foreign individuals in custody added up to 20,224 people . By the end of 2018 , the number of prisoners from Morocco amounted to 3.7 thousand individuals . Inmates from the European Union accounted for the second largest group of foreign prisoners .
