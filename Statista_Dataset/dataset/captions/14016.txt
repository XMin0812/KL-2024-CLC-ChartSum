In 2019 , 27.8 percent of Italians aged 25 to 29 years held a university degree . On the other hand , the population aged 65 years and over represented the group with the lowest share of people who achieved a university degree ( 8.1 percent ) . In the academic year 2018/2019 , Italy counted 1.7 million students . The largest part of students were enrolled in a bachelor program , accounting for over 60 percent of all students nationwide .
