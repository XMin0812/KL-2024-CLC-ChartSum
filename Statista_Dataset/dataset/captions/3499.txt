The graph presents data on the age at which comic book readers started reading comic books as of June 2013 . During a survey , 60 percent of respondents stated they started reading comic books before they turned ten . In the same survey , 67 percent of respondents stated that they read more comic books than they used to and 75 percent preferred print comic books over digital editions . Some of the leading comic book publishers include Marvel Comics , DC Entertainment , and Image Comics .
