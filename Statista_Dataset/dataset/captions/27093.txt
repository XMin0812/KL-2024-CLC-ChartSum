How many university students are registered in Denmark ? During the past ten years , the number of registered university students in Denmark increased . The fewest students were enrolled in Danish universities in 2009 , amounting to 122.4 thousand students . In comparison , the highest amount of students was registered in 2014 , with nearly 162 thousand university students . The following years , fewer students admitted to university institutions , amounting to 148,981 registrations as of 2019 .
