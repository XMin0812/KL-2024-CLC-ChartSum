This statistic shows the total annual turnover of the retail sale of carpets and other floor coverings ( excluding bathroom mats , rush and door mats ) in the United Kingdom from 2008 to 2018 . Carpet retail sales turnover declined in from 3.2 billion in 2011 to 2.8 billion British pounds in 2013 . By 2018 , this figure peaked at 3.8 billion British pounds for the year .
