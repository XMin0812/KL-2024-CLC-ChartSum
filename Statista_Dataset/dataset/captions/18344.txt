As of March 23 , 2020 , around 45 percent of Canadians aged 18 to 24 years stated they had personally lost work or been laid off due to coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) . This statistic shows the percentage of Canadian adults who had personally lost work or been laid off as a result of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) as of March 23 , 2020 .
