In a survey of marketers and exhibitors in the United States gauging the state of the exhibition industry , respondents were asked about their perception of trade shows as a marketing tool in 2020 . The results of the survey show that two percent of respondents reported feeling pessimistic about its effectiveness , while 42 felt hopeful . However , the questions were asked before the global coronavirus pandemic , which greatly affected the industry .
