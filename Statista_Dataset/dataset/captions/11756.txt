The global mining industry has been impacted by the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) crisis just as every industry has . There have been supply chain delay interruptions around the world for parts , equipment , and consumables which impact productivity . Access to mining sites have also been an issue , as many countries have engaged in social distancing and quarantine measures . AngloAmerican has announced that they will reduce their capital expenditure by one billion U.S. dollars in 2020 . For further information about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , please visit our dedicated Fact and Figures page .
