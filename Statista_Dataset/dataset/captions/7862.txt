The median age of the population of Belgium was slightly aging from 2010 to 2020 and was predicted to further age in the years to come . The median age divides a population into two equal-sized groups . This single index summarizes the age distribution of Belgians . In other words , half of the Belgian population was younger than 41.87 years old in 2020 , and the remaining half was aged over 41.87 years .
