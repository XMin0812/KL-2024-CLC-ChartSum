This statistic shows the number of cars sold by Toyota in Austria between 2010 and 2019 . In the period of consideration , sales of Toyota cars has been decreasing despite some oscillation . Volumes sales recorded its peak in 2010 , when 9,040 Toyota vehicles were sold in the Austrian market . In 2019 , Toyota sold 6,925 units in Austria , a decrease of roughly 23 percent in comparison with the peak year .
