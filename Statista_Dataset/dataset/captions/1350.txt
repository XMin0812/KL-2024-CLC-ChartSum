Italy edged ahead of Spain to become the world 's top exporter of wine in 2019 . The country shipped 21.6 million hectoliters of the beverage in that year , compared to Italy 's 21.3 million and France 's 14.2 million . 2019 saw a dip in wine production worldwide compared to 2018 . Overall , 260 million hectoliters of wine were produced compared to 294 million in 2018 .
