This statistic shows the total annual turnover of the retail sales of pharmaceutical products in the United Kingdom from 2008 to 2015 . Throughout the eight year period , pharmaceutical products retail turnover has increased from 3.52 billion British pounds in 2008 to 4.99 billion British pounds in 2015 . In 2014 , the annual turnover of retail sales of pharmaceutical products amounted to 5.56 billion British pounds the was the highest amount during this period .
