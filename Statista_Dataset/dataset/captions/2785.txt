Telenor AS had the biggest turnover among other leading telecom companies in Denmark in 2020 . The enterprise had a turnover of over 3.9 billion Danish kroner . HI3G Denmark ApS followed relatively closely with a turnover of 2.7 billion Danish kroner . Regarding the mobile Telenor subscribers in Denmark in 2018 , nearly 1.7 million were mobile subscriptions . The fixed telephony subscriptions were 48 thousand and the fixed internet ones were 123 thousand that year .
