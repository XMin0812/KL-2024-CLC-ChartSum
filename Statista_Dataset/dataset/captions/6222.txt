The timeline shows Walmart 's operating income worldwide from fiscal year 2006 to 2020 . In 2020 , Walmart 's global operating income amounted to about 20.6 billion U.S. dollars . Walmart , founded in 1962 , is an American multinational retailer corporation that runs chains of large discount department stores and warehouse stores . Walmart is the world 's largest retailer and the United States ' largest grocery retailer as well .
