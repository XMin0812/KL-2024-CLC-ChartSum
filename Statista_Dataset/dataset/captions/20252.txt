As of January 10 , 2021 , the total number of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) cases in Algeria reached 102,144 . The cumulative number of cases in the country particularly increased in November 2020 , concurrently with the increment of the daily infections . As of January 6 , 2021 , Algeria recorded the seventh-highest number of total COVID-19 cases within the African countries since the beginning of the pandemic .
