On January 6 , 2020 , eight patients were admitted to intensive care in Sweden due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) . The number of new patients in intensive care with confirmed coronavirus infection in Sweden increased rapidly since March 6 , 2020 , when the there was one patient , and peaked on April 22 , 2020 when the new number of patients had grown to 49 . The following period , the number started to drop and was down to just a few or no new patients per day . In October , however , an increasing number of patients were transferred to intensive care . The number of people who were confirmed infected by the virus in the country had reached a total of 482,284 as of January 6 , 2021 .
