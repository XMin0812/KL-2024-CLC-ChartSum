This statistic illustrates how many Vauxhall Mokka passenger cars were registered for the first time annually in Great Britain , between 2012 and 2018 . A peak in sales of the Vauxhall Mokka was observed in 2015 , when over 44,000 units were newly registered . By 2019 , sales volume declined , with a total of 22,162 Vauxhall Mokka units newly registered .
