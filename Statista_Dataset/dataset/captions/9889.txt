Which brand sells the most motorcycles in Belgium ? BMW topped a ranking for newly sold motorbikes in Belgium in 2019 , with Yamaha and Honda completing the top three . The German brand sold around 3,300 bikes in the country , whereas Yamaha and Honda both sold around 3,100 and 2,900 two-wheeled vehicles , respectively . Honda observed a noticeable decline in its two-wheeler sales following organizational issues in the Benelux region between 2017 and 2018 .
