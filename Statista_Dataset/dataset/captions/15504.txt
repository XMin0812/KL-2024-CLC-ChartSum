The statistic shows the average number of viewers of the finale of the Eurovision Song Contest in Italy from 1987 to 2020 . As of the survey period , the average audience peaked in 1991 at about 6.7 million viewers . Due to the impact of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , the Eurovision Song Contest 2020 was cancelled and a replacement show named Eurovision : Europe Shine a Light was broadcasted instead of the contest 's finale .
