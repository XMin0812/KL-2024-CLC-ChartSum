This statistic shows the number of Megafon mobile subscribers from 2014 to 2018 . In 2017 , Megafon had a total mobile subscriber base of 76.9 million . Megafon operates in 82 different regions , and uses its mobile subsidiaries Aquafon , Ostelecom , TT Mobile , Smarts , and Scartel to cater to some regions outside of Russia .
