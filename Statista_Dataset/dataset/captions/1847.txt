Belgium has a multicultural landscape ; a variety of nationalities coexist in the country . Brussels houses most of the European Union institutions . This is the reason behind the nickname `` capital of Europe '' . In 2019 , over one million foreigners were living in the country . Of the variety of nationalities in Belgium , three main origins were present on its soil . In 2018 , over 470 thousand people originated from France , Italy , or the Netherlands .
