In the final quarter of 2020 , The Walt Disney Company reported that Hulu had 36.6 million paid subscribers , up from 28.5 million in the corresponding quarter of the previous fiscal year . Hulu has several different pricing plans to cater to varying consumer preferences , with the most basic option costing $ 5.99 per month and the priciest monthly subscription package fixed at $ 50.99 as of September 2019 . The latter includes premium membership ( which offers customers ad-free viewing ) , as well as live TV access , giving subscribers the opportunity to watch content from over 60 channels including CBS , NBC , and Food Network among many others .
