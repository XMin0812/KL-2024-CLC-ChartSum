The highest growth in sales in the e-commerce industry during the COVID-19 pandemic in Romania was registered in the beauty sector , which totaled a growth of 309.5 percent in April 2020 , compared to 2019 . At the same time , books , movies and music recorded a growth in their online sales of 22.4 percent .
