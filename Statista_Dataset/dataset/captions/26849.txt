This statistic displays the number of adults admitted to hospital for a respiratory disease caused by smoking in England ( United Kingdom ) from 2000/01 to 2019/20 . In 2000/01 there were approximately 152 thousand adults admitted to hospital for a respiratory disease caused by smoking , by 2019/20 the number of hospital admissions had increased to approximately 410 thousand , this was the largest number of admissions during the seventeen-year period .
