In season 2019 , the winning team of the Finnish Football League ( Veikkausliiga ) was KuPS , a professional footballers ' club from Eastern Finland . FC Inter , a professional football club based in Turku , ranked second . For the season 2019 , Veikkausliiga adopted a new league format . After the regular season ( Runkosarja ) , the top six teams advanced to the Championship Series ( Mestaruussarja ) , which determines the champion and European tournament qualification places . Football is a beloved hobby sport in Finland , but it falls behind ice hockey in popularity as professional spectator sport .
