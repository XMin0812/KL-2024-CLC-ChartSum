Businessman and rally driver Michał Sołowow was the wealthiest person in Poland , with assets of 15.6 billion zloty in 2020 . Businessman Zygmunt Solorz-Żak was next , with 12 billion zloty . Two leading representatives of the game developing industry in Poland made it in the top ten - CD Projekt 's Marcin Iwiński and Techland 's Paweł Marchewka .
