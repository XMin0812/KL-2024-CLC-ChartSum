In the academic year 2018/2019 , over 100 thousand students were enrolled at La Sapienza University of Rome , making it the largest Italian university . Furthermore , the University of Bologna , which also ranks as the best Italian university , followed in the list with 81 thousand students , while the University of Naples Federico II , ranked third . In total , 1.7 million students were enrolled at Italian universities in the academic year 2017/2018 .
