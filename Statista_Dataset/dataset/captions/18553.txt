The Legal Amazon , known as `` Amazônia Legal '' in Portuguese , is an area of more than five million square kilometers comprising the Brazilian states of Acre , Amapá , Amazonas , Maranhão , Mato Grosso , Pará , Rondônia , Roraima and Tocantins . This political and geographical region was created by Brazilian federal law dating back to 1953 in order to promote special protection and development policies for the Amazon area . The Brazilian state that occupies the largest area of the Legal Amazon is Amazonas , amounting to almost 1.6 million square kilometers , followed by the state of Pará , with 1.25 million square kilometers .
