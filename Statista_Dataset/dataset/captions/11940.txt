This statistic shows the richest self-made women billionaires in the United States as of 2017 . That year , Marian Ilitch was the richest female self-made billionaire in the United States with a net worth of 5.1 billion U.S. dollars . Marian Ilitch is one of the co-founders of Little Ceasars Pizza . Oprah Winfrey shares the third rank with businesswoman Judy Love , both with a net worth of 2.9 billion U.S. dollars each .
