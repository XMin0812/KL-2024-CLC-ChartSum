As of January 4 , 2021 , the number of confirmed COVID-19 cases in Finland reached a total of 36,919 . The majority of the cases have been discovered in the region of Helsinki and Uusimaa , where the health care district has confirmed in total 21,659 cases . Most of the new COVID-19 cases , reported in December , were also found in Helsinki and Uusimaa , although the number of cases has risen in other regions . The first case of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Finland was confirmed on January 29 , 2020 . The worldwide number of confirmed cases of coronavirus was roughly 85.5 million on January 4 , 2021 .
