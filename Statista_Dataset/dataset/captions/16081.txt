In the second quarter of 2020 , the revenue index of the hotel industry in the Netherlands decreased to 30.5 ( index 2015=100 ) . This is an decrease compared to the same quarter in the previous year , when the revenue index was 146.1 . The number of hotels in the Netherlands increased between 2013 and 2019 . While there were just over 3,500 hotels in the Netherlands in 2013 , this figure increased to approximately 3,800 hotels by June 2019 .
