From 2009 to 2018 , the average duration of marriage was on an overall rise in Belgium . Although the rise was modest , similar figures were found in the Netherlands , thus showing a similar tendency . From a regional perspective , marriage duration was higher in the region of Wallonia than in Flanders . However , the Brussels-Capital region registered the lowest average duration of marriages . Regardless of duration , Belgium 's townhalls registered around 45.000 Marriages in 2018 .
