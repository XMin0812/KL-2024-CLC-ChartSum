This statistic shows the sales volume of tableware and kitchenware of plastic based on PRODCOM estimates in the United Kingdom ( UK ) between 2010 and 2019 . The UK experienced an increase in sales volume over this period of time . The sales volume was at its lowest at a value of 41.5 million kilograms in 2011 , before peaking at 67.2 million kilograms in 2013 . In 2019 , the sales volume stood at 46.8 million kilograms .
