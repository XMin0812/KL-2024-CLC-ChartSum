As of January 6 , 2021 , Malaysia had around 125 thousand confirmed cases of COVID-19 , and 513 deaths from the virus . Malaysia is currently anticipating another lockdown restrictions as confirmed cases od COVID-19 hit new record high . Malaysia is currently one out of more than 200 countries and territories battling with the novel coronavirus .
