This statistic shows the monthly trend in the amount spent on hardware , paints and glass ( sales value ) in Great Britain from January 2016 to October 2020 , as an index of sales per week . Over this period , sales of hardware , paints and glass remained relatively stable , with the exception of April 2020 , where index of sales reached a low of 60.5 due to the coronavirus crisis . In October 2020 the value index measured at 108.6 .
