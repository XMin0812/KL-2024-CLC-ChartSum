This statistic shows the annual net income of the Chinese search engine Baidu from 2008 to 2019 . In 2019 , Baidu 's reported a net loss of 328 million U.S. dollars for the first time over the past decade . That year , there was a significant rise in the enterprise 's operating costs and expenses . The tech giant increased its research and development costs to 2.6 billion U.S. dollars .
