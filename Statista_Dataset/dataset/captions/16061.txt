Due to the outbreak of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Italy in February 2020 , during the first days of March the Italian government discussed the possibility of closing all schools and universities nationwide . According to the result of an analysis of Twitter posts , the sentiment towards the possibility of stopping all activities in schools was negative . On March 5 , all schools and universities in Italy closed due to the rising number of coronavirus cases .
