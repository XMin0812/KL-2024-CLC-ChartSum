This statistic displays the annual number of registered crimes in Belgium from 2009 to the first semester of 2019 . The number of registered crimes reached a peak in 2011 with about 1.06 million registered crimes . The number gradually decreased after 2011 , to roughly 884 thousand registered crimes in 2018 . As of the first semester of 2019 , the number of registered crimes in Belgium reached 427 thousand crimes .
