In 2020 , the highest paid baseball player in South Korea was Dae-ho Lee from the Lotte Giants team , according to data from the Korea Baseball Organization ( KBO ) . He was paid an annual salary of approximately 2.5 billion South Korean won , and Kia 's Hyeon-jong Yang came in second with a salary of 2.3 billion won . The amount Dae-ho Lee was paid comes as no surprise as he is the best batter Lotte has ever had and is regarded as one of the most popular players in the whole KBO . He has previously played in both the American Major League Baseball and the Japanese Nippon Professional Baseball .
