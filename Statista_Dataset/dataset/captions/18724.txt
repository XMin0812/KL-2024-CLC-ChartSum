The timeline depicts the gate receipts of the San Francisco Giants from 2009 to 2019 . In 2019 , the gate receipts of the San Francisco Giants were at 154 million U.S. dollars . In 2009 , the gate receipts of the franchise were at 82 million U.S. dollars , but by 2014 they had doubled , reaching 165 million U.S. dollars .
