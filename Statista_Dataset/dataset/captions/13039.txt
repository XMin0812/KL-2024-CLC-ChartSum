This statistic shows a timeline with the global number of monthly active King Digital Entertainment users as of the fourth quarter of 2015 . As of the most recently reported quarter , 128 million people accessed the mobile gaming company 's apps per day . Popular titles include Candy Crush Saga , Farm Ville Saga as well as Bubble Witch Saga , all of which are monetized through in-game purchases . King 's gaming apps are available on Facebook as well as on mobile platforms .
