This statistic represents the number of Terex employees worldwide between the fiscal year of 2009 and the fiscal year of 2015 . In the fiscal year of 2012 , the Connecticut-based company employed 20,900 people . The company 's major business segments include materials processing and mining equipment , aerial work platforms , and cranes . Terex shares plunged in the second quarter of 2016 after merger talks with China 's Zoomlion went nowhere .
