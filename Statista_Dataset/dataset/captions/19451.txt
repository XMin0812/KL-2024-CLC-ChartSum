As of 2017 , the expenditure on health in Nigeria equaled to 3.8 percent of the country 's GDP . Nevertheless , Nigeria 's spending on health experienced a slight increase in the past years . In the OECD countries , the average percentage of GDP spent on healthcare was 8.8 percent . The member countries of OECD are , however , mostly high-income countries , whereas Nigeria is an emerging economy and it belongs to countries with lower middle-incomes .
