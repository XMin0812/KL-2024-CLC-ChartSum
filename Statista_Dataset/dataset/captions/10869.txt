This statistic depicts the share of global distribution of Luxottica sales in 2019 , by channel . In 2019 , 65.66 percent of Luxottica 's sales came from their retail channel . Founded in 1961 in Agordo , Italy , the Luxottica Group S.p.A. is the world 's largest eyewear company . Its best known brands include Ray-Ban , Persol and Oakley , Inc . The company also makes sunglasses and frames for a multitude of designer brands such as Chanel and Prada .
