The statistic shows the leading merger and acquisition ( M & A ) deals in Hungary ( CEE ) in 2019 , by transaction value . The largest deal amounted to 302.2 million euros ; this was the price paid for the acquisition of 25 percent stake of Telenor Magyarorszag ; Telenor Real Estate Hungary by Antenna Hungaria ( Hungary ) . The smallest deal in the top five amounted to 100 million euros by Indotek Group ( Hungary ) to acquire 100 percent stake of Sofitel Budapest Chain Bridge .
