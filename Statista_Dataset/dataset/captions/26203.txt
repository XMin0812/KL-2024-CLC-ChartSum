As of March 23 , 2020 , around 44 percent of Canadian households stated they had already lost work due to coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) , while a further 18 percent anticipated a future loss of work . This statistic shows the percentage of Canadian households who had lost work as a result of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) as of March 23 , 2020 .
