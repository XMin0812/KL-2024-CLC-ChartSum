This statistic shows the results of a biennial survey , which asked British adults if they are entertaining at home more often by having friends over instead of going to a pub or restaurant , between 2009 and 2017 . According to the 2017 survey , 75 percent of respondents claimed to have friends over instead of going to a pub or restaurant . In 2011 , only around half of respondents claiming to entertain more often at home said they have friends over instead of going out .
