The statistic represents the annual average U.S. domestic first purchase price for crude oil from 1975 to 2019 . In 2019 , the average price for domestic crude oil stood at about 55.55 nominal U.S. dollars per barrel . According to the source , nominal prices reflect the price paid for a product or service at the time of the transaction ; these prices have not been adjusted to remove the effect of changes in the purchasing power of the dollar .
