The solid waste industry in the United States generated 63.4 billion U.S. dollars of revenue in 2017 . Municipal solid waste is more commonly known as the garbage produced by households and can include packaging , grass clippings , bottles , and newspapers . Material that is recovered from the waste stream can be recycled and composted , the remainder will end up in landfills . The average American generated 4.51 pounds of trash per day in 2017 , an increase from 2.68 pounds per day in 1960 . Food and other organic wastes made up the largest share of waste materials landfilled .
