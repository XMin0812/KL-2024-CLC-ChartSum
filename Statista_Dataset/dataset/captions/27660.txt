As a result of the coronavirus outbreak in Poland in March 2020 , the government closed down cinemas and entertainment centers out of fear of the virus spreading . Eighty-five percent of the respondents plan to visit a cinema within two months of its reopening . Twenty percent declare that they will go to a film screening immediately after the opening of the cinemas . For further information about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , please visit our dedicated Facts and Figures page .
