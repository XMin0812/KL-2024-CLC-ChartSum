More than 75 percent of the respondents agreed that the Romanian authorities are doing their utmost to stop the spread of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Romania . Moreover , the Ministry of Health has done an excellent job of informing the population about the coronavirus situation in Romania . Nevertheless , 70 percent of people thought that the number of infections in Romania would increase in the coming days .
