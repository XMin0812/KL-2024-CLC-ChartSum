This statistic presents the total number of reported incidents of malware , hacking and `` logical '' attacks on the automated teller machines in European countries from 2014 to 2019 . Malware and hacking incidents are based on ever evolving cybercriminal techniques , designed to rob customers of their savings either through card or PIN skimming methods ( lower-level ) or without having to compromise their debit cards , designed to attack the ATMs directly ( higher-level ) . In some of the more sophisticated hack attacks , the access to back-end of ATM machines is used , in order to compromise their security . Reports filed over time showed that the number of malware and `` logical '' attacks have fluctuated overall from 51 reported cases in 2014 to 140 in 2019 .
