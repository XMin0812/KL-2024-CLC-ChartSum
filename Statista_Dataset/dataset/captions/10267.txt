This statistic shows the number of Turkcell mobile subscribers in Europe in 2017 , broken down by country . Turkey had by far the most mobile subscribers with a total of 35.8 million in the first quarter of 2017 . Turkcell operates in several countries and with several subsidiaries : in the Ukraine they operate under Ukrayna-lifecell ; in Belarus under Belrus-BeST ; in Northern Cyprus under KKTCELL ; and in Europe under Turkcell Europe .
