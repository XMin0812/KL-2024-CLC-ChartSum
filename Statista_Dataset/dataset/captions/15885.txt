This statistic shows the pass rates for large goods vehicle tests in the United Kingdom ( UK ) between the financial years 2007/2008 and 2017/2019 . The pass rate was consistently around 50 percent , although it has been increasing steadily . At the same time , the number of tests being taken has dropped . The pass rate of LGV driving tests had risen to 58.3 percent by 2018/2019 .
