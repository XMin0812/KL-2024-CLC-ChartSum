This statistic shows the breakdown of employees working for Total S.A. in 2019 , by region . In that year , 27.4 percent of Total S.A. 's employees were based in Europe , excluding France . Total S.A. is one of the world 's largest oil and gas companies . The French company , headquartered in Paris-Courbevoie , specializes on the entire oil and gas chain , as well as chemical manufacturing .
