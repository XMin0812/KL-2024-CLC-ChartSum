This statistic shows the number of cars sold by Hyundai in the Republic of Ireland between 2009 and 2019 . In the period of consideration , Irish sales of Hyundai cars oscillated . From 2009 to 2016 , Hyundai sales grew to its peak , at approximately 15.4 thousand units . From 2017 onwards , Hyundai sales declined and has fluctuated . In 2019 , Hyundai sales amounted to 11.1 thousand units , a decrease of roughly 28 percent in comparison with the peak year . Irish passenger car sales amounted to 117,100 units as of 2019 . Hyundai held 9.44 percent of the market share on that year , being the third leading marque .
