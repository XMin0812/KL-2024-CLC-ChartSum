This statistic shows the average number of children per female in the Netherlands from 1950 to 2019 . In 2019 , women in the Netherlands had an average of approximately 1.5 children . That was half the number of children women in 1950 had . As this statistic on the average number of children per female in the Netherlands in the past ten years shows , this number has been relatively stable in recent years , around 1.6 to 1.7 children per female . The same applies to the average number of children per male in the last ten years , which can be viewed here . Both men and women in the Netherlands had an average of 1.6 to 1.7 children , although the average for women seemed slightly higher in the most recent years .
