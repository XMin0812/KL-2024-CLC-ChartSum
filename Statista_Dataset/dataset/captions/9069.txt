This statistic shows the 19th presidential election results of South Korea that took place on the ninth of May 2017 . Moon Jae-in from the Democratic Party won 41.08 percent of votes , being the president-elect , followed by Hong Jun-pyo from Liberty Korea Party with 24.03 percent . The results indicate that the president-elect Moon Jae-in won the election by the most significant margin in the history of South Korea 's presidential elections .
