The expenditures of the central government in Sweden generally increased over the period , with two peaks in 2013 and 2018 . The highest expenditure was in 2018 and was 992.1 billion Swedish kronor . During the same year , the central government revenue reached around 1072 billion Swedish kronor , which was the highest revenue in ten years . In 2019 , the central government expenditure amounted to 943.6 billion kronor .
