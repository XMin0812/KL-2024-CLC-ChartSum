In 1990 , 48.1 percent of all Black families with a single mother in the United States lived below the poverty level . In 2018 , that figure had decreased to 27.3 percent . Poverty is the state of one who lacks a certain amount of material possessions or money . Absolute poverty or destitution is inability to afford basic human needs , which commonly includes clean and fresh water , nutrition , health care , education , clothing and shelter .
