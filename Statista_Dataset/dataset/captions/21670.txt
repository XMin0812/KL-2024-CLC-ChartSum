At a regional level , the number of employees working in the Romanian health system who tested positive for coronavirus appeared to be inversely proportional to the amount of expenditure on uniforms and equipment per employee that each local administration decided to invest . Suceava had 897 employees infected with COVID-19 , while spending only 3.1 Romanian lei per employee . By contrast , Valcea spent 76.79 Romanian lei per employee for equipment against COVID-19 and only had 53 infections in the healthcare system .
