This statistic illustrates the number of new upper medium car registrations in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in 2019 , by model type . In 2019 , there were a total of around 168,100 vehicles registered in the upper medium segment . The leading five models accounted for around 51 percent of all vehicles registered in that segment . BMW models , namely 3 and 4 Series , ranked first and fourth , with a combined 24 percent of the segment share . Except for the Skoda Octavia , all models displayed are produced by German automakers , BMW and Audi .
