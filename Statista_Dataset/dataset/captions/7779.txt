This statistic shows the number of foreigners in Germany according to the Central Register of Foreign Nationals from 1990 to 2019 . In 2019 , the share of foreigners in Germany amounted to 12.5 percent . According to the source , foreigners are those who are not German based on Article 116 Paragraph 1 of the German constitution . These include stateless persons and those with unclear citizenship as well as the population group with a migration background . Individuals with a migration background can either have immigrated into Germany or been born in the country to at least one parent who was born a foreigner .
