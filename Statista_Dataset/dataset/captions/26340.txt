This statistic displays the total global human growth hormone market in 2016 and a forecast for 2026 , measured in billion U.S. dollars . In 2016 , the total market was estimated to stand at approximately 3.7 billion U.S. dollars . Among the leading application areas of growth hormones are growth hormone deficiency , Idiopathic Short Stature ( ISS ) , Turner syndrome , Prader-Willi syndrome , and Small for Gestational Age ( SGA ) .
