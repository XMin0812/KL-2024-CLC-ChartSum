The value of the DAX index amounted to 13,362.87 as of December 9 , 2020 , down from 13,681.19 in the middle of February . All global stock markets were hit by the growing panic about the coronavirus pandemic , which accounts for this drop . The DAX ( Deutscher Aktienindex ) is the most important German stock index , showing the value trends of the 30 largest and most liquid companies listed on the Frankfurt stock exchange .
