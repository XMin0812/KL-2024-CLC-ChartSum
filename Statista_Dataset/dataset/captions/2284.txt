Since the first half of 2010 , household electricity prices in Portugal have seen an overall increase , rising to 22.31 euro cents per kilowatt-hour ( kWh ) in the first half of 2020 . The highest price during the reporting period was in the first half of 2016 , at 23.5 euro cents per kWh . Households with an annual consumption of 2,500 kWh to 5,000 kWh tended to pay less for their electricity compared to those with a consumption of between 1,000 kWh and 2,499 kWh .
