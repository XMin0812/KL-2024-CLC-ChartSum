This statistic shows the number of poor families in the U.S. from 1990 to 2019 . In 2019 , there was a total of 6.5 million poor families living in the United States . Poverty is the state of one who lacks a certain amount of material possessions or money . Absolute poverty or destitution is inability to afford basic human needs , which commonly includes clean and fresh water , nutrition , health care , education , clothing , and shelter .
