Data on the amount of stop and searches carried out by the police in England and Wales under section 1 of the Police and Criminal Evidence Act 1984 ( PACE ) from fiscal year 2002/03 to fiscal year 2019/20 shows that searches peaked in this period in 2008/09 when the number carried out exceeded 1.5 million . Throughout this period the amount of stop and searches has been decreasing and in 2018/19 it amounted to only 366,912 . However , in the most recent period 2019/20 , the numbers picked up again to 558,973 searches .
