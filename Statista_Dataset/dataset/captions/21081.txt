According to the findings of a survey by IPSOS , every country in the study underestimated the number of people who die each year from cardiovascular diseases such as heart disease . Those in South Africa and Hong Kong had the most accurate guesses , with the average guess being 9 percent below the actual percentage . This statistic shows the actual vs public estimates for number of deaths per 100 which occur as a result of cardiovascular disease worldwide as of 2019 .
