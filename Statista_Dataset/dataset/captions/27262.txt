As of March 2020 , it was estimated that the number of visits to shopping malls in Mexico would decrease by 63 percent , should the government announce phase 2 of the novel coronavirus disease ( COVID-19 ) outbreak . In addition , visits to stores within this establishments were expected to decrease by 71 percent in that stage . On February 28 , the first cases of COVID-19 were confirmed in the country . As of March 30 , 2020 , the number of COVID-19 registered cases in Mexico amounted to 993 .
