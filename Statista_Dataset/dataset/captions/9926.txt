Venture capital investors specialize in identifying and investing in start-ups and small businesses that have the potential for long-term growth . These investment companies often focus on companies that are either innovating a current market or in fact creating a new market segment . These companies not only invest with money but will also offer technical or managerial support . VC investment firms either take a broad approach to companies in which they invest in or will specialize in a specific technology . In 2017 , the Deep tech sector and the Fintech industry where the leading sectors for VC investments in Europe .
