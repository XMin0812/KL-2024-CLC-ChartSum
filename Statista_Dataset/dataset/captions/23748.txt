One of the most prestigious events for the art and cultural sector , the Biennale International Art Exhibition in Venice , also known as Art Biennale , is the contemporary art exhibition organized every two years by La Biennale di Venezia . The 58th edition of the Biennale , titled May You Live In Interesting Time , was curated by Ralph Rugoff and took place from the 11th of May to the 24th of November 2019 . Overall , it welcomed approximately 593 million visitors , roughly 92 thousand more visitors compared to the 56th edition in 2015 .
