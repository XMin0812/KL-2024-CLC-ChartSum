This statistic shows the forecasted annual growth in worldwide spending in the telecom services industry from 2019 to 2024 . Global telecom services spending is projected to grow decline by 1.4 percent in 2020 compared to the previous year , mainly due to the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic . In 2021 , the telecom services market is forecast to increase again , growing by around one percent compared to 2020 .
