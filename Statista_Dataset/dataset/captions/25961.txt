This statistic depicts the distribution of the value of all merger and acquisition ( M & A ) deals in Western Europe in 2019 , by target country . The United Kingdom ( UK ) had the highest share of M & A deal value in 2019 with 26 percent of the total 1.1 trillion U.S dollars seen by Western European countries . Germany and France were the two other highest recipients of M & A deals by value with 16 percent and 14 percent respectively in 2019 .
