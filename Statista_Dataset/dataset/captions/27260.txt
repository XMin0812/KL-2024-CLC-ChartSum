Scania delivered around 12.300 commercial vehicles in the second quarter of 2020 , a decrease of over 20 percent year-on-year . Scania Aktiebolag is an automotive industry manufacturer of trucks and buses for heavy transport applications , as well as industrial and marine engines . Its largest shareholder is the Volkswagen Group . The German motor vehicle manufacturer also holds a majority stake in MAN SE , Scania 's second-largest shareholder .
