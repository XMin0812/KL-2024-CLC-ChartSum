In the week ended February 23 , 2020 , the dollar sales of hand sanitizers in drug stores rose by 80.7 percent compared to the same period of the previous year . In the same time period , sales of hand sanitizers in multi-outlet and convenience stores grew by about 58 percent compared to the previous year . These increases were likely triggered by the coronavirus pandemic . For further information about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , please visit our dedicated Facts and Figures page .
