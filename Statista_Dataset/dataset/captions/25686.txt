In 2020 , VF Corporation 's active segment revenues amounted to 4.9 billion U.S. dollars worldwide . VF Corporation is an internationally operating company covering the lifestyle and footwear industry . The group owns more than 30 brands . The company 's largest ones are `` The North Face '' , `` Timberland '' , and `` Vans '' .
