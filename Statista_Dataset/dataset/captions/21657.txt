This statistic shows the voter turnout for presidential elections that took place in Ukraine since the country became independent in 1991 after the collapse of the Soviet Union . The largest share of the Ukrainian population , or 84.2 percent , voted in the first national election of 1991 . The lowest turnout of 59.48 percent was observed in the extraordinary election of 2014 . In the most recent election of 2019 , 62.09 percent of Ukrainians cast their vote .
