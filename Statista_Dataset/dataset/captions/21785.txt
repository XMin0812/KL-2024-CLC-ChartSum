Roman Catholic was the most common religion affiliation in Argentina in 2018 . In a survey carried out between July and August of 2018 , almost 65 percent of Argentinian respondents claimed to be of catholic faith , whereas the second most chosen religion was Evangelism , with 10.8 percent of the people interviewed . Almost 19 percent of respondents answered they did n't profess any religion and 1.5 percent defined themselves as atheists .
