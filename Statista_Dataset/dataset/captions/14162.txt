This statistic depicts the net revenue of Williams-Sonoma worldwide from 2007 to 2019 . In 2019 , global net revenue of Williams-Sonoma amounted to about 5.9 billion U.S. dollars.Williams-Sonoma is a high-end American retail company that sells kitchenware , furniture and linens , as well as other housewares and home furnishings . The company was founded in 1956 by Charles E. Williams , who was one of the titans of the American food revolution .
