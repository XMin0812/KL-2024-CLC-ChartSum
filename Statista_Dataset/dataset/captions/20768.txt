In March 2020 , 61 percent of businesses in the Danish employers association , Dansk Erhverv , stated that exports had decreased due to the COVID-19 outbreak . In comparison , international exports remained unchanged among 27 percent of the surveyed businesses , while eight percent of the respondents experienced an increase in exports . The first case of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Denmark was confirmed on February 27 , 2020 .
