As of January 5 , 2021 , Milan ( Lombardy ) was the Italian province which registered the highest number of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) cases in the country . Rome ( Lazio ) came second in this ranking , while Naples ( Campania ) and Turin ( Piedmont ) followed . These four areas are also the four most populated provinces in Italy . The region of Lombardy was the mostly hit by the spread of the virus , recording around one third of all coronavirus cases in the country . In fact , four out of the 11 provinces with the highest number of coronavirus cases in Italy were located in Lombardy .
