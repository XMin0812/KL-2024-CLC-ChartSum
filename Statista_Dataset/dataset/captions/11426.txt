As of January 6 , 2021 , the daily increase of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) cases in Egypt reached 1,007 infections , increasing the cumulative cases to 145,590 . As of the same date , there were in total 7,975 deaths and 115,975 recoveries recorded in the country . The highest daily confirmed cases was recorded on 19 June 2020 at 1,774 . It is worth noting that the first case in the African continent was registered in Egypt on February 14th .
