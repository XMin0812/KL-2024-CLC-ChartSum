According to a survey carried out in April 2020 , 77 percent of respondents in Brazil reported buying or using more alcohol-based hand sanitizer in response to the COVID-19 outbreak . Disinfectants and disposable face masks were also the most bought or used hygiene products , with more than half of respondents using them more frequently . Almost 45 percent of the Brazilians surveyed were also prone to purchase and use liquid soap for the same reason .
