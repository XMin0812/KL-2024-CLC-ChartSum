This statistic presents the value of fresh or chilled onions , garlic and leeks imported to the United Kingdom ( UK ) annually from 2001 to 2019 . During this period the value of onions , shallots , garlic and leeks imported to the United Kingdom generally increased . In 2001 the value of onion , shallots , garlic and leeks amounted to 76.1 million British pounds , by 2019 the value of imports had increased to approximately 293 million British pounds .
