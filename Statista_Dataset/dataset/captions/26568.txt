The statistic shows gross domestic product ( GDP ) per capita in Burundi from 1985 to 2025 . All figures are estimates . GDP is the total value of all goods and services produced in a country in a year . It is considered to be a very important indicator of the economic strength of a country and a positive change is an indicator of economic growth . In 2019 , the estimated GDP per capita in Burundi amounted to around 269.83 U.S. dollars .
