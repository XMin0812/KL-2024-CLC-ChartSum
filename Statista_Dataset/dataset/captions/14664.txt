As of September 25 , 2020 , 25 percent of Singaporean respondents stated that they had been avoiding going to work during the COVID-19 outbreak , down from a high of 54 percent between April and May . Singapore has started to gradually open up after implementing lockdown measures to control the spread of COVID-19 .
