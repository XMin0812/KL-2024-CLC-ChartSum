This statistic shows the percentage of unemployed who were jobless for 27 weeks or longer in the U.S. in 2019 , by age group . In 2019 , there were total 1.27 million people unemployed for 27 weeks or longer , amounting to 21.1 percent of all unemployed in the U.S . Among all unemployed , aged between 25 to 34 years , about 21.32 percent people were unemployed for 27 weeks or longer .
