In 2019 , roughly 1,150 murders and manslaughters have been recorded by the Belgian police . Although Belgium in general is not a dangerous country , the number of murder and manslaughter cases has been on the rise for the last decade , peaking in 2018 at nearly 1,200 . By comparison , ten years ago the number of victims was 300 lower , at 886 . By comparison , the number of murders in neighboring country the Netherlands was around 2,700 in this period .
