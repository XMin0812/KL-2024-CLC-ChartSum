According to a survey on the coronavirus ' impact on the tourism industry in Denmark , 88 percent of businesses report having experienced a revenue loss as of April 14 , 2020 . Furthermore , 79 percent of the businesses have experienced a decrease in number of guests and furthermore 14 percent of businesses had to dismiss employees because of the effects of the coronavirus . The first case of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Denmark was confirmed on February 27 , 2020 .
