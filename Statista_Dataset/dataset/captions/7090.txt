As of March 16 , 2020 , over 60 percent of the patients who died from novel coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in South Korea had circulatory system diseases such as hypertension , myocardial infarction , cerebral infarction , and arrhythmia . South Korea confirmed a total of 8,413 cases of infection including 84 deaths as of March 18 , 2020 .
