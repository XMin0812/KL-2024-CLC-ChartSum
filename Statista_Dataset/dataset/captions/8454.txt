In fiscal year 2019 , the value of gross non-performing assets at Kotak Mahindra Bank across India was around 45 billion Indian rupees . This was a jump in the non-performing assets filed by the bank compared to the previous fiscal year 's 39 billion rupees . ICICI bank had the highest non-performing asset value across all private commercial banks in the country , followed by Axis Bank and HDFC Bank in the given time period .
