In March 2020 , some entities in Poland saw a decrease in average employment compared to the previous month . According to the source , this was due to , i.a. , retirements , termination of fixed-term contracts , and non-renewal of such contracts . In some cases , it may have been caused by fear of the COVID-19 pandemic . Moreover , the decrease in average employment was also affected by the termination of employment contracts or unpaid leave . There was also an increase in the collection of care and sickness benefits by employees .
