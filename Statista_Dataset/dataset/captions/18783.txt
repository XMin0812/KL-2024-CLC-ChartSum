The statistic shows gross domestic product ( GDP ) per capita in Liechtenstein from 2007 to 2017 . GDP is the total value of all goods and services produced in a country in a year . It is considered to be a very important indicator of the economic strength of a country and a positive change is an indicator of economic growth . In 2017 , the GDP per capita in Liechtenstein was estimated to be around 173,356.05 U.S. dollars .
