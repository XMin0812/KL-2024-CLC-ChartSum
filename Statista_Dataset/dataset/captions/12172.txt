The average self-isolation index in Russian cities with over one million inhabitants reached 3.1 points on Sunday , January 3 , 2021 , meaning that there was not a very high number of people on the streets . Russia 's non-working period to prevent the spread of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) ended on May 11 , 2020 .
