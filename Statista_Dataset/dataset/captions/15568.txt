In 2019 , Moncler reported earnings before interests , taxes depreciation and amortization ( EBITDA ) of 574.8 million euros , resulting in an EBITDA margin of 35.3 percent . When looking at the years from 2013 to 2019 , the growth of Moncler 's EBITDA was steady and quite impressive . The average growth for this important financial indicator amounted to roughly 63 million euros per year .
