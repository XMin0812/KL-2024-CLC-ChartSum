Port-au-Prince was one of the most dangerous cities in the Caribbean , at least when it comes to the number of homicides . This graph presents the number of murder victims in selected Caribbean cities in 2018 or the latest year with available data . For instance , the Haitian capital registered a total of 261 homicides in 2015 . In San Juan de Puerto Rico , 155 people were reportedly murdered in 2016 . In Kingston , Jamaica , the number of homicide victims amounted to 153 in 2018 .
