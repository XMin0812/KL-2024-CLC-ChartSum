This statistic presents the percentage of students in the United States who were cyber bullied , divided by geographic region . During the school year 2012 - 13 , 7.8 percent of students between the ages of 12 and 18 years in the Midwest region of the United States were harassed or bullied online . Cyber bullying includes unwanted contact by threatening or insulting the respondent via digital communications as well as sharing private information online .
