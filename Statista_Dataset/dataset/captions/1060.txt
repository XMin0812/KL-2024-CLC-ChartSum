In the third quarter of 2020 , revenues of AWS grew 29 percent compared to the preceding quarter . AWS is one of Amazon 's strongest revenue segments , generating 35.03 billion U.S. dollars in 2019 net sales , up from 17.46 billion U.S. dollars in 2017 . AWS ranks as one of the most popular public cloud infrastructure and platform services running applications worldwide in 2019 , ahead off Azure and Google cloud services .
