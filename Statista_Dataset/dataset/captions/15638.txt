Since the president of Spain declared the state of alarm in March 14 , 2020 , Spaniards have lived under very restraining isolation rules . Under these circumstances , media consumption habits have changed dramatically in the course of March 2020 in a society very used to interacting outdoors . The results of a survey conducted in March that year revealed that most of the Spaniards found entertainment in watching TV , as stated by 67 percent of respondents . Subscription-based platformts such as Netflix or Movistar+ accounted for 59 percent of responses .
