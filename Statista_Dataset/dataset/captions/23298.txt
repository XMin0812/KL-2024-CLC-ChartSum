In April 2020 , the main reasons why Russian adults refused getting vaccinated against the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) was that they did not see a need in getting the vaccine or they did not believe in the vaccine 's effectiveness . In total , nearly one quarter of Russians preferred to not get a vaccine against COVID-19 if one was developed .
