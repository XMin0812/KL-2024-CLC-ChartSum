Basketball is a commonly watched sport both in the United States and worldwide . The National Basketball Association is a professional basketball league for men with 30 teams competing against each other in the United States . Over the past years , NBA has gained significant popularity , offering great entertainment to fans while generating significant earnings from broadcasting rights . In 2019 , the NBA generated more than 2.6 billion U.S. dollars from domestic rights and 450 million U.S. dollars from overseas rights .
