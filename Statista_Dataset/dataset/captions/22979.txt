In the financial year 2019/2020 , the total retail revenue of Colruyt reached over 7.9 billion euros . Colruyt Belgium and Luxembourg generated close to six billion euros in revenue in the financial year ending March 31st , 2020 , while Dreambaby and Dreamland Belgium and France brought in roughly 250 million euros of revenue . Colruyt is a Belgian retailer in food and non-food products and known for the supermarket chain which has the same name in Belgium , Luxembourg and France . The company was founded by Franz Colruyt in 1925 and is headquartered in Halle , Belgium . The Group also supplies wholesalers , bulk customers , and affiliated independent merchants . Other activities include operations of gas stations , printing and engineering activities , and alternative energy .
