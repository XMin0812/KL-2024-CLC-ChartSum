This statistic shows the number of foreigners in Germany according to the Central Register of Foreign Nationals in 2019 , by state . In 2019 , North-Rhine-Westphalia had the most foreign nationals at 2.7 million , followed by Bavaria with 1.92 million and Baden-WÃ¼rttemberg with 1.82 million . Foreigners are those who are not German based on Article 116 Paragraph 1 of the German constitution . These include stateless persons and those with unclear citizenship as well as the population group with a migration background . Individuals with a migration background can either have immigrated into Germany or been born in the country to at least one parent who was born a foreigner .
