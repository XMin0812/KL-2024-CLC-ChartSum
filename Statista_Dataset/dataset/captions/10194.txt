As of March 22 , 2020 , just 4 percent of adult Canadians were of the opinion that the threat of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic was being blown way out of proportion . In comparison , 58 percent of Canadians had the view that the level of response to the outbreak was appropriate as the threat was very real . This statistic shows the percentage of Canadian adults who believed the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic is being blown out of proportion as of March 22 , 2020 .
