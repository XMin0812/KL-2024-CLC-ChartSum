The only industries that registered a positive change in the GDP in the 2nd quarter of 2020 compared to the 1st quarter , were health services , and public administration and defense . In contrast , the most affected industry by the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic in Romania was tourism and hospitality , followed by culture and arts . By the 2nd quarter of 2020 , both industries registered a negative change in GDP of more than 50 percent .
