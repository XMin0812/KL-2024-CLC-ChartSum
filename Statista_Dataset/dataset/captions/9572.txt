The statistic shows the gross domestic product ( GDP ) in New Zealand from 1984 to 2019 , with projections up until 2021 . Gross domestic product ( GDP ) denotes the aggregate value of all services and goods produced within a country in any given year . GDP is an important indicator of a country 's economic power . In 2019 , New Zealand 's gross domestic product had amounted to around 208.74 billion international dollars .
