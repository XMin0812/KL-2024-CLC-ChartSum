The statistic shows the inflation rate in the EU countries in August 2020 . Inflation or currency devaluation ( drop in the value of money ) , is characterized by a steady rise in the prices of finished products ( consumer goods , capital goods ) . The consumer price index tracks price trends of private consumption expenditure , and shows an increase in the indexes current level of inflation . In August 2020 , the inflation rate in Latvia was at -0.5 percent compared to the same month of the previous year .
