In recent days , as a result of the outbreak of coronavirus in Poland , we can observe an increased demand for goods from the category of consumer electronics and which are necessary for work or remote learning , such as laptops , printers , monitors , video conferencing equipment - cameras and headsets . The greatest customer interest was recorded for internet webcams , reaching an increase of 1300 percent compared to the previous year .
