How many people use Twitter ? In the first quarter of 2019 , the number of monthly active U.S. Twitter users amounted to 68 million , an increase from 66 million in the previous period . In recent times , Twitter 's user growth has been not endeared the company to its investors - the growth compared to the previous quarter only amounted to three percent and has hovered in the low single digits since 2017 as the company continues to struggle to attract and retain users .
