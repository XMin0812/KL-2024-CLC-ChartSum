Individuals who do not have a job but would like to be in full-time employment , are defined as unemployed in Denmark . The number of unemployed Danes decreased constantly since 2012 and was down to nearly 104 thousand in 2019 , among them most of the unemployed were in the ages of 25 to 34 years . The unemployment rate among the elderly aged 60 years and older was 3.8 percent , and from the age of 65 years on , the retirement pension is being paid .
