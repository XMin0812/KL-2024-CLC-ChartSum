This statistic displays the number of candidates on organ donation waiting list in the United States , as of October 6 , 2020 , by ethnicity . During this time , there were 22,574 Hispanic candidates on the organ waiting list in the country . Organ donation can be given through both a deceased and living donor if blood and oxygen are flowing through the organs until the time of recovery to ensure viability . There are over 120,000 people in the country waiting for an organ transplant .
