Since 4 March , when the first case of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) was confirmed in Poland , the number of people hospitalized due to suspicion has been increasing . The most significant increase occurred on 9 March , the number of new patients hospitalized increased by 178 percent compared to the previous day . Since then the change has been fluctuating .
