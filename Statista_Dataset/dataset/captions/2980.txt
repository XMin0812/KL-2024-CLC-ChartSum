In 2019 , LG Electronics reported a net income figure of about 161 million U.S. dollars . In general , annual net income figures report a company 's revenues minus expenses over the course of a year and are highly affected by both expenses and corporate strategy . Changes between years are often quite variable as they include the expenses incurred in the form of large long-term investments in the expense column in addition to the usual yearly operating costs . That being said , 2018 marked a profitable year for LG with high net income figures , and a total revenue of about 55 billion U.S. dollars .
