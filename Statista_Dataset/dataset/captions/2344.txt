This graph shows the monthly United Kingdom ( UK ) market share values for Audi between October 2016 and September 2020 . The company 's market share had a range between 3.1 and 9.8 percent , meaning it consistently controlled a relatively high portion of the UK car market . Audi had the sixth biggest market share in the UK in 2019 and is a subsidiary of Volkswagen , whose namesake brand had the second largest share of the UK market in 2019 .
