India reported over 9.9 million confirmed cases of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) as of December 17 , 2020 . Out of these , over 9.5 million patients had recovered , while 144 thousand cases were fatal . The number of people infected with the virus was growing across the south Asian country and the government had swung into action to curtail further spread of the outbreak . Globally , over 75 million cases of the coronavirus were reported as of December 17 , 2020 .
