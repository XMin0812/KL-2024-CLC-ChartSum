In 2020 , most households in Denmark were households without children . The largest number of households with children had only one child living in it , amounting to about 328 thousand . The second most common household with children , was households with two children . About 4 thousand Danish households with kids had five or more children . The number of households in Denmark grew by over 150 thousand in the period from 2010 to 2020 . It peaked in 2020 , reaching approximately 2.73 million households .
