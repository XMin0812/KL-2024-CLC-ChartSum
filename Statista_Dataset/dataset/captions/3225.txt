The outbreak of the novel coronavirus in Wuhan , China saw infection cases spread throughout the Asia Pacific region . By December 13 , 2020 India had faced over 9.85 million coronavirus cases . Indonesia followed behind India as having had the second highest number of coronavirus cases in Asia Pacific , with over 611.63 thousand cases . At the beginning of the outbreak , people in South Korea had been optimistic and predicted that the number of cases would start to stabilize .
