The COVID-19 pandemic that spread across the world at the beginning of 2020 was not only a big threat to public health , but also to the entire sports industry . Several professional sports leagues , including the NBA and NHL , as well as most professional soccer leagues in Europe , took the decision to postpone or suspend their seasons in order to mitigate the spread of the disease . During a March 2020 survey , some 56 percent of respondents believed that the pandemic has had a major negative impact on the sports industry as a whole .
