After peaking at 6.26 euro cents in the second semester of 2014 , the household price of natural gas remained below six euro cents per kilowatt-hour until the first half of 2018 . In the second semester of 2018 , it peaked at 6.21 euro cents per kilowatt-hour . This was mainly due to the increase in the international price of natural gas . Italy , in fact , relies mostly on gas imports to cover its energy demand .
