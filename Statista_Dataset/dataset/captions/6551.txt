The 2017 Observatoire du Cycle report revealed the average price of various types of bicycles in France . It appears that the most expensive type of bike were electric bikes , which cost on average 1,564 euros . Race bikes followed closely behind , with a unit price of about 1,404 euros . When looking at sales figures the total value of bicycles sold in France increased steadily since 2012 : and reached more than one billion euros in 2016 .
