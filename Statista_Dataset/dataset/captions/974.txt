The statistic shows the growth of the real gross domestic product ( GDP ) in the EU member states in the second quarter 2020 compared to the same quarter of the previous year . GDP is the total value of all goods and services produced in a country in a year . It is considered to be a very important indicator of the economic strength of a country and a positive change in it is a sign of economic growth . In the second quarter of 2020 , the real GDP in Denmark decreased by 8.5 percent compared to the same quarter of the previous year . The overall EU GDP amounted to almost 14 trillion euros around the same time .
