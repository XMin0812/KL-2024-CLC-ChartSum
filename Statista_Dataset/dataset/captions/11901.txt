The adolescent birth rate in Latin America and the Caribbean stood at an average of 60.7 births per 1,000 women aged 15 to 19 , according to September 2020 estimates . The highest rate in the region was registered in French Guyana and Panama , where it surpassed 75 births per 1,000 women in that same age group as of 2017 . In continental Latin America , Chile and Uruguay recorded some of the lowest teenage birth rates .
