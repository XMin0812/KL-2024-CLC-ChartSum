This statistic shows the gross sales of Mattel 's Fisher-Price and Thomas & Friends brands worldwide from 2016 to 2019 . In 2019 , Mattel 's Fisher-Price and Thomas & Friends brands generated gross sales amounting to over 1.13 billion U.S. dollars globally . Fisher-Price is an American company that produces educational toys for children and infants . Fisher-Price has been a wholly owned subsidiary of Mattel since 1993 .
