This statistic shows the number of trail/enduro motorcycles ( a type of off-road bike ) sold in the United Kingdom ( UK ) between January 2016 to December 2019 . As with other vehicles , sales tend to spike in March and September due to the release of new registrations . This also explains the lower sales in February . In 2019 , there were 6,480 trail/enduro motorcycles sold in the United Kingdom , an increase of roughly 6 percent in comparison with 2018 .
