Walmart , formerly known as Wal-Mart Stores , Inc. , is one of the most well-known and valuable brands in the world . Walmart began in the United States as a single discount store , whose model was to sell more for less . Nowadays , Walmart has discount stores , supercenters , and neighborhood markets around the world . The multinational company has developed into the largest retailer in the world , generating revenues of almost 524 billion U.S. dollars in its most recent fiscal year .
