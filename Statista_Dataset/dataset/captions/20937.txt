This statistic shows a state ranking of least-affordable child care for an infant in a center in the U.S. in 2018 . The cost of care is presented as a percentage of state median income for a two-parent family . In 2018 , the state of California had the least affordable child care . About 17.5 percent of the median income of a two-parent family had to be spent for full-time care for an infant in a child care center .
