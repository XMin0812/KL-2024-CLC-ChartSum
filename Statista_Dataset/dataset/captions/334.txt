The first two cases of the new coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Italy were recorded between the end of January and the beginning of February 2020 . Since then , the number of cases in Italy increased steadily , reaching more than 2.1 million as of January 5 , 2021 . The region most hit by the virus in the country was Lombardy , with more than 487 thousand cases . On November 13 , 2020 , 40,902 new cases were registered , which represented the biggest increase in daily cases in Italy since the start of the pandemic .
