This statistic shows the percentage of married-couple families in the U.S. who live below the poverty level from 1990 to 2019 . In 2019 , four percent of married-couple families in the U.S. lived below the poverty level . Poverty is the state of one who lacks a certain amount of material possessions or money . Absolute poverty or destitution is inability to afford basic human needs , which commonly includes clean and fresh water , nutrition , health care , education , clothing , and shelter .
