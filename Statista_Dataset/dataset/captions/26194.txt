As of 2020 , 47 percent of the respondents indicated to be in favor for a BeNeLiga , whereas nearly just as many voted to be against a competition in which the Eredivisie and Belgian Jupiler Pro League are combined . The BeNeLiga has been discussed for many years but recently got more media attention due to the feasibility research by Deloitte , which indicated the new league could have a potential significant value increase and generate higher revenues from television deals . Executing this plan would mean a merger between the top clubs from the Eredivisie and the Belgian Jupiler Pro League .
