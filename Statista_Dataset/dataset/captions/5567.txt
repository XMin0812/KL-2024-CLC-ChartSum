China 's travel and tourism industry directly contributed to the largest amount of employment of any country worldwide with a total of over 29 million workers in 2019 . India was not far behind with just under 27.5 million travel and tourism industry employees . This may come as no surprise given the size and population of both of these Asian nations . Comparatively , the European country that took the highest place in the ranking was Germany with a total of just over three million staff that are a direct result of the travel and tourism industry .
