In 2017 , outbound visitor numbers from the Middle East decreased by 2.6 percent , while it was expected to grow by 4.5 percent in 2019 . By 2020 , the middle east is expected to have around 4.6 percent growth of outbound tourism . The region with the highest number of tourist arrivals in 2017 was Europe , with around 634.6 million international arrivals .
