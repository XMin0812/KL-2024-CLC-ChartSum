The number of people playing golf in England holds rather steady , standing at 883,500 adults who play as regular as twice per month . As for the share of children playing golf , there is a little bit more of a decline , especially in 2017/18 , where 4.2 percent of 5 - 10 year old and 3.6 percent of 11 - 15 year old kids play on a monthly basis , which are the lowest proportions in the past eight years .
