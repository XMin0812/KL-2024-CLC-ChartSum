In July 2020 , YaraskyGaming was the most-followed Twitch streamer who originated from the Netherlands . All entries in this ranking are Dutch game streamers with no mention of how many people from the Netherlands follow foreign streamers , such as former Counter-Strike professional Ninja who is currently the world 's most popular Twitch streamer due to his sessions of Fortnite with celebrities such as hip hop artist Drake .
