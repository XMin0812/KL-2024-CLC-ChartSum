As of 2018 , South Africa 's population increased and counted approximately 57.4 million inhabitants in total , of which the majority inhabits Gauteng , Kwazulu-Natal and Western-Eastern Cape . Gauteng ( includes Johannesburg ) is the smallest province of South Africa , though highly urbanized with a population of nearly 15 million people according to the estimates . Region Kwazulu-Natal had roughly 11.2 million inhabitants , whereas Western and Eastern Cape counted 13.1 million people .
