This statistic shows the development of the revenue of U.S. health company PerkinElmer from 2012 to 2019 . In 2016 , the revenue of this company stood at around 2.12 billion U.S. dollars . PerkinElmer Inc. is headquartered in Waltham , Massachusetts , and is operating in many countries around the world . The company specializes in environmental analysis , food and consumer product safety , medical imaging , drug discovery , diagnostics , biotechnology , industrial applications , and life science research .
