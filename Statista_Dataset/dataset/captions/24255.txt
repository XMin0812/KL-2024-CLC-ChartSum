This statistic illustrates the development of the monthly vacancy rate for the office real estate market in London 's West End , United Kingdom ( UK ) , from January 2017 to August 2020 . The vacancy rate is used to illustrate the share of total office real estate that is currently unoccupied.It can be seen that the vacancy rate of office real estate in the West End of London fluctuated during the period under observation , reaching a value of 4.8 percent as of August 2020.Vacancy rates in London 's West End has remained relatively stable during the period under observation , with a slight increase in the July and June 2020 .
