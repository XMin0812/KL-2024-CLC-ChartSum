Developed by King , Candy Crush Saga is a puzzle video game in which players swap different types of candy on a game board in order to match three or more of the same type . The game was first released on Facebook in 2012 and has since become available on most mobile operating systems and on browser . Despite being in its eighth year on the market , the original Candy Crush Saga is still the leading game in the series , generating revenue of 473 million U.S. dollars in the United States between January and September of 2020 .
