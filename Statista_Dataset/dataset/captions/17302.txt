A survey from March 2020 asked people aged between 16 and 30 years in Italy how they would like to spend their free time as soon as the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic will be over . 31 percent of respondents claimed they will meet with their friends . Similarly , 23 percent of interviewees would like to visit their relatives . Moreover , eating out was also a popular free time activity , chosen by roughly one in ten respondents .
