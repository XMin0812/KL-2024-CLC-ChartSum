This statistic shows the busiest train stations across Great Britain in the financial year of 2018/19 , according to the number of passengers passing through the station . Waterloo was the busiest station with over 94 million people entering and exiting that station in the year . Victoria station came second with approximately 74.7 million people entering and exiting . Cannon Street station came last among these stations , with roughly 20.6 million people entering and exiting that station in that year .
