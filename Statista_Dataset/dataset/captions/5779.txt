The autonomous Spanish city of Melilla , located in the Northern coast of Africa , had the highest birth rate of Spanish cities in 2019 : 14.77 births per 1,000 inhabitants . Asturias , on the other hand , had the lowest birth rate , with 5.05 births per 1,000 inhabitants . As of January 2019 , Andalusia was the most populated autonomous community in Spain , with a total amount of approximately 8.5 million inhabitants .
