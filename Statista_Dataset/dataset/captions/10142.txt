Daddy Yankee is known for being one the pioneers of reggaeton . His contribution to the musical genre has been key for its worldwide success . In 2017 , Luis Fonsi and Daddy Yankee released the hit `` Despacito '' , today 's most viewed music video in the history of YouTube , with more than 6.9 billion views as of August 2020 . Released in January 2019 , the single `` Con Calma '' by the Puerto Rican artist and Canadian rapper Snow has now accumulated over two billion views on the platform .
