Considering nuclear families , referring to couples ( married and in consensual union ) and children below the age of 18 , the most common family composition in Iceland in 2020 was married couples without children . Even so , close to 23 thousand families consisted of a married couple and children . When examining single-parent households , it can be observed that single mothers living with their children were close to eight times more common than single fathers living with their children in Iceland in 2020 .
