The Ibex 35 is Spain 's main stock market index , featuring 35 of the companies with the most liquid assets that are traded in the Madrid Stock Exchange or Bolsa de Madrid in Spanish . Out of the 35 entities , energy company Repsol SA had the expected highest dividend yield at the time of consideration , at 12.84 percent . The financial firm Bankia came in second , with an expected dividend yield of 12.35 percent .
