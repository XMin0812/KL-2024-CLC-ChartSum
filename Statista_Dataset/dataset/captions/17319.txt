This statistic depicts the percentage of silicon metal imports from different countries into the U.S. between 2015 and 2018 , by country . As a primary exporter of silicon metal to the United States , Brazil accounted for 28 percent of U.S. silicon metal imports . Silicon alloys and metals are important in various industries . Ferrosilicon is commonly used in ferrous foundries and steel industries . Whereas , silicon metal is often used for aluminum , aluminum alloys , and the chemical industry . Silicon is also often used for semiconductors in solar panels , computer chips , and as a part of the automotive industry .
