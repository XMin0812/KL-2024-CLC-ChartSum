This graph presents the number of police violence cases recorded during the `` yellow vests '' movements in France between November 2018 and July 2019 according to the journal Mediapart , by type . It shows that the majority of recorded police violence cases were head injuries : 315 head injuries resulting from police violence have been reported since the beginning of the movement , while 164 cases of threats , insults or restrictions on freedom of the press were also recorded .
