This statistic presents the share of global internet users who believe that the chance of having their personal information compromised ever on internet is so small that its really not worth worrying about as of November 2014 , sorted by region . During the survey period it was found that 36 percent of internet users in Europe felt that the chances of having their personal information compromised on internet is small enough to be not worth worried about . Overall , 41 percent of global internet users agreed with the statement .
