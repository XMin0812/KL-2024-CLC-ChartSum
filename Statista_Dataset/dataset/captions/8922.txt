The population of Iceland amounted to 364,134 people in 2020 . This was a growth of around 47 thousand individuals compared to 2010 . Considering the gender distribution , there were slightly more Icelandic men than women in 2020 . What is more , the largest age group in Iceland in 2020 consisted of 20 to 39-year-olds . In spite of this , the average age was 38.3 years in 2020 .
