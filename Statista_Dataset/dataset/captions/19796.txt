This statistic shows the turnover of the foodservice sector in Luxembourg from 2005 to 2016 . The foodservice sector is made up of food and beverage service activities , restaurants and mobile food service activities , event catering and other food service activities , and beverage serving activities . The turnover of the sector has increased in the period under consideration , rising from roughly 810 million euros in 2007 to 1.32 billion euros by 2017 .
