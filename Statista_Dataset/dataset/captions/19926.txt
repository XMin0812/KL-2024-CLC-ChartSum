As of August 2020 , the number of prisoners in Italy was 53.9 thousand . Between 2000 and 2019 , Italy 's prison population increase . In 2010 , the population of prisoners in custody reached its peak at 68 thousand inmates , whereas by the end of 2019 , the number of inmates amounted to 60.8 thousand individuals . During the coronavirus emergency , Italy adopted a decree for the supervised release of certain prisoners in order to reduce overcrowding . Thus , the number of detainees in 2020 experienced a decrease .
