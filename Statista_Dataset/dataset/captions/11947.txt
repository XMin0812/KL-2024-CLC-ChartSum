This statistic shows the market size forecast of the security management segment , which is a part of the cyber security market and is strongly related to the other solution segments , in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2010 to 2017 . The estimated size of the security management segment in 2017 is 612 million British pounds ( GBP ) . The security management segment includes cyber security strategy , risk management , architecture , audit , intrusion , tests & post , regulation and certification controls , recovery and continuity plans , security incident and event management ( SIEM ) , asset and vulnerability management .
