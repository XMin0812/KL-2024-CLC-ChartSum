This statistic shows Catalonia 's share of the national gross domestic product ( GDP ) of Spain between 2006 and 2019 . Before the financial crisis hit Spain , Catalonia 's share of GDP was about 19.8 percent , this fell to 19.3 percent in 2009 , suggesting that Catalonia was affected worse than Spain at this time . However , its share grew since and reached almost 20 percent by 2016 .
