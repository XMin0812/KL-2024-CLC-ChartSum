According to the Economic Times survey conducted across Indian metro cities in 2019 , about 30 percent of respondents wanted to move out to a non-metro city within the next five years . Whereas , six percent of the respondents were already in the process of moving out of the metro city in India . The rise in the level of pollution in the metro cities was the leading reason for more than half of the respondents not wanting to stay in the metro cities across the country . Since 2014 , there are eight metros in India - Chennai , Delhi , Mumbai , Kolkata , Bengaluru , Hyderabad , Ahmedabad and Pune .
