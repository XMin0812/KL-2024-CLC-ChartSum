This statistic shows the results of a survey question asking participants when they last made a buying decision influenced by the ethical standards of a retailer or a product . The survey , conducted for the Department for Business , Innovation and Skills in June 2014 , aimed to gauge the publics ' attitude to ethical standards in retail in the UK and the impact this had on their purchase decisions . Collectively , 45 percent of respondents claimed to have ever made a buying decision influenced by the ethical standards of the retailer or product , 8 percent of whom had done so in the last week . However 28 percent stated they never have and a further 26 percent did not know or could n't say .
