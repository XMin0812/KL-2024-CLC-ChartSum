In the third quarter of 2020 , Netflix revealed that it had 73.08 million paying streaming subscribers in the United States and Canada , making North America the company 's largest market in the world , followed by the EMEA region . Asia Pacific remains the smallest market for Netflix , but is also the fastest-growing . Previously , the number of subscribers located in countries across that region amounted to roughly half the number of paying customers in Latin America , but Netflix 's subscriber base in APAC almost doubled between early 2019 and the third quarter of 2020 .
