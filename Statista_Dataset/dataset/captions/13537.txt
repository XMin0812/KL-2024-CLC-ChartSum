Emil Bergquist Pedersen , known as `` Nyhrox '' , is the best earning Norwegian eSports player . In 2019 , the Fortnite player gained more than 1.5 million U.S. dollars . Hearthstone player Casper `` Hunterace '' Notto ranked second , with earnings of roughly 285 thousand U.S. dollars that year . Ludvik `` Aitzy '' Jahnsen who is playing PUBG ( PLAYERUNKNOWN'S BATTLEGROUNDS ) made the third highest income among eSports players in Norway .
