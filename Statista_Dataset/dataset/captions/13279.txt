A lot of pressure is put on soccer players at a young age and the most exciting prospects are often touted as being the next big star in world soccer . Despite being just 20 years of age , Borussia Dortmund 's Jadon Sancho was valued at over 198 million euros in March 2020 . His impressive performances for his club led to his first England senior call up in October 2018 and he made his debut for his country against Croatia in the same month .
