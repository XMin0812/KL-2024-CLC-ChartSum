How much bottled water do people in the United Kingdom drink ? In 2019 , approximately 2.8 billion liters of bottled water were consumed in the UK . Consumption of bottled water has slightly declined in 2019 , after increasing annually between 2013 and 2018 . However , during 2013 and 2019 , total consumption increased by over 745 million liters . Growth was highest in 2014 with 9.6 percent YOY growth . Per capita consumption amounted to an average of 44.9 liters per person in 2018 .
