In 2019 , Tod 's Group , which controls the brands Tod 's , Hogan , Fay and Roger Vivier , boasted a total of 234 directly operated stores ( DOS ) . About half of these DOS ( 137 ) belonged to the brand Tod 's . In the same year , alongside the network of DOSs , the Group also counted 114 franchised stores , most of which belonged to the Tod 's brand .
