More and more production and manufacturing companies in Norway give temporary layoffs to employees due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) crisis . In this sector , 36 percent of the surveyed companies had already carried out employee layoffs , as of March 20 , 2020 . Some production companies still had close to normal production at that time , and some had already given layoffs to a considerable share of their employees . Several manufacturing companies also announced , that they have just started giving layoffs to employees , and that their situation might get markedly worse by Easter .
