As a consequence of the coronavirus pandemic , many people have faced lay-offs and dismissals in Denmark . Since the country officially shut down on March 11 , 2020 , the number of dismissals increased significantly , peaking on July 2 , when nearly 186 thousand people registered as unemployed . In the following months , the number of unemployed individuals decreased . In the Capital Region of Denmark , the unemployment rate reached seven percent as of August 2020 . Nevertheless , the highest unemployment rate was documented in the Region of North Denmark .
