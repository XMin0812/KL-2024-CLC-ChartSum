This statistic illustrates the annual operating profit or loss of Amey UK plc from 2012 to 2018 , in thousand British pounds . In the period of consideration , Amey UK plc profit presented a trend of decline . Between 2012 and 2015 , Amey recorded operating profits , with the highest amount in 2014 , at 137 million British pounds . Since 2016 , Amey UK plc recorded operating losses . In 2018 , Amey UK plc recorded a loss of 414 million British pounds , the highest operating loss among these years .
