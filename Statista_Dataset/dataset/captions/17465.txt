This statistic shows the results of a survey on ad blocker users in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in 2019 , broken down by age groups . According to the survey , 33 percent of internet users aged 46 to 55 years used ad blockers in the United Kingdom ( UK ) that year . On the other hand , 41 percent of interne users aged 15 to 25 year did n't use ad blockers during during the same period .
