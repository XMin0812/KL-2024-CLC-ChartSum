This statistic shows the result of a survey on Finns ' opinions on Donald Trump strengthening the US position in the world as of June 2018 . According to the survey , 14 percent of survey respondents ( representative of the Finnish adult population ) believed that Trump had strengthened the world power status of the United States . The survey was conducted prior to the Helsinki Summit where Donald Trump met with the Russian president Vladimir Putin on July 16 , 2018 .
