The healthcare spending growth rate in the United Kingdom ( UK ) has experienced fluctuations since 1998 . Then , the healthcare spending growth rate was 6.9 percent and by 2018 the healthcare spending growth was only 5.3 percent . 2002 saw the highest healthcare spending growth rate at 9.5 percent , while in 2010 , the growth rate was only 3.2 percent , a significant decrease from the preceding years .
