The statistic shows the trend-indicator-value ( TIV ) of Australian arms exports in 2018 , by country . In 2018 , the TIV of Australian arms exports to the United States amounted to about 30 million . The TIV is based on the known unit production costs of a core set of weapons and is intended to represent the transfer of military resources rather than the financial value of the transfer . The depicted export value is only an indicator and does not correspond to the actual financial value of the transfers .
