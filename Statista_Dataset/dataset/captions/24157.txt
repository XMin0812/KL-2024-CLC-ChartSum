This statistic shows the national debt of Kiribati from 2015 to 2017 in relation to the gross domestic product ( GDP ) , with projections up until 2025 . The figures refer to the whole country and include the debts of the state , the communities , the municipalities and the social insurances . In 2017 , the national debt of Kiribati amounted to approximately 21.42 percent of the GDP .
