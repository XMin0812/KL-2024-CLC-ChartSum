In 2019 , around 33.9 percent of total alcoholic beverage sales were Soju . Soju is a traditional Korean spirit . Soju is a clear distilled spirit with and alcohol content between 16 to 53 percent . It is traditionally made from rice , wheat , or barley , but is also often produced from potatoes or sweet potatoes . South Korea produced around 1.36 million tons of Soju in 2018 .
