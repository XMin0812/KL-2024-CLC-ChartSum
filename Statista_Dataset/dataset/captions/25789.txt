This statistic displays the annual sales figures of passenger cars in Slovenia from 2005 to 2019 . From 2008 to 2009 the annual sales figures of passenger cars declined from over 71,000 sold cars in 2008 to a less than 58,000 sold cars in 2009 . This amounts to a decline of approximately 19 percent . By 2019 , the amount of cars sold increased to more than 70,000 units .
