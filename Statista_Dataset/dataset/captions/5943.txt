As of October 23 , 2020 , 62 percent of Singaporean respondents stated that they feared catching the infection COVID-19 , caused by the novel coronavirus . This was an increase from 51 percent on February 21 . Singapore is gradually opening up after implementing lockdown measures to control the spread of COVID-19 , which had managed to bring the number of cases back down to manageable numbers .
