This statistic shows the out-of-pocket cost of citalopram in the U.S. from 2004 to 2017 , in U.S. dollars . In 2004 , citalopram cost around 45 U.S. dollars . As of 2017 , the out of pocket cost for citalopram was just over 5 U.S. dollars . Citalopram is is an antidepressant drug of the selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor ( SSRI ) class .
