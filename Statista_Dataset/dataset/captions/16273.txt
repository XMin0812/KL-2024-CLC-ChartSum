This statistic displays the distribution of capital expenditure ( capex ) on mobile mining equipment worldwide in 2015 , by equipment type . The figures presented in this statistic are based on a survey of 120 of the world 's leading mining companies carried out in the second half of 2015 . According to the survey results , in that year , some 32 percent of the global capex on mobile mining equipment was attributable to surface and underground trucks .
