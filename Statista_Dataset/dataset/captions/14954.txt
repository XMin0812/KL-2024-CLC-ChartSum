This statistic displays the leading five countries in the European Union for the import of glucose syrup into the United Kingdom ( UK ) in 2019 . In this year , Belgium was the leading market for the import of glucose syrup into the UK , with an import value of approximately 32 million British pounds , followed by France at 19.4 million British pounds .
