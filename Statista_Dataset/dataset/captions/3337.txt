As of October 12 , 2020 , Matteo Berrettini was the Italian tennis player scoring the highest number of ATP ranking points . As of the survey period , he scored 3,075 points and ranked ninth in the world ranking by the Association of Tennis Professionals ( ATP ) . Fabio Fognini came after with a score of 2,400 ranking points . Fognini ranked 16th in the ATP world ranking as of the survey period.ATP rankings are officially known as FedEx ATP Rankings and colloquially called by tennis fans as the world rankings . In this way , the tennis player who ranks first in the ATP ranking is usually defined as the best tennis player in the world .
