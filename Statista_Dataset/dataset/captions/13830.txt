This statistic outlines the number of employees at Goldcorp from 2009 to 2018 . Goldcorp Inc. was one of the world 's leading producers of gold , silver , and copper . Goldcorp was based in Vancouver , Canada , and operated in the Americas . In 2018 , the company had approximately 15,500 employees . On April 18 , 2019 , the merger between Newmont Mining Corp and Goldcorp was completed , forming Newmont Corporation .
