In 2020 , due to the situation caused by the global coronavirus pandemic ( COVID-19 ) , most car parts distributors in Poland feel uncertain about the future . Thirty-five percent of distributors expect the company 's turnover to fall by about 20 percent to 40 percent and almost 30 percent to fall by 40 - 60 percent . For further information about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , please visit our dedicated Facts and Figures page .
