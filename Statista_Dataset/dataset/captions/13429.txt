This statistic shows a list of organic food brands of supermarkets in Italy in 2017 , ranked by number of product references . According to data , the supermarket offering the largest number of organic foods was Coop , with 604 items of the Vivi Verde brand . This was about double the figure for the organic food product line of Esselunga ( 300 product references ) . Other supermarket chains offering a significant assortment of organic foods were Iper ( 371 items ) and Carrefour ( 308 items ) .
