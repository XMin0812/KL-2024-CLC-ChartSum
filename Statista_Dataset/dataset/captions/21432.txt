This statistic shows the number of cars sold by Opel in Turkey between 2012 and 2019 . In the period of consideration , Turkish sales of Opel cars presented a trend of decline with some oscillation . Opel sales peaked at 55.7 thousand units in 2013 . In 2019 , Opel sold approximately 16.7 thousand cars , a decrease of roughly 70 percent in comparison with the peak year .
