The coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak caused massive troubles in Italy . The resilience of the Italian healthcare system and the limited capacity of hospitals were among the most challenging issues facing authorities . As the graph shows , the spread of the virus put hospitals and medical staff under a lot of pressure . At its peak , the number of COVID-19 patients treated in intensive care units ( ICU ) across the country exceed four thousand . Since the beginning of April , when the peak was recorded , this figure decreased gradually , before increasing steadily again from mid-August . After reaching the second-wave peak at the end of November , the number of patients treated in ICU started gradually decreasing . The figure amounted to 2,571 as of January 5 , 2021 .
