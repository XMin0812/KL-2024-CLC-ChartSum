University of Helsinki was the largest university in Finland in 2019 , with over 31 thousand registered students . The second largest university was Tampere University , which was established on 1 January 2019 when the University of Tampere merged with Tampere University of Technology . Aalto University , located in the capital region and offering education in fields such as art , business , and engineering , had over 17 thousand students in the same year .
