A quick service restaurant drive thru is where a customer can place their order , pay and be served all while remaining in their vehicle . This typically takes place through speakers and windows , allowing for speedier customer service . In 2020 , 84 percent of respondents in the U.S. were highly or somewhat satisfied with the quality of menu items ordered through KFC drive thru service . Meanwhile , the service that respondents were least satisfied with was cleanliness and sanitation , with 79 percent being highly or somewhat satisfied .
