In 2018 , over 1.7 thousand baby girls in Belgium were named Emma , Olivia , Mila , or Louise . For instance , more than 500 baby girls were registered with one of these names . However , with 608 baby girls registered in Belgium , Emma was the most common girl name . In Belgium , Alice , was also a popular name in 2018 . Over 420 babies were named Alice , making it the fifth most common name . On the other hand , in 2018 , popular names were more evenly given for boys born in Belgium .
