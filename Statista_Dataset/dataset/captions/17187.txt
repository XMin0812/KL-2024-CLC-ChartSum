In the 2019 - 2020 academic year , the number of students pursuing an university degree in Kenya was estimated at approximately 509,4 thousands . Roughly 519,5 thousand students were enrolled in university in the previous academic year ( 2018 - 2019 ) . The drop by 1.9 percent may be related to a decline in the number of candidates joining universities through self-sponsored programs . The enrollment in universities in the country has being declining since 2016 , when the government adopted measures against examination cheating in schools .
