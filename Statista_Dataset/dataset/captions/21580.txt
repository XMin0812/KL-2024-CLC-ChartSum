In a survey carried out in May 2020 , 13 percent of Brits expected the coronavirus lockdown measures to stay in place until the end of July . Another 13 percent of respondents were more optimistic and thought the lockdown measures would be in place until the end of June , although 14 of respondents expected the lockdown to carry on until 2021 .
