As of January 1 , 2021 , the case fatality rate ( CFR ) of coronavirus COVID-19 ranged at five percent in China , higher than the global level of 2.2 percent . Health authorities in Wuhan , the Chinese epicenter , revised its death toll on April 17 , adding some 1,290 fatalities to its total count . The 50 percent increase of death cases in the city raised the overall CFR in China from 4.01 percent to 5.54 percent . The Chinese Center for Disease Control and Prevention reported that mortality increased with age among infected patients .
