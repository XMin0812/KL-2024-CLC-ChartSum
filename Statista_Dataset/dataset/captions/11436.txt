The American drama `` After We Collided '' was the highest-grossing movie in Spain during all September weekends in 2020 , amounting to approximately 626 thousand U.S. dollars . The Marvel-Comic-based `` The New Mutants '' ranked second on the list with a weekend box office during the same period , clocking up around 434 thousand euros . In 2020 , the cinema industry was very hardly hit by the restrictions implemented in Spain to curb the spread of the COVID-19 pandemic .
