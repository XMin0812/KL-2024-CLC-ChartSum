This statistic shows the net sales of DowDuPont in the Safety & Construction segment from 2015 to 2018 . In 2018 , the company generated some three billion U.S. dollars of revenue in this segment . DowDuPont was formed in September 2017 when companies Dow Chemical and DuPont completed a merger of equals . In April 2019 , the DowDuPont conglomerate separated into three companies : Dow , dedicated to commodity chemicals , DuPont , dedicated to specialty chemicals , and Corteva , dedicated to agricultural chemicals and seeds . DowDuPont was headquartered in Midland , Michigan .
