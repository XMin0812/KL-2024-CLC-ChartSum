Almost every Automated teller Machine ( ATM ) in the United Kingdom ( UK ) is a part of the Link network . During the previous 12-month period , an average of almost 206 million transactions using Link cash machines take place every month , accounting for an average of 10 billion British pounds in withdrawals . Since the global outbreak of Covid-19 and the subsequent lockdown of businesses and individuals , both the number of cash withdrawals and the value of those withdrawals has plummeted to the lowest figures displayed during the period under observation .
