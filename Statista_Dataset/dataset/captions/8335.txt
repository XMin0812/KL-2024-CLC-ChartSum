The statistic shows gross domestic product ( GDP ) per capita in Bosnia & Herzegovina from 2005 to 2025  . GDP is the total value of all goods and services produced in a country in a year . It is considered to be a very important indicator of the economic strength of a country and a positive change is an indicator of economic growth . In 2018 , the GDP per capita in Bosnia & Herzegovina was estimated to be around 6,073.93 U.S. dollars .
