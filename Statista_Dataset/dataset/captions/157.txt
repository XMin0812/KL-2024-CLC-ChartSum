India tested around 27,824 samples for the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) on April 19 , 2020 . Of these , 1,135 samples were found to be positive for the coronavirus . Overall , the country reported to have cumulatively tested 401,586 samples from 383,985 individuals as of that day .
