According to the latest survey conducted in June 2020 , the current President of Poland , Andrzej Duda , has the highest chance of winning next year 's presidential election . The poll estimates that he may count on 40 percent of votes . Rafał Trzaskowski ( Koalicja Obywatelska ) took second place with the support of 27 percent of the respondents . On May 14th , Małgorzata Kidawa-Błońska , the former candidate of the KO ( Koalicja Obywatelska ) in the presidential election , resigned from running . The Management Board recommended the Mayor of Warsaw Rafał Trzaskowski as a new party candidate .
