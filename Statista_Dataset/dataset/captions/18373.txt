This statistic displays the number of companies in the life science industry sector in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in 2019 , by sector . The life science industry sectors include a variety of industries such as medical technology , biopharmaceuticals , biomedical devises and biomedical technologies . In 2019 , there were 2,850 medical technology companies in the United Kingdom , the largest number in this year . Biopharmaceutical service and supply sector accounted for the second largest number of life science companies in the United Kingdom at 1,500 .
