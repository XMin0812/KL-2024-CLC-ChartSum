How many Italians watch TV ? From 2006 to 2018 , the number of individuals watching television in Italy increased slightly . According to the data , the TV viewers were approximately 53.4 million in 2006 . Subsequently , this figure rose to roughly 54.2 million viewers in 2018 . However , the peak of Italian TV watchers was reached in 2011 , at almost 54.5 million .
