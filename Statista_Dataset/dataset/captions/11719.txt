As of May 2020 , 32 percent of Brits expected the social distancing instructions as a result of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) to stay in place until 2021 . Four percent thought the measures would stay until the end of December 2020 , while eleven percent thought social distancing would be over at the end of July .
