On March 19 , 2020 , the hotel occupancy rate in Colombia fell to 14.9 percent , 50 percentage points less than the occupancy recorded on the same day in 2019 . This drop in hotel occupancy was associated to the COVID-19 pandemic , caused by the novel coronavirus ( SARS-CoV-2 ) . The Colombian lodging industry also saw a decline in bookings as COVID-19 cases started to spread in the country .
