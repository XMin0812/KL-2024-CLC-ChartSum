As of March 2020 , sales of homecare and laundry products in Mercado Libre , an online marketplace , increased by 403 percent in Mexico as a result of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak . Mass-consumption products , and pharmaceuticals rose by 120 percent and 114 percent , respectively . On February 28 , the first cases of COVID-19 were confirmed in the country . As of March 30 , 2020 , the number of COVID-19 registered cases in Mexico amounted to 993 .
