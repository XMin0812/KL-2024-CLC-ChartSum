As of November 5 , 2020 the total number of death caused by coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Iran was over 36.5 thousand . The total number of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) cases to date Iran was around 655 thousand with over 505 thousand recovered . The first confirmed case of the coronavirus occurred in Qom on February 19 , 2020 . For further information about the coronavirus pandemic , please visit our dedicated Facts and Figures page .
