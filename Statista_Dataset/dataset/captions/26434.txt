Net electricity imports volume oscillated over the years in the United Kingdom . Between 1990 and 2019 , figures increased by 0.8 million metric tons of oil equivalent . From 1990 to 2014 , figures increased from one million metric tons of oil equivalent to 1.8 million metric tons of oil equivalent , respectively . By 2016 , it decreased to 1.5 million metric tons of oil equivalent and kept falling until 2017 , at 1.3 million metric tons of oil equivalent . Since 2018 , net electricity imports rose once again , amounting to 1.8 million metric tons of oil equivalent in 2019 .
