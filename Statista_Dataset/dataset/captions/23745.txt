In fiscal year 2019 , YES bank had gross non-performing assets to the value of almost 79 billion Indian rupees . This was a jump in the non-performing assets filed by the bank compared to the previous fiscal year 's 26.27 billion rupees , indicating a difficult year for the bank . ICICI bank had the highest non-performing asset value across all private commercial banks in the country , followed by Axis Bank and HDFC Bank in the given time period .
