In 2019 , over 115,500 babies were born in Belgium . This was the lowest number of births in the last decade . The number of children born in the country peaked in 2010 , at just over 129,000 . The Flemish Region saw the largest number of newborns in 2018 , at roughly 64,300 . The number of children born in the Brussels-Capital Region was less than one third of this , at nearly 17,400 .
