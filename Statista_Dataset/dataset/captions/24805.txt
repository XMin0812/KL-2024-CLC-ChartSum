United Kingdom based airlines uplifted 153.7 million passengers in 2019 . In the past six years , passenger volume has steadily increased , growing by 23 percent . There were 32 passenger airlines registered in the UK that year . This number includes Thomas Cook Airline , which ceased operations in September 2019 following its parent company 's compulsory liquidation . This is not the first time that a major UK airline was permanently grounded , whereas others have been reaping increasing profits .
