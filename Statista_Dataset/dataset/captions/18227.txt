This statistic shows the quarterly value of the reward-based crowdfunding market within the alternative finance sector in the United Kingdom ( UK ) , from the first quarter 2014 to the fourth quarter of 2015 . Alternative finance refers to instruments and channels , which developed outside of the traditional and highly regulated finance system , such as banks and capital markets . The reward-based crowdfunding market value was at the level of 5.1 million British pounds in the third quarter of 2014 . In the third quarter of 2015 , it amounted to 13.12 million British pounds .
