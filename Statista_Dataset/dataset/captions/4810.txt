Gujarat confirmed over 232 thousand cases of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) as of December 17 , 2020 , with over four thousand fatalities and over 215 thousand recoveries . India reported over 9.9 million cases , including this state that same day . The country went into lockdown on March 25 , the largest in the world , restricting 1.3 billion people and extended until end of May . As per the new guidelines , districts were divided into red , orange and green zones . Red zones were hotspots and did not see many relaxations in comparison to the areas considered less prone for the spread of the virus .
