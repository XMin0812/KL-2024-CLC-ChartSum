This statistic depicts the number of employees of Luxottica worldwide in 2014 , by geographical area . In that year , Luxottica employed 42,975 people throughout North America . Founded in 1961 in Agordo , Italy , the Luxottica Group S.p.A. is the world 's largest eyewear company . Its best known brands include Ray-Ban , Persol and Oakley , Inc . The company also makes sunglasses and frames for a multitude of designer brands such as Chanel and Prada .
