This statistic shows the number of households in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in 2019 , by type . A household is defined as one person living alone , or a group of people ( not necessarily related ) living at the same address who share cooking facilities and share a living room or sitting room or dining area . At 7,982,000 households , couples living without children is the most common type of household in the United Kingdom ( UK ) . The second most common household type is couples living with children   .
