In the United States , around 47 percent of Alzheimer 's patients are 75 to 84 years old . Additionally , around 17 percent of Alzheimer 's patients are aged 65 to 74 years . Alzheimer 's disease is a form of dementia which impacts memory , behavior and thinking and can lead to symptoms becoming so severe that patients require support with basic daily tasks . Alzheimer 's remains a relevant problem around the world .
