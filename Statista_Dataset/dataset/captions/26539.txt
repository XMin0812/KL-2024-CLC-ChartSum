This statistic shows the total number of passengers uplifted by Titan Airways Ltd from 2008 to 2019 . Titan Airways is a charter airline with its head office at Stansted Airport . In the period of consideration , the number of passengers of Titan Airways oscillated . The peak year was in 2017 , when Titan Airways uplifted 216,271 passengers . In 2019 , this number rose to 212,464 .
