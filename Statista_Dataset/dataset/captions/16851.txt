This statistic presents the share of children aged 11 to 15 who recently participated in sports in England , by activity . According to the survey conducted between April 2017 and March 2018 , around 44.7 percent of the children had taken part in a football game in the past 4 weeks . Further information about sports in the United Kingdom can also be found in the Dossier : Sport in England - Public funding and participation .
