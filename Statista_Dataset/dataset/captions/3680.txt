This statistic shows the revenue of Bayer CropScience from 2005 to 2019 , with a company forecast figure for 2020 . Bayer CropScience is a subgroup of Bayer AG and produces products for crop protection and nonagricultural pest control . It is also active in seeds and plant traits . In 2005 , Bayer CropScience generated around 5.9 billion euros of revenue . In 2019 , the revenue of Bayer CropScience stood at over 19.5 billion euros .
