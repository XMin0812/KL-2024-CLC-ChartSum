This statistic displays the leading five countries in the European Union for the export of glucose from the United Kingdom ( UK ) in 2019 . In this year , the Republic of Ireland was the leading export market for glucose from the UK with a value of approximately 4.5 million British pounds , followed by Germany at around 0.3 million British pounds .
