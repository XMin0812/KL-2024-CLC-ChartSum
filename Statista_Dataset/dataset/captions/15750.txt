This statistic shows the results of a survey on the most watched Disney princess movies in Great Britain as of May 2018 . The survey found that 74 percent of British adults had watched Snow White and the Seven Dwarves , making it the best known Disney princess movie . Aladdin was the second most watched movie starring a Disney princess , at 61 percent , followed by Sleeping Beauty and the 1950 version of Cinderella , both at 60 percent .
