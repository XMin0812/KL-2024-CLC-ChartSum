In 2019 , a square meter for retail space in Venice cost on average 4.3 thousand euros , making it the most expensive city in Italy for retailers . The ranking presented in the graph shows that Milan , the Italian fashion capital , came second , with an average price per square meter of 3.54 thousand euros . When looking at office space , Venice boasted again the highest prices in Italy . In this real estate segment , second in the ranking was Rome , followed by Milan .
