This statistic shows the result of the UK general election of May 7 , 2015 . The Conservative party won the most seats , and in contrast to the 2010 election , enough to form a majority government . Having confounded the pollsters who had widely predicted a much tighter election , David Cameron 's Conservatives and the Scottish National Party ( SNP ) were by far the biggest winners . The SNP took 56 of the 59 seats that they contested in Scotland . Following the results the leaders of the Labour party , Liberal Democrats and UKIP resigned - Miliband , Clegg and Farage respectively .
