This statistic ranks the leading films by weekend box office revenue in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in January 2020 . The movie `` 1917 '' was the highest grossing film that month , at roughly 23.7 million US dollars . `` Star Wars : Episode IX - The Rise of Skywalker '' and `` Little Women '' ranked second and third highest , at ten million US dollars and 9.3 million US dollars , respectively .
