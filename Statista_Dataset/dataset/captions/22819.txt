This statistic shows the national debt of Sub-Saharan Africa from 2014 to 2018 in relation to gross domestic product ( GDP ) , with projections up until 2024 . The figures refer to the whole country and include the debts of the state , the communities , the municipalities and the social insurances . In 2018 , the national debt of Sub-Saharan Africa amounted to approximately 49.03 percent of GDP .
