Among the most successful Italian fashion companies in terms of turnover , Luxottica Group , without a doubt the global leader in the eyewear sector , ranked first . In 2018 , the group registered a turnover of approximately 8.9 billion euros . During the same year , Prada , well known for producing high-end clothing , accessories , and footwear , came second in the ranking , reporting a turnover exceeding 3.1 billion euros .
