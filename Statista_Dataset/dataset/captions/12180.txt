This statistic illustrates the annual total number of customers who switched their current bank account provider in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2012 to 2019 . It can be seen that the number of customers switching their current bank account provider fluctuated during the period under observation , reaching a total of one million switches in the year 2019 . The largest number of switches were found in the year 2014 , when a total of 1.16 million customers switched their current bank account provider .
