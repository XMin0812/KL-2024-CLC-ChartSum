In 2020 , Costa Rica scored 0.61 in the gender gap index in the area of economic participation and opportunity ( women are 39 percent less likely to have equal economic participation and opportunities than men ) . That year , Costa Rica scored 0.55 in estimated earned income , which shows a gender pay gap of approximately 45 percent ( on average , women 's income was estimated to be 45 percent lower than men 's ) .
