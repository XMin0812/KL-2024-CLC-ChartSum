People aged between 45 and 54 years made up the largest part of population in Italy in 2019 , counting in total 9.7 million individuals . Infants aged up to two years were roughly 1.3 million , the less numerous among all the presented groups . Italy 's population is one of the oldest populations in the world . In the last years , the share of population aged 65 years and over constantly grew , whereas the percentage of younger people declined .
