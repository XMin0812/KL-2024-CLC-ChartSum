This statistic shows the quarterly reach of the Discovery channel in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from the first quarter of 2012 to the third quarter of 2019 . In the fourth quarter of 2018 , Discovery reached approximately 7.45 million viewers , a decrease compared to the one quarter before at roughly 7.67 million viewers . During the period in consideration , Discovery channel 's reached a peak of over 10 million viewers in the first quarter of 2013 .
