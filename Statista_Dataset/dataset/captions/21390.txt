Between 2014 and 2019 , the number of sexual violence cases reported to the authorities in Italy increased overall . In 2019 , 4,884 reports were recorded in Italy . Lombardy was the region with the highest reports nationwide , with over one thousand cases of sexual violence . Sexual violence is any sexual act or attempt to obtain a sexual act by violence or coercion , acts to traffic a person or acts directed against a person 's sexuality , regardless of the relationship to the victim .
