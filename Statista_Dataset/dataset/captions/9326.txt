In 2019 , the Belgian Federal Police reported over 32 thousand cases of cyber-criminality . A peak increase from 2018 . Cyber-crime is a term who refers to crimes committed via a computer system or an online network . This relatively new form of criminality is therefore different than traditional crime . Such crimes happen in a virtual territory , the cyberspace . The democratization of software access , as well as the globalization of access to networks , have both been factors of its development .
