This statistic shows the share of female directors working on the board of FTSE 100 companies in the United Kingdom ( UK ) ( both executive and non-executive ) from 2012 to 2019 . An increase of 17 percent in female professionals holding a position on the board of directors can be observed . As of June 2019 , there was a share of 32.1 percent of female held directorships in the UK .
