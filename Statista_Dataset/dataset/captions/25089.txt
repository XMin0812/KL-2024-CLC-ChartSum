In 2018 , the B2B e-commerce value of computer and electronic products manufacturing shipments in the United States amounted to 207.2 billion U.S. dollars , up from 193.2 billion U.S. dollars in the previous measured period , representing a 7.3 percent year-over-year segment growth . In 2018 , e-commerce accounted for almost 64 percent of total shipments in the computer and electronics manufacturing sector .
