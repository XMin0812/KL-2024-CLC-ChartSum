This graph shows the major donors to Barack Obama in the 2012 election as of June , 2012 , by amount donated . Only individuals who donated 1 million U.S. dollars or more during 2011 and 2012 were taken into account , and only donations to the campaign or the DNC , not to super PACs.The most important fund raiser was Andrew Tobias from Florida . He donated about 4 million U.S. dollars to Barack Obama in 2011 and 2012 .
