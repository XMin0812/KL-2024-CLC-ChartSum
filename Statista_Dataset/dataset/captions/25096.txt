The transportation operator ONE managed shipments of about 1.56 million TEU in volume as of October 12 , 2020 . ONE 's chartered ships recorded a capacity of around one million TEUs . Ocean Network Express ( ONE ) formed as a joint venture between Nippon Yusen Kaisa ( NYK Line ) , Mitsui O.S.K . Lines ( MOL ) , and K Line in 2016 . The company began operating under `` Ocean Network Express '' from April 1 , 2018 .
