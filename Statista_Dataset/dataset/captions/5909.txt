In November 2020 , gasoline prices were around 19.3 percent lower than in November 2019 . The data represents city averages in the United States . The defined base period is : 1982 - 84=100 . CPI is defined by the United States Bureau of Labor Statistics as `` a measure of the average change over time in the prices paid by urban consumers for a market basket of consumer goods and services '' . It is based on prices of food , clothing , shelter , fuels , transportation fares , charges for doctors ' and dentists ' services , drugs , and other goods and services that people buy for day-to-day living .
