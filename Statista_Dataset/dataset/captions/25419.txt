Between 2011 and 2018 , the value of gross written premiums by life insurance companies on the domestic market in Germany fluctuated , reaching a value of approximately 92 billion euros in 2018 . The highest value registered during the observation period was in 2014 at almost 93.7 billion euros . In comparison , in 2011 , the life insurance written premiums were at their lowest at 86.8 billion euros . The United Kingdom had the highest value of life insurance premiums among all European counties in 2018 , followed by France and Italy . Together , the top three countries accounted for more than 60 percent of the European market .
