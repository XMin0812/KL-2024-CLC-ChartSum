This statistic shows the number of characters belonging to particular races inhabiting Middle Earth . Middle Earth is a fictitious world invented by J.R.R . Tolkien for his books `` The Lord of the Rings '' , `` The Silmarillion '' and `` The Hobbit '' . According to the source , the figures only include those characters which are explicitly mentioned in the books . According to this calculation , at least 246 hobbits live in Middle Earth ( and were mentioned in the books ) .
