This statistic shows the total annual number of citalopram prescriptions in the U.S. from 2004 to 2017 , in millions . In 2004 , citalopram was prescribed nearly 11 million times . As of 2017 , the number of citalopram prescriptions had exceeded 24 million . Citalopram is is an antidepressant drug of the selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor ( SSRI ) class .
