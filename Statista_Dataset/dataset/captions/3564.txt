In 2020 , the highest number of deaths due to COVID-19 in Poland occurs in the elderly over 85 years ( 23.2 percent ) . Mortality in the 80 - 85 age group is 16.9 percent , while people aged 75 - 80 represent 13.2 percent of deaths . The first cases of coronavirus infection in Poland were reported on 4 March 2020 .
