In November 2020 , about 61.5 percent of the United States civilian labor force participated in the job market . Civilian labor force is a term used by the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics ( BLS ) to describe the subset of Americans who have jobs or are seeking a job , are at least 16 years old , are not serving in the military and are not institutionalized . In other words , all Americans who are eligible to work in the everyday U.S. economy .
