As of November 2020 , the share of respondents who trusted Giuseppe Conte was 56 percent . The Italian Prime Minister reached the largest share of supporters during the first wave of COVID-19 in the country . In particular , according to survey from March 2020 , Italian interviewees appreciated the government 's response to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic . The current government is supported by M5S , PD , LeU , and Italia Viva and was sworn in September 2019 , representing Conte 's second cabinet .
