This statistic shows the monthly trend in the volume of electrical household appliances sales ( quantity bought ) in Great Britain from January 2016 to June 2020 . The figures are seasonally adjusted estimates , measured using the Retail Sales Index ( RSI ) and published in index form with a reference year of 2016 equal to 100 . Over the time period in question , sales peaked this past June 2020 at an index level of 127.5 .
