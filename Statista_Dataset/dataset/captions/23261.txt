This statistic shows the number of Internet of Things ( IoT ) transport units in the European Union ( EU ) in 2017 , 2020 and 2025 ( in million ) . The number of IoT transport units was expected to increase slightly through the years . It was at 2.6 million units in 2017 , and it was expected that it would reach 3.7 million units by 2025 . IoT transport means that the means of transportation is in some way connected to the internet .
