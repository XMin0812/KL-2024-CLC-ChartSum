The global tea market was valued at over 52 billion U.S. dollars in 2018 , and is expected to rise to over 81 billion dollars by 2026 . Tea has a long history of popularity worldwide . It is thought to have originated in China , with records of its use dating back to the 3rd century AD . Originally used as a medicinal beverage , the drink was popularized for recreational use during the Tang dynasty and spread to other Asian countries . After European traders brought the leaves back to Europe in the 16th century its popularity spread in the West as well .
