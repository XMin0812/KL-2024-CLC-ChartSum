Overall , the weekly number of people tested for the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Denmark increased in 2020 . From the week covering the 7th to the 13th of December , 708 thousand tests were performed . In total , over seven million tests had been performed for the coronavirus in Denmark , and among these , around 124 thousand had tested positive , as of December 17 , 2020 . The first case of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Denmark was confirmed on February 27 , 2020 , and the number of cases in Denmark increased significantly at the beginning of March .
