The meningitis death rate in Canada has remained relatively stable since 2000 , with minor yearly changes . According to the data , the death rate dropped from 0.2 per 100,000 population in 2011 to 0.1 in 2012 . The same drop occurred in 2014 and 2015 . Meningitis occurs when there is an inflammation of the meninges , the membrane that surrounds the brain and spinal cord .
