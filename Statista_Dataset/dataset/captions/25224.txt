This statistic displays the annual volume of passenger transport by all modes of road transportation in Lithuania from 2002 to 2018 . In the period under consideration , passenger transport volume oscillated . In 2018 , passenger volume amounted to approximately 32.7 billion passenger-kilometers . The highest volume was recorded in 2006 at over 42.75 billion passenger-kilometers , whereas the lowest volume was recorded in 2002 at around 18.5 billion passenger-kilometers .
