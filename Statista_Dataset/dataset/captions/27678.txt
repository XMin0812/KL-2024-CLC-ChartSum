This statistic shows Boehringer Ingelheim 's expenditure on research and development as a percentage of revenues between 2004 and 2019 . In 2019 , 18.2 percent of the company 's total revenues were issued on research and development . The Boehringer Ingelheim group is one of the world 's top 20 pharmaceutical companies . The company is headquartered in Ingelheim , Germany .
