The statistic shows the annual average Consumer Price Index ( CPI ) of coffee , tea and cocoa in the United Kingdom from 2003 to 2019 , where 2015 equals 100 . In 2019 , the price index value of coffee , tea and cocoa in the UK was measured at 106.3 , an increase from the previous year ..
