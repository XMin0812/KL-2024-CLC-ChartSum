As per recent data , NXP Semiconductor 's revenue reached 8.88 billion U.S. dollars in 2019 , a reduction from the 9.41 billion U.S. dollars that was seen in 2018 . The revenue figure for 2019 was the lowest posted by the company since 2015 , with 2016 to 2018 revenues totaling in excess of nine billion U.S. dollars each year .
