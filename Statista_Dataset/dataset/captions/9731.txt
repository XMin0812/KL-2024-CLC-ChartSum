This statistic shows the gross domestic product ( GDP ) per capita in Nepal from 1985 to 2018 , with projections up until 2025 . GDP refers to the total market value of all goods and services that are produced within a country per year . It is an important indicator of the economic strength of a country . In 2018 , GDP per capita in Nepal amounted to around 1,033.91 U.S. dollars .
