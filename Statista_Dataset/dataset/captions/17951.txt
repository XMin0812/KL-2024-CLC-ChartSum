This statistic illustrates the expenditure of AbbVie on research and development as a percentage of revenue from 2012 to 2019 . In 2019 , AbbVie spent some 19 percent of its revenue on research and development . Abbot was a U.S.-based global pharmaceutical and healthcare products company , headquartered in Chicago , Illinois . In January 2013 , Abbott was separated into two companies : Abbott Laboratories and AbbVie . Abbott Laboratories is now specialized on medical products , while AbbVie is responsible for research-based pharmaceuticals .
