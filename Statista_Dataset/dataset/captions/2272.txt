On December 16 , 2020 , ten deaths were attributed to the coronavirus in Denmark . The first death related to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Denmark was reported on March 11 , 2020 . The number of deaths has since risen to a total of 992 . During the pandemic , with 22 deaths , the heftiest daily death toll was registered on April 1 . The number of people who were confirmed infected by the virus in the country had reached a total of 123,813 as of December 17 , 2020 .
