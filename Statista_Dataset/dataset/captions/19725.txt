In the period from 2009 to 2019 , the number of asylum applications in Finland peaked in 2015 , as large numbers of migrants arrived in Europe . From 2016 onwards the number of asylum seekers decreased again . Since the peak of nearly 32.5 thousand applications in 2015 , the corresponding figure of 2016 was roughly 5,600 applications . In 2019 , the number of asylum applications amounted to 4,550 in total .
