The number of civilians killed as a result of police intervention in the state of Rio de Janeiro , Brazil has more than quadrupled between 2013 and 2019 . In the latter year , more than 1.8 thousand people were killed in circumstances related to police operations in the state of Rio de Janeiro , up from 1.5 thousand deaths a year earlier . In the first seven months of 2020 , more than 800 deaths by state agents were reported .
