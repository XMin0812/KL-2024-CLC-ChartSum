In 2018 , the Italian fashion company Benetton Group reported a turnover of almost one billion Euros . Despite the impressive figure , the turnover of 2018 represented a decrease for Benetton compared to the previous years . Indeed , in 2011 , the first year considered in this graph , the turnover of the company amounted to approximately 1.6 billion Euros . Despite reporting a decreasing turnover over the years considered , the company increased its workforce almost threefold , growing from 1.3 thousand employees in 2011 to over 3.5 thousand in 2017 .
