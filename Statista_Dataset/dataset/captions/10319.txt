The statistic represents the U.S. income tax revenues from 2000 to 2019 with an additional forecast from 2020 to 2030 as a percentage of the Gross Domestic Product . Income tax revenues amounted to 1.72 trillion U.S. dollars in 2019 , which was about 8.1 percent of the U.S. GDP . The forecast predicts an increase in income tax revenues up to 3.05 trillion U.S. dollars in 2030 , which would be about 9.5 percent of the projected U.S. GDP .
