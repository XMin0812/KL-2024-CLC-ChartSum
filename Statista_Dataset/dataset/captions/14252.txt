This statistic shows the seats won in the national elections for the Senate in the Netherlands on the 27th of May 2019 . Prime Minister Mark Rutte 's liberal VVD ( People 's Party for Freedom and Democracy ) remained the largest party in the senate . However , the Forum voor Democratie , a Eurosceptic party led by Thierry Baudet , also won 12 seats in the 2019 elections . The opposition Christian-democratic party CDA ( Christian Democratic Appeal ) became the third party in the Senate , with nine seats .
