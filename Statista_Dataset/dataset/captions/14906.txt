This statistic displays the distribution of seats in the Flemish Parliament after the regional elections of May 26 , 2019 in Belgium . The Flemish nationalist party , N-VA , managed to obtain 35 seats ( 24.83 percent of the votes ) , followed by Vlaams Belang and the Flemish Christian democrats , who respectively won 23 and 19 seats ( 18.50 percent and 15.40 percent of the votes ) .
