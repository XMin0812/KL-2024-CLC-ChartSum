This statistic shows a timeline with the global number of unique monthly paying King Digital Entertainment users as of the fourth quarter of 2015 . As of the most recently reported quarter , 6.58 million people made a purchase of a virtual item through the mobile gaming company 's apps per month . Popular titles include Candy Crush Saga , Farm Ville Saga as well as Bubble Witch Saga , all of which are monetized through in-game item purchases . King 's gaming apps are available on Facebook as well as on mobile platforms .
