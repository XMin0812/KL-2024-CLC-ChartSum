This statistic shows the gross value added ( GVA ) of retail trade , excluding motor vehicles and motorcycles , in the United Kingdom from 2008 to 2018 . In 2012 , the retail trade of goods contributed almost 73.9 billion British pounds to the GVA of the UK Non-Financial Business Economy . Since then , the GVA of the UK retail trade has increased year over year , with 2018 showing figures of 88.4 billion British pounds .
