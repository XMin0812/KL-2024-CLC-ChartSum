This statistic presents the average sales of the most popular user-generated LINE sticker sets . As of April 2014 , chat app LINE permitted users create and sell their on LINE chat stickers in the app 's digital marketplace . As of August 2014 , the average sales of the top ten sticker set were 22.3 million yen ( approximately 215,200 U.S. dollars ) each . The top 100 sticker sets generated 6.5 million yen in average sales . According to LINE , about half of sales generated from sticker purchases were distributed to the creator .
