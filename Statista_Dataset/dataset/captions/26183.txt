Ecuador 's total central government debt in 2018 reached 42.6 percent of the country 's gross domestic product ( GDP ) . In 2018 , the public debt that Ecuador 's central government owed to foreign creditors equaled 30.1 percent of the country 's GDP . The volume of external debt in relation to GDP has been decreasing since 2000 , reaching its lowest share in 2019 with 10.4 percent , before another steady increase from 2012 to 2018 .
