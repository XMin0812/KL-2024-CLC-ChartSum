This timeline depicts the revenues of Luxottica worldwide from 2007 to 2019 . In 2019 , the global revenues of Luxottica amounted to approximately 9.5 billion euros . Founded in 1961 in Agordo , Italy , the Luxottica Group S.p.A. is the world 's largest eyewear company . Its best known brands include Ray-Ban , Persol and Oakley , Inc . The company also makes sunglasses and frames for a multitude of designer brands such as Chanel and Prada .
