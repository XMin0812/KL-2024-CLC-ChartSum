As of November 2020 , the average price in Nigeria for a kilogram of beef with bone added up to 1,081.17 Nigerian Naira , which equaled to roughly 2.8 U.S. dollars . One loaf of unsliced bread cost 307 Naira ( 0.81 U.S. dollars ) , whereas frozen chicken reached 1,936.97 Naira ( some five dollars ) . Food prices in Nigeria have been rising . In November 2020 , food 's CPI peaked at almost 400 points .
