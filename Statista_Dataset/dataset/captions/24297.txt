Groningen Airport Eelde is a small international airport near Eelde , northeastern Netherlands . In the first quarter of 2019 , approximately 26,500 passengers utilized the airport This was the lowest number of passengers seen within the time-frame considered . However , in the third quarter passenger numbers roughly doubled , to 62,028 . The highest passenger volume was registered in the third quarter of 2018 , when Groningen Airport Eelde welcomed nearly 80,000 passengers . Another small airport in the Netherlands is Maatricht Aachen Airport . approximately 19,000 people utilized this airport in the second quarter of 2019 , an increase compared to the same quarter in the previous year .
