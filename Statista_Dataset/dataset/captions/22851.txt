The eSports market has boomed in recent years with more and more viewers tuning in to watch their favorite games being played by some of the best gamers in the world . As a result , a new market has opened up for sponsors and advertisers to sell their products . According to a 2019 report , sponsorship and advertising spending on the eSports market is estimated to increase from 124 million U.S. dollars in 2017 to over 634 million U.S. dollars in 2023 .
