The crime rate in Germany for 2018 was 6,548 crimes per 100 thousand people , making it the third year in a row that the crime rate has fallen in the country . Between 2000 and 2004 the crime rate in Germany increased from 7,625 to 8,037 , before declining to 7,253 by 2010 . The years between 2010 and 2015 saw an increase in the crime rate , but after 2015 , the recent trend of declining crime started , leading to the generally low figures seen in the most recent years .
