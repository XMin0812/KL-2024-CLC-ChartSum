The graph shows China 's gross domestic product ( GDP ) until 2019 , with IMF forecasts until 2025 . In 2019 , GDP in China was around 14.73 trillion U.S. dollars . That particular year , per capita GDP in China exceeded 10,000 U.S. dollars for the first time . In comparison to the GDP of the other BRIC countries India , Russia and Brazil , China came first that year and second in the world GDP ranking . See the Russian GDP for comparison .
