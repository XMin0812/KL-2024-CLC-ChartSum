The statistic shows gross domestic product ( GDP ) per capita in Botswana from 1985 to 2025 . GDP is the total value of all goods and services produced in a country in a year . It is considered to be a very important indicator of the economic strength of a country and a positive change is an indicator of economic growth . In 2019 , the estimated GDP per capita in Botswana amounted to around 7,772.67 U.S. dollars .
