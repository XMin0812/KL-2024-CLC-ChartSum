The highest number of confirmed coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) cases in Denmark as of December 17 , 2020 , was in the Capital Region , with 61,583 cases . The second highest number of cases was in the Central Denmark Region , which had 22,537 cases . The first case of the coronavirus in Denmark was confirmed on February 27 , 2020 . The number of cases in Denmark increased significantly at the beginning of March . By December 17 , 2020 , the number of confirmed cases in the country had reached a total of 123,813 .
