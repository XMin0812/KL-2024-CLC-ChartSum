The statistic shows gross domestic product ( GDP ) per capita in Myanmar , also known as Burma , from 2005 to 2015 with projections up to 2025 . GDP is the total value of all goods and services produced in a country in a year . It is considered to be a very important indicator of the economic strength of a country and a positive change is an indicator of economic growth . In 2015 , the GDP per capita in Myanmar amounted to around 1,223.74 U.S. dollars .
