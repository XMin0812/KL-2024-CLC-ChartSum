In 2019 , the revenue of IBM reached more than 77 billion U.S. dollars , continuing a trend of declining revenues over the past eight years since 2011 when IBM 's revenue peaked at a record 106.9 billion U.S. dollars . Despite the declining revenues over the past few years , IBM is still one of the most valuable technology brands in the world behind only Google , Apple , Microsoft , Tencent , and Facebook .
