This statistic illustrates the total value of venture capital private equity investments in Europe from 2007 to 2019 . Private equity is the OTC provision of equity capital through private or institutional investments with the participation of companies in another company for a limited time in order to generate financial benefits . It can be seen that the total value of venture capital investments has seen an annual increase from 2012 onwards . In 2019 , the value total value of venture capital funds raised amounted to 14.8 billion euros .
