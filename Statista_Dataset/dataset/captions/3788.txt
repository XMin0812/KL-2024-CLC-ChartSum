In 2019 , Indonesia 's most valuable brand in terms of brand value was Bank Central Asia ( BCA ) , with a brand value of 13.05 billion U.S. dollars . In terms of growth , however , retail e-commerce site tokopedia was the fastest-rising brand , with a 487 percent growth in 2019 . This was followed by travel e-commerce site Traveloka at 111 percent , and ride-hailing start-up Go-Jek , which had 88 percent growth . All three of the fastest-growing brands were Indonesian start-ups which had attained unicorn status .
