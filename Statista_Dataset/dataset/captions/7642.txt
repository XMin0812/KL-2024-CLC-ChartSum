In 2016 , Belgium was in the top five European countries for its number of suicide casualties . That year , the country held the fifth position behind Lithuania , Latvia , Slovenia , and Hungary . From 2006 to 2016 , the number of committed suicides was relatively stable , fluctuating between 1,9 to 2 thousand per year . The number of deaths reached a peak in 2011 with 2.084 casualties .
