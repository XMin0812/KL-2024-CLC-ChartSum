In Italy , the per capita consumption of soft drinks gradually decreased to 50.1 liters as of 2019 . Seven years before , the per capita consumption of soft drink amounted to 63.8 liters . In the country , in 2017 , the annual consumption reached 3.1 billion liters , approximately . Within the Italian market , the no or low calories soft drinks held a market share of nine percent , a percentage change of one point compared to 2016
