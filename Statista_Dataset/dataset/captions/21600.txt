Dow 's Industrial Intermediates & Infrastructure segment consists of two customer oriented global businesses : industrial solutions and polyurethanes , and construction chemicals . In 2019 , Dow generated around 13.44 billion U.S. dollars of revenue in this segment . On September 1 , 2017 , Dow Chemical and DuPont successfully completed their merger of equals . From that date until April 1 , 2019 , the single company was known as DowDuPont . After that point in time , the conglomerate split into three entities : Dow , DuPont , and Corteva .
