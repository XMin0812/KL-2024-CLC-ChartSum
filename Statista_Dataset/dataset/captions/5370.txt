The German household appliances manufacturer Miele generated 4.16 billion euros in revenue in its 2018/19 financial year . This marks the highest revenue ever for the company and it is only the second time Miele has surpassed four billion euros in revenue in a financial year . From 2020 onwards Miele switched its reporting to match the calendar year . In line with the switch for the reporting period , Miele reported revenues for the second half of 2019 ( July to December ) in a one-off stub year separately . During this period the company 's revenue amounted to 2.24 billion euros .
