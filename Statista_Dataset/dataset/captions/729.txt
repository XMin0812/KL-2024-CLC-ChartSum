In June 2020 , the gasoline price per liter in the United States stood at 0.55 U.S. dollars , the cheapest among the selected eight countries . However , this is a considerable price change compared to June 2019 , when figures were 23.4 percent higher . Italy recorded the highest fuel price of all countries listed here , at 1.56 U.S. dollars per liter . The prices concern unleaded premium ( 95 RON ) for France , Germany , Italy , Spain , and the United Kingdom and regular unleaded for Canada , Japan , and the United States ( AKI 87 ) .
