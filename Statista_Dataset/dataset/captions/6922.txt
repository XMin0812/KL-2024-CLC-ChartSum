This statistic presents a ranking of the most popular pets on Instagram as of June 2020 , sorted by the number of followers . According to InfluencerDB , pomeranian Jiff Pom was the most-followed petfluencer on the photo sharing app with 10.4 million followers . The Pomeranian is also an animal actor , having appeared in the music video for the 2014 Katy Perry song `` Dark Horse '' .
