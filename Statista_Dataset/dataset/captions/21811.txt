The statistic represents the U.S. estate and gift tax revenues from 2000 to 2015 with an additional forecast from 2016 to 2026 as a percentage of the Gross Domestic Product . Estate and gift tax revenues amounted to 19 billion U.S. dollars in 2015 , which is about 0.1 percent of the U.S. GDP . The forecast predicts an increase in estate and gift tax revenues up to 30 billion U.S. dollars in 2026 , which would be about 0.1 percent of the U.S. GDP .
