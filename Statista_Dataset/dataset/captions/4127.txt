The first case of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Denmark was confirmed on February 27 , 2020 , by December 17 , the number of confirmed cases in the country reached a total of 123,813 . The number of cases in the country increased significantly at the beginning of March , and then decreased for a period before they started to grow again in the end of July . The worldwide number of confirmed cases of coronavirus was over 74 million as of December , 2020 .
