The statistic shows the largest merger and acquisition ( M & A ) deals in Poland ( CEE ) in 2019 , ranked by transaction value . The largest foreign investment in Poland , worth nearly 1.2 billion euros , was the purchase of the DCT Gdansk container terminal , on a joint bid by PSA International port group , owned by Temasek Holdings of Singapore , the Polish Development Fund , and Australia 's IFM Global Infrastructure .
