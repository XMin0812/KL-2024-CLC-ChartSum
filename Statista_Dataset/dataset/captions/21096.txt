This statistic shows the total assets of Starwood Hotels & Resorts worldwide from 2008 to 2015 . Starwood Hotels & Resorts had total assets amounting to approximately 8.27 billion U.S. dollars in 2015 . Starwood Hotels & Resorts was founded in 1969 and now has more than 1,200 properties worldwide . In 2014 , Starwood Hotels & Resorts was the forth largest hotel group in the world in terms of revenue , followed by the Accor hotel group .
