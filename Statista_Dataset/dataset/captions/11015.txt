This statistic shows the number of employees of the Kraft Foods Group in North America in 2014 , by geographic region . In that year , the Kraft Food Group employed about 20,100 people in the United States . The total workforce of the Kraft Food Group amounted to about 22,100 employees . On July 2 , 2015 , the Kraft Foods Group completed the merger with Heinz to become The Kraft Heinz Company .
