This statistic shows the total number of old age pensions ( AOW ) in the Netherlands from 1998 to 2019 ( in thousands ) . In 2019 , there were approximately 3.4 million old age pensions in the Netherlands . The AOW scheme is an insurance scheme which covers everyone who lives or works in the Netherlands , regardless of nationality and is a basic state pension . As a rule , everyone who has reached the AOW pension age and lives or has lived in the Netherlands is entitled to this . It is paid under the National Old Age Pensions Act or AOW .
