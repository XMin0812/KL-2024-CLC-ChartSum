According to survey results , 64 percent of Russians continued to work during the holiday week the country announced as a measure to prevent the mass spread of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) from March 30 to April 5 , 2020 . Furthermore , 16 percent of respondents stated that their employers sent them to paid vacations .
