While the number of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) cases in Italy increased , the Italian leading stock index FTSE MIB registered one of its most significant drops ever . In fact , the last week of February 2020 was the eighth worst week for the Italian stock market since 1998 ( as of February 2020 ) . However , the worst day overall was registered on March 12 , 2020 . For further information about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , please visit our dedicated Facts and Figures page .
