In 2020 , Peru scored 0.65 in the gender gap index in the area of economic participation and opportunity , which means that women are 35 percent less likely to have equal economic participation and opportunities than men . That year , Peru scored 0.67 in estimated earned income , which shows a gender pay gap of approximately 33 percent ( on average , women 's income was estimated to be 33 percent lower than men 's ) .
