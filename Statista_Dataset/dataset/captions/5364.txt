As of April 2018 , the channel of EDM festival Tomorrowland had the most subscribers of all YouTube channels coming from Belgium . It outperformed other channels from the country , reaching more than nine million subscribers . Tomorrowland archives most live performances of A-list DJs who ever performed at the music festival on YouTube , which might explain the popularity of the channel . Belgian DJ Dmitri Vegas , ranked along with his brother as the number two DJs of the world according to DJ Mag in 2018 , had around 2.9 million subscribers . The numbers concern worldwide subscribers , although YouTube is a popular music source in Belgium itself .
