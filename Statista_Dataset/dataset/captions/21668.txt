Green bond indices make it easier for investors to track the performance of green bonds and compare it with other investments . Bloomberg Barclays MSCI Global Green Bond Index was launched in 2014 with the aim provide a benchmark for the green bonds market . Between 2015 and 2019 , the Bloomberg Barclays MSCI Global Green Bond Index saw an overall increase , reaching 108.08 index points as of the end of 2019 .
