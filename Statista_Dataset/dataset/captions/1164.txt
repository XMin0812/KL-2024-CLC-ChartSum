In 2018 , Nokia had 98,322 employees , situated in over 130 locations throughout the world , with around 40 thousand each in Europe and the Asia-Pacific region . In comparison , Nokia 's net revenue was largest in North America at 6.95 billion euros , where about 13 percent of Nokia 's employees are located , and second to that was Europe with 6.62 billion euros . Twenty-two percent of Nokia 's workforce is comprised of women , similar to competitor Ericsson , which has 23 percent of its workforce made up of women employees .
