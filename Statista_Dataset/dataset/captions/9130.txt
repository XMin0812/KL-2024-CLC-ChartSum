Toyota 's market capitalization reached 197 billion U.S. dollars at the end of March 2020 . Toyota 's shares peaked at around 7,929 Japanese yen ( or about 73.5 U.S. dollars ) during the fiscal year ended March 31 , 2020 . The Toyota Motor Corporation is the automaker with the second highest market cap , trailing only that of Tesla . The company is one of the largest automobile manufacturers in the world and the incumbent leader in terms of global car sales by manufacturer .
