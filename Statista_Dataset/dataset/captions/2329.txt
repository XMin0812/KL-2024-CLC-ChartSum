This statistic shows the leading 20 brands of soup sold in the United Kingdom ( UK ) , ranked by their total sales value in the year to December 2014 . The ranking covers the overall soup market , including canned , chilled and instant soup products . Heinz Classic ranked highest with total sales valued at 162.8 million British pounds , followed by New Covent Garden at 43.9 million British pounds . The most popular soup format in the United Kingdom is soup in tins , pouches and tubs , with a majority of consumers using soup products two to three times per month or less .
