This statistic displays the share of football players with a migration background on FIFA World Cups from 1930 to 2018 . As of 2018 , approximately 13 percent of the football players were not born in the country where they are currently playing for . This is the highest percentage in the given time period , with the lowest in 1958 and 1974 when only roughly one percent of the players that were active during the World Cup had a migration background .
