The statistic shows the gross domestic product ( GDP ) per capita in Iraq from 2005 to 2025 . All figures have been estimated . GDP is the total value of all goods and services produced in a country in a year . It is considered to be a very important indicator of the economic strength of a country and a positive change is an indicator of economic growth . In 2019 , the estimated GDP per capita in Iraq amounted to around 5,883.68 U.S. dollars .
