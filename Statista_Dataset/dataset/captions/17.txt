As of January 3 , 2021 , there were 26,399,030 confirmed cases of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) across the whole of Europe since the first confirmed cases in France on January 25 . Russia has been the worst affected country in Europe with 3,260,138 confirmed cases , followed by France with 2,655,728 cases . The United Kingdom and Italy have approximately 2.65 and 2.16 million cases respectively .
