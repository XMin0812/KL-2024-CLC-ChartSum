Amazon UK Services Ltd generated 2.35 billion British pounds in turnover in 2018 . The United Kingdom based company is responsible for the operation of the e-commerce giant 's distribution centers in the country . It increased its turnover in each year between 2009 and 2018 , with figures for the latter year being 21 times the amount seen in 2009 . As of 2019 , Amazon was the most valuable brand worldwide .
