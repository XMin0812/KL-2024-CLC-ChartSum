The highest paid Korean football player in Korean professional football , the K-League , in 2019 was Kim Jin-su with an annual salary of over 1.4 billion South Korean won . The second and third best-paid players earned around two-thirds as much . All five of the top-earning Korean football players were from the same football club , Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors .
