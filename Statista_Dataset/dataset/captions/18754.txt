After the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic outbreak in Italy , all economic activities started being affected significantly , including the social media and user-generated content sector . According to a survey conducted as of March 2020 , 75 percent of influencers declared that they posted more Instagram Stories during the coronavirus crisis , while 40 percent went live on Instagram more often.For further information about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , please visit our dedicated Fact and Figures page .
