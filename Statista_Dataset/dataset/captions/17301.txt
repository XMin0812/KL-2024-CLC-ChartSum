Lehman Brothers had assets worth 691.06 billion U.S. dollars when the investment bank filed for bankruptcy on September 15 , 2008 , which is still the all-time largest bankruptcy filing in U.S. history . This event was one of the major points in the timeline of the Great Recession , as it was the first time a bank of its size had failed and had a domino effect on the global banking sector as well as wiping almost five percent of the S & P 500 in one day .
