This statistic displays the percentage of primary Sjögren 's patients in the U.S. and the EU with comorbidities , as of 2015 . Some 28 percent of primary Sjögren 's patients in the U.S. and EU had some thyroid disease as well . Sjögren 's syndrome is characterized by a person 's white blood cells attacking moisture-producing glands which can cause dry eyes and mouth , as well as fatigue and joint pains . This condition is considered a chronic autoimmune disease .
