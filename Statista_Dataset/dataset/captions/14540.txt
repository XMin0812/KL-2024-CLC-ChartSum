In 2019 , 1.3 thousand migrants died by crossing the Central Mediterranean route . The Mediterranean Sea recorded worldwide the largest number of deaths and missing cases of people who migrated . In particular , the Central Mediterranean Sea registered the highest number of fatalities . Between January and November 2020 , 724 people were estimated to have died in the attempt to cross the Central Mediterranean route . Furthermore , 2016 was the year in which the number of deaths on the Central Mediterranean Route peaked . In fact , about 4.6 thousand migrants died in the Central Mediterranean Sea in 2016 . This route includes the crossing from North Africa to Italy , and Malta . The main departing country is Libya , while Tunisia , Egypt , and East Algeria represent minor departing shores .
