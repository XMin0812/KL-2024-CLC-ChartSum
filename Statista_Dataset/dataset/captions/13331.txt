This statistic shows the results of a survey released in 2017 in which respondents were asked whether they are members of loyalty programs offered by fast moving consumer goods ( FMCG ) retailers in the United Kingdom ( UK ) . The largest percentage of respondents ( 55 percent ) said they are members of loyalty programs at multiple retailers , while 28 percent have just one loyalty program membership . Out of those surveyed , only 17 percent are not members of any loyalty schemes at FMCG retailers .
