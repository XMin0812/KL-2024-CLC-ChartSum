This statistic displays the annual direct and indirect costs of multiple sclerosis ( MS ) per patient in the United States as of 2015 . MS cost a minimum annual average of 8,528 U.S. dollars per patient in the country . There are about 200 new cases that are diagnosed every week in the country . MS is a disease that damages nerve cells in the brain and spinal cord which disrupts the ability of the nervous system to communicate with the body .
