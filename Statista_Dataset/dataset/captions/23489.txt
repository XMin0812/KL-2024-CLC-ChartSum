The statistic shows gross domestic product ( GDP ) in Sub-Saharan Africa from 2014 to 2018 , with projections up until 2024 . Gross domestic product ( GDP ) denotes the aggregate value of all services and goods produced within a country in any given year . GDP is an important indicator of a country 's economic power . In 2018 , Sub-Saharan Africa 's aggregated gross domestic product amounted to around 1,642.18 billion U.S. dollars .
