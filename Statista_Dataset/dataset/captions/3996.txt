This statistic shows the profile of Millennial travelers in the United States as of July 2016 . Millennial travelers were characterized most by their cultural interest with an average Traveler Psychographic Intensity Index score of 70.3 in this category . Millennial travelers were the generation group whose activity choices were most influenced by what was posted on social media . They were more than double as likely to be influenced by sites such as Facebook and Instagram than Baby Boomer travelers .
