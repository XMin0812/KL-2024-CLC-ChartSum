In an annual assessment of advertising campaigns , the top three spots were taken by promotional activities in the U.S. and Japan . The Japanese ad `` Oyster Kanji Dictation '' for Hiroshima Tourism secured the second spot , following the `` It 's a Tide ad '' for Tide by the agencies Saatchi & Saatchi New York , Hearts & Science New York and MKTG New York , which topped the list in 2019 .
