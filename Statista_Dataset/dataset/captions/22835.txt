Presumably due to the coronavirus outbreak , the salary of Norwegian musicians decreased by about 3,000 Norwegian kroner by the end of the first half of 2020 compared to the beginning of the year . The average monthly wage amounted to 45,820 kroner in the second quarter . The income loss was most significant among women , as they earned more than 4,000 kroner less than the previous quarter . However , in the third quarter of 2020 , the salary grew again , reaching the same level as it was in the first quarter of the year .
