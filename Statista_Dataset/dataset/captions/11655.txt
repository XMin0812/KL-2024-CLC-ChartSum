In 2017 , the Italian fashion company Moschino S.p.A. reported a net loss of approximately 180 thousand euros , whereas the previous year the company had registered a net profit of 0.7 million euros . On the other hand , the turnover of Moschino S.p.A. increased steadily from 2011 to 2017 , when it reached a value of 53.2 million euros .
