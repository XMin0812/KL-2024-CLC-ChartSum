This statistic illustrates the amount of Volkswagen Golf passenger cars registered for the first time in Great Britain from 2001 to 2019 , in thousand units . In the period of consideration , number of VW Golf new registrations oscillated . The peak sales in VW Golf was recorded in 2002 , at about 85,100 new registrations . In 2019 , 58,372 first time registrations for VW Golf were recorded . This value presents a decline of roughly 31.4 percent in comparison to the peak year .
