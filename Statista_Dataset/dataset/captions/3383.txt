This statistic shows the Gini coefficient on household income after taxes ( based on the modified OECD scale ) in Singapore from 2010 to 2019 . During the time surveyed , the Gini coefficient in Singapore was highest in 2012 , with an index score of 0.41 . From 2016 to 2019 , the Gini index score was at its lowest at 0.38 .
