This statistic presents the most popular tax havens of Mossack Fonseca as of April 2016 , by number of companies located in a country . Mossack Fonseca , based in Panama , is one of the world 's largest offshore services providers . The data about the company and its clients was revealed in April 2016 as a result of a leak . As of April 2016 , Mossack Fonseca operated 48,360 offshore companies in Panama .
