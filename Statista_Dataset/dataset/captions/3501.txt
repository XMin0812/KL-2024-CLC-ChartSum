In 2019 , Peugeot ranked as the leading make in Spain with a market share of 8.73 percent . SEAT ranked second with a market share of 8.51 percent , followed by Volkswagen with 7.24 percent . The Spanish car brand saw its sales increase , with the most registered SEAT models being the SEAT Leon and SEAT Ibiza , both exceeding 30 thousand registrations during 2018 .
