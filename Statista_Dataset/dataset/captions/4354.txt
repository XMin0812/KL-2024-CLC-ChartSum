Harley-Davidson sold 516 motorcycles in the United Kingdom in March 2020 . Across all years recorded , sales figures were highest in March , as this is one of the months when the Driver & Vehicle Licensing Agency ( DVLA ) issues new registration plates . This phenomenon is not limited to motorcycles alone , but can also be seen when looking at car sales . The total number of Harley-Davidson motorcycles on the road in Great Britain was over 52,000 units in 2019 .
