This statistic shows the revenue of Bayer HealthCare from 2005 to 2018 . Bayer HealthCare is a pharmaceutical and medical products subgroup of the Bayer AG . It is active in the research , development , manufacture and marketing of health products for people and animals . In 2005 , Bayer HealthCare generated almost eight billion euros of revenue . Since 2019 , there is no single reported Bayer HealthCare segment .
