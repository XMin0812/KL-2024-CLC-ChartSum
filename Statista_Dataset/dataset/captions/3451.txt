From November 23 to 29 , 2020 , `` Caught In Time '' - a Chinese action crime film - led China 's box office revenue , adding about 27.5 million U.S. dollars to its total 56.2 million U.S. dollars box office revenue in China . DreamWorks Animation 's `` The Croods : A New Age '' ranked second with 17.9 million U.S. dollars of box office revenue after 3 screening days .
