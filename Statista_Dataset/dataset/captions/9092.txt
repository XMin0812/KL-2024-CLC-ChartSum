The statistic illustrates the distribution of total sales of the Personal Health segment of the Philips Group in 2019 , by area of business . In that year , domestic appliances had with 40 percent the most share in sales of personal health . The total sales of Personal Health was approximately 5.85 billion euros . After spin-offs of other product segments , Koninklijke Philips N.V. today is a company focused on healthcare/medical technology .
