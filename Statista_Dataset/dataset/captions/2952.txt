Bayer is an international pharmaceutical company based out of Germany . As of end-2019 , the company had a total of some 104,000 employees globally . This is a significant increase from the previous years . In general , the number of employees at Bayer had remained stable until 2016 when Convestro , Bayer 's Material Science division , spun off into its own company . It increased again with the acquisition of Monsanto during 2018 .
