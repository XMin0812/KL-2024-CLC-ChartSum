The preliminary average annual price of one barrel of West Texas Intermediate crude oil stood at 38.52 U.S. dollars as of November 2020 . WTI is a grade of crude oil also known as `` Texas light sweet . '' It is measured to have an API gravity of around 39.6 and specific gravity of about 0.83 , which is considered `` light '' relative to other crude oils . This oil also contains roughly 0.24 percent sulfur , and is therefore named `` sweet . ''
