1,462 of the confirmed coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) cases in Iceland were reported to have been infected in the home country . 346 of the Icelandic cases reported to have been infected abroad . The first case of the coronavirus in Iceland was confirmed on February 28 , 2020 . The number of cases has since risen to a total of 1,815 as of June 16 , 2020 .
