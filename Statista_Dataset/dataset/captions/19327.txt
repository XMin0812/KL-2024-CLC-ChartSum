This statistic shows the gross gambling yield ( GGY ) of the total gambling industry in Great Britain from April 2010 to September 2019 . During the period October 2018 to September 2019 , the regulated gambling industry in Great Britain generated a GGY of 14.26 billion British pounds , a decrease compared with the previous reporting period . The GGY is a key metric used within the gambling industry to refer to the difference between the amount of money received by a given agent , and the amount of money payed out by the agent . Sales revenue can be thought of as a comparable metric from outside the gambling industry .
