Cosmetics and toiletries are bought in larger quantities each passing month in the UK . However , sales reached an all-time low in April and May 2020 , when sales were comparable to 2012 . Most recently , in June 2020 , the retail sales volume of articles in these two categories has leveled out and reached an index number of 112.9 as presented in the graph . However , this is still lower than the volume recorded for the same month of the year prior by an index of 31.9 .
