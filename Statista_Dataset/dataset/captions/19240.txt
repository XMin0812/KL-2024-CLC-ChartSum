Due to the outbreak of coronavirus in Poland in March 2020 , hotels must fight for survival . More than 80 percent of hotels plan to obtain funding to support their employees . At the same time , more than half of them have applied for tax deferrals . Over 70 percent of hotels issued vouchers to avoid reimbursement of advance payments . Nevertheless , as much as nearly 80 percent of hotels had to be closed down during the epidemic . Eighty-six percent had to cancel their bookings and return the advance payment , as well as send staff members on overdue and current holidays .
