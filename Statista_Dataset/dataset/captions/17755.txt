This statistic shows the gross domestic expenditure on research and development ( GERD ) as a percentage of GDP in Denmark from 2001 to 2017 . Over the period , the GERD share experienced an increase , peaking in 2016 , when gross expenditure on research and development made 3.12 percent of Denmark 's GDP . In 2015 , GERD amounted to 2.96 percents of the GDP , the highest ratio in the European Union ( EU ) after Austria .
