In 2019 , the urban population in Vietnam stood at approximately 35.69 million people . The six largest urban agglomerations include Hanoi , Hai Phong , Da Nang , Bien Hoa , Ho Chi Minh City and Can Tho . On the other hand , Ben Tre , Thai Binh and Bac Giang had the lowest rates of urbanization in the country .
