This statistic shows the annual development of customer deposits , entrusted to the Banco Santander group from 2004 to 2019 . It can be seen that during the observation period , the the value of customer deposits increased . In 2004 , it was approximately 283.2 billion euros , whereas in 2019 , it reached a value of over 824 billion euros . This was the highest value recorded since 2004 .
