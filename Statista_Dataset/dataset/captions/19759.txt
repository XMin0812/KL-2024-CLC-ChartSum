The statistic represents U.S. trade tariffs against Chinese solar cell and PV module manufacturers and exporters as of May 2012 . Following a petition filed by SolarWorld , the U.S. Department of Commerce determined that Chinese producers and exporters sold solar cells in the United States at dumping margins ranging from 31.14 percent to 249.96 percent . As a consequence , the company that had received a preliminary dumping margin of 31.14 percent , Trina Solar , now faces tariffs of 31.14 percent .
