This statistic represents the percentage of people of different ages who owned bicycles in England according to combined surveys from 2016 , 2017 and 2018 . Bicycles are clearly most popular amongst young children , as the proportion of 5 - 10 year-old who owned bikes was approximately double the average for all ages ( 5+ ) , and was more than 50 percent higher than the proportion of bicycle-owners in the 21 - 29 age group .
