The statistic shows trends in technical consumer goods revenue in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from the third quarter of 2012 to the first quarter of 2020 . In the first quarter of 2020 , technical consumer goods generated a revenue of approximately nine billion British pounds . Technical consumer goods include all of the electronics goods that are included in the report : consumer electronics , photography , major and small domestic appliances , information technology , telecommunications , and office equipment and consumables .
