The market capitalization of the Italian banking Group UniCredit reached a value of about 15.8 billion euros as of September 2020 . Despite this impressive figure , the bank ranked 13th in a list of the leading banks in Europe in terms of market capitalization as of December 2019 . UniCredit 's direct competitor in Italy , Intesa Sanpaolo , ranked 7th in the same ranking with a market capitalization of 35.87 billion euros .
