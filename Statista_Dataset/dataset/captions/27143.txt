This statistic shows the number of freight vehicles which were transported on the Le Shuttle in the United Kingdom ( UK ) between the opening of the channel tunnel in 1994 and 2019 . Despite a large drop in freight vehicle numbers on the Le Shuttle in 2009 , the volume has since increased . In 2019 , the number of freight vehicles which were transported on the Le Shuttle amounted to close to 1.6 million .
