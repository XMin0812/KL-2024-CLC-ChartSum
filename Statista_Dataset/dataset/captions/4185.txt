The first death caused by the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Finland was reported on March 20 , 2020 . As of January 5 , 2021 , the number of deaths has since increased to a total of 576 . The number of confirmed COVID-19 cases in the country reached 37,218 on January 5 , 2021 . For further information about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , please visit our dedicated Facts and Figures page .
