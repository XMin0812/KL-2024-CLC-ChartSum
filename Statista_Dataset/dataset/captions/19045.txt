This statistic depicts the year-on-year change in the retail value of food , drink and tobacco sales in Great Britain ( GB ) from January 2015 to August 2020 . The retail sales value grew by 8.1 percent in August 2020 compared to the same month a year earlier , the highest percentage increase in the sales value of food , drink and tobacco within the period under consideration .
