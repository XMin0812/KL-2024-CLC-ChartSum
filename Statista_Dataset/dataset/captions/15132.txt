The statistic above presents the most successful movies at the North American box office in 2019 . According to the source , as of December 2019 'Avengers : Endgame ' is currently the highest grossing movie of 2019 , with a domestic box office revenue of over 858 million U.S. dollars . Disney 's remakes of 'Aladdin ' and 'The Lion King ' also performed well at the box office and earned 355 and 543 million U.S. dollars respectively .
