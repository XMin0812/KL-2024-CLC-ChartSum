This statistic displays the number of organ transplant candidates in the United States by organ , as of as of September 6 , 2020 . At this moment , there were 235 candidates in the country waiting for an intestine donation . Organ donation can be given through both a deceased and living donor if blood and oxygen are flowing through the organs until the time of recovery to ensure viability . There are over 108,000 people in the country waiting for an organ transplant .
