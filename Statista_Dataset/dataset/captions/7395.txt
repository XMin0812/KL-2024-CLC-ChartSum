This statistic shows the average milk yield per cow per year in the United Kingdom for the milk years 2002/03 to 2018/19 . During the period 2011/12 , the average milk yield was measured at 7,641 liters per cow per year . Despite a drop in 2012/13 , the average milk yield per cow has increased in the following years , reaching 7,968 liters per cow per year in 2018/19 .
