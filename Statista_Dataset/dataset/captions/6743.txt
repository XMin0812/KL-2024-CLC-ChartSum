An in depth study on patients admitted to hospital and later deceased from the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) infection in Italy revealed that three out of four individuals showed symptoms such as fever and dyspnea at the moment of admission to the hospital . Additionally , cough was found in 38 percent of individuals hospitalized with coronavirus . The virus originated in Wuhan , a Chinese city populated by millions and located in the province of Hubei .
