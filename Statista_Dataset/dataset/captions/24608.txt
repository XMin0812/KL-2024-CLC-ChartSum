This statistic depicts the results of a written survey conducted by the Food Waste Reduction Alliance and the Business for Social Responsibilty in 2012 . Survey respondents were asked regarding the distribution of food waste in the United States in 2012 , broken down by industry sector . Full-service restaurants are estimated to have generated 22 percent of total food waste in the United States in 2012 . That corresponded to a value of about 15,851 pounds .
