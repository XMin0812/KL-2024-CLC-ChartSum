Between August 2019 and July 2020 , the number of people expelled from Italy for terrorism slightly decreased . According to data , 71 individuals were forced to leave the country for terrorism-related charges . Furthermore , the number of monitored foreign terrorist fighters ( FTFs ) in Italy increased . In particular , 146 individuals were monitored between August 2019 and July 2020 , whereas in 2017 they amounted to 125 .
