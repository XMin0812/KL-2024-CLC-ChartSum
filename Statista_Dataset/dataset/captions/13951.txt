This statistic shows the total annual number of sertraline hydrochloride prescriptions in the U.S. from 2004 to 2017 , in millions . In 2004 , sertraline hydrochloride was prescribed over 28 million times . As of 2017 , the number of prescriptions for sertraline hydrochloride had increased to over 38 million . Sertraline hydrochloride is primarily used as an antidepressant of the selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor ( SSRI ) class .
