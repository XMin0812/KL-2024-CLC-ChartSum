This statistic illustrates the total value of venture capital private equity investments in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2007 to 2016 . Private equity is the OTC provision of equity capital through private or institutional investments with the participation of companies in another company for a limited time in order to generate financial benefits . It can be seen that the total value of venture capital investments fluctuated during the period under observation , reaching a value of almost 1.34 billion euros as of 2016 . The largest value of investments was found in 2007 , when venture capital investments of almost 1.9 billion euros were recorded .
