This statistic shows the operating income of Hyatt Hotels Corporation worldwide from 2008 to 2017 . Hyatt Hotels had an operating income of approximately 250 million U.S. dollars in 2017 . The Hyatt Hotel Corporation owns and operates the brands Park Hyatt , Andaz , Grand Hyatt , Hyatt Regency , Hyatt Place , Hyatt House , Hyatt Resorts and Hyatt Vacation Club .
