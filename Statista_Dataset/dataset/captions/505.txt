The National Football League is one of the most lucrative leagues in the world , with big sponsorship deals and high TV ratings to match . The players within the NFL are compensated handsomely for being part of one of the biggest leagues in the world . The highest paid player in the NFL in 2020 was Dak Prescott of the Dallas Cowboys . The Cowboys put a franchise tag worth 31.4 million U.S. dollars on Prescott in March 2020 , securing his salary for the 2020 season . However , Prescott 's season was ended prematurely in Week 5 when he suffered a serious injury to his right ankle in a game against the New York Giants .
