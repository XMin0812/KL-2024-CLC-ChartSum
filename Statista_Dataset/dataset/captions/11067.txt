The average self-isolation index in Yerevan , the capital of Armenia , reached 3.7 points on January 3 , 2021 , meaning that most people were at home . For the first time in history , subway was closed in Yerevan due to the spread of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) ; this restriction was lifted on May 18.For further information about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , please visit our dedicated Facts and Figures page .
