Nearly seven entrepreneurs out of 10 plan to reduce their employment due to the outbreak of coronavirus in Poland . And every second company in the next two months expects to lay off between 20 and 50 percent of its staff . Almost every company in Poland feels the effects of the pandemic on its businesses , and every second company assessed the situation as very serious . Nearly 70 percent of a company may close down its operations if it does not receive support from the state .
