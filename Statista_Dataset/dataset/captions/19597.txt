This statistic portrays the import trade value of bamboo and rattan furniture in the United Kingdom ( UK ) between 2011 and 2016 . Over the total time frame observed , the import trade value for these items increased by almost nine million U.S. dollars from 12.22 million U.S. dollars in 2011 to 21.07 million U.S. dollars in 2015 . Import trade value then fell down by around two million dollars between 2015 and 2016 , reaching approximately 18.7 million .
