This statistic shows the quarterly trend in the retail sales volume of footwear and leather goods ( quantity bought ) in Great Britain from 1st quarter 2015 to 2nd quarter 2020 . Overall sales generally fluctuated over this period of time , peaking at 110.4 during the third quarter of 2019 . Sales volume saw a sharp decrease in the second quarter of 2020 with an index value of 55.2 . The figures are seasonally adjusted estimates , measured using the Retail Sales Index ( RSI ) and published in index form with a reference year of 2016 equal to 100 .
