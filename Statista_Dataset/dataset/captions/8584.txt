In 2019 , the biggest shirt sponsor of the leading 50 football clubs in Europe was the airline industry . The shirt sponsorship deals with airline companies amounted to 207 million euros . Second position was taken by the automotive industry with value of the shirt sponsorship deals amounting to 159 million euros . Football sponsorship allows brands to increase brand awareness , reach new markets and raise profits . In 2019 , the value of shirt sponsorship deals from the Middle East region amounted to 250 million euros . This made the region the number one sponsor , followed by Japan with 106 million euros .
