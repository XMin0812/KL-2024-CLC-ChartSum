The statistic illustrates the total number of British banknotes of all denominations in circulation in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2004 to 2020 . This includes all banknotes of denominations five , ten , twenty and fifty British pounds . It can be seen that the number of British banknotes in circulation increased overall during the period under observation . The amount of British banknotes rose from almost 1.9 billion notes in 2004 to approximately 3.9 billion notes as of 2020 , an increase of 2 billion notes .
