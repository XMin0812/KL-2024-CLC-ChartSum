In 2018 and 2019 , most Danes were never married ; the number of never married inhabitants was around 2.8 million in the fourth quarter of 2019 . By contrast , among all Danes , the fewest were divorced . In general , the number of divorces between different sexes fluctuated in recent years , peaking in 2014 at about 19 thousand divorces . As of 2018 , the number decreased to nearly 15 thousand . Spouses in Denmark may divorce , if either or both parties do not wish to continue being together . If one does not agree , they can be divorced after a period of six months of separation .
