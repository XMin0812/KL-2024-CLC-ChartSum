The statistic shows the biggest five merger and acquisition ( M & A ) deals in Central and Eastern Europe ( CEE ) in 2019 , by transaction value . The largest deal amounted to over five billion euros ; This was the price paid for the acquisition of a 20 percent stake in Arctic LNG-2 in Russia by the China National Petroleum Corp ( CNPC ) ( China ) . The ranking also includes a deal by Allianz , Macquarie Group and British Columbia Investment Management to acquire a 50.04 percent stake of innology Grid Holding .
