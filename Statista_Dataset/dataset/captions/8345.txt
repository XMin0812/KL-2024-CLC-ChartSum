This statistic shows the total assets of the BP Group from 2009 to 2019 . In 2019 , BP reported some 295.2 billion U.S. dollars of total assets . BP is a globally operating oil and gas company , and the seventh largest company generally worldwide as of 2018 , measured by revenue . It is headquartered in London , UK . Based on revenue , BP was the fourth-largest oil and gas company worldwide in 2019 .
