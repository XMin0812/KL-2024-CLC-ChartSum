This statistic shows the development of SPD party member numbers in Germany from 1990 to 2019 . At the end of 2019 , the SPD party counted 419,340 members , compared to 943,402 in 1990 . Thus the number of party members has more than halved since then . The SPD is one of the oldest and biggest people 's parties in Germany . SPD stands for `` Sozialdemokratische Partei Deutschlands '' or `` Social Democratic Party of Germany '' .
