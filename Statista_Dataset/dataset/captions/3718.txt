There were over 26 thousand marriages in Scotland in 2019 , a decrease of around 1.5 thousand marriages when compared with the previous year . All marriages prior to 2014 are for opposite-sex marriages , same sex marriage was legalized in Scotland on December 16 , 2014 following the Marriage and Civil Partnership Act . During this time period , the year with the most marriages was 1940 , when there were over 53.5 thousand marriages .
