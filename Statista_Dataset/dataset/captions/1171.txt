In 2019 , a year after World Cup matches dominated the list of most viewed television programs in the United Kingdom , sitcom Gavin and Stacey which aired on BBC1 emerged as the most popular TV show . Its viewer count totaled nearly 18 million , outstripping reality shows `` I'm a Celebrity ... '' and `` Strictly Come Dancing '' in second and third places by a wide margin of nearly 5 million individuals .
