As of October 23 , 2020 , 92 percent of Singaporean respondents stated that they were wearing face masks when in public places during the COVID-19 outbreak , up from 24 percent on Feb 21 , 2020 . Singapore has since started to open up after imposing lockdown measures to control the spread of COVID-19 .
