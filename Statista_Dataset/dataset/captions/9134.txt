The results of a survey showed a spike in the involvement of household chores as a result of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) lockdown across India in April 2020 . Other popular activities included watching movies and TV shows online , exercising at home and making video calls to family/friends . A lockdown was enforced on March 25 , 2020 for 21 days , the largest in the world , restricting 1.3 billion people , now extended until May 3 , 2020 .
