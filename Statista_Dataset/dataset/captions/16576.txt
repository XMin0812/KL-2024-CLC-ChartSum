The fintech company Konfío , headquartered in Mexico City and owned by Red Amigo DAL , was one of the enterprises with the highest growth in Latin America between 2015 and 2018 . In that period , Konfío 's revenue grew 185.7 percent per year , based on its compound annual growth rate ( CAGR ) . ComparaGuru.com , another Mexican fintech platform , ranked second with a CAGR of 109 percent .
