The statistic presents the results of the presidential elections held in Panama on May 5 , 2019 . Laurentino 'Nito ' Cortizo was elected the new Panamanian president , having obtained around 33.4 percent of valid votes cast . In April 2019 , voting intention polls had predicted Cortizo would be elected president with between 27.9 and 48.7 percent of votes cast .
