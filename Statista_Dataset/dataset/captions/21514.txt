This statistic depicts the global carbon composite revenue in the sport and leisure market in 2013 and a forecast from 2014 to 2022 . In 2013 , carbon composite products generated 1.47 billion U.S. dollars in this market globally . Carbon fiber consists of tiny fibers that are mostly derived from carbon atoms . The global demand is expected to increase in the next five years alongside with its potential in reinforcing materials such as replacing aluminum in the aerospace sector .
