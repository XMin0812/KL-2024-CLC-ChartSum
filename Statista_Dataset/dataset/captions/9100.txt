The Yamalo-Nenets Autonomous Okrug had the highest number of tests for the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) per 100 thousand population among Russian federal subjects , measuring at approximately 253.5 thousand as of December 16 , 2020 . Overall , the highest amount of COVID-19 tests in Russia as of that date was recorded in the city of Moscow .
