This statistic shows the results of a survey question asking consumers whether they agreed with a set of statements regarding the ethical standards of retail companies in the United Kingdom ( UK ) . The survey , conducted for the Department for Business , Innovation and Skills in June 2014 , aimed to gauge the publics ' attitudes to ethical standards in retail in the UK and the impact this had on their purchase decisions . Of respondents , 38 percent agreed , to some extent , that they try to buy from companies that act in an ethical way , even it means spending more money .
