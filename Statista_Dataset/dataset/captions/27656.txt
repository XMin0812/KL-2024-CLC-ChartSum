It is measured in base points , which reflect the difference between the return rates paid by emerging countries ' government bonds and those offered by U.S. Treasury bills . In July 2020 , Venezuela had the highest average EMBI , amounting to more than 30,700 points . Ecuador followed second , with an index of over 2,800 . In the same period , the Latin American economy with the lowest financial risk and return rates according to the EMBI was Peru , with around 172 base points .
