In 2018 , public debt in France represented 96.8 percent of the country 's GDP . According to the source , public debt in France is supposed to decrease in the upcoming years , while staying at a high level . The French Ministry of Economy has stated that the French public debt will normally reach 91.4 percent in 2022 , compared to 96.8 percent in 2017 . Since 2005 , public debt in France is increasing making the country one of the most in debt of the European Union .
