On March 19 , 2020 , attendance in Russian movie theaters declined by 43 percent compared to the first day of the previous cinema weekend . The reason behind that were the outbreak of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) and consequent measures taken by the country 's authorities . Some cinema sites across the country were closed , while Moscow cinemas allowed no more than 50 viewers per screening . On March 24 , 2020 , all cinemas in the country were set to be closed .
