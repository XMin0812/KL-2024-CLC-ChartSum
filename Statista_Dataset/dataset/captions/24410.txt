At the end of fiscal year 2019 , the IT/ITES/Electronic/Hardware/Semiconductor SEZs amounted to 58 percent of the total share of Special Economic Zones throughout the country . Special Economic Zones ( SEZs ) are regions within the country that have , geographically , more liberal economic laws to promote Foreign Direct Investments from foreign investors as compared to the domestic law of the land . SEZs have their own specific set of laws .
