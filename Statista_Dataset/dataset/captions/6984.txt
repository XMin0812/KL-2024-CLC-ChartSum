In Sweden , taxes for cigarettes , cigars and cigarillos are burdened per unit , whereas the other tobacco products ( smoking and chewing tobacco , snus ) are burdened per kilogram . As of January 1 , 2019 , the per kilogram tax on smoking tobacco amounted to 1,922 Swedish kronor and cigarette tax was 1.58 Swedish kronor per unit .
