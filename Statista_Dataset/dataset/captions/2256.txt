The number of cars sold by Ford in the United Kingdom ( UK ) between January 2017 and December 2020 fluctuated considerably . In December 2020 , sales volume amounted to almost 11,500 units following a difficult year for sales amid the global Covid-19 pandemic . Ford was one of the leading car companies in the UK . The Driver & Vehicle Licensing Agency issues new registration plates in September and March , which usually corresponds with the peak months of Ford sales .
