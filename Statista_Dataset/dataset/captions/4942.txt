China is by far the leading hard coal producer worldwide . In 2018 , the economic powerhouse was responsible for over 3.5 billion metric tons of hard coal produced . In 2019 , China was also the leading global coal consumer , at 82 exajoules . Hard coal refers to anthracite , which has the highest density and least volatile matter among all forms of coals . In the past it was the main fuel for locomotives and today is used primarily for metallurgy . It is differentiated from soft coal , or bituminous coal , which has a lower fixed carbon content and includes greater amounts of sulfur and nitrogen .
