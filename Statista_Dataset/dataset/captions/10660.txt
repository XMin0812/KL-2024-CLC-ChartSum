The statistic depicts the number of car recalls from 1971 to 2010 , by manufacturer . Between 2007 and 2010 , Toyota recalled some 5.7 million cars after some crashes were shown to have been caused by floor mat incursion . Large recent recalls in the United States include the Hyundai Sonata recall in 2012 , where nearly 225,000 vehicles were recalled , as well as the 2013 Chrysler Group recall in the United States , where at least 490,000 U.S. customers were affected .
