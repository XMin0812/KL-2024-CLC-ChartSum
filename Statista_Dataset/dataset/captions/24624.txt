This statistic illustrates the total amount of passenger cargo uplifted by Flybe Ltd in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2008 to 2019 . Flybe Ltd was a low-cost regional airline based in Exeter , Devon . Flybe was sold to Connect Airways in February 2019 . During the period in consideration , Flybe 's amount of excess cargo uplifted presents a trend of decline . The peak was recorded in 2012 , at 1,187 metric tons , before declining to 221 metric tons by 2019 .
