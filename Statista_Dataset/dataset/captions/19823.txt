This statistic shows the median weekly earnings of Black or African American full-time wage and salary workers in the United States from 1979 to 2019 . In 1979 , the median usual weekly earnings of a Black full-time employee in the United States amounted to 199 current U.S. dollars , which was 42 U.S. dollars below the general average . In 2019 , the median usual weekly earnings of an African American working full-time was 735 current U.S. dollars .
