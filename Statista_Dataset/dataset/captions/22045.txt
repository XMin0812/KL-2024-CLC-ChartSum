Oil and gas group OMV Petrom announced a donation of one million euros for the acquisition of the necessary test equipment for the rapid diagnosis of COVID-19 infections in Romania . At the same time , Banca Transilvania , communicated a donation worth 290,000 euros for mechanical ventilators , equipment and materials for state hospitals in Romania .
