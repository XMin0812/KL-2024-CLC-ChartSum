How many games in the World Cup ? The 1998 World Cup Finals in France saw a change to the format that had been used for the previous four tournaments . The format for the finals was initially changed for the 1982 World Cup in Spain in which 24 teams participated . The current format , first implemented in France 1998 , sees 32 teams split into eight groups of four . As a result , the last six editions of the World Cup , including the latest World Cup in Russia in 2018 , featured 64 games .
