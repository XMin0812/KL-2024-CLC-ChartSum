As of 2019 , the average annual wage of Germany was 42.4 thousand Euros per year , a growth of just over 5.8 thousand Euros when compared with 2000 . From 2000 until 2007 , wages rose by less than a thousand euros , with wage growth accelerating mainly in the period after 2010 . The 607 Euro increase recorded between 2010 and 2011 was the largest wage rise seen during this period .
