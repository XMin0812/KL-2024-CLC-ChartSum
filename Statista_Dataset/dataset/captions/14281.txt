This statistic presents the value of investment in Fintech ( financial technology companies ) in selected European regions or countries , throughout all of 2014 . The innovative financial services developed by UK and Irish Fintech companies attracted approximately 623 million U.S. dollars in 2014 . The investments in other European regions grew in the last year : approximately 345 million U.S. dollars was invested in new companies in the Nordic region and 306 million dollars in Dutch companies .
