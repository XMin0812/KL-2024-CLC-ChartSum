Awareness of the novel coronavirus COVID-19 was high in France . This statistic shows the share of survey respondents who had seen , read , or heard about the coronavirus outbreak as of February 9 , 2020 . According to Ipsos , 67 percent of French people stated having heard a great deal about the coronavirus outbreak and 28 percent had heard a fair amount .
