The statistic illustrates the leading financial advisors to merger and acquisition ( M & A ) transactions , by the total aggregate value of deals , in Greater London ( United Kingdom ) in 2019 . It can be seen that Goldman Sachs led the value rankings with roughly 64 billion British pounds in value of supported deals at that time . Morgan Stanley and Jefferies completed the top three , with aggregate deal values of approximately 52 billion British pounds and 33 billion British pounds in 2019 .
