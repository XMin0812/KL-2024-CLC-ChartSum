As of December 15 , in the Netherlands , 531 patients are in ICU due to the coronavirus . To this day , Dutch intensive care units treated 5.9 thousand COVID-19 patients . In early April , at the height of the coronavirus pandemic , 1,300 patients were under intensive care in the Netherlands . From mid-April to August , the number of ICU beds occupied by patients with COVID-19 progressively declined . Since early September , however , the daily number of patients present in ICU increased .
