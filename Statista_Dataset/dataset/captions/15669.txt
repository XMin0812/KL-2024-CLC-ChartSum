Hotels and public catering were the industries with the most significant decline in value added estimated for 2020 in Russia , measuring at nearly 84 percent . The sector of clothing , leather , and shoes manufacturing was expected to lose roughly 53 percent in added value over the course of the year due to the crisis caused by the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) .
