Since 2005 , the gross minimum wage per hour in France has been gradually increasing . In June 2005 , the gross minimum wage per hour in France amounted to 8.03 euros . Fourteen years later the hourly minimum wage increased by two euros , reaching 10.03 euros . In 2017 , France had an average annual wage higher than countries like Spain and Italy , but lower than those in Germany or in the United Kingdom .
