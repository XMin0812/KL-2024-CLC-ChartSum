Globalization translates into important flows of people , merchandise , capital , and information . Public opinion on globalization in Belgium was quite contrasted in 2020 . In August 2020 , roughly eight percent of the respondents in Belgium did not consider globalization to be an opportunity for economic growth at all , and a further 23 percent tended to feel the same way . On the other hand , 13 percent of the respondents considered globalization as an economic opportunity , and approximately 52 percent of respondents shared this sentiment , although to a lesser degree .
