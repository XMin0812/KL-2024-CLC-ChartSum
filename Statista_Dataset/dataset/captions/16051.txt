This statistic presents the domestic tourism expenditure in Norway from 2012 to 2018 , with a forecast for 2028 . Over this period , the expenditure of domestic tourists in Norway has increased , reaching about 123 billion Norwegian kroner in 2017 . This figure is estimated to rise to around 127 billion kroner in 2018 and to continue rising over the next ten years . In Norway , domestic spending is over twice as high as international tourist spending .
