On December 20 , 2020 , 313 people were under a medical ventilator in Belgium due to the coronavirus . From March 20 to December 20 , numerous patients have been placed on these devices in Belgian hospitals . The peak of ventilator use to treat coronavirus patients was reached in early April when roughly 1,000 people were on a ventilator . As the pandemic grew in the country , so did the number of patients under ventilators .
