As of 2019 , the highest number of fatalities in road traffic accidents in Finland was reported in the age group of 15 to 24 years , with 41 casualties . There were 32 road casualties reported in the age group of 55 to 64 years , followed by 31 casualties in the age group of 35 to 44 years , as well as in the age group of 75 years and older .
