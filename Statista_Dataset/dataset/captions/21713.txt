This statistic shows the number of cars sold by Nissan in Turkey between 2012 and 2019 . In the period of consideration , Turkish sales of Nissan cars presented a trend of decline with some oscillation . Nissan sales peaked at 32 thousand units in 2017 . In 2019 , Nissan sold approximately 12 thousand cars , a decrease of roughly 51 percent in comparison with the previous year .
