This statistic shows the development of concrete block production in Great Britain ( GB ) from 2007 to 2017 , in thousand square meters . Overall , production has fluctuated significantly , dropping off sharply from 2007 to 2009 . The production rate has been recovering slowly , but has not approached the levels seen in 2007 . In 2018 , approximately 71.1 million square meters of concrete blocks were produced .
