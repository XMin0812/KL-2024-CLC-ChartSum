Infant mortality has been falling in Bangladesh in the past decade , from 40.5 deaths per 1,000 live births in 2009 to 25.6 in 2019 . This figure helps to assess the overall healthcare system 's efficacy , because childbirth and infant care require more direct patient care than any other period of life . Similarly , measures taken to combat infant mortality often have spillover effects , improving the entire healthcare system .
