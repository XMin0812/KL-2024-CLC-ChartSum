This statistic shows the number of cars sold by Nissan in the Republic of Ireland between 2009 and 2019 . In the period of consideration , Irish sales of Nissan cars rose to a peak of approximately 11.9 thousand units sold in 2016 . In 2019 , there were approximately 8.2 thousand units of Nissan cars sold in Ireland , a decrease of roughly 31 percent compared to the peak year .
