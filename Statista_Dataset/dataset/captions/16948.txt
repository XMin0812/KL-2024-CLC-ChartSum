The impact of the crisis caused by the coronavirus pandemic is still not visible in the construction industry in Poland . Of all types of buildings , only commercial buildings , e.g. , office buildings , have seen their production fall by 20 percent year-on-year . For engineering structures , the least affected were those financed from EU funds . Local government investments , such as sports and leisure facilities , achieved a significant drop in production compared to March 2018 .
