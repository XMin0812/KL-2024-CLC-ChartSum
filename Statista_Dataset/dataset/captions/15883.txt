This statistic shows BayWa AG 's revenue from 2007 to 2019 . In 2007 , BayWa generated a total revenue of around 7.2 billion euros . That figure stood at approximately 17.06 billion euros in 2019 . BayWa AG is a retail and agribusiness company , based in Munich , Germany . The company operates in the agriculture , building materials and energy sectors .
