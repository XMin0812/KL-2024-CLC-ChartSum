With over 60 percent of the market share in 2020 , Engie Electrabel reached the highest market share of electricity supply in the Brussels-Capital Region . The Belgian daughter of the French multinational Engie was , therefore , leading the market in this region of Belgium in 2020 . Lampiris was the second-largest supplier , holding a market share of just under 19 percent . In Belgium , the major electricity providers were Engie Electrabel , Lampiris , and EDF-Luminus .
