As of January 1 , 2020 , with more than 41 thousand people living in the country , most immigrants came from Poland . The second and third most popular origins were Syria and Turkey , amounting to almost 36 thousand and 33 thousand people , respectively . Only one Nordic country was represented among the 10 most frequent nationalities of immigrants in Denmark , Norway . Indeed , close to 16 thousand Norwegians lived in Denmark in 2020 .
