In 2018 , the rural population in France reached 19.5 percent of the total . In 2018 , Europe was ranked third continent worldwide in terms of degree of urbanization . 80 percent of the European population was living in cities in 2018 , but this figure is expected to decrease by 2050 . In France , studies have shown that most of the population lives in urban areas , but many French citizens seemed to aspire to live in the countryside .
