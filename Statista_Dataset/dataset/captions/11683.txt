This statistic illustrates the forecasted consumption volume of biodiesel fuel in OECD countries from 2015 to 2027 , in million liters . In the period of consideration , biodiesel consumption volume is expected to oscillate at an average of 21.9 billion liters , annually . The forecasted year with the highest consumption volume is 2020 , when it is expected that 22.7 billion liters of biodiesel would be consumed in OECD member states .
