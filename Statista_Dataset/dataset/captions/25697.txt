This statistic shows the number of specialized stores for the retail sale of textiles in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2008 to 2018 . Between 2008 and 2012 , the number of textile retail stores in the UK saw a considerable decrease , falling from 2,007 stores to 1,397 stores . Since then , however , numbers have increased year-over-year , with 2018 seeing 1,942 stores specialized in selling textiles in the UK .
