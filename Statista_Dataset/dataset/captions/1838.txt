Toyota 's revenue in the 2020 fiscal year varied significantly across Toyota 's sales regions . Japan generated the largest amount of revenue for the Toyota Motor Corporation in 2020 with 16 trillion Japanese yen ( or about 153 billion U.S. dollars ) . North America , the second largest revenue generator for Toyota , brought in 10.6 trillion Japanese yen during that year ( or about 98.7 billion U.S. dollars ) .
