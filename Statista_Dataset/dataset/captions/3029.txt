This statistic shows the mid-year population estimate for different regions of Scotland in 2019 . There were around 633 thousand people estimated to be living in Glasgow , with a further 524 thousand people living in the Scottish capital , Edinburgh . The region of Fife is also heavily populated with approximately 373 thousand people living there . The least populated areas are the islands of Scotland such as Orkney , estimated to have only 22,000 people there .
