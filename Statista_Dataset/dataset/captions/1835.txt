Merck & Co. is a global health care and pharmaceutical company that is headquartered in Kenilworth , New Jersey in the U.S . The company operates globally . In 2019 , the company had a total of around 71 thousand full-time employees . The number of employees at the company has been on the decline since 2009 when the company experienced their largest number of employees . At that time Merck & Co. had around 100 thousand employees globally .
