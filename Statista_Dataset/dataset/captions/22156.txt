This statistic shows the annual net revenue generated by Tivit Terceirização de Processos Serviços e Tecnologia S.A. in Brazil from 2013 to 2017 . In 2017 , the company reported a net revenue of 1.46 billion Brazilian reals , up from 1.41 billion reals a year earlier . Tivit was among the leading IT and telecommunications companies in Brazil in 2017 .
