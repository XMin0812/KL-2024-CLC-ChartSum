Land-based sports betting has been legal in the U.S. state of Indiana since September 2019 . This means that it is possible to play college sports betting , MLB sports betting , and NBA sports betting in the state . In September 2020 , Indiana generated a total of 14.28 million U.S. dollars in revenue from sports betting , up from the previous month 's total of 9.83 million U.S. dollars .
