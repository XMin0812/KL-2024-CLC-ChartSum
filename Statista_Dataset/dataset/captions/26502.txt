As of March 2020 , 86 percent of Norwegians interviewed stated that they were avoiding cultural and sports events due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outrbreak . 84 percent of people were avoiding meetings , courses , and conferences . The first case of COVID-19 in Norway was confirmed on February 26 , 2020 . For further information about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , please visit our dedicated Fact and Figures page .
