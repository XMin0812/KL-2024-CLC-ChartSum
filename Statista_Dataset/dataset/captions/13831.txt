This statistic displays the yearly number of prepaid SIM cards of the mobile operator Wind Tre in Italy between 2015 and 2017 . Over this period , the number of of Wind Tre SIM cards decreased , passing from 31.2 million in 2015 to 29.5 million in 2017 . In 2018 , Wind Tre accounted for a third of the prepaid SIM cards in the country .
