Germany was the greatest employer within the European steel industry . A total of 86,000 people were employed in the German steel sector in 2019 , making it nearly three times the size of its nearest competitor – Italy . That same year , the European Union ( EU ) made up only 8.5 percent of global crude steel production , whereas China – as the world 's largest producer – is responsible for over half of steel output alone . Cheap pricing and heavily increased manufacturing levels have made the industry particularly competitive .
