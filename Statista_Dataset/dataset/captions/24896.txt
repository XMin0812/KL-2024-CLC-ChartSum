Natural gas consumption by the textile and leather industry in the United Kingdom presented a trend of decline , despite some fluctuation over the years . From 2012 to 2014 , figures remained between 4.48 and 4.76 terawatt hours , falling to 2.94 terawatt hours in 2015 . By 2019 , the British textile industry consumed 2,834 gigawatt hours worth of natural gas .
