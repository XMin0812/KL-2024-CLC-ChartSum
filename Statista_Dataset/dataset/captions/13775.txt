Based on the results of a survey , 83 percent of Indian respondents were in favor of ramping up the production and export of face masks , drugs , facial tissues and other useful products to help address the novel coronavirus COVID-19 pandemic . At the same time , 13 percent of the respondents were in favor of maintaining production of these products but withholding the country from exporting them , focusing on domestic demand . As of February 2020 , India had three confirmed cases of people infected with COVID-19 .
