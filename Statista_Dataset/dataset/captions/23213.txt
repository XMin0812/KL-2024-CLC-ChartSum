This statistic outlines the revenue of rare earth producer Molycorp from 2008 to 2014 . In 2009 , the corporation reported some 7.1 million U.S. dollars of revenue . Molycorp Minerals Inc. was headquartered in Greenwood Village , Colorado . In June 2015 , Molycorp filed for bankruptcy following a significant drop in the price of rare earth minerals , with a plan to restructure its debt .
