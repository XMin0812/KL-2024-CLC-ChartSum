The real estate prices in Sweden increased with over 50 % from 2008 to 2018 . As of 2019 , the price index reached 388 , with 1990 as base year . In comparison , the corresponding figure for 2009 was 246 . How many newly constructed buildings had been built in Sweden over the years ? The number of completed multi-dwelling buildings increased significantly from 2010 to 2019 , reaching over 45 thousand in 2019 . In contrast , the number of one- or two-dwelling buildings stayed relatively stable over the past decade and amounted at over 10 thousand completed buildings in 2018 .
