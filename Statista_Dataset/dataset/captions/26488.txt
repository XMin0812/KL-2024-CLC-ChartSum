The companies depicted in the graph were chosen in a study featuring Mexico 's 25 best companies to work for according to LinkedIn users . The anonymous actions of LinkedIn users in Mexico were analyzed based on four main pillars : interest in the company , interaction with the company 's employees , demand for jobs and employee retention . Among the best employers included in the ranking , FEMSA was one of the companies with the highest number of employees , amounting to 220,000 in 2019 .
