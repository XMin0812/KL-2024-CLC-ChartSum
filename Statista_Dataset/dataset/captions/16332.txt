The cost per thousand impressions of Facebook and Instagram ads seemed to be affected by the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , too . In Italy , the CPM ( cost-per-mille ) decreased from 1.39 euros as of mid-February 2020 to 0.47 euros as of March 21 , 2020 . The value increased again to 1.08 euros by April 11 , 2020 . The drop in spending can be explained with the lower capacity to invest in social media advertising . Unsurprisingly , about 44 percent of Italian professionals estimated a decrease in ad investments of more than 30 percent over the first half of 2020 , a survey showed .
