According to the latest reports , the coastal Region of Valencia was the Spanish autonomous community that featured the highest consumption of tobacco , with a quarter of its population reported to be daily smokers in 2019 . Most of the Spanish regions , however , registered a prevalence of tobacco use of over 20 percent with the exception of the communities of Melilla , Madrid and Galicia . Andalusia ranked , on the other hand , as the Spanish autonomous community with the highest number of smokers , totting up almost 1.6 million daily smokers in 2018 .
