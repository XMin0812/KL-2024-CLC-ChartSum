As a response to the COVID-19 outbreak , the average number of monthly orders made by grocery delivery and pickup customers in the United States increased by 19 percent to 1.2 orders in March 2020 , up from 1 order in August 2019 . Usage grew by another 58.33 percent from March to June before declining nearly 16 percent in August .
