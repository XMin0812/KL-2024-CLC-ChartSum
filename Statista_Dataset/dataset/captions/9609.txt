This chart represents the annual olive oil exports from Italy to Extra-EU countries in thousand tons in the period from 2005 to 2020 . According to this statistic , olive oil exports reached two peaks in the crop years 2011/2012 and 2013/2014 , when Italian producers sold above 233 thousand tons olive oil outside the European Union . Projections suggest that in 2019/2020 exports will amount to 209.1 thousand tons .
