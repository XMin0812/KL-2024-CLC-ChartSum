This statistic shows the distribution of daily time spent with media in Finland in 2018 , by type of media . Out of the nearly 8 hours spent daily on following media , 30 percent was spent on watching linear , online , and paid television . The second most used media was online and broadcast radio ( 18 percent ) , followed by social media ( 12 percent ) . Print and digital magazines accounted for five percent of Finns ' media usage .
