This statistic illustrates the findings of a survey on tablet PC usage to go online in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in 2019 , broken down by age group . In 2018 , it was found that the share of responding individuals who had reported using tablet devices to access the internet ranged from 50 to 68 percent . Among surveyed persons aged between 25 and 34 years , 51 percent used such devices to browse the internet . Furthermore , according to another study in 2016 , the highest tablet ownership share was among those aged 35 to 44 and 25 to 34 years , with 70 and 67 percent , respectively .
