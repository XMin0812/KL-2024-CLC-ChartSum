This statistic shows the distance traveled by passengers on the London Underground in the United Kingdom ( UK ) between 1950 and 2020 . From 1950 to 1980 there was a general trend in decreasing usage , but since then the distance traveled by passengers on the Underground has been rapidly growing . In 2018 , the total passenger journeys reached its peak and stood at 12.6 billion kilometers travelled . By 2020 , the distance traveled by passengers on the London Underground declined to 11.8 billion kilometers .
