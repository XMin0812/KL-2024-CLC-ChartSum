As of October 26 , 2020 , 40 percent of Malaysian respondents stated that they had been avoiding going to work during the COVID-19 outbreak . Malaysia is currently going through a second wave of COVID-19 infections . To stop the spread of the virus , the government has introduced a partial lockdown until December 6 .
