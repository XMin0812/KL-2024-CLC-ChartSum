During the winter months , most UK residents keep warm using gas central heating . In a 2019 survey conducted by the UK Department for Business Energy & Industrial Strategy ( BEIS ) , it found that 85 percent of respondents used this method of heating . The next most common heating source was electric , with a combined share of five percent . Since 2014 , the coldest month on record was February 2018 , when the average temperature dropped to 2.4 degrees Celsius .
