This statistic shows the annual worldwide revenue of Sabre Corporation from 2012 to 2019 . In 2019 , the corporation generated 3.97 billion U.S. dollars in revenue , up from 3.87 billion U.S. dollars the previous year . In 2019 , the revenue of Amadeus , one of Sabre 's largest competitors , reached 6.25 billion U.S. dollars .
