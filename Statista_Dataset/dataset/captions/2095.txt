The statistic shows gross domestic product ( GDP ) per capita in Malaysia from 1985 to 2019 , with projections up until 2025 . GDP is the total value of all goods and services produced in a country in a year . It is considered to be a very important indicator of the economic strength of a country and a positive change is an indicator of economic growth . In 2019 , the GDP per capita in Malaysia amounted to around 11,193.02 U.S. dollars .
