The movie 'Marriage Story ' led the way when it came nominations per motion picture at the 77th Golden Globe Awards with a total of six nominations , followed by 'The Irishman ' and 'Once Upon a Time ... in Hollywood ' with five nominations each . American comedy-drama movie 'Marriage Story ' was well-received by critics and won a number of awards after its release , including three Gotham Independent Film Awards and one from the American Film Institute .
