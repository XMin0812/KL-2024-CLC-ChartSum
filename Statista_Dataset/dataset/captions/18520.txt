Recycling rates of LA collected waste in England increased in the period of consideration , starting at 12.3 percent in 2000/01 and more than doubling by 2005/06 . The recycling rate dropped for the first time in 2015/16 and has largely stagnated in the following years , with 43 percent of LA collected waste recycled in 2018/19 . Recycling rates tended to be slightly higher for household waste .
