The statistic shows gross domestic product ( GDP ) per capita in Zimbabwe between 1994 and 2024 . All figures have been estimated . GDP is the total value of all goods and services produced in a country in a year . It is considered to be a very important indicator of the economic strength of a country and a positive change is an indicator of economic growth . In 2018 , the estimated GDP per capita in Zimbabwe amounted to around 1,434.06 U.S. dollars .
