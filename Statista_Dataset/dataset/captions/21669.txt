More than 300 medical units in Romania had patients who tested positive for the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) . Out of the total number of coronavirus infections in Romania , 1.9 percent were represented by the entire personnel working in the medical sector . By January 11 , 2021 , the number of infected employees working in the Romanian healthcare system reached 13,031 .
