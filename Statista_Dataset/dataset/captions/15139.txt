This timeline depicts GAFO sales in the United States from 1992 to 2018 . In 2018 , U.S. GAFO sales amounted to about 1.3 trillion U.S. dollars . GAFO represents sales at stores that sell merchandise normally sold in department stores . GAFO includes the following kinds of retail businesses : general merchandise stores ; clothing and clothing accessories stores ; furniture and home furnishings stores ; electronics and appliance stores ; sporting goods , hobby , book , and music stores ; and office supplies , stationery , and gift stores .
