The average price per kilogram of fresh oysters has fluctuated between 2009 and 2019 : it went from 5.6 euros to 7.1 euros during that time . From 2009 to 2014 , the average selling price steadily increased , reaching its peak in 2014 at eight euros per kilograms . After that year , the average price of fresh oysters remained between 7.1 and 7.4 euros .
