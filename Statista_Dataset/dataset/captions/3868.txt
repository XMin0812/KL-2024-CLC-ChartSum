The COVID-19 pandemic that spread across the world at the beginning of 2020 was not only a big threat to public health , but also to the entire sports industry . Many professional and amateur leagues and events were canceled and the public was advised to not spend time in large groups or in public areas . During an April 2020 survey in the United States , 68 percent of respondents stated that they were much less likely to go to the gym based on what they knew about the coronavirus .
