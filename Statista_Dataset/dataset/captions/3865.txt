Overwatch is a team-based online shooter video game published by Blizzard Entertainment . The player can choose between 32 varied heroes , all of whom have different skills , and each character fulfills a specific role . The game was released in May 2016 on Xbox One , Windows and PlayStation 4 . Overwatch events on the video streaming service Twitch were watched by an average of 16.79 thousand viewers in November 2020 .
