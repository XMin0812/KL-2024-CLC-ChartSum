This statistic shows the price of tickets for a single journey on the London Underground in zones 1 - 4 paying by cash between 2000 and 2015 . The price for a single ticket remained at four British pounds between 2007 and 2010 , but increased to five British pounds in 2011 and increased every year since . In 2016 , the price of a single ticket on the London Underground was 5.90 British pounds . Single-ticket prices remained at 5.90 British pounds in 2019 .
