As of November , 2020 , over 30 thousand infections were confirmed , including around 7.24 thousand in Seoul and 7.2 thousand in Daegu . Seoul and the metropolitan areas were hit hard by a few group infections during the second wave in August . Currently , Korea is facing the third wave with 200 to 300 daily confirmed cases mainly in Seoul and its neighboring areas .
