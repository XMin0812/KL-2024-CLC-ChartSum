The business confidence index provides information on the development in the production , sales , orders , and stocks in the main economic sectors . The data are based on the results of surveys conducted among companies . An index above 100 points generally indicates a positive trend , whereas an index under 100 represents a negative development . In March 2020. the business confidence in Nigeria reached the lowest figure over the period of consideration ( 6.6 points ) , decreasing by 20 points compared to February 2020 .
