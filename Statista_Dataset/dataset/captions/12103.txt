This statistic shows the annual percentage change in the Gross Domestic Product of the United States from 2010 to 2020 , by quarter . The Gross Domestic Product refers to the market value of all final goods and services produced in a country within a given period . In the third quarter of 2020 , the GDP of the United States was 2.8 percent lower compared to the third quarter of 2019 . The GDP of the United States amounted to 21.43 trillion U.S. dollars in 2019 .
