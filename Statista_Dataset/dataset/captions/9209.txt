This statistic shows the share of gem and near-gem quality diamonds in major diamond producing countries for the period from 2009 to 2018 . For this period , it is expected that 76 percent of all the diamonds from Botswana will be of gem or near-gem quality . The diamond industry , unlike other precious metals and natural resources , relies almost exclusively on consumer demand for diamond jewelry . The diamond industry is expected to flourish despite weak global economic growth .
