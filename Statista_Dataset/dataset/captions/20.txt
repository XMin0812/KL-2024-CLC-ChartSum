As of December 14 , 2020 , there have been 479,789 deaths in Europe overall due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) since the first recorded European death in France on February 15 . December 2 was the virus ' deadliest day so far in Europe with 6,195 deaths . Italy has the highest number of deaths in Europe at 64,520 , as of December 14 . The worldwide number of confirmed cases of coronavirus was over 72 million as of December 14 , 2020 .
