Around 70 percent of people in the United States who had COVID-19 from January 22 to May 30 , 2020 experienced fever , coughing , or shortness of breath . This statistic shows the percentage of people in the U.S. with COVID-19 from January 22 to May 30 , 2020 who experienced select symptoms .
