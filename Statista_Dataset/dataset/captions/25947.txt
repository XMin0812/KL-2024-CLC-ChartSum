This statistic shows the share of maritime financial services for international financial markets supplied by the United Kingdom ( UK ) , by type of service as of 2014 . According to the source , the UK is the leading centre in the supply of a broad range of professional and business services to the global maritime community . In 2014 , the UK market was responsible for 62 percent of maritime insurance protection and indemnity insurance ( P & I Clubs ) and for 10 percent of ship finance .
