This statistic shows the median weekly earnings of Hispanic full-time wage and salary workers in the United States from 1979 to 2019 . In 1979 , the median usual weekly earnings of a Latino full-time employee in the United States amounted to 194 current U.S. dollars , which was 47 U.S. dollars beneath the general average . In 2019 , this figure was 706 current U.S. dollars .
