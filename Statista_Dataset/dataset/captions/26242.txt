This statistic illustrates the housing construction starts carried out by housing associations in England from the third quarter 2014 to fourth quarter 2019 . It can be seen that the number of housing starts by housing associations fluctuated overall during the period under observation , reaching just under six thousand as of the fourth quarter of 2019 . The largest number of housing starts by housing associations during this period was found in the first quarter of 2017 , when a total of over 7.2 thousand construction starts were recorded .
