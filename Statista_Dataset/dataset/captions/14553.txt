In recent years , the value of alcoholic beverages imported into Sweden has been growing strongly . In 2019 , it amounted to approximately 11.2 billion Swedish kronor . Among this , the value of imported wine into the country was nearly 7.5 billion Swedish kronor . The climate in Sweden makes wine cultivation rather difficult . There are only a few vineyards producing small quantities of wine . That 's why Sweden is open for foreign wines and imports a high value of it .
