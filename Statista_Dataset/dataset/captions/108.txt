The statistic shows the inflation rate in the United Kingdom from 1985 to 2019 , with projections up until 2025 . The inflation rate is calculated using the price increase of a defined product basket . This product basket contains products and services , on which the average consumer spends money throughout the year . They include expenses for groceries , clothes , rent , power , telecommunications , recreational activities and raw materials ( e.g . gas , oil ) , as well as federal fees and taxes . In 2019 , the average inflation rate in the United Kingdom was at about 1.79 percent compared to the previous year . For comparison , inflation in India amounted to 4.76 percent that same year .
