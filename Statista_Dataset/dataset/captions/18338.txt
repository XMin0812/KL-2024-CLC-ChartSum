This chart shows the average value of unworn clothing that British men and women accumulate over a lifetime as of 2019 . The survey revealed that in total UK consumers will have 32,951 British pounds worth of garments lying unworn in their wardrobes . While women accounted for the larger portion of the total amount with an average of 22,140 British pounds worth of unworn cloths , men were attributed to owning unworn clothing worth 11,811 pounds .
