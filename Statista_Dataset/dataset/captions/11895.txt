With a market cap of over 28.6 billion U.S. dollars , Peloton ranks first among the leading vertical e-commerce companies worldwide . According to September 2020 data compiled by GP Bullhound , the US-based company ranks ahead of competitors Wayfair , Ocado , and HelloFresh . Second-ranked Wayfair 's market cap amounted to 27.7 billion U.S. dollars .
