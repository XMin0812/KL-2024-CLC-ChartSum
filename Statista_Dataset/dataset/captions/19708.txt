As of September 2020 , digital companies in the e-commerce vertical had the lowest enterprise-value-to-revenue multiple . According to GP Bullhound , companies in the segment had an average EV/R of 2.4 . Companies in the classifieds vertical segment had an average EV/R of 11.8 . Overall , companies in the e-commerce segment might be considered undervalued and therefore may be of interest to potential acquirers .
