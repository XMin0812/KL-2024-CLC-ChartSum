In 2019 , the most valuable corporate brand in Europe was valued at approximately 76 billion euros . LVMH ( Moët Hennessy – Louis Vuitton SE ) , is a French multinational luxury goods group , which was formed by the merger between the luxury fashion brand Louis Vuitton and the high-end drinks brands Moët and Hennessy in 1987 . The group boasts numerous exclusive brands across industries including wine and spirits , fashion , jewelry , cosmetics and perfumes to name a few .
