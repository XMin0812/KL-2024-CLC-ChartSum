This statistic shows the leading 20 shopfitters in the United Kingdom ( UK ) ranked by sales in the companies ' latest financial year , as of November 2014 . For the majority of shopfitters , the latest financial year ended between the end of March and December 2013 , with the exception of ISG , where the financial year ended June 30 , 2014 . ISG had the highest recorded sales at 283 million British pounds , followed by Wates Retail who generated 110 million GBP in sales .
