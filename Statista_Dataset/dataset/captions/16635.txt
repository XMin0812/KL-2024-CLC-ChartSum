This statistic shows the number of foreigners in Germany according to the Central Register of Foreign Nationals in 2019 , by region of origin . That year , there were approximately 4.88 million nationals from EU states living in Germany . Foreigners are those who are not German based on Article 116 Paragraph 1 of the German constitution . These include stateless persons and those with unclear citizenship as well as the population group with a migration background . Individuals with a migration background can either have immigrated into Germany or been born in the country to at least one parent who was born a foreigner .
