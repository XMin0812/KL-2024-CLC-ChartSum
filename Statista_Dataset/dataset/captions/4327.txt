Marcus & Martinus ( @ marcusandmartinus ) had the most followed TikTok account in Norway in 2019 . The two young boys had approximately four million followers , whereas Emilie Lien ranked second in the country , with two million followers . The iOS and Android social media app is focused on short lip-sync and comedy video sharing and grew largely popular over the past few years . What is more , TikTok ranked as the most popular social networking app in the United States , by average session duration , as of June 2019 . Pinterest and Facebook ranked respectively second and third in the list .
