This statistic shows the total number of registered Yamaha YZF R1 model motorcycles in Great Britain from 2000 and 2019 . The figures in the earlier years experienced exponential growth , with the highest number of motorcycles registered amounting to 17,395 in 2013 . The figures began to decline in the succeeding years , with a total of 7,827 units being registered in 2019 , which was also the lowest number of motorcycles recorded in a year in this period .
