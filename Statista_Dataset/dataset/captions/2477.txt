Sears Holdings had global retail sales amounting to about 6.63 billion U.S. dollars in 2019 . The company 's revenues have steadily declined since 2006 , when the company had a revenue of over 53 billion U.S. dollars . Sears Holdings is an American retailer , which operates through two main business segments , Kmart and Sears . Even though the company 's revenues have declined substantially in recent years , Sears remains one of the leading retailers in the United States .
