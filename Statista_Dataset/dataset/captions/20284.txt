This statistic shows the monthly trend in the volume of sales ( quantity bought ) in household goods stores in Great Britain from January 2016 to June 2020 . The figures are seasonally adjusted estimates , measured using the Retail Sales Index ( RSI ) and published in index form with a reference year of 2016 equal to 100 . In June 2020 , sales in household goods stores were at an index level of 104.4 .
