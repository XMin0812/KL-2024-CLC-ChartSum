On December 19 , 2020 , the real-time coronavirus reproductive rate ( Rt ) stood at a value of 1.02 in Belgium . During the coronavirus pandemic , the transmission rate reached a value of 2.62 in March and then decreased gradually . Although the reproductive rate increased again in September , the rate decreased back below a value of one in November . The Rt rate represents the average number of people who become infected by an infectious person with COVID-19 .
