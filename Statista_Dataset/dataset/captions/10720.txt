This statistic shows the number of cars sold by Peugeot in France between 2009 and 2018 . French sales of Peugeot cars declined from 400.7 thousand units sold in 2010 to approximately 290 thousand units in 2013 , before recovering slightly in 2014 and increasing since . In 2016 , 335,881 Peugeot cars were sold in France .
