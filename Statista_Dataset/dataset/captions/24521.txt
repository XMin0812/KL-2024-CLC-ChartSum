This statistic shows the total amount of statutory profits and losses of Balfour Beatty Group from 2011 to 2018 , in million British pounds . During the eight years recorded here , the Group 's profit has oscillated considerably , ranging from a loss of 206 million British pounds to a profit of 186 million British pounds . In 2018 , the group generated a statutory profit of 135 million British pounds , a decrease of nearly 20 percent compared to the previous year .
