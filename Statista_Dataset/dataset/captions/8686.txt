With a market value of nearly 141 million U.S. dollars , Neymar was the most valuable player in Brazil 's national soccer team as of November 2020 . Following the famous left-winger was Roberto Firmino , who plays center forward , worth 79.2 million U.S. dollars . The center-back Marquinhos completes the Seleção 's top three that year , with a market value of 77 million U.S. dollars . In 2018 , Neymar also topped the ranking of Brazil 's most valuable soccer players at the FIFA World Cup .
