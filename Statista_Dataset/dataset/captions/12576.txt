The United States manufacturing sector output increased by 54.8 percent in the third quarter of 2020 . The data are seasonally adjusted at annual rates . Manufacturing sector output is a chain-type , current-weighted index constructed after excluding from the gross domestic product ( GDP ) the following outputs : general government , nonprofit institutions , and private households ( including owner-occupied housing ) . Corresponding exclusions also are made in labor inputs . Business output , including manufacturing sector accounted for about 78 percent of the value of the GDP in 2019 .
