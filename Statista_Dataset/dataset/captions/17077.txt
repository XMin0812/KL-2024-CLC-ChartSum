In 2016 , 1,028 people died as a result of intentional homicide in Haiti . A year earlier , the number of victims had reached 1,071 , the highest number at least since 2007 . The Caribbean country continues to suffer from crime and violence . The number of murders registered in 2016 has doubled in comparison to 2007 , year which showed the lowest quantity of homicide victims in the indicated time period , amounting to 486 .
