As of 2020 , the wheat production in Egypt amounted to approximately 8.9 million metric tons , which represented and increase of 1.48 percent from the preceding year . During the last decade , the Egyptian wheat production ranged between 7.2 and 8.9 million metric tons in 2010 and 2020 respectively . Throughout the period observed , an overall positive can be noticed with a comprehensive increase of around 23.6 percent during the 10 year period . Wheat is one of the most important crops for Egypt , and this can be recognized through Egypt being the one of the largest wheat importers in the world .
