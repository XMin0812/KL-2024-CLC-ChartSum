Nubank , a neobank headquartered in Brazil , was the most valued Latin American unicorn in November 2020 , with a market capitalization value of around ten billion U.S. dollars . A unicorn company is defined as a privately held company worth one billion U.S. dollars or more . Seven out of the 11 most valuable Latin American unicorn companies listed by the source were based in Brazil . The Colombian on-demand delivery platform , Rappi , made it second in the ranking , with a market value of 3.5 billion U.S. dollars .
