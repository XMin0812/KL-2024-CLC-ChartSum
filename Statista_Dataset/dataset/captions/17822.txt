The highest number of confirmed coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) cases in Iceland as of June 11 , 2020 was in the Capital Region , reaching a total of 1,323 cases . Southern Region followed , with 182 confirmed cases . The first case of the coronavirus in Iceland was confirmed on February 28 , 2020 . The number of cases has since risen to a total of 1,824 .
