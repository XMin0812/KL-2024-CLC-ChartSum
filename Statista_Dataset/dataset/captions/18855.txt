This statistic displays the average per capita expenditure on food and beverages in Belgium in 2018 , by type ( in euros ) . In 2018 , on average Belgians spent approximately 2,460 euros per person on food and beverages . Approximately 2,000 euros was spent on food , and another 455 euros was spent on beverages . On average , Belgians spent roughly 190 euros per capita on fruit and approximately 240 euros on vegetables .
