This statistic shows the most significant merger and acquisition ( M & A ) transactions in the food and food service industry in the Republic of Ireland throughout 2014 , ranked by deal value and listing the target company . The highest priced transaction was Cloverhill Pastry-Vend Corp. , bought by Aryzta AG at the price of 485 million euros . It was followed by Old Bushmills Distillery Co , bought by Grupo Cuervo SA de CV from Mexico for 408 million euros .
