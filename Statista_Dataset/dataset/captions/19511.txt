Takenaka Corporation generated a total revenue of approximately 1.35 trillion Japanese yen in 2019 . As a breakdown by region shows , an overwhelming proportion of the company 's revenue can be attributed to the Japanese market . Takenaka Corporation , which belongs to one of Japan 's largest construction companies , can be credited for building Japanese landmarks such as the Tokyo Tower . In 2019 , the company celebrated the 120th anniversary of its founding .
