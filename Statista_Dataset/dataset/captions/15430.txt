This statistic shows the share of guests returning to hotels worldwide as of July 2014 . During the survey , 38 percent of respondents said that up to 25 percent of their guests returned for another stay at their establishment . An amenity that was considered by the majority of guests to be a must have was air conditioning in 2015 . In the same year , around 53 percent of hotel guests stated that they would complain if the hotel had unpleasant carpet odors .
