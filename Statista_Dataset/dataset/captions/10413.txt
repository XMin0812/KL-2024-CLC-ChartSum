This statistic shows the highest-grossing directors of all time as of July 2019 , ranked by their domestic box office results . Topping the list is Steven Spielberg , whose films have generated total gross revenue of over 4.67 billion U.S. dollars in the domestic box office . His biggest hit was the 1982 film E.T. , which made over 435 million U.S. dollars domestically and almost 793 million U.S. dollars worldwide . Newcomers to the top 20 directors in July 2019 were brothers Anthony Russo and Joe Russo , who moved from 19th and 20th in the ranking in March 2019 to third and fourth place after the success of 'Avengers : Endgame ' _ .
