In fiscal year 2019 , Fast Retailing rented a total sales floor area of approximately 2.88 million m² , up from about 2.67 million m² in the previous fiscal year . That year , the Japanese holding company operated almost 3,600 stores worldwide , with the majority being located overseas . Fast Retailing is a Japanese clothing and accessories retailer headquartered in Yamaguchi prefecture . The company is globally best known through it 's subsidiary and fashion brand Uniqlo .
