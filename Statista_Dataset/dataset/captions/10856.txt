Consumer demand for face masks has dramatically increased globally due to the COVID-19 coronavirus , to the extent that many stores have run out of them . As one of the world 's largest manufacturers of N95 respiratory masks and other protective products , it is estimated that 3M Company could have a sales boost of up more than one billion U.S. dollars in 2020 because of coronavirus-related demand . Comparatively , during the the 'swine flu ' H1N1 virus pandemic in 2009 - 2010 , 3M 's sales were boosted by 350 million U.S. dollars . President Trump 's administration reportedly plans to order millions of face masks from 3M .
