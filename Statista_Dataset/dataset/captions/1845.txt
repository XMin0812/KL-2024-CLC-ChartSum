It was estimated that around 30 percent of those aged 80 years and older who had COVID-19 in the United States from January 22 to May 30 , 2020 died from the disease . Deaths due to COVID-19 are much higher among those with underlying health conditions such as cardiovascular disease , chronic lung disease , or diabetes . This statistic shows the percentage of people in the U.S. who had COVID-19 from January 22 to May 30 , 2020 who died , by age .
