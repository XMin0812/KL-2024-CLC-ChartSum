In 2020 , Jamaica scored 0.77 in the gender gap index in the area of economic participation and opportunity , which means that women are 23 percent less likely to have equal economic participation and opportunities than men . That year , Jamaica scored 0.62 in estimated earned income , which shows a gender pay gap of approximately 38 percent ( on average , women 's income was estimated to be around 38 percent lower than men 's ) .
