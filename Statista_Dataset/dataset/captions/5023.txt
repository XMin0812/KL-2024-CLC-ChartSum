Between 2008 and 2019 , the immigration rate in Luxembourg increased . Indeed , in 2008 , the immigration rate was at 36.34 percent for 1,000 inhabitants , by 2019 it was at 43.01 percent . The lowest rate was reached in 2009 , when the immigration was at 31.64 percent . In 2020 , approximately 296 thousand out of Luxembourg 's 626 thousand inhabitants were foreign nationals .
