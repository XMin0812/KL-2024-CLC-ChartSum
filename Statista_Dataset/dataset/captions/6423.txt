This statistic illustrates the median age of the Italian population with a five-year frequency from 1980 to 2018 . The median age is the age that divides a population into two numerically equal groups ; that is , half the people are younger than this age and half are older . According to data , in 1985 the median age in Italy was of 35.5 years ; 30 years later - in 2015 - the median age increased by roughly 10 years , reaching an average of 45.9 years .
