In 2018 , the turnover of Moschino S.p.A. reached a value of 60.7 million euros . This figure increased steadily since 2012 when it was equal to 39.8 million euros . Despite registering a positive trend in turnover , Moschino S.p.A. could not turn up a profit in 2018 . According to financial data , the company experienced a net loss of approximately 74 thousand euros .
