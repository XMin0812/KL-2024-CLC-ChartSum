In 2019 , the North Italian region Emilia-Romagna had the largest increase in inhabitants , with a net migration of 5,2 per 1,000 inhabitants , followed by Lombardy . On the contrary , Calabria recorded the lowest net migration rate , with a negative value of 7.1 . Similarly , several regions in the South and in the Center showed negative figures , which means that the amount of residents who moved out of these regions was higher than the number of residents moving into the same areas .
