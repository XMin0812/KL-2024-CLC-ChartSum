In 2019 , the Italian home appliances company De'Longhi Group counted approximately 8.2 thousand employees worldwide . This figure represents an increase compared to 2014 , when De'Longhi employed 6.7 thousand individuals globally . During this period the group 's turnover also increased steadily , growing from 1.72 billion euros in 2014 to 2.1 billion euros in 2019 .
