This statistic ranks the leading 20 films by weekend box office revenue in France in August 2017 . The movie `` War for the Planet of the Apes '' was the highest grossing film that month , at roughly 13 million dollars . `` Valerian and the City of a Thousand Planets '' and `` Cars 3 '' ranked second and third highest at 11,2 million dollars and around 10 million dollars respectively .
