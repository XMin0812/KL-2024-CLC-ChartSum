This timeline depicts the annual donations by IKEA Foundation worldwide from 2010 to 2015 . In 2013 , the IKEA Foundation donated ( including soft toy donations ) 101 million euros to children across the globe . IKEA is a privately held , international home products company that designs and sells ready-to-assemble furniture such as beds , chairs , desks , appliances and home accessories . The company is the world 's largest furniture retailer .
