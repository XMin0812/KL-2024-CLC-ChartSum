This statistic provides information on the juvenile arrest rate for violent crimes in the United States in 2014 , sorted by state . Arrest rate is defined as the number of arrests of persons under age 18 for every 100.000 persons aged 10 - 17 . In 2014 , the violent crime arrest rate for persons under age 18 in Alabama stood at 34 . The number of reported violent crimes in the country can be accessed here .
