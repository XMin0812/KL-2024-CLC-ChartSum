This statistic displays the annual income/losses of Roofoods limited from 2015 to 2018 . Roofoods is the parent company of the British food delivery company Deliveroo and their income includes the Deliveroo group of companies in all international locations . Over each period the company has experienced a loss ; Despite revenues of over 476 million British pounds in 2018 , the group had a net loss before tax of 243 million .
