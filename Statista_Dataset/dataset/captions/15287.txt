This statistic shows the total annual turnover of the retail sales of natural or artificial plants and flowers in the United Kingdom from 2008 to 2018 . In 2009 turnover from the retail sale of plants and flowers dropped from 4.19 billion British pounds ( GBP ) in 2008 to 2.97 billion GBP in 2009 . Since then sales have somewhat recovered , reaching around 3.8 million GBP in 2013 . This number rose in 2015 , where figures reached 4.8 billion British pounds .
