In 2019 , an average of 5.3 percent of all workers in the U.S. publishing industry were unemployed . Whilst the industry is in a slightly better position in this regard than motion picture and sound recording , which had an unemployment rate of 6.5 percent , the U.S. broadcasting industry 's figure far outperformed other media sectors with just 2.5 percent of workers unemployed in 2019 .
