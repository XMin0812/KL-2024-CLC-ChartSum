According to the results of a survey conducted during the first week of a nation-wide lockdown due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in India , mobile apps in the health and fitness category saw a spike in usage . About 76 percent of the respondents said that they used the app HealthifyMe more than in the previous week . India went into a lockdown on March 25 , 2020 , the largest in the world , restricting 1.3 billion people , extended until May 3 , 2020 .
