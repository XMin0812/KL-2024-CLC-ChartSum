This statistic shows the annual cost of decriminalized abortions in Brazil from 2009 to 2017 . In 2017 , the annual cost of decriminalized abortions in Brazil amounted to 386.31 thousand Brazilian reals . In Brazil , abortion is decriminalized in three cases : if the pregnancy is a result of rape , if performing an abortion is the only way to save the woman 's life , or if the fetus suffers from anencephaly .
