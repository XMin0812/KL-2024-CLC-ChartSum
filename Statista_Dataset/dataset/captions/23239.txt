The statistic illustrates the leading financial advisors to merger and acquisition ( M & A ) transactions , by deal volume , in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in 2019 . It can be seen that Grant Thornton advised on 179 deals , making the company the leading financial M & A advisor in terms of deal volume in the UK during that time . RSM and BDO completed the top three , with deal volumes of 166 deals and 157 deals respectively as of 2019 .
