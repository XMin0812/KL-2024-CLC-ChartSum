The average monthly exchange rate of euro ( EUR ) to Swedish kronor ( SEK ) increased during the first months of 2020 . The increase was especially steep from February to March , when the average exchange rate of one euro went up from 10.57 to 10.85 Swedish kronor . The average monthly exchange rate decreased steeply again after April , and was down to 10.31 Swedish kroner for one euro in August 2020 .
