In a survey carried out between 2018 and 2019 , around 13.3 percent of respondents in Colombia said they had been asked or had to pay a bribe in different interactions with public and private authorities ( police officers , government employees , schools , hospitals , at work , among others ) . The share of respondents who claimed to be victims of bribery in Colombia has decreased by 4.1 percentage points compared to the previous edition of the survey , conducted in 2016 and 2017 .
