As of January 7 , 2021 , Tunisia registered a total of 152,254 confirmed cases of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) since the beginning of the pandemic . On the same date , there were 2,373 new cases and 48 daily deaths in the country . From September 2020 onwards , the number of infections in Tunisia particularly increased . On October 17 , 2020 , the highest daily increase in cases was recorded at 5,752 , while on December 4 , 2020 , the country crossed the threshold of 100 thousand cumulative cases .
