During the first five days of January 2021 , 265 migrants arrived by sea in Italy . In 2020 , 34.1 thousand migrants arrivals on the Italian coasts . Between 2014 and 2020 , the number of migrants setting foot in the country peaked in 2016 at 181 thousand individuals . Among the most frequent countries of origin declared upon arrivals in 2020 , Tunisia and Bangladesh ranked first and second . More specifically , 12.5 thousand people declared coming from Tunisia , while 4.1 thousand individuals were Bangladeshi .
