This statistic shows the value of the packaged organic food and beverage market in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2007 with a forecast to 2017 . In 2012 , the retail sales of the packaged organic food market amounted to 1,878 million U.S. dollars . It was forecast that the packaged organic food market would have a value of almost 1.63 billion U.S dollars and the organic beverages market a value of 296.4 million U.S dollars in 2014
