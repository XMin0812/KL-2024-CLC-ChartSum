This statistic illustrates monthly figures of gross lending by monetary financial institutions ( MFIs ) to small and medium enterprises ( SMEs ) in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from April 2018 to March 2020 . It can be seen that the monthly value of gross lending fluctuated during this time period , reaching a value of over 5.1 billion British pounds as of March 2020 . The largest value of gross lending by MFIs to SMEs during this period was found in February 2019 , when gross lending reaching a value of over 5.2 Billion British pounds was recorded .
