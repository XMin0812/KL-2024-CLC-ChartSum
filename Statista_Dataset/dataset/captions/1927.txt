Amazon.com is an international e-commerce company offering online retail , computing services , consumer electronics and digital content , as well as other local services such as daily deals and groceries . As of 2020 , Amazon was ranked as the most valuable U.S. brand , with a brand value of around 220.8 billion U.S. dollars . Google , Apple , and Microsoft were the next most valuable brands in the United States that year . These companies also featured at the top of the rankings for most valuable global brands .
