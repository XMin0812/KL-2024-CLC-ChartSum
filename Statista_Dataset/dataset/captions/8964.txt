This statistic shows the annual average Consumer Price Index ( CPI ) of mineral waters , soft drinks and juices in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2003 to 2019 , where 2015 equals 100 . In 2019 , the price index value of mineral waters , soft drinks and juices was measured at 105.3.Consumer price indices are designed to measure changes in the price of everything consumers buy .
