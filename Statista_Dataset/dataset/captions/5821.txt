In November 2020 , the average hourly earnings for all employees on private nonfarm payrolls in the United States stood at 29.58 U.S. dollars . The data have been seasonally adjusted . Employed persons are employees on nonfarm payrolls and consist of : persons who did any work for pay or profit during the survey reference week ; persons who did at least 15 hours of unpaid work in a family-operated enterprise ; and persons who were temporarily absent from their regular jobs because of illness , vacation , bad weather , industrial dispute , or various personal reasons .
