The awareness towards food waste , its causes as well as its economic impact has increased over the years in Italy . As of 2019 , a survey was conducted among Italian households aiming at determining the food wasted weekly , ranked by category . It was found that an average of 114 grams of non-alcoholic beverages was wasted every week . The food wasted the least appeared to be cheese , as approximately 68 grams were thrown away by Italian families .
