This statistic shows the number of cars sold by Mini in Portugal between 2008 and 2019 . Portuguese sales of Mini cars experienced growth over this period , rising from 1,533 units sold in 2008 to the peak of 3,003 units sold in 2018 . Mini sold 2.6 thousand vehicles in Portugal as of 2019 , a decrease of roughly 14 percent in comparison with the previous year .
