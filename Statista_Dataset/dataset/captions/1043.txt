During the 2019 - 2020 season of the French top professional football league , Ligue 1 , Paris-Saint-Germain ( PSG ) was the football club with the highest average annual player salary . A football player of PSG earned close to nine million euros on average per year . For comparison , this is more than eight times what a player at OGC Nice makes . The football club based in Paris , which should be crowned champion for this season , shows once again its supremacy upon its Ligue 1 competitors .
