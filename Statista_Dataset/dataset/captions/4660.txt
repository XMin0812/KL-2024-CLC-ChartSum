Pure Gym Limited is currently the leading operator of low cost , or no frills , fitness clubs in the United Kingdom ( UK ) with approximately 34 percent of the market segment . Low cost fitness clubs generally do not provide saunas or swimming pools and have any non-essential features removed to keep prices as low as possible . As of 2017 , Pure gym had 173 fitness clubs in the UK , followed by The Gym Group with 89 clubs , Fit4Life with 55 clubs and Xercise4Less with 47 clubs .
