According to a 2019 survey , over half of all respondents from the United Kingdom prefer the Royal Mail as a parcel delivery option . Of respondents , 52.7 percent said they would choose the longstanding UK based courier company , which was publicly owned until 2015 . Despite its popularity with consumers , Royal Mail was only the second most commonly listed delivery option by retailers in the country . It was bested by Germany based Hermes Group , which also operates in Italy , Austria , Russia and China .
