More than 2.35 million people recovered from coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Africa as of January 03 , 2021 . As the most affected country in the continent , South Africa also registered the highest number of recoveries , roughly 903.7 thousand , or 38.4 percent of the total . In Morocco , 413,393 patients recovered from the disease . The third country with more recoveries in the continent was Egypt , with 113,898 . It is worth noting that , as of the same date , the cases of coronavirus in Africa amounted to more than 2.8 million , while the fatalities due to the disease reached 67,344 . By the period , there were 425,329 active cases in the continent .
