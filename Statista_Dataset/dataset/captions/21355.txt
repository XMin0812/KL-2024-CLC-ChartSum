This statistic illustrates the total value of financial assets of investment funds in Hungary as of 2019 , broken down by asset type . It can be seen that the total value of currency and deposits owned by investment funds amounted to approximately 1.6 trillion Hungarian forints at that time . The total financial assets of investment funds held in equity and investment fund shares reached a value of over 2.8 trillion Hungarian forints in Hungary as of 2019 .
