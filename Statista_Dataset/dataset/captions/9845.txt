Home office in Sweden was established well before COVID-19 , as the share of enterprises with home office grew by more than 20 percentage points between 2017 and 2019 . The industry within which home office was most commonwas the Information and communication sector , with over 90 percent of the organizations implementing home office . They were followed by the real estate and the energy and waste disposal companies in 2019 . Regarding the companies ' size , it seemed like the bigger the company , the more likely it was to provide remote working options in Sweden .
