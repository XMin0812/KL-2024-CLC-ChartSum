This statistic shows the gross domestic product ( GDP ) per capita in Russia from 1995 to 2019 , with projections up until 2025 . GDP refers to the total market value of all goods and services that are produced within a country per year . It is an important indicator of the economic strength of a country . In 2019 , the GDP per capita in Russia was around 11,601.42 U.S. dollars . See the Russian GDP for comparison .
