The total aggregate attendance at stadiums during football matches in the United Kingdom ( UK ) was highest in the Premier League in 2019 . The Premier League is the most successful football league worldwide and in total there were 14.5 million spectators in 2019 . The UK is a prominent football nation and aggregate attendance in the Championship had a slight increase in recent years from 9.19 million in the 2013/14 season to 11.12 million in the 2018/19 season .
