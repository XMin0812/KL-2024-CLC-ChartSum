Telia had the greatest revenue within the telecom companies in Sweden in 2020 . Telia Company was also the first one in the ranking of telecom companies with largest net profit that year . As of June 2020 , Telia Company AB reached a revenue of over 8.3 billion Swedish kronor , whereas Tele2 followed with roughly 2.4 billion Swedish kronor . Other leading telecommunication companies in the evaluated period were Eltel and Telenor Sverige , both of which had more than revenues of one billion Swedish kronor .
