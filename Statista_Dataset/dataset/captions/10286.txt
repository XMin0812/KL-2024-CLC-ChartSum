This statistic shows the number of ESPRIT Shop-in-Stores worldwide in 2019 , by country . As of June 30 , 2019 , ESPRIT operated 1,887 Shop-in-Stores in Germany . The ESPRIT brand is owned by ESPRIT Holding . The corporation operates in the fashion and retail industry and sells apparel , footwear , accessories , jewelry , and housewares under the brand label .
