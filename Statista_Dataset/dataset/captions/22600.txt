This statistic shows the number of cars sold by Mercedes in the Republic of Ireland between 2009 and 2019 . In the period of consideration , Irish sales of Mercedes cars oscillated . From 2009 to 2017 , Mercedes sales grew to its peak , at approximately 4.8 thousand units as of 2017 . From 2018 onwards , Mercedes sales declined and has fluctuated . In 2019 , Mercedes sales amounted to 3.3 thousand units , a decrease of roughly 20 percent in comparison with the year prior .
