Rocket League is a sports video game developed by Psyonix and released in July 2015 on Xbox One , Windows and PlayStation 4 . The game combines elements from both racing and soccer games - each player controls a vehicle which is used to strike a ball with the aim of scoring in the opposing goal . Rocket League events on the video streaming service Twitch were watched by an average of 26.59 thousand viewers in November 2020 .
