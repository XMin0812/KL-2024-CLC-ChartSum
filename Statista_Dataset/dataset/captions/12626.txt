The net sales of Otsuka Holdings Co. , Ltd. in 2019 amounted to about 1.4 trillion Japanese yen , up from around 1.29 trillion yen in the previous year . Otsuka Holdings is a holding company of 189 group companies worldwide such as Otsuka Pharmaceutical Co. , Ltd. , Taiho Pharmaceutical Co. , Ltd. , and Otsuka Chemical .
