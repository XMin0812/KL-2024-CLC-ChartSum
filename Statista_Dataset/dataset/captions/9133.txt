The statistic shows gross domestic product ( GDP ) in Bhutan from 1984 to 2019 , with projections up until 2024 . Gross domestic product ( GDP ) denotes the aggregate value of all services and goods produced within a country in any given year . GDP is an important indicator of a country 's economic power . In 2019 , Bhutan 's gross domestic product amounted to around 7.94 billion international dollars .
