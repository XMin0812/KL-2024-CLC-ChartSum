A quick service restaurant drive thru is where a customer can place their order , pay and be served all while remaining in their vehicle . This typically takes place through speakers and windows , allowing for speedier customer service . In 2020 , 85 percent of respondents in the U.S. were highly or somewhat satisfied with the quality of menu items ordered and the speed of service at McDonald 's drive thrus . Meanwhile , the service that respondents were least satisfied with was cleanliness and sanitation , with 81 percent being highly or somewhat satisfied .
