During a survey in late March and early April of 2020 about 19 percent of respondents from Spain stated that they currently smoke more cigarettes than before the coronavirus outbreak . Among the five European countries in question the share of respondents that now smoke more was largest in Spain . About two percent behind Spain was the share of respondents from Italy . The share of respondents who smoke more in France and in the UK was less than half that of respondents in Spain .
