According to a survey carried out in Chile on March 16 2020 , 64 percent of respondents did not agree that the government had taken timely and appropriate decisions to address the novel coronavirus ( which causes the COVID-19 ) crisis . According to the same survey , 51 percent of Chilean respondents said the country is little or not at all prepared to protect the population against COVID-19 .
