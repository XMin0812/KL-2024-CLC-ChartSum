According to the results of a survey conducted during the first week of a nation-wide lockdown due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) , educational mobile apps saw a spike in usage compared to week preceding the survey . Over 76 percent of respondents stated that they had increased their use of the educational app , Extramarks . India went into a lockdown on March 25 , 2020 , the largest in the world , restricting 1.3 billion people , extended until May 3 , 2020 .
