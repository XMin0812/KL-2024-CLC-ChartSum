In an international survey from June 2020 , most Italian respondents ( 80 percent ) stated that the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic had already impacted or will impact in the future their personal incomes . Among the different surveyed G7 countries , Italy registered the largest share of interviewees declaring that the coronavirus outbreak had an impact on their income .
