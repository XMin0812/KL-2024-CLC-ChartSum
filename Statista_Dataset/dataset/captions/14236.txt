Since the outbreak of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) , as of November 20 , 62580 cases have been confirmed in Slovenia . The highest number of infections remains within the 45 - 54 age group , from which 12866 people are infected . People in the age group 35 - 44 years are the second biggest group , with 12469 infections .
