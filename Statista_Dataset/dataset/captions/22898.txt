This statistic shows the share of cross-border bank lending in international financial markets assigned to the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 1993 to 2016 . The source indicates that `` cross-border bank lending or international bank flows grew mainly as a result of on ongoing process of financial globalization since the beginning of the 1990s '' . In 2004 the UK 's share in international bank lending amounted to 20 percent . Ten years later , the share equaled 17 percent .
