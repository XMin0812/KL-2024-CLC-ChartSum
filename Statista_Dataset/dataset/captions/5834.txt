This statistic presents the top ten Twitter accounts worldwide from November 2018 to February 2019 , by number of interactions . According to the findings , the K-Pop group BTS ( @ bts_twt ) was ranked first during the recorded period with a total of 407 million interactions . Coming in third place was the music artist Ariana Grande ( @ ArianaGrande ) , who had a total of 101.8 million interactions on Twitter .
