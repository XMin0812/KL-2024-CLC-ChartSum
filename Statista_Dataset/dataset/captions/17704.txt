This statistic depicts the net income of AbbVie from 2010 to 2019 . In 2019 , AbbVie garnered a net income totaling some 7.9 billion U.S. dollars . Abbot Laboratories was a U.S.-based global pharmaceutical and healthcare products company , headquartered in Chicago , Illinois . In January 2013 , Abbott was separated into two companies : Abbott Laboratories and AbbVie . Abbott Laboratories is now specialized on medical products , while AbbVie is responsible for research-based pharmaceuticals .
