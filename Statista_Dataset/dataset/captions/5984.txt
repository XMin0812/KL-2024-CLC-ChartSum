Between 2009 and 2019 , the population of Maastricht increased , from just over 118,000 to roughly 121,600 inhabitants . This was just over ten percent of the total number of people living in the Limburg province , of which Maastricht is the capital . In 2019 , Limburg 's population amounted to roughly 1.1 million inhabitants , making it the seventh province in the country based on population size .
