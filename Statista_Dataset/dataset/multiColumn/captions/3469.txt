Poland 's Manufacturing Purchasing Managers Index ( PMI ) decreased from 48.2 points in March 2020 to 31.9 in May 2020 due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak in Poland . The PMI index registered a record decline in April , to the lowest level in the history of research , i.e . since 1998 . In December 2020 , the index increased to 50.8 points .
