In 2019 , most individuals of the Roma and Sinti population living in North-Italy were Italians . More specifically , the share of people with the Italian nationality was equal to 88 percent . On the contrary , 70 percent of Roma and Sinti residing in Central Italy held a nationality of an Ex-Jugoslavian country . The Roma and Sinti population in Italy was often victim of episodes of hate speech . In 2018 , 125 cases of hate speech against Roma and Sinti were recorded nationwide .
