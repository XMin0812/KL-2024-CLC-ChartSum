In the United Kingdom ( UK ) , the British convenience store retailer McColl 's had a greater presence in the North . In the North West of the UK , there were 207 McColl 's stores , and an additional 23 newsagents also belonging to McColl 's . In Scotland , a total of 199 McColl 's stores was in operation in 2019 . Over the years , the British convenience store retailer McColl 's increased its number of convenience stores , whereas newsagents also operating under the name of McColls 's went down in number .
