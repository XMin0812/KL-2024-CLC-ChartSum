This statistic shows a ranking of fraternities in the United States in 2012 . To measure this , the daily beast considered the number of active collegiate chapters , the number of alumni who had since become members of the U.S. Senate , House of Representatives or President and finally the amount of money donated by the fraternity to nonprofit organizations . Sigma Alpha Epsilon , the second ranked fraternity in the U.S. also had William Mckinley , the 25th U.S. President amongst its members .
