This statistic represents the McKesson Corporation 's revenues in the Distribution Solutions segment in fiscal years 2012 to 2018 . In FY 2018 , the total revenues in the medical-surgical distribution & services segment amounted to some 6.6 billion U.S. dollars . McKesson is a health corporation , headquartered in San Francisco , California . Its main business consists of pharmaceutical distribution , health information technology , medical supplies , and care management tools .
