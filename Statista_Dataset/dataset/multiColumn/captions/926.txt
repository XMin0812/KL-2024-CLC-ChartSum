The coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic is having a damaging impact on the global hotel industry . Preliminary results for London hotels showed that compared to the same period in 2019 , occupancy rates from March 1 to 7 , 2020 fell by 21 percent , resulting in a fall in RevPar by 27.7 percent .
