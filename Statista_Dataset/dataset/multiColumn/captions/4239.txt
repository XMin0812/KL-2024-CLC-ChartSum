Streaming is the new downloading , according to the most recent figures . Most of the revenues generated from the digital music industry in Spain originated in streaming services , with a turnover that amounted to 123 million euros in 2018 that was expected to reach 188 million by the year 2023 . These earnings were majorly a result of the distribution of streaming subscriptions , which returned revenues of about 115.3 million euros during the same period .
