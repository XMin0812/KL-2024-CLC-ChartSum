According to a survey conducted in the Philippines , about 78.1 percent of households with a member earning below Php 9.5 thousand Philippine pesos , lost a job from the Enhanced Community Quarantine ( ECQ ) that was implemented in the country on March 16 , 2020 , because of the coronavirus COVID-19 pandemic . On the other hand , about 66.7 percent of households with a member earning Php 190.4 thousand Philippine pesos had not lost a job during the ECQ .
