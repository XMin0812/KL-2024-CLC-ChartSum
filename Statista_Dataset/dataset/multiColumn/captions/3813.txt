In 2019 , Moncler 's revenues from retail reached a value of 1.26 billion euros . This figure represented an almost fourfold increase from 2013 , when retail revenues amounted to 333.6 million euros . During the same period , wholesale revenue for Moncler also increased , growing from 247 million euros to 370.8 million euros . When considering the two distribution channels together , revenues of the company amounted to 1.62 billion euros in 2019 .
