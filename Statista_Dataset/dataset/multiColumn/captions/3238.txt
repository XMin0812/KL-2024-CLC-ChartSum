This statistic displays the Spanish-born and foreign population residing in Spain from 2006 to 2020 . As of January 2020 , the Spanish-born population accounted for 42.09 million out of the national total . Foreign-born residents grew during the analyzed period of time , with slightly over five million in 2019 and 2020 . The population in Spain has also been analyzed by autonomous community , with Andalusia topping the list of the most populated regions .
