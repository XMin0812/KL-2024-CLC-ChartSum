The amount of heat pumps in operation in Germany has increased over the years , both aerothermal and ground source heat pumps . Between 2013 and 2019 , figures increased by roughly 593 thousand heat pumps . In all years , aerothermal heat pumps were by far more common than ground source heat pumps . In 2019 , Germany had 1.2 million heat pumps in operation , of which , roughly 762 thousand were aerothermal heat pumps . In the same year , the 28 European Union members had approximately 40 million heat pumps in operation .
