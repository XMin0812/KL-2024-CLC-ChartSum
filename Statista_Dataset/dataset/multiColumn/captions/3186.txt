This statistic shows the number of children in the United States who live with their parents , one parent or no parent on a timeline from 1970 to 2019 . Detailed figures for children living with either a mother or a father only can be found here . The figures for children living with other relatives or non-relatives have been added up for the `` No parents '' column . Detailed figures can be found at the source . In 2019 , about 19 million children in the United States lived with one parent .
