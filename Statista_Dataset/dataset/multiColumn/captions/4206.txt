Germany 's bicycle industry remained flat between 2018 and 2019 , but is beginning to embrace e-bikes . German manufacturers produced roughly 960,000 electric bicycle units in 2019 , up from around 470,000 electric bike units in 2019 . The most popular manufacturers include Cube , Prophete , Kalkhoff , and Haibike . Haibike is a subsidiary of the Dutch company Accell .
