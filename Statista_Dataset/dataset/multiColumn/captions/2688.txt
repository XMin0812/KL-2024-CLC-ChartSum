This statistic shows a comparison of the top U.S. pharmaceutical manufacturer vs. the top U.S. pharmaceutical distributor as of 2019 . In general , pharmaceutical distributors generate much higher revenues than manufacturers , as it can be seen clearly comparing top distributor McKesson and top manufacturer Pfizer . On the other hand , manufacturers ' return on sales are much higher . In 2017 , a large controversy was started about who is responsible for increasing drug prices whereby pharmaceutical manufacturers and distributors were especially in focus , passing the responsibility for high pricing to each other .
