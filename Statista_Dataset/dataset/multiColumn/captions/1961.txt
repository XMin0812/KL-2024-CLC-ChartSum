According to the most recently available data , the frequency of visiting a movie theater to see a film among U.S. adults fluctuated greatly according to age - 20 percent of adults aged 18 to 29 years reported watching a movie in a theater once per month or more , compared to just nine percent of respondents aged 55 to 64 years old . The majority of adults aged 45 or above only saw movies in the cinema once a year or less .
