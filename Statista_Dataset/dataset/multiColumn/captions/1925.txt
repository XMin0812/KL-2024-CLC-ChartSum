The statistic shows the number of companies operating on the insurance market in Portugal from 2011 to 2018 . During this period the both domestic and total insurance market fluctuated . The domestic market had a boost in 2015 , achieving 45 companies . It declined right after , presenting the lowest number in this period by the end of 2018 with 39 companies . Very differently , the total insurance market showed a trend of increase , with the number slightly decreasing to 643 companies by the end of 2018 .
