A survey conducted by Vase.ai on Malaysian consumer patterns during the COVID-19 outbreak , 83 percent of Malaysian respondents stated that they had been stocking up eggs during the COVID-19 outbreak . Eggs remained the most stocked-up on food item amongst the majority of consumers in Malaysia during the Movement Control Order ( MCO ) period . Malaysia introduced the MCO on March 18 in a bid to stem the spread of COVID-19 . It has been extended again to June 9 .
