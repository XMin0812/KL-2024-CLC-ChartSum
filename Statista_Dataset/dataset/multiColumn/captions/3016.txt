On March 7 , 2020 , roughly 3.23 million people watched the final of the Swedish music TV show Melodifestivalen . Thus , it was the most popular broadcast of all Melodifestvalen 's broadcasts that year . The first semi-final broadcasted on February 1 , came second , with 2.95 million television viewers . In 2019 , 71.3 percent of the Swedish population watched at least three minutes of any of Melodifestivalen 's main broadcasts .
