The statistic illustrates the distribution in gender at Philips Group in 2019 , by type of employees . As of that year , 52 percent of the staff was male and 48 percent female . Within the executive board , 78 percent was male and 22 percent female . It can be seen that the higher the position , the more it was dominated by males . Philips ' country of origin , which is the Netherlands , had 522 million euros of sales . After spin-offs of other product segments , Koninklijke Philips N.V. today is a company focused on healthcare/medical technology .
