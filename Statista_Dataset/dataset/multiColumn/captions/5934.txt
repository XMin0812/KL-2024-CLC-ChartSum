According to a survey conducted in August 2018 , after eight months in office , the rejection rate of Brazil 's president Jair Bolsonaro was highest among people aged 16 to 24 and 45 to 59 . In both age ranges , 41 percent of respondents said Bolsonaro 's performance had been either poor or very poor . In contrast , 32 percent of respondents aged 60 years or older said Bolsonaro 's performance as president was ( very ) good .
