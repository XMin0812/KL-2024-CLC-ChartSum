This statistic shows the attitude of several motorcycle dealerships towards the profit returns earned from their franchise in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in spring and autumn 2018 . The respondents gave a score out of five . Rating was relatively low across all dealerships , with Harley Davidson the only one standing out as offering the greatest profit returns according to their dealers . Dealers of the U.S. motorcycle brand were also the most optimistic about future profitability of their business .
