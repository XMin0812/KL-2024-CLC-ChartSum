This statistic displays the number of mono-brand stores of the Italian fashion company Geox between 2014 and 2019 , broken down by geographical area . As December 31 , 2019 there were 272 Geox mono-brand stores in Italy , 270in Europe , 37 in North America and 398 in the the rest of the world . Moreover , it can be seen that the overall number decreased steadily in all geographical areas .
