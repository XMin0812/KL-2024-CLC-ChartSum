During the time of the coronavirus pandemic , global consumer sentiment towards brand communication seems rather positive , with 65 percent saying that hearing form brands about their response to the situation is comforting and reassuring . In China and Brazil especially , where 89 and 78 percent of consumers were of this opinion . On the other hand , 58 percent fo consumers in India thought all brand communication during the outbreak was just adding to their anxiety and concern , and so did 67 percent of Chinese respondents .
