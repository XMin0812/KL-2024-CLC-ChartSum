The average length of marriage until it was dissolved by death was 46.8 years in Sweden in 2019 . In contrast , the average length of marriages dissolved by divorce was 11.4 years in 2019 . In the past decade , the length of marriages dissolved by both divorce and by death were relatively stable . The number of divorces in Sweden decreased from 2013 to 2017 , but increased again thereafter , and amounted to more than 25 thousand in 2019 .
