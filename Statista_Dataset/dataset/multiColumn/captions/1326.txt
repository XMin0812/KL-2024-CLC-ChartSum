The statistic shows the divorce rate in Norway in 2019 , by gender and age group . The divorce rate was highest among females aged 15 to 19 years old ( 21.9 divorces per 1,000 females ) . Among men the divorce rate was highest in the age groups 20 to 24 years and 40 to 44 years ( 17.9 and 18.1 divorces per 1,000 males respectively ) .
