This statistic shows the distribution share of ESPRIT employees worldwide in the fiscal years 2008/09 to 2013/2014 , by region . In fiscal year 2009/2010 , ESPRIT employed 60 percent of their total workforce in Europe . The ESPRIT brand is owned by the ESPRIT Holding . The corporation operates in the fashion and retail industry and sells apparel , footwear , accessories , jewelry , and house ware under the brand label .
