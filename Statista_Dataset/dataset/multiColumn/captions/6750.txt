This statistic shows the Standard Grade exam results by gender in the academic year 2012/2013.1 is the highest grade , and 6 is the lowest pass grade . A higher percentage of female candidates received grades 1 and 2 than their male counterparts ; at the top level there was a difference of five percent . The most common grade received was 2 , with 26 percent of boys and 29 percent of girls achieving this level .
