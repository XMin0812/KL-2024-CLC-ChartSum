This statistic presents the potential voting intention of the United Kingdom parliament in a vote on Theresa May 's Brexit deal with the European Union , as of 2018 . In order for her to get her Brexit deal through parliament , May requires at least 320 votes to secure a majority . Although May can rely on up to 235 loyalists in her own party to support her , she also needs the support of 77 other Conservative MPs who may vote against her , as well as the 10 MPs who belong to the DUP .
