The statistic presents box office revenues of Academy Award `` Best Picture '' winners and the highest grossing films of the year from 2000 to 2018 . The highest grossing movie in 2012 was `` Marvel 's The Avengers '' , with a box office revenue of 623.36 million U.S. dollars . The winner of the `` Best Picture '' Academy Award for that year was `` Argo '' , with an annual revenue of 134.58 million . The highest grosser of 2018 was `` Black Panther '' , which generated box office revenue of over 700 million U.S. dollars . The Marvel movie was nominated for Best Picture , but was beaten by `` Green Book '' , which generated approximately 71 million U.S. dollars at the domestic box office .
