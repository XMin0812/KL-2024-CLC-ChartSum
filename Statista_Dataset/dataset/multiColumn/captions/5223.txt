This statistic represents the usage penetration of mobile internet in France from 2010 to 2019 , by age group . It reveals that the share of respondents aged 30 to 44 who accessed mobile internet rose by over 60 points between 2010 and 2019 . Also , the share of people aged 15 to 29 who accessed mobile internet in the three months prior to this survey was 95.1 percent .
