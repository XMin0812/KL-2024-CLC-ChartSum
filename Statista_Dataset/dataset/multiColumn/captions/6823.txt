Telehealth and telemedicine options are increasingly popular health care options in the U.S. With rising healthcare costs globally , it is expected that telehealth and telemedicine will reduce the cost burden of health care for some people . As of 2016 , there were about 47 thousand Medicare beneficiaries under the age of 65 that utilized telemedicine . Those 47 thousand beneficiaries utilized telehealth services about 144.5 thousand times during 2016 . While telehealth is increasing in popularity , there are still many barriers to telehealth adoption among physicians .
