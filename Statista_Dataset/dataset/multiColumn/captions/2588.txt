This statistic shows the total cost of United States midterm elections from 1998 to 2018 , by party . In 2018 , it is projected that the midterm elections will cost about 5.2 billion U.S. dollars . Democrats are projected to spend around 2.5 billion U.S. dollars , Republicans 2.2 billion U.S. dollars , and third party candidates 460.3 million U.S. dollars .
