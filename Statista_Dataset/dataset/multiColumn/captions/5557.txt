The export value of Italian white wines from Italy varied significantly between the first semester of 2012 and the first semester of 2020 . As of the survey period , the export value of Italian PGI white wines decreased passing from 255 million euros in the first half of 2012 , to 140 million euros as of the first half of 2020 . On the other hand , the export value of Italian PDO white wines increased over the period , reaching 455 million euros in the first semester of 2020 .
