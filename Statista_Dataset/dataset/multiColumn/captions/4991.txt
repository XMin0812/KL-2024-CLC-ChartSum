This statistic presents the share of children who recently participated in cycling or riding a bike in England , according to annual surveys conducted between 2010/11 and 2017/18 . Over this period , the proportion of children cycling has been relatively constant . In the period 2017/18 , around 22.6 percent of the children aged between 11 and 15 had ridden a bike in the last 4 weeks prior to the survey .
