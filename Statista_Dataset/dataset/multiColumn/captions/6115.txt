Between 2013 and 2016 , hydropower was the main renewable source in the 28 European members electricity mix . From 2017 onnwards , wind power became the major renewable source in the EU-28 , generating 377.5 terawatt hours worth of electricity in 2018 . In the same year , ocean and geothermal power were the renewable sources with the lowest electricity generation , with 0.5 and 6.7 terawatt hours , respectively .
