This statistic shows birth rates among teenagers aged 15 to 17 years in 1991 , 2007 , and 2014 to 2018 , sorted by ethnic origin . About 70 out of every 1,000 Hispanic women aged 15 to 17 gave birth in 1991 . In comparison , the birth rate for Hispanic women in that age group was much lower in 2018 , at around 12.4 out of every 1,000 women .
