This statistic shows the opinion of Americans on whether there should or should not be a law that would ban the possession of handguns , except by the police and other authorized persons . The survey was conducted from 1990 to 2020 . In 1990 , about 41 percent of surveyed Americans felt that there should be a law that would ban the private possession of handguns . In 2020 , only 25 percent felt the same way .
