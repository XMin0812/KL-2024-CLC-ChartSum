As of March 2020 , McDonald 's was the leading brand among consumers in Singapore during the COVID-19 pandemic . For disinfectants , Dettol has been the most purchased brand among the respondents in Singapore , with 32 percent of respondents who were more worried about COVID-19 having purchased a product from that brand .
