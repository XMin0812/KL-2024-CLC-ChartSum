On 20 November , over 22 thousand new cases of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) were confirmed , raising the total count to 819,262 ( 424,748 active cases ) in Poland , an increase compared to the previous day . However , 22 thousand people were under epidemiological supervision . According to the Minister of Health , over 12 thousand infected patients died , and most of them had serious health problems before the infection .
