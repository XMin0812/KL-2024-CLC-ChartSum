This statistic depicts the global views towards homosexuality in 2013 . The majority people from Asian countries believe that homosexuality is unacceptable . In contrast to this , Europeans are much less likely to say that homosexuality is unacceptable . This is especially true in Spain ( 6 % ) , Germany ( 8 % ) , France ( 14 % ) , Italy ( 19 % ) , and Britain ( 17 % ) .
