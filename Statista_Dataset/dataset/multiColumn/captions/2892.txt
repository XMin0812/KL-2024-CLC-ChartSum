Electric bicycles were the most expensive type of bicycle Spanish cyclists could purchase , at an average price of almost 2.2 thousand euros in 2019 . Road bikes ranked as the second most costly type of bike during the same year , with average prices amounting to almost 1.8 thousand euros . In comparison to 2017 , e-bikes experienced the greatest sales growth during 2018 in the Mediterranean country , recording almost 59 percent more sales than the previous year . This trend seems to be on par with the rest of Europe , which saw the number of electric bikes in their streets increase significantly in the last decade .
