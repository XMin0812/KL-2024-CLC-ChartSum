The most popular Ad-supported Video-on-Demand ( AVoD ) service in North America was YouTube in both 2018 and 2019 , with usage increasing from 57.9 percent to 73.1 percent . A recent survey tracked growth rates across several AVoD services , with Facebook and Snapchat also increasing their viewers . Additionally , there was a significant increase in the usage of free video , which grew from 13.4 percent in 2018 to 45.3 percent in 2019 .
