Swimming is the most popular sport among 5 to 10 year olds in England by far . In an annual governmental survey , it was found that half of children in said age group had been participating in either swimming , diving or lifesaving activities . The second most popular type of sport was football , which becomes more popular overall as children get older . Among 11 to 15 year olds roughly a third had been swimming in the past four weeks , however almost half had been playing football at this point .
