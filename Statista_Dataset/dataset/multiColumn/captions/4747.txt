This statistic shows the attitudes towards Britain 's possession of nuclear weapons of the British public in Great Britain in 2013 , by region . Whilst the people of Great Britain appear to be somewhat divided on the issue , there is a noticeably less positive reaction from people living in Scotland . This may be due to anxiety over accidents in the production , transportation and maintenance of British nuclear weapons , all of which take place primarily in or around Scotland 's largest city , Glasgow .
