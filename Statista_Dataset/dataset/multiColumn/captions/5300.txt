This statistic shows the market shares of the grocery retail chains Rema 1000 and Kiwi in Norway from 2007 to 2017 . In 2007 , the grocery retail chain Rema 1000 had a market share of 17.3 percent , whereas as of February 2017 , it held a share of 23.5 percent of the grocery retail market in Norway . The market share of the grocery retail chain Kiwi had also increased to over 20 percent in the beginning of 2017 .
