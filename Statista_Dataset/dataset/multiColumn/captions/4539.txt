This statistic describes a projection of the total number of people aged 65 and older with Alzheimer 's disease in the United States in 2020 and 2025 , by state . Currently , there are some 29,000 people aged 65 years and older in Hawaii with Alzheimer 's . According to a forecast , this number is expected to increase to 35,000 by 2025 . Scientists believe that early detection of Alzheimer 's can be the best way to prevent or slow the course of the disease .
