In 2018 , only about 14 percent of households in Sicily had access to ultrafast broadband ( minimum download speed of 100 Mbps ) . The coverage was expected to reach roughly 40 percent of households by the end of 2021 . Between 2017 and 2018 , the coverage of fiber-to-the-home/building connections increased dramatically in Italy , reaching 6.3 million households . Optical fiber is a key infrastructure for ultrafast broadband access . Between 2018 and 2019 , the number of ultrafast broadband connections in the country nearly doubled , passing from 2.1 million to 3.49 million .
