This statistic illustrates the total amount of investments by private equity firms in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2014 to 2016 , broken down by business sector . Private equity is the OTC provision of equity capital through private or institutional investments with the participation of companies in another company for a limited time in order to generate financial benefits . It can be seen that private equity investments in consumer goods and services reached a value of more than four billion euros in 2016 .
