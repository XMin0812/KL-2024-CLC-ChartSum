On the 3rd of February , 2020 , the newly built Huoshenshan Hospital started receiving coronavirus patients in Wuhan . From January 25 to February 3 , it took ten days and seven thousand workers to construct the hospital . Compared to Beijing Xiaotangshan Hospital built in 2003 for treating SARS patients , the construction area of Huoshenshan hospital was one third larger than that of Xiaotangshan Hospital .
