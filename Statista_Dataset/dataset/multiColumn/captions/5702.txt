This statistic illustrates the dependency of the top ten defense industry suppliers on the Ministry of Defence ( MOD ) business in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2005/06 to 2019/20 , in percent of global revenue of the top ten suppliers . QinetiQ Group was the MOD supplier with the highest dependency , with 60.3 percent of its global revenue made through MOD sales in 2019/20 . The source explains this dependency with a change in the sale of the company 's US services business in 2014 .
