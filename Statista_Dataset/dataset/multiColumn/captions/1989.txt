According to a survey carried out during May , 2020 in Mexico , 76.5 percent of respondents believed that their economic situation was either highly or somewhat affected by the COVID-19 crisis . In comparison , 23.5 percent considered that the situation was only marginally , or not at all affecting their economy . In the Latin American country , the first cases of COVID-19 were confirmed on February 28 , 2020 . As of July 15 , 2020 this figure amounted to 311,486 .
