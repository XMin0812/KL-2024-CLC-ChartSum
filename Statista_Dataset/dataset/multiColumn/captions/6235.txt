The survey results as of April 2020 showed that the majority of Russians expected the situation in the national economy to worsen in the course of the following year . In the capital Moscow , 16 percent of respondents predicted an economic downturn for the next several years as a consequence of the crisis caused by the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak .
