This statistic displays the number of organ donors in the United States by gender , from 1988 to 2020 . In 2019 , there were 9,455 female organ donors in the country . Organ donation can be given through both a deceased and living donor if blood and oxygen are flowing through the organs until the time of recovery to ensure viability . There are over 120,000 people in the country waiting for an organ transplant .
