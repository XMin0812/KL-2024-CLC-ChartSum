The import value of avocados to the Netherlands increased by nearly 60 percent in 2019 . Nonetheless , the export value was still higher , measured at approximately 786 million euros in that year . Since 2014 , both the import and export of avocados have more than tripled , indicating a surge in popularity for the beloved berry . With Rotterdam as the main port of Europe , the high export value signifies that the hunger for this versatile fruit exists in other European countries as well , such as Spain .
