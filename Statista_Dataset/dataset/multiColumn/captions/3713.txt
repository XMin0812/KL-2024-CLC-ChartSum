In 2019 , the total population of Finland was approximately 5.5 million people . Slightly more than half of the population , 2.8 million , were women , compared to around 2.73 million men . In the ten-year period from 2009 to 2019 , the number of men increased slightly faster than the number of women . Overall , the population of Finland has been steadily increasing , but showed a slowing trend in recent years .
