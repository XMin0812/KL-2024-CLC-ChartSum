Due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , the Serie A football league in Italy suspended all matches of the season 2019/2020 until April 3 , 2020 . Thus , 124 Serie A games were at risk of being postponed or canceled as of March 2020 . Data reveal that the TV rights value of the matches at risk amounted to roughly 316.5 million euros . Specifically , the TV rights value of the 86 matches to be broadcasted on Sky amounted to 252.2 million euros , while the value of the 38 matches to be shown on Dazn amounted to 64.3 million euros .
