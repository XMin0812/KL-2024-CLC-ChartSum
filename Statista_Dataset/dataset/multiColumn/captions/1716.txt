This statistic depicts the share of retail net sales of Luxottica in 2018 , broken down by geographical area . In 2018 , 73 percent of Luxottica 's retail net sales came from North America . Founded in 1961 in Agordo , Italy , the Luxottica Group S.p.A. is the world 's largest eyewear company . Its best known brands include Ray-Ban , Persol and Oakley , Inc . The company also makes sunglasses and frames for a multitude of designer brands such as Chanel and Prada .
