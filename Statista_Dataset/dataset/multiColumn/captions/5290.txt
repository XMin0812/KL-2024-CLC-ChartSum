This statistic shows the median net worth of families in the United States from 1983 to 2013 , by income tier . In 2013 , middle class families had a median net worth of about 98,057 U.S. dollars . This study defined middle class income households as those with an income between 67 and 200 percent of the U.S. median household income , after adjustment for household size . In 2014 , the middle class income ranged from about 42,000 U.S. dollars to about 126,000 U.S. dollars per year for a three-person household .
