The impact of the coronavirus epidemic on the Polish economy will have a considerable effect . In a pessimistic scenario , GDP growth is predicted to fall to nearly 11 percentage points , the unemployment rate will amount to over 10 percent , and inflation will rise to over 2.5 percent annually . According to the forecast , the Polish economy may not emerge from the crisis until 2021 .
