The crime rate of the United Kingdom in 2019/20 was 96.4 per thousand people , compared with 110 in 2002/03 . During this time period the crime rate of England and Wales has been the highest in the UK , while Scotland 's crime rate has improved the most , falling from 82.6 crimes per thousand people in 2002/03 , to just 45.1 by 2018/19 .
