This statistic presents the number of the new supermini car registrations in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in 2018 and 2019 , by model type . In 2019 , there were around 686,000 vehicles registered in the supermini segment . That year , the leading five models accounted for roughly 35 percent of all registered cars in the segment . Ranking in first place was the Ford Fiesta model with approximately 77,800 registered vehicles and a segment share of 11.3 percent .
