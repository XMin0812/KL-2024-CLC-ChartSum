This statistic represents proved coal reserves worldwide between 2010 to 2019 , by type . In 2019 , global proved reserves of anthracite and bituminous coal amounted to around 749 billion metric tons . As of 2019 , the life years of coal is predicted to be a about 132 years at the current rate of production . The world 's largest coal reserves are found in the United States , Russia , and China .
