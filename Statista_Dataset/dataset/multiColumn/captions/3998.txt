In Italy , the price of coffee was highly reliant on the type sold . During the first half of 2019 , the most expensive coffee type was found to be the capsules , which reached 40.59 euros per kg , followed by instant coffee ( 19.58 euros per kg ) and coffee pods ( 19.34 euros per kg ) . However , as far as the average price per package was concerned , coffee beans appeared to be the most expensive .
