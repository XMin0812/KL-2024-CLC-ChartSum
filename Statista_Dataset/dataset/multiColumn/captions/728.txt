The wages to turnover ratio is a key performance indicator used by businesses to evaluate the efficiency of staff ( players ) as a generator of income as a function of the clubs revenue . The lower the ratio , the more revenue is being generated per employee . On the other hand , clubs with a high wage to sales ratios may consider selling players and or staff in order to improve their ratio . In the Premiere league , 58 percent of clubs used the ratio as a KPI .
