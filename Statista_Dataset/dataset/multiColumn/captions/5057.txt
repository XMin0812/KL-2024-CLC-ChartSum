This statistic presents the main underserved international airline routes in the world , based on the number of non-stop and one-stop bookings in 2015 . The route between Jakarta and Jeddah ( CGK - JED ) had the highest international traffic in terms of indirect flights in 2015 , with almost 280,000 one-stop bookings in addition to more than 540,000 non-stop passengers traveling on that route .
