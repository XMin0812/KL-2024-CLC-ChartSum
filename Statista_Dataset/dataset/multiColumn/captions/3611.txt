This table shows the number of Zika virus disease cases in the U.S. from 2015 to 2020 . In 2017 , there were over 452 cases of the Zika virus in the U.S . However , this number decreased to 74 in 2018 . The World Health Organization announced an end to the Zika virus epidemic that spread from Brazil to other parts of South and North America in November 2016 .
