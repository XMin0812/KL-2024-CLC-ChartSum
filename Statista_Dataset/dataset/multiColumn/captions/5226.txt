This statistic illustrates the annual primary rent costs per square meter for warehouses over 5,000 square meters in the occupier logistics market in Lisbon , Portugal from the 4th quarter 2014 to the 4th quarter 2018 . It can be seen that the annual primary rent costs in Lisbon remained constant between 2016 and 2018 at a value of 42 euros per square meter during that time .
