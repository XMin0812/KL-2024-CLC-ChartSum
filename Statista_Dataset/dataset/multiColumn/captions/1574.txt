From October 2018 to September 2019 , bingo in the United Kingdom ( UK ) generated over one billion British pounds in revenue . The highest share was generated by main stage bingo with approximately 626.45 million British pounds . Overall , during the observation period , bingo revenues have dropped . In 2009/2010 , they stood at 1.34 billion British pounds , falling to 1.04 billion British pounds by 2017/2018 .
