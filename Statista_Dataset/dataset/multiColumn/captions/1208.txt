As of May 2020 , over 39 percent of women and nearly 38 percent of men surveyed in Mexico reported to have increased the usage of sex video calls and sexting during the COVID-19 pandemic . On the other hand , 32 percent of female survey participants and nearly 30 percent of male respondents in the Latin American country said that they were doing less video calls with sex content or sending and receiving less sexually explicit text messages . Over 40 percent of Mexican respondents claimed their sexual activity had decreased since the pandemic .
