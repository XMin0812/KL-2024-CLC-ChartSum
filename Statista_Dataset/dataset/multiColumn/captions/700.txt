Zalando.dk was the most often used web-shop in Denmark in recent years . Over seven thousand Danish people were asked which web-shop they did their last purchase from and four percent of them answered Zalando in the first half of 2020 . Two percent of the respondents did their last purchase on Amazon , the second most used web-shop , and 1.9 percent did it on H & M , the web-shop ranked third . Some worldwide famous web-shops , such as Aliexpress and Asos only came in use from 2018 onwards in Denmark .
