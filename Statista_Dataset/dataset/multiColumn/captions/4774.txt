This statistic presents the share of children who had participated in basketball in the last 4 weeks in England , as of annual surveys conducted between 2010/2011 and 2017/18 . In the period 2013/14 , around 33 percent of children aged between 11 and 15 had participated in basketball in the 4 weeks prior to the survey . Since then , the participation rate has been falling , reaching 20.9 percent in 2017/2018 .
