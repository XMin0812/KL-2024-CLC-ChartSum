This statistic shows the revenue of Total from 2011 to 2019 , distributed by business segment . In 2019 , the company 's Marketing & Services segment generated around 87.28 billion U.S. dollars . Total S.A. is one of the world 's largest oil and gas companies . The French company , headquartered in Paris-Courbevoie , is specialized on the entire oil and gas chain , as well as on chemical manufacturing .
