Although the production volume of apples decreased generally in Denmark from 2015 to 2018 , it was still the most widely produced fruit in the country . In 2017 , approximately 20.5 thousand metric tons of apples were produced , while the production volume amounted to more than 32 thousand metric tons in the following year . By comparison , pears were the second most produced fruit in 2017 , with an amount of nearly 4.5 thousand metric tons , while in 2018 , rasperries came second with around 6.5 thousand metric tons .
