This statistic compares the pass rates of practical motorcycle road tests for different age groups in the United Kingdom ( UK ) between April 2015 and March 2018 . 21 to 30-year-olds had the highest pass rate and 21 to 50-year-olds all had a higher pass rate than the youngest group . Pass rates for the practical motorcycle road test were quite a bit higher than for car practical tests . Pass rates for candidates over 61 years old and candidates aged between seventeen and twenty years old averaged out at 58.9 and 65.9 percent respectively , the lowest pass rates of any age groups except that of candidates 51 to 60 years old , of whom 64.9 percent passed .
