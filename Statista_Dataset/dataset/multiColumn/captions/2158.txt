Temporary suspension by the Polish government of international ( from March 15 ) and domestic ( from March 16 ) air connections to and from the territory of the Republic of Poland automatically resulted in a significant decrease in traffic in Polish airspace compared to the same period in the previous years . During the first day of suspension , over 82 percent decrease in traffic at Polish airports was recorded , compared to the same period in the last year . The decision to suspend air connections in Poland was related to the threat of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic .
