The outbreak of coronavirus in Poland changed the purchasing behavior of the society . Installment loan-concerned purchases , especially those made through large retail chains and e-commerce , such as electronics , household appliances , furniture and cars . In the 14th week of March , the downward trend in installment loans slowed down compared to the previous week . However , the collapse in this segment might be intensified by sales drop due to shopping malls ' shutdown and trade restrictions across the country . In the case of the residential market , the first symptoms of the reconstruction of the demand for housing loans are visible . The housing loans ' demand volume in the week 30.03 . - 05.04 . increased by 17.4 percent compared to the previous week figures .
