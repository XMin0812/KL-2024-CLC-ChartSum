In 2019 , Cineworld Group 's global revenue surpassed 4.36 billion U.S. dollars , approximately 3.21 billion of which was generated from the company 's operations in the United States . After Cineworld acquired Regal Entertainment in early 2018 , the company became the second biggest cinema chain in the world by number of screens . Cineworld is headquartered in the United Kingdom , where it operates its additional brand , Picturehouse .
