The statistic shows the adult mortality rate in Egypt from 2008 to 2018 , by gender . According to the source , the adult mortality rate is the probability of dying between the ages of 15 and 60 -- that is , the probability of a 15-year-old dying before reaching age 60 , if subject to age-specific mortality rates of the specified year between those ages . In 2018 , the mortality rate for women was at 105.46 per 1,000 female adults , while the mortality rate for men was at 182.7 per 1,000 male adults in Egypt .
