As of November 6 , 2020 , 43 percent of hospital beds in Okinawa Prefecture reserved for coronavirus disease ( COVID-19 ) inpatients was occupied . On the same day , there were a total of around 26.9 thousand hospital beds available for inpatients with COVID-19 in the country , of which around 13 percent was in use .
