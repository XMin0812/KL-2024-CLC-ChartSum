In 2019 , Spain had the highest rate of deceased donors in Europe . The rate of organ donation , including donation after brain death ( DBD ) and after cardiac death ( DCD ) , was 49.6 per million population . Portugal had the second highest rate of deceased organ donation in Europe after increasing to a rate of 33.7 per million population . Finland experienced the largest increase in the rate of organ donation between 2018 and 2019 , going from 21.3 to 25.9 donors per million population .
