In 2019 , the total value of U.S. trade goods amounted to 4.14 trillion U.S. dollars . This shows the significance and scale of international trade for the economy of the United States . In 2019 , the United States imported goods valuing around 2.5 trillion U.S. dollars from international trading partners , in comparison the value of goods exported from the United States to other countries amounted to around 1.65 trillion U.S. dollars .
