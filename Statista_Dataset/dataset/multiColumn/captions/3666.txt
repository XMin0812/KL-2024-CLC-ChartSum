Quite a few respondents in Germany , United States and United Kingdom seem to be struggling with media 's response to COVID-19 : On May 31 , just about 37 percent of respondents in the U.S. were satisfied with the media 's response to the pandemic . What worries people the most about coronavirus ? A disease which is thought to have originated from a food market in Wuhan , China , COVID-19 has now confirmed cases in over 200 countries across the globe . The pandemic has disrupted life across 6 continents as people are unable to go to work , maintain social life , or pursue their hobbies . Different people have different concerns when it comes to the COVID-19 pandemic but when it comes to the United States , Germany and the United Kingdom , a major share of respondents are worried about their family 's health during the outbreak , as of May 2020 .
