In 2018 , Danish millennials used their smartphone to a great extent when taking care of financial activities . In detail , 67 percent of the respondents used their phone to check their account balance , while another 61 percent used it to send money domestically . However , it was more common to show up in person or use the computer when applying for a loan . In line with this , 60 percent of the respondents went to the bank to withdraw money in 2018 .
