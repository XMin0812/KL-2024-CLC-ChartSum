Nearly 37 percent of men and 25.5 percent of women surveyed in Mexico reported an increase in their consumption of pornography during the COVID-19 pandemic , as of May 2020 . Meanwhile , 35 percent of women and 23 of men surveyed in the Latin American country said they were consuming less porn during the quarantine . Nearly four out of ten respondents of both genders said that their porn consumption was the same as it was prior to the social isolation caused by the pandemic . Nearly 38 percent of Mexican participants of the survey were sharing more sexual content during the pandemic .
