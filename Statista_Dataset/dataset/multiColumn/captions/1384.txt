The graph shows the likelihood of subscribing to Disney 's new online video streaming service ( Disney+ ) among adults in the United States as of February 2019 , sorted by age group . The data reveals that 16 percent of surveyed U.S. adults aged 18 to 29 years old said that they were very likely to sign up to Disney+ upon its launch in 2019 , compared to just two percent of those aged 65 or above .
