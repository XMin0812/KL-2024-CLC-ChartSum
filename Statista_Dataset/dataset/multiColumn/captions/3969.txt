This statistic shows the price of a one day travel card for use on London public transport between 2002 and 2015 . The price for a one day travelcard remained at 10 British pounds between 2009 and 2011 before increasing to 10.60 British pounds in 2012 . The price has increased every year since . In 2016 , a one day travelcard cost 12.10 British pounds , while the Oyster pay as you go zones were capped at 9.30 British pounds in the same year .
