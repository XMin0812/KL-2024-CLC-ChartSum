More than one half of surveyed members of IAB Russia stated that the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak helped reduce costs that would not be cut otherwise in May 2020 . The share of business representatives finding an advantage in their employees being ready to work for less money increased from 3 to 35 percent from March to May 2020 .
