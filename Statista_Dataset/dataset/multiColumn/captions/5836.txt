The amount of heat pumps in operation in Ireland presented a trend of continuous growth throughout the years . Between 2013 and 2019 , total heat pumps increased by roughly 34.6 thousand heat pumps . In all years , aerothermal heat pumps were by far more common than ground source heat pumps . In 2019 , Ireland had approximately 41 thousand heat pumps in operation , of which roughly 36 thousand were aerothermal heat pumps . In the same year , the 28 European Union members had approximately 40 million heat pumps in operation .
