The sport of cricket is on a slight decline in popularity among children in England . Especially among those aged 11 to 15 , whereas in 2013 a total of 22.5 percent played on a monthly basis , in 2018 it was only 12.6 percent . The most popular sport among 11 to 15 year olds was football with 44.7 percent playing at least monthly . Among 5 to 10 year olds it was swimming , then followed by football .
