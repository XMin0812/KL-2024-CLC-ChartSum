This statistic presents the average number of vloggers watched by adult vlog viewers in the United States on a weekly basis as of November 2018 , sorted by age group . According to the findings , 32 percent of respondents aged 18 to 34 years reported that they watched 3 different vloggers each week on various social media platforms , while in comparison only 26 percent of respondents aged 35 to 54 years stated the same .
