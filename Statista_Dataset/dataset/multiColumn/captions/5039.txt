This graph shows the number of licensed taxis in England and Wales , with separate figures for London , between 1999 and 2019 . The number of taxis increased both inside and outside of London up till 2017 . Since 2018 , the number of licensed taxis has been declining . In 2019 , number of licensed taxis in London equaled 20.1 percent , whereas the total for the rest of England and Wales was 55.4 percent .
