The purpose of the present survey question was to find out the first two actions the Romanian public believe the Romanian government should take to fight the phenomenon of global warming . Of respondents , 26 percent stated that the first Governmental action should be directed towards putting an end to deforestation , while 11 percent said the first action should be regulations on traffic restrictions and encouraging alternative means of transport . The share of responses for prohibiting plastic products was only one percent for both first and second Governmental measures .
