How many airports are in the U.S. ? While the number of public use airports in the United States has fallen since 1990 , the number of private use airports has increased in recent years . In 2019 , there were 5,080 public airports in the U.S. , a slight decrease from the 5,145 public airports operating in 2014 . Conversely , the number of private airports increased over this period from 13,863 to 14,556 .
