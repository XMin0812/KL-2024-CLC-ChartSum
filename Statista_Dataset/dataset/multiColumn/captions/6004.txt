Even though the overall number of women who were victims of homicide in Brazil has decreased recently , the number of femicides — cases in which people who identify as female were killed on account of their gender — has risen . Throughout 2019 , over 1.31 thousand femicides were registered in Brazil , up from less than 1.23 thousand recorded one year before . That represents an annual increase of more than seven percent . Brazil 's femicide rate has also increased in the past few years . In 2019 , the highest number of femicides in Brazil was registered in the state of São Paulo .
