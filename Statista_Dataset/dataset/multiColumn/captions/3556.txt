This statistic presents the split of investements for non-performing loans investment on the European loan portfolio market as of 2014 and 2015 , listed by types of loans . Secured retail non-performing loans attracted a greater share of investment in 2015 , moving from being the second-least popular investment type in 2014 at 23 percent , to the second-most popular investment type in 2015 at 27 percent .
