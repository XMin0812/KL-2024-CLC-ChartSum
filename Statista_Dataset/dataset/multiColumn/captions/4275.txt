This statistic shows the number of Bonefish Grill restaurants in the United States from 2011 to 2019 , by ownership type . In 2019 , there were 190 company-owned Bonefish Grill restaurants in the U.S. , the same number as the previous year . Bonefish Grill is owned by Bloomin ' Brands , Inc. , a U.S.-based restaurant company .
