The timeline shows the quarterly dating revenue of the Match Group from the the first quarter of 2016 to the third quarter of 2020 . In the most recently measured period , North American revenue amounted to 321.8 million U.S. dollars . Match Group , formerly owned by IAC , owns and operates online dating platforms such as the eponymous Match.com , OkCupid , Tinder , PlentyofFish and others .
