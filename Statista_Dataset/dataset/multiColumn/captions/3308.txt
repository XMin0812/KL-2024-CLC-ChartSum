A recent poll shows the level of concern about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) situation around the world varies widely between countries . 62 percent of respondents in the Philippines report being extremely concerned , whereas only 8 percent of those in China feel the same way . This statistic shows the level of concern regarding the COVID-19 situation by country , worldwide as of 12 March 2020 .
