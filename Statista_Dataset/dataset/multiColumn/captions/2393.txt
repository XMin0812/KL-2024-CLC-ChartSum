As of 2019 , most of the passenger cars sold in the Netherlands were petrol fueled vehicles . 71 percent of the sales volume of passenger cars had a petrol engine , whereas diesel fueled cars had a share of 7.3 percent . Furthermore , the share of petrol cars that were sold in the Netherlands increased between 2015 and 2019 , whereas diesel cars decreased between the period from 2015 to 2019 . The total number of passenger cars sold amounted to approximately 445,000 in 2018 , an increase compared to 2017 when there were 414,306 passenger cars sold in the Netherlands .
