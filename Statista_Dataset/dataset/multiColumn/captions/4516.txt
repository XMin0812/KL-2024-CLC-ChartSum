It was estimated that around 16 percent of males and 12 percent of females in the United States who had COVID-19 from January 22 to May 30 , 2020 were hospitalized . Hospitalizations due to COVID-19 are much higher among those with underlying health conditions such as cardiovascular disease , chronic lung disease , or diabetes . This statistic shows the percentage of people in the U.S. with COVID-19 from January 22 to May 30 , 2020 who were hospitalized .
