This graph illustrates data on the ARPU in the mobile communications segment of Vodafone in Germany from the second quarter of 2011/12 to the first quarter of 2020/21 . The abbreviation ARPU stands for average revenue per user . In the first quarter of 2012/13 ( April to June , 2012 ) , Vodafone generated a mobile communications ARPU of roughly 14.9 euros per month . By the first quarter of 2020/21 this figure had decreased to 12.5 euros per month .
