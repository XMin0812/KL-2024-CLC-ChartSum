This statistic displays the youth unemployment rate ( 20 - 24 years old ) in Spain from the first quarter of 2014 to the second quarter of 2020 , by gender . In the second quarter of 2020 , the unemployment rate of females aged between 20 and 24 stood at approximately 38.2 percent , exceeding that of male professionals at the same age , which accounted for 35.8 percent .
