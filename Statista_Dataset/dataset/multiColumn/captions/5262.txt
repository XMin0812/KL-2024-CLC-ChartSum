This statistic shows the share of Canadians who multi-task on their phone while watching TV in Canada as of March 2017 , broken down by age group . The findings show that 33 percent of television viewers in Canada over the age of 18 never multi-tasked on their phone whilst watching TV , with 24 percent of viewers aged between 25 and 54 reporting that they almost always multi-tasked while they were watching TV .
