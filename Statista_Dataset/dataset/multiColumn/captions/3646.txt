Since the outbreak of the coronavirus in Denmark in March 2020 , unemployment rates increased all over the country . Between March 8 and November 22 , unemployment rates in the Capital Region of Denmark grew from 4.8 percent to 6.1 percent . In the Region of North Denmark , numbers increased from 5.7 percent to 6.2 percent during this period . The first case of COVID-19 in Denmark was confirmed on February 27 , 2020 .
