As of march 22 , 2020 , around 23 percent of Canadian adults reported feeling very satisfied with the measures set in place by the federal government to fight the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , compared to 39 percent who indicated feeling very satisfied with their own provincial government 's response . This statistic shows the proportion of Canadians satisfied with the measures put into place to fight the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic as of March 22 , 2020 .
