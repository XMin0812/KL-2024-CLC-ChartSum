Individuals working in the private sector were more concerned about the potential impact of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) on their jobs than those working in the public sector . As a result , 66 percent of respondents working in the private sector stated that the pandemic would affect their work . In comparison , only 50 percent of respondents working in the public sector reported the same concern .
