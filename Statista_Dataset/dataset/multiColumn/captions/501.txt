Abbot Laboratories is an international pharmaceutical company headquartered in Chicago , Illinois . As of 2013 , Abbott Laboratories was split into two companies : Abbot ( medical products ) and AbbVie ( research-based pharmaceuticals ) . As of 2019 , the medical device segment was the largest segment within the company and generated 12.2 billion U.S. dollars in revenues . Abbot 's diagnostics segment also generated a significant amount for the company with about 7.7 billion U.S. dollars in revenue during that year .
