The statistic depicts the revenue of pharmaceutical and chemical company Merck KGaA ( EMD ) from 2014 to 2019 , distributed by segment . During 2015 , Merck KGaA 's healthcare division generated some 6.63 billion euros of revenue . In the United States , Merck KGaA is also known as `` EMD Chemicals '' or `` German Merck '' . The company is headquartered in Darmstadt . Founded in 1668 , Merck is the oldest operating chemical and pharmaceutical company worldwide .
