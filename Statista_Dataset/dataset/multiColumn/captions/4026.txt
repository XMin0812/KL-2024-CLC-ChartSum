This statistic shows the revenue of the remote sector of the gambling industry in the United Kingdom from 2009 to 2014 . Between October 2011 and September 2012 , revenue from remote sector bingo was 33.78 million GBP . The global online gambling market was valued at 35.7 billion U.S. dollars in 2013 . This was forecasted to reach 56.1 billion U.S. dollars in 2018 .
