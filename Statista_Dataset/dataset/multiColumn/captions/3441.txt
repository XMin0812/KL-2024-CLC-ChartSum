This statistic presents the main concerns of UK football clubs ' finance directors with regards to their biggest financial hurdles and challenges over the upcoming twelve months , as of 2019 . In the original survey respondents were asked to rank from one to four their main concerns . This statistic only displays concerns that were ranked as a number one issue by surveyed directors . Relegation and increasing player salaries were reported as the most pressing financial concerns by the majority of interviewed directors . Premiere league directors that responded to the survey also showed concern with the increasing costs of player transfer fees .
