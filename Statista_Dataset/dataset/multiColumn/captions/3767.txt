Brazilian share prices have been the highest performing of the major developed and emerging economies , with an index value of 210.58 in November 2020 . Conversely , the lowest-performing were China and the United Kingdom , both with index values of 90.51 and 94.36 respectively at this time . Share prices have dropped nearly across the board due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic . The index value is calculated with 2015 values as the baseline ( i.e . 2015 = 100 ) .
