This statistic shows the rate of registrations of newly diagnosed cases of thyroid cancer per 100,000 population in England in 2018 , by region and gender . In this year , the rate of newly diagnosed cases of thyroid cancer among women was highest in Yorkshire and the Humber region of England at 10.8 cases per 100,000 population , whereas the highest rate among men was in North East at 5.5 per 100,000 population
