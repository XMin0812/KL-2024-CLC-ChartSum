This statistic shows the total number of same-sex marriages in the Netherlands from 2009 to 2019 , by gender . The statistic shows that in 2019 , 675 same-sex marriages took places between two men and 744 same-sex marriages took place between two women . Marriages between two women outnumbered marriages between two men in the last ten years , with the largest number of same-sex marriages between two women taking place last year . The total number of same-sex marriages in the Netherlands also peaked this year , with over 1,500 same-sex marriages .
