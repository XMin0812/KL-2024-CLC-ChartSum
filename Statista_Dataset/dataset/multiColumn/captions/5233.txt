This statistic displays the number of stores of the Spanish group Inditex in France as of January 2016 , 2017 and 2018 , by brand . Inditex , one of the biggest fashion group in the world , also spread all over France . Between 2016 et 2018 the number of stores of Inditex in France remained stable growing from 292 stores the first year to 298 in 2018 . There were also around 40 Pull & Bear stores in France that same year .
