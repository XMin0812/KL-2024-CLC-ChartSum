This statistic displays the past , present and future ( predicted ) market demand value for raw materials used in medical disposables worldwide from 2005 to 2025 , by product group . In 2005 , paper and paperboard had a demand valued at 179 million U.S. dollars , while nonwoven fabrics , had a demand valued at 1,36 billion dollars . The United States is the largest market for disposable medical products . The raw materials market is forecasted to increase at an annual rate of 5.3 percent until 2020 .
