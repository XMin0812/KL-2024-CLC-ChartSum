Since 2002 there has been a significant increase in the number of hospital admissions for obesity in England . In 2018/19 , there were 8,237 hospital admissions of women with a primary diagnosis of obesity and 2,880 hospital admissions for men , although both genders had their peak number of admissions in 2011/12 . The highest number of admissions due to obesity were found in the age group 45 to 54 years , with almost 3.2 thousand admissions .
