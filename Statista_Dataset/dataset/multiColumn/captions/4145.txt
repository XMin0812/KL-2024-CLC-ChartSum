According to a survey carried out in Chile in March 2020 , the main problem faced by the industrial and services sectors in the Santiago Metropolitan Region during the COVID-19 pandemic was the decrease in demand and sales . Up to 81 percent of the industrial companies and 76 percent of the services providers surveyed suffered a drop in sales . Businesses in the industry sector were specially affected by supply chain disruptions , with 65 percent of respondents reporting such difficulties . On the other hand , four out of ten service providers had to close their offices .
