This statistic depicts the import value of chewing gum into the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2004 to 2018 , by European Union and non-European Union trade . In 2018 , over 7.5 million British pounds worth of chewing gum was imported into the United Kingdom from countries within the European Union , which has decreased throughout the period under consideration .
