The statistic shows the results of a survey among users on the tastebuds.fm website regarding their biggest musical turnoffs in 2011 , sorted by gender . Tastebuds.fm is an online dating site that matches users with people who share their taste in music . The total of all respondents voted Nickelback their biggest musical turnoff in 2011 , who received 11.8 percent of the men 's vote and 14 percent of the women 's vote respectively .
