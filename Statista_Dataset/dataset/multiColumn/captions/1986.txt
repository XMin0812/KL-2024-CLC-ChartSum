This statistic shows the occupancy rate in workstations of International Workplace Group ( IWG ) , formerly Regus , worldwide from 2012 to 2019 , by region . In 2019 , the occupancy rate of IWG 's workstations in the Americas stood at 74.8 percent . In 2016 , Regus changed its holding company to IWG but hold the Regus name as their brand .
