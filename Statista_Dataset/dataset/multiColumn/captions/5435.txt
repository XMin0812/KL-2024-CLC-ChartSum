This statistic shows the results of a survey on TV show binge watching habits among Generation X and Baby Boomers in the United Kingdom ( UK ) as of December 2016 . Respondents were asked how many episodes of a TV box set that they loved they could watch in one sitting while still enjoying it . The survey found that 15 percent of respondents aged between 40 and 59 years stated that they could watch four one-hour episodes of the same television show without losing interest .
