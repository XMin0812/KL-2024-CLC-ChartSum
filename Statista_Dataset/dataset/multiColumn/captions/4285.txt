In April 2020 , a survey of healthcare workers in the United Kingdom ( UK ) found that majority are worried about their personal health as well as the health of those they live with during the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak . 28 percent of healthcare workers reported to be very worried about their personal health , while 37 percent were very worried about the health of those in their household .
