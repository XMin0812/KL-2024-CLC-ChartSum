The situation of companies in the hotel and catering market in Poland is gradually improving . In July 2020 , the number of respondents for whom the COVID-19 pandemic posed a threat to their companies ' operations decreased . Nevertheless , almost every second company in the accommodation and catering sector felt minor negative effects of COVID-19 in their business operations .
