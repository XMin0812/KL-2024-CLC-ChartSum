The population of Iceland was forecasted to reach approximately 387 thousand by 2030 . Within the given time frame from 2019 to 2030 , the group of 60 to 79 year olds was expected to increase by over 17 thousand individuals . In 2019 , about 16 percent of the population belonged to this age group . By 2030 , close to 20 percent of the population was expected to belong to this age group . In other words , the average age of the Icelandic population was expected to keep increasing .
