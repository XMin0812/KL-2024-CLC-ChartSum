In May 2019 , there were 1,560 full-time independent musicians working in the United States , up from 1,290 in the previous year . Unfortunately , 2020 will be a difficult year for independent artists , with the coronavirus having led to canceled tours and postponed releases , although many musicians around the world improvised by streaming content to their fans via social media or uploading music to YouTube . Whilst this can not necessarily make up for lost revenue , smaller artists have worked hard to raise or maintain their profile during the unprecedented crisis .
