The political affiliation of Americans strongly determined their attitudes to whether Donald Trump is influenced by FOX News , as a recent survey found 54 percent of Democrats believed the President is influenced a lot by the news channel compared to 30 percent of total American adults . This figure further decreases to just 10 percent of Republicans who share the same view . Republicans were shown to have a much greater trust in Trump , as 61 percent responded that FOX News has no influence at all on the president 's decision making .
