In the second quarter of fiscal year 2020 , Kontoor Brands ' Lee brand 's global revenue decreased by 58 percent , when compared to the same period in the previous year . Kontoor Brands , Inc. is a global lifestyle apparel company with a portfolio of some of the world 's most iconic denim brands : Wrangler and Lee . Kontoor Brands was created as part of a strategic repositioning campaign by the VF Corporation . As part of this campaign VF 's Jeanswear and Outlet businesses were transferred to Kontoor Brands .
