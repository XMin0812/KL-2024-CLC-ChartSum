This statistic shows the prospects of investment and development in the healthcare property real estate market in Europe from 2018 to 2020 according to industry experts . According to real estate experts , both investment ( 4.21 ) and development prospects ( 4.09 ) in 2020 improved for healthcare properties in Europe as compared to 2018 and 2019 . The sectors with highest prospect scores in 2020 were retirement/assisted living , logistic facilities and co-living .
