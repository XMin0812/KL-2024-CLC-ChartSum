This statistic presents the number of new million dollar app publishers in the United States in 2017 and 2018 , by platform . In 2017 , Apple 's App Store had 143 new app publishers reaching a net revenue of one million U.S. dollars or greater . In the following year , this amount was higher than the previous year 's , with a new total of 164 app publishers reaching similar status . In comparison to Google Play 's app store , there was a total of only 88 new app publishers in 2018 who reached a net revenue amount of one million U.S. dollars or more .
