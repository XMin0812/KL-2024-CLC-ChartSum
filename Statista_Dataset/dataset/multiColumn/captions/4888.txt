This statistic displays the results of a survey on the hours of internet use per week per person in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2005 to 2019 , by location . In 2013 , the average internet user spent four hours per week using the internet at a workplace or school . This number increased to 6.7 hours per week in 2018 . Time spent online has increased over the years , now reaching over 24 hours a week with the majority of hours being spent online at home .
