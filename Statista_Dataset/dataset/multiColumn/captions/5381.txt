The share of children who play golf , putting or pitch and putt on a regular basis in England is slightly decreasing . While in 2014 , around 7.4 percent of 11 to 15 year olds had been golfing in the past four weeks of being surveyed , it was less than half as of 2018 , when only 3.6 percent confirmed this . The surveys is conducted by Sport England , the national sports governing body of England on an annual basis to determine children 's sport participation and preferences .
