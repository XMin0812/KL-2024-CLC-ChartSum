This statistic shows the results of a 2018 survey which asked people in Great Britain if they agreed with statements about Donald Trump , divided by how they voted in the Brexit vote in 2016 . The majority of both leave and remain voters agreed that Trump makes the world a more dangerous place , with 81 percent of remain voters agreeing with this statement . Of those who voted leave in the EU referendum , 51 percent agreed that Trump would make a success of Brexit .
