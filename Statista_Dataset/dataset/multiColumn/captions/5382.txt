This statistic presents the share of children who recently participated in horseback riding in England , according to annual surveys conducted between 2010/11 and 2017/18 , by age group . Over this period , the proportion of children taking part in horse riding has been relatively constant . In the period 2017/18 , 3.7 percent of the children aged between 11 and 15 had participated in horse riding or pony trekking in the 4 weeks prior to the survey .
