This statistic displays the number of Ebola cases related to the outbreak in West Africa that lasts since 2014 . Until March 30 , 2016 , there have been 3,351 confirmed cases reported in Guinea . The Ebola virus causes extremely severe hemorrhagic fever and is considered a Risk Group 4 Pathogen by the World Health Organization ( WHO ) . The health sector will focus on cross-border regions to strengthen treatment , testing , and contact tracing .
