Between 2009 and 2019 , men 's average age at marriage was always higher than the average age of women in Denmark . In 2019 , it peaked at 38.8 years on average for men and 36.2 years for women . The number of marriages in Denmark was highest in 2009 with nealy 33 thousand marriages . In the following ten years , the number fluctuated but decreased overall . As of 2019 , there were roughly 30.6 thousand marriages registered .
