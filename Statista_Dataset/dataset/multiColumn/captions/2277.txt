In 2019 , Spain had the highest rate of kidney transplant procedures at 73.8 per million population . This was a slight increase from 2018 , when the rate was 71.4 per million . France followed in 2019 with the second highest rate at 55.6 kidney transplants per million . Finland had the largest rate increase between the years increasing from 43.3 to 52.3 per million population .
