The breakdown by age and gender in the Czech Republic shows that the highest incidence of coronaviruses has been recorded among women . The most vulnerable to COVID-19 infection are people in the age group from 25 to 54 years . The first cases of coronavirus infection in the Czech Republic were reported on 1 March . On 20 November 2020 , the Czech Republic reported 481,755 confirmed cases of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) .
