This statistic displays the value of cane and beet sugar imports into the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2004 to 2018 , split between European Union and non-European Union trade . The value of cane and beet sugars imported into the UK has increased in value since 2004 but decreased following 2013 . In 2018 , there were 156.7 million British pounds ( GBP ) worth of cane or beet sugar imported into the UK from non EU countries .
