In May 2020 , a survey carried out in the United Kingdom found that five percent of Brits had been frequently visiting friends they do n't live with during the coronavirus lockdown period , while five percent also say they have been regularly visiting family during this period . The government recommends that during the lockdown period people should only go outside for one form of exercise a day , but eight percent of survey respondents said they periodically go out for more than once for exercise .
