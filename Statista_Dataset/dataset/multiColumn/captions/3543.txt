Most of the patients active on the organ transplant waiting list in Denmark from 2015 to 2019 needed a new kidney . These patients amounted to 852 in 2019 , a lower number than in two previous years . The number of patients refers to the total number of people that were ever active on the waiting list during the year . The number of patients who received a new kidney in Denmark during 2019 amounted to 276 .
