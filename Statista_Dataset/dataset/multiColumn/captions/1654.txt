Kidneys were the most common organ that patients for a transplant were waiting for in 2019 , according to recent studies . Approximately 7.4 thousand patients were on the organ transplant waiting list for a kidney , followed by far by patients that were on the list for a liver , who amounted to slightly under 1.8 thousand . In 2019 , the latest figures reveal that 47 patients died while they were waiting for their liver transplant .
