This statistic displays the market share of the mobile data traffic in Serbia from 2012 to 2015 . Telenor was the market leader in terms of the mobile data traffic for the given time period . Telenor 's market share increased from 45.2 percent in 2012 to 49 percent in 2015 . Telekom Srbija increased its market share by approximately 8 percent over the given time period . Only VIP experienced a decrease .
