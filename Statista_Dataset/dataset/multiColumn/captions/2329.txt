This statistics shows the opinion of Americans and Germans on the free trade agreement between the U.S. and the EU which is known as the Transatlantic Trade and Investment Partnership ( TTIP ) . In 2015 , around 21 % of Americans and 36 % of Germans thought that TTIP would be a bad thing for their country . Out of those , 61 % of Germans feared that TTIP would lower the food , environment and auto safety standards .
