This statistic deals with soda consumption habits among different ethnic groups in the United States in 2013 . The results were derived from a survey conducted by Gallup via a telephone interview . American adults were polled as to whether they mostly drink diet soda , regular soda or do not drink soda at all . Some 27 percent of Whites in the United States reported to consume mostly regular soda compared to 46 percent of Nonwhites .
