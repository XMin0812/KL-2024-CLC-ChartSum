This statistic shows the marriage rate in Great Britain from 2000 to 2011 . Marriage rate is defined as the amount of people marrying per 1,000 unmarried people in the population aged 16 and over . The trend is downward , with an overall fall of 7.8 . There were two periods of increase though . Most notably from 2001 to 2003 when the rate increased by 1.1 .
