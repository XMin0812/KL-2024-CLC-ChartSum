This statistic shows a forecast for selected new nickel operations and projects worldwide in 2015 . By that year , the Koniambo mine in New Caledonia is expected to be one of the largest new projects/operations . The mine is held by mining company Xstrata , and is expected to have nickel production of some 50,000 metric tons annually , while the targeted capacity for this mine is approximately 60,000 metric tons .
