The statistic shows the result of the Survey Stili d'Italia conducted by Italiani.Coop from May to June 2018 , broken down by gender . When Italians were asked if they usually have multiple sex partners , five percent of male respondents claimed to often have more than one sex partner , while eleven percent declared to have this experience only sometimes . On the other hand , four percent of female respondents claimed to usually have sex with more than one partner , while eight percent only sometimes do that .
