As of 2018 , about 12.5 percent of 11 to 15 year old and 7.8 percent of 5 to 10 year old children in England stated that they played tennis within the last month of a survey , taken annually by the Taking Part : Annuals Report on Child Behavior . Both age groups have shown a slight decline in recent years and also adult tennis participation looks similar as it dropped from close to 890 thousand regular players in 2016 to roughly 840 thousand players in 2018 .
