This statistic displays the percentage of persons in the U.S. who had binge used alcohol in the past month among persons aged 18 to 25 years , by gender , from 2002 to 2019 . In 2019 , 34.8 percent of males between 18 and 25 year claimed to have binged on alcohol in the past month . Alcoholism refers to problems with alcohol and often refers to alcohol addiction . Alcohol has the potential to cause damage to all organs of the body .
