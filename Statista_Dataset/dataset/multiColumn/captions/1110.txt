This statistic shows number of total and repeat Kickstarter crowdfunding project backers as of November 19 , 2020 . As of that month , close to 19 million people had pledged their monetary support towards any of the ove 500,000 projects launched on Kickstarter ( successfully completed or not ) . Also , over 6.3 million people had backed more than one project on the crowdfunding site . From July 2012 to November 2020 , a total of nearly 5.5 billion U.S. dollars had been pledged to projects on the Kickstarter crowdfunding platform .
