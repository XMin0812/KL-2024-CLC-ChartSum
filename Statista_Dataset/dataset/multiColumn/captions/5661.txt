When asked how far in advance they booked their last main holiday , the majority of participants from the United Kingdom ( UK ) said they booked over a month in advance . According to the 2019 survey , the largest proportion of respondents booking less than a month ahead , at 18 percent , were aged over 65 . In contrast , 25 to 34 year olds represented the largest share of people booking more than 12 months in advance ( 14 percent ) .
