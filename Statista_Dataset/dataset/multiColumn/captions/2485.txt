This statistic shows the life expectancy at birth in the Netherlands from 1950 to 2018 , by gender . It reveals that women on average were expected to live longer than men , and that the life expectancy in the Netherlands has increased by roughly ten years between 1950 and 2018 ; from 70.3 years to 80.2 years for men and from 72.6 years to 83.3 years for women .
