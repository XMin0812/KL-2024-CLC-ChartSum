Coffee growers raise two species of coffee bean : Arabica and robusta . The former is more expensive , selling for 2.93 U.S. dollars per kilogram in 2018 and projected to increase in price to 3.37 U.S. dollars in 2025 . Robusta , named because it can grow at a wider range of altitudes and temperatures , sold for 1.87 U.S. dollars in 2018 , projected to sell at 1.73 U.S. dollars per kilogram in 2025 .
