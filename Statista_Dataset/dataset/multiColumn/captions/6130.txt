Hawaii has taken part in 16 U.S presidential elections , and has sided with the winning candidate in ten of these , giving a success rate of 63 percent . Hawaii has voted for the Democratic nominee in 14 elections , and the only times where the Republican nominees won were in 1972 and 1984 , where Richard Nixon and Ronald Reagan respectively won re-election in landslide victories . In the 2020 election , Joe Biden carried the state with a near thirty percent margin in the popular vote .
