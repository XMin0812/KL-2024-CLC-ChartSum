This statistic shows the results of a survey on TV show binge watching habits among Millennials in the United Kingdom ( UK ) as of December 2016 . Respondents were asked how many episodes of a TV box set that they loved they could watch in one sitting while still enjoying it . The survey found that 15 percent of respondents aged between 18 and 24 years stated that they could watch more than 10 one-hour episodes of the same television show without losing interest .
