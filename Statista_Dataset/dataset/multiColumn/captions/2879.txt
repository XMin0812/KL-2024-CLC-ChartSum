This statistic shows the number of politically motivated crimes with a right-wing or left-wing extremist background in Germany in 2019 , by type . That year , there were 781 cases of bodily harm recorded which were motivated by right-wing extremism , and 355 motivated by left-wing extremism . According to the source , criminal offences with an extremist background aim to eliminate or make inoperative certain constitutional principles which are essential for a liberal democratic order .
