In April 2020 , a survey carried out in the United Kingdom found that since the lockdown was imposed due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , 37 percent of respondents aged between 18 and 24 years have experienced feeling lonely a little more than usual , while a further 15 percent said they experienced feeling lonely a lot more than usual .
