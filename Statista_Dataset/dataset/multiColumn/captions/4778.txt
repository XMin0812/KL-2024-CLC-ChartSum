A U.S. poll shows that , although the percentage of respondents who believe the worst of the COVID-19 outbreak in the U.S. is yet to come has decreased from March to April , the majority still feel this way . This statistic shows the percentage of U.S. respondents who feel that the worst of the COVID-19 outbreak in the U.S. is behind or yet to come , from March 25 to April 20 , 2020 .
