This survey represents the top mobile apps in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in 2017 according to gender . The most popular app is Facebook , 28 percent of men claim it is on of the three apps they can least do without and 37 percent of women claim so . It is followed by WhatsApp , which is essential for 18 percent of the men and 20 percent of the women . Messenger , the Facebook instant messenger app is important for 9 percent of the men and 12 percent of the women .
