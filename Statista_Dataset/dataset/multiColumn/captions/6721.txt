This statistic displays the incidence rate of acute myocardial infarction , referred to as a heart attack , per 100,000 population in Scotland from 2004/05 to 2018/19 , by gender . In 2018/19 the incidence rate for men was 269.2 per 100,000 population and for women the incidence rate was 153.8 per 100,000 population .
