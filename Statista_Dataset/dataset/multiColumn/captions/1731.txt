This statistic illustrates the number of passenger cars produced in the United Kingdom ( UK ) , categorized by the five leading brands , from 2014 to 2019 . From 2016 onwards , Jaguar Land Rover was the leading brand in terms of automotive manufacturing . In 2019 , the brand saw a decrease of approximately 15.3 percent in the production compared to 2019 . Despite the fall in 2019 , Jaguar Land Rover produced more units than BMW and Toyota combined . Toyota was the only brand shown to increase output in 2019 .
