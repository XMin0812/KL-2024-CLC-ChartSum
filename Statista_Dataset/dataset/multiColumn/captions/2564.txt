This statistic presents the share of children influenced in a way by the 2012 London Olympic Games in England , according to annual surveys conducted in 2015/16 . In that period , 60 percent of the children aged 5 to 10 became more interested in sports in general under the influence of the 2012 London Olympics . Further information about sports in the United Kingdom can also be found in the Dossier : Sport in England - Public funding and participation .
