As of December 17 , 2020 , confirmed coronavirus cases in Denmark were mostly women aged 20 to 29 years old . Indeed , 12,328 women aged 20 to 29 years old were infected by the coronavirus . This was also the age group with the highest number of confirmed cases among men . The first case of the coronavirus in Denmark was confirmed on February 27 , 2020 . The number of cases in Denmark increased significantly at the beginning of March . By December 17 , the number of confirmed cases in the country had reached a total of 123,813 .
