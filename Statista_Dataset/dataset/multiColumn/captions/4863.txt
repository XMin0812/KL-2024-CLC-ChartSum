The statistic shows the results of surveys conducted monthly from May 2014 to August 2017 in Norway on opinions regarding the politician best suitable as Prime Minister of Norway . Jonas Gahr Støre is the leader of the Labour Party ( Arbeiderpartiet ) and Erna Solberg is leading the Center Party ( Høyre ) . Since the election of 2013 , Erna Solberg is as well the Prime Minister of Norway . In the 34th week of August 2017 , 46.8 percent of the respondents considered Erna Solberg as the most suitable for the role of the upcoming Prime Minister of Norway . The corresponding share for Jonas Gahr Støre was 36.8 percent . Elections of the Norwegian Parliament ( Storting ) are held every four years . The next parliamentary election is set for September 11 , 2017 .
