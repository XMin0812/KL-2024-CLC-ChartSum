This statistic displays the U.S. cities with the most pest infestations in 2015 . In that year approximately , 38 percent of homes in Houston , Texas had cockroaches , while 8 percent had rats . A city 's climate greatly influences how vulnerable it is to pest infestations . Cities in warmer regions face more infestations from insects as the warm climate allows them to live longer than they would in colder climates .
