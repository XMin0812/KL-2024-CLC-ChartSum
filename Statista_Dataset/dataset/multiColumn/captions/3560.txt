This statistic shows spending predictions over the course of the Black Friday weekend ( from Friday to Cyber Monday ) in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2016 to 2020 , broken down by online and offline spending . Consumers are expected to spend a total of 7.5 billion British pounds over the course of the Black Friday weekend in 2020 , 5.76 billion of which will be spent online . Although Friday is the main day of the event , according to the forecast most offline spending will occur on the Saturday and Sunday .
