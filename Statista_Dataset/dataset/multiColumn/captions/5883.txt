This statistic displays the results of a survey on the frequency with which individuals exercise or play sport in the European Union ( EU ) in 2017 , characterised by employment . The survey was conducted via face-to-face interviews and gathered data from 28,031 respondents across Europe , in December 2017 . Within this survey there is a clear distinction between the frequency with which individuals from different employment categories exercise or play sport . The employment categories that most frequently exercise or participate in sports are the students , with only 16 percent suggesting that they never participate .
