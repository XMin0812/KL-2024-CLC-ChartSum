In 2019 , vocational education and training was the highest education completed among the Danish population . In detail , 32.6 percent of males and 26.4 percent of females had undertaken this kind of education . The second most common educational level for both genders was primary education , which consists of ten years of compulsary education in Denmark . Generally , a larger share of women had completed higher educations of various types , but more males held a doctorate degree .
