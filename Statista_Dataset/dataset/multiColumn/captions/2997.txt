This statistic shows the sales revenue at retail of wearable technology devices in four European countries in 2013 and with a forecast for 2014 . The countries include the United Kingdom , Germany , Spain and The Netherlands . The wearables market in the UK ranked as the second highest after Germany , with sales revenues reaching 140.67 million euros ( 111.55 million British pounds ) in 2013 . The UK market is expected to be worth 395.45 million euros ( 313.6 million British pounds ) in 2014 .
