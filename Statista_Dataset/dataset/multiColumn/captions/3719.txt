A recent worldwide survey showed that respondents in China were more likely to take extra measures to protect themselves from coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) as compared to the U.S. and U.K. as of March 1 , 2020 . This statistic shows the percentage of respondents in the U.S. , China , and the UK who have taken extra measures in the past two weeks specifically to protect themselves from COVID-19 as of March 1 , 2020 .
