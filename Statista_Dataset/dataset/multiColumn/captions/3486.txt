This statistic illustrates the findings of a survey among internet users regarding the creation of profiles on social networking platforms in the United Kingdom ( UK ) between 2010 and 2019 , broken down by socio-economic group . Over the period in consideration , profile creation increased among all socio-economic group . During the survey in 2019 , it was revealed that 78 percent of responding individuals from social grade C1 reported setting up their personal profile on a social networking platform .
