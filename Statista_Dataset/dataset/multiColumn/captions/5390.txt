The timeline shows the share of households with access to subscription video on demand ( SVOD ) in the United States in June 2018 and June 2019 , broken down by ethnicity . The findings revealed that the share of Hispanic households in the United States with subscription video on demand access increased by six percent in the space of a year , and the share of Black households with SVOD access grew from 58 to 65 percent in the same time period .
