India reported over 1,400 cases of swine flu , also known as influenza A ( H1N1 ) between January and March 2020 , with 28 deaths . The country saw an outbreak of this disease in 2015 , recording over 42 thousand cases and 2,990 deaths . The influenza A ( H1N1 ) strain of virus was first recognized in 1919 but was declared as a global pandemic by the World Health Organization in 2009 , lasting until August 2010 across the world .
