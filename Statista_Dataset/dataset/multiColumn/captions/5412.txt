During the week of February 25 to March 2 , 2020 , the average ticket in general merchandisers and grocers was 53 U.S. dollars . This represented an increase over the average transaction value in prior weeks of 46 U.S. dollars in general merchandisers and 49 U.S. dollars in grocers .
