This statistic displays the gross transaction value ( overall sales including those from concession brands ) of Debenhams from financial year 2013 to 2018 , split by UK and international sales . UK sales outweighed those from international operations , valuing at approximately 2.28 billion British pounds in the year ending September 1st , 2018 . Debenhams is a British retailer selling clothing , furniture and other household items in a department store format . The brand owns and operates stores in the UK , Ireland and Denmark as well as franchises in other international locations .
