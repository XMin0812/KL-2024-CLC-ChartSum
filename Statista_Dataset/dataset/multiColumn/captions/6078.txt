This statistic presents client opinions on the possibility of usage of 'Pure Digital ' bank as a new type of financial services provider - by age group , in the United Kingdom ( UK ) , July 2014 . Negative reactions to the new banking option outnumbered positive ones for age brackets : 18 - 24 years old customers and 35+ years old customers , in both categories in ratio of 2:3 . The age bracket of 25 - 34 reacted to this possibility in a more positive way , with 33 percent of respondents being in favour of this banking possibility , and 18 percent being rather against .
