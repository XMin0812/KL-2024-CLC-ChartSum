Augmented reality ( AR ) and virtual reality ( VR ) are emerging technologies in the healthcare industry . AR and VR could be used in a variety of settings including physician training , patient treatments and hospital management . As of 2018 , the North American healthcare AR and VR industry was valued at 477 million U.S. dollars . By 2028 , that is expected to increase dramatically to 4.64 billion U.S. dollars .
