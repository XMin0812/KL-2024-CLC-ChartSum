This statistic contains Warc 's forecast of global advertising spending growth in 2016 and 2017 , broken down by medium . Warc expects the cinema ad market to grow by 3.2 percent in 2017 compared to 2016 . The growth rate for the entire ad market is projected to amount to 4.2 percent . Among major economies , India 's ad spend will grow the fastest in 2017 ( 13.4 percent on 2016 ) , followed by China ( 7.1 percent ) and Russia ( 6.1 percent ) .
