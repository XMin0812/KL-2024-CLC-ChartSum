This statistic shows the employment rate in Belgium from 2008 to 2019 , by age . In 2019 , the employment rate for people between 15 and 24 years old was 26.6 percent , for people between 25 and 49 years old it was 81.2 percent , and for people between 50 and 64 years old it was 61.2 percent . Over the most recent years , regardless of age , the employment rate increased in Belgium .
