This statistic illustrates the share of female employees at the United Kingdom ( UK ) based Nationwide Building Society from 2015 to 2020 . It can be seen that in total , 62 percent of Nationwide 's employees were female as of 2020 . The share of females senior managers for the Nationwide Building Society increased during the period under observation , reaching a share of 36 percent in 2019 followed by a decrease to 30 percent in 2020 .
