This statistic depicts the value of the global aesthetic medical and surgical market from 2012 to 2018 , by segment . In 2013 , the aesthetic medical and surgical market dedicated to body contouring and energy devices was valued at some 1.5 billion euros , and it was estimated to grow up to 2.3 billion until 2018 . Most of the world including the United States , Latin America , and Asia-Pacific regions are expected to maintain growth in the cosmetic surgical and aesthetic medical market . However , there has been a decrease in Europe , especially in Southern Europe .
