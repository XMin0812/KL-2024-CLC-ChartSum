Finland had the highest rate of pancreas transplants in 2019 , at seven per million population . This was an increase from 2018 , when the rate was 4.2 per million . Czechia followed with the second highest rate in 2019 at 3.9 pancreas transplants per million . Pancreas transplant procedures are used to treat individuals with diabetes by replacing their pancreas with one that can produce insulin from deceased donor .
