This statistic shows the medical cost savings in remote patient monitoring ( RPM ) due to internet of health things ( IoHT ) services in U.S. , by value , according to a survey conducted among health care providers and payers between 29th January 2016 and 16th February 2016 . Almost 52 percent of the providers said that medical cost savings were moderate whereas 42 percent of the payers said it was extensive as per the survey in the U.S. as of 2016 .
