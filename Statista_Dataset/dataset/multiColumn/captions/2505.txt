How has the coronavirus epidemic affected the sales of B2B marketing agencies compared to B2C companies in Denmark ? In general , a higher share of B2C marketing companies experienced a decline in sales than the share of B2B marketing companies did . Around 42 percent of the surveyed CMOs and marketing managers in B2C agencies indicated a major sales decrease due to the coronavirus epidemic . By contrast , the share of respondents who experienced the same was roughly 38 percent for B2B companies . The first case of the coronavirus in Denmark was confirmed on February 27 , 2020 .
