This statistic presents the share of children who had recently participated in dodgeball in England , as of annual surveys conducted between 2010/11 and 2017/18 , by age . In the period 2015/16 , around 27.5 percent of the children aged between 11 and 15 had participated in dodgeball in the 4 weeks prior to the survey . Since then , the share of dodgeball participation has dropped , reaching 20.5 percent in 2017/2018 .
