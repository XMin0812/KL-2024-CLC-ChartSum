This statistic shows the percentage of EAL ( English as an additional language ) pupils at primary and secondary schools in England from 1997 to 2018 as a share of the total number of primary aged pupils . Over this 21 year period , the number of EAL pupils increased from 7.8 percent to 21.2 percent for primary school students and from 7.3 to 16.6 for secondary school ones .
