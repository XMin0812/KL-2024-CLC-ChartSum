This statistic presents the frequency of use of various banking channels in United Kingdom from 2011 to 2015 . Over time , internet ( online ) banking proved to be the dominant channel used by clients . It translated to approximately 80 percent of users over the years , notably though online banking use diminished in 2015 to 74 percent . It could be connected to the growth of mobile banking use , where banking is done `` on the go '' on mobile devices . This channel use noted a significant growth , from 10 percent in 2011 to 34 percent in 2015 . Branch banking vacillated between 43 and 52 percent , also noting a growth , which is an interesting outcome in the context of many banking branches of the leading British banks closing down .
