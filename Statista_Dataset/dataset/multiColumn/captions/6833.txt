The coronavirus pandemic would have a significant impact on businesses in India according to a CIO survey carried out twice in March 2020 . On a scale from one ( indicating no impact ) to ten ( disastrous impact ) , around 46 percent of respondents ranked the impact of the coronavirus between eight and ten on the scale between March 23 and 25 , 2020 . This is a significant jump from just under 20 percent in early March , when the first edition of the poll was carried out .
