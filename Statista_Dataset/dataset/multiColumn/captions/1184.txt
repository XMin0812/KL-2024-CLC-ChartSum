This survey shows the opinion on legalization of same-sex marriages in the United States from 1996 to 2020 . In 1996 , 27 percent of respondents agreed that same-sex marriages should be recognized by law as valid with the same rights as traditional marriages , while 68 percent of respondents opposed this notion . In 2020 , 67 percent state that same-sex marriages should be recognized as valid , while only 31 percent do not agree .
