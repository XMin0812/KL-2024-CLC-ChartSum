In 2020 , three banks from the Netherlands made it into a top 100 of most valuable banking brands in the world . That year , the brand value of ING was estimated at approximately 11.3 billion U.S. dollars . Rabobank saw a two-year decrease between 2016 and 2018 , whilst ABN AMRO 's brand value continued to increase until 2019 . These three Dutch banks were also the leading banks of the country , based on their total assets .
