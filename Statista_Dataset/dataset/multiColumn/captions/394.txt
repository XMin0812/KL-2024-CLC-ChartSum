BMW cars were the most expensive automobiles sold in the EU in 2018 . With an average price tag of 48,185 euros , the car maker ranked ahead of fellow German manufacturer Mercedes-Benz . Dacia and Citroen both saw average sales prices drop between 2014 and 2018 . Mercedes-Benz is part of the Daimler AG and was the leading luxury car brand by global sales in 2018 .
