This graph depicts the net sales by product type of the sporting goods company Cybex from 2009 to 2011 . In 2009 , sales of cardiovascular products reached 66.7 million U.S. dollars . Cybex is a fitness equipment manufacturer for commercial and consumer use from the U.S. Cybex mainly produces cardiovascular and strength equipment such as arc trainers , treadmills , stationary bicycles and steppers .
