This statistic reveals the level of concern of French people linked to the spread of the COVID-19 coronavirus from January to April 2020 . On March 25 , a vast majority of French people ( 85 percent ) declared themselves worried about the spread of this virus in their country . Although the data is changing every day , over 140,000 people with COVID-19 have currently been listed in France on May 19 , 2020 .
