This statistic displays the economic contribution of the high-end fashion manufacturing sector in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in 2013 and a forecast thereof for 2018 . Contribution is represented as the total annual turnover of the sector and the gross value added ( GVA ) of the sector to the UK economy . In 2013 the sector generated revenues of 1.76 billion British pounds and contributed around 732 million in GVA . Turnover is predicted to rise to over 2 billion British pounds by 2018 , with a GVA of 854 million .
