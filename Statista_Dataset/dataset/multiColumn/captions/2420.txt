Due to the impact of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) on the Chinese and world economy , business and government purchases for technology goods and services in China are predicted to drop between two and six percent in the first quarter of 2020 . However , tech spending in China is expected to resume growth by the second quarter , ultimately growing by 2.3 to 4.5 percent in the full year of 2020 .
