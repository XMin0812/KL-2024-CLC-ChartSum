This statistic displays the common side effects of sleeping pills used for insomnia , Ambien and Lunesta , as of 2012 . As of that year , dizziness was experienced in 5 % of both Ambien and Lunesta users . Newer sleep disorder medications are less likely to cause dependence or abuse problems . Ambien became available to the public in 1992 as a remedy for insomnia . Headaches and dizziness are the most common side effects in these insomnia drugs .
