Black men and women in the United Kingdom were four times more likely to die from Coronavirus than white people of the same gender as of April 2020 . Several other ethnic groups were also at an increased risk from Coronavirus than the white population , with men of Bangladeshi or Pakistani origin 3.6 times more likely , and women 3.4 more likely to die from Coronavirus .
