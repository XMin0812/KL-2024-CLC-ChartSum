The statistic shows the range of sustainable and responsible investment ( SRI ) strategies biannually in Europe in 2011 , 2013 , 2015 , and 2017 . Approximately 9.5 trillion euros were invested with application of the exclusion strategy in 2017 , making it the dominant strategy this year . Exclusion-led investment strategies grew exponentially from the investment level of 3.6 trillion euros in 2011 .
