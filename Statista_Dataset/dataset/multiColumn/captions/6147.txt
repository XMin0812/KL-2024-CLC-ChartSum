This statistic shows the spending expectations of consumers on Christmas in Great Britain in 2014 , by product category . Categories include food and drink for consumption at Christmas , Christmas cards , Christmas tree and decorations and Christmas gifts for friends and family . Generally most survey respondents expect to spend the same as last year . Only 12 percent expect to spend a little or a lot more on food and drink , compared to 13 percent who expect to spend a little or a lot less in 2014 compared to Christmas 2013 .
