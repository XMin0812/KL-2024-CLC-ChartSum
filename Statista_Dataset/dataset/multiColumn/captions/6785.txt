This statistic illustrates the average weekly time spent gaming in the United Kingdom from 2012 to 2015 , broken down by gender ( inclusive weekends and weekdays ) . On average , the volume of gaming reported by female respondents was 3.5 hours in 2013 , and 4.9 hours in 2015 . Statista provides a curated dossier of statistics covering the video game market in the United Kingdom .
