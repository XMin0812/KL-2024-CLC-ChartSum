This graph lays out the compound annual growth rate ( CAGR ) of the inorganic chemical industry from 2009 to 2015 , by type of inorganic chemicals . Chemicals other than those listed had the highest CAGR of this industry from 2009 to 2012 , with over 6 percent . It has been predicted that this will change in the period 2012 - 2015 , with this segment receiving a CAGR of estimated 3.5 percent .
