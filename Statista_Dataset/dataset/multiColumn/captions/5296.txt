This statistic shows the content of THC and CBD in medical marijuana concentrates that are typically sold at dispensaries in Oregon , as of 2016 . Gorilla Glue # 4 , a shatter , had the highest percentage content of THC with 87.6 percent , while EP Honey Oil , an oil , had the highest content of CBD with 1.31 percent . CBD is especially interesting from the medical aspect , because it is non-psychoactive and has many positive effects such as including antipsychotic properties and promoting wakefulness .
