How many Walmart stores are in the United States ? Walmart had a total of 4,756 stores throughout the United States as of January 31 , 2020 . Walmart , formerly known as Wal-Mart Stores , Inc. , is one of the most well-known and valuable brands in the world . As of 2020 , Walmart had a total of 11,501 stores throughout the world . Walmart began in the United States as a single discount store , whose model was to sell more for less . Nowadays , Walmart has discount stores , supercenters , and neighborhood markets around the world . The multinational company has developed into the largest retailer in the world .
