Swedish e-shoppers seemed to care about beauty and healthcare products in the first two quarters of 2020 , after the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak . The most frequently purchased products online by Swedish consumers were these products , with 43 percent of respondents ' vote in Q2 2020 , compared to 33 percent in the corresponding quarter one year earlier . Clothing and footwear products followed , with 42 percent of respondents ' vote .
