From January to July 2020 , there were around 733 thousand international visitor arrivals in all accommodation establishments in Finland . In January and February , the numbers of internationals visitors remained at the same level as in the previous year , but the arrival figures dropped in March as a result of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak . By July , international visitor arrivals slightly increased from the previous months . The biggest groups of international tourists came from Russia ( over 102 thousand ) , Germany ( 70 thousand ) , the United Kingdom ( 46 thousand ) , and Sweden ( 42 thousand ) .
