This statistic shows the global mine production of palladium from 2015 to 2019 , sorted by major producing countries . The mine production in Russia reached an amount of some 86 metric tons in 2019 . The average price of palladium was 950 U.S. dollars per ounce in 2019 . Palladium is a metal , close to platinum in chemical composition , used primarily within catalytic converters , a key component of internal combustion engines that power the majority of motor vehicles produced worldwide .
