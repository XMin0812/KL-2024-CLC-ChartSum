In 2019 , the average size of a Victoria 's Secret store was about 6,551 selling square feet . The average size of a Bath & Body Works store was 2,631 selling square feet that year . Limited Brands ( currently known as L Brands , Inc ) is an American fashion retailer based in Columbus , Ohio . The most notable brands the company owns are Victoria 's Secret and Bath & Body Works . The company 's net sales amounted to 12.91 billion U.S. dollars in 2019 .
