The statistic shows the inflation rate in the European Union and the Euro area from 2009 to 2019 , with projections up until 2021 . The term inflation , also known as currency devaluation ( drop in the value of money ) , is characterized by a steady rise in prices for finished products ( consumer goods , capital goods ) . The consumer price index tracks price trends of private consumption expenditure , and shows an increase in the index 's current level of inflation . In 2019 , the inflation rate in the EU was about 1.44 percent compared to the previous year .
