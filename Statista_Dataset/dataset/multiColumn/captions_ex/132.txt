As of November 20 , over 22 thousand cases of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) were confirmed in Poland . Thus , since March 4 2020 , the total number of cases in the country reached 819,262 ( 424,748 active cases ) . However , over 23 thousand people were under epidemiological supervision . According to the Minister of Health , over 12 thousand infected patients died , and most of them had been suffering from concurrent diseases .
