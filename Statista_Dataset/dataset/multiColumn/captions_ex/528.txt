This statistic describes Royal Dutch Shell 's revenue from 2010 to 2019 , distributed by region . Royal Dutch Shell is one of the top five oil and gas companies worldwide , operating in every segment of the oil and gas industry . The company is headquartered in the Hague , Netherlands . In 2019 , Shell generated some 139.92 billion U.S. dollars of revenue in Asia , Oceania , and Africa . The company generates most of its revenue in these regions .
