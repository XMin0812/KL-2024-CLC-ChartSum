The statistic shows figures on the television ratings and viewership figures for NFL games in the United States from September 11 ( week 1 ) to February 5 , 2017 ( Super Bowl ) . The game in week nine between the Denver Broncos and Oakland Raiders , broadcast on NBC , was watched by a total of 18.3 million viewers . The final and most important game of the season , the Super Bowl , was watched by over 111 million people in the United States . The most-watched Super Bowl since 1990 was Super Bowl XLIX in 2015 , which was contested between the New England and the Seattle Seahawks .
