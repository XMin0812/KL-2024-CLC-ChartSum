The statistic shows the global internet penetration rate from 2009 to 2019 . In 2012 , internet penetration in Africa was was the worldwide lowest at 10 percent and grew to 28.2 percent in 2019 . As of that year , Europe had the highest internet penetration among individuals with 82.5 percent of the population accessing the internet . As of 20198 , the total global internet reach translated to more than 3.9 billion internet users worldwide .
