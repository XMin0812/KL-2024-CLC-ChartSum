The statistic shows the share of the various classes in the online computer game World of Warcraft ( WoW ) as of December 2019 . Approximately 9.5 percent of the created characters in U.S. realms were warriors . In terms of race , despite such possibilities as Night Elf , Orc , or Goblin , most players chose to be human in the game . All in all , World of Warcraft 's popularity as such is reflected in the game being one of the bestselling PC games worldwide .
