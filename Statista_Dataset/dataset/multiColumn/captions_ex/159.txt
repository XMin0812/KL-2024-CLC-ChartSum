The impact of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic had not only brought the global economy to a standstill but set the clock backwards on the developmental progress of several nations . While the rate of infection in India did not appear to be as high as in other countries , precautionary measures adopted dealt a severe blow to the country 's major industries - with construction and trade , hotels bearing the largest brunt of estimated loss . Manufacturing made a swift recovery in the following months .
