This timeline shows the total monthly value of the United States trade in goods ( export and import ) with China from October 2019 to October 2020 . In October 2020 , the total value of the U.S. trade in goods with China amounted to nearly 59.6 billion U.S. dollars composed of a 14.7 billion U.S. dollar export value and over 44.8 billion U.S. dollar import value .
