In an Ofcom survey looking into the use and attitudes towards media usage of UK adults , a total of 62 percent of respondents indicated that they played some form of game . Since 2014 , the popularity of mobile gaming overtook games consoles and TV for the first time . As of 2019 , 39 percent of respondents said that they played games in the home or elsewhere on their mobile phone . In 2019 , consumer spending in the UK on mobile games was over 1.2 billion British pounds .
