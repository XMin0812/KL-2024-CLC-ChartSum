This statistic shows the quarterly average daily rate of hotels in Los Angeles in 2016 and 2017 . In the first quarter of 2017 , the average daily rate of hotels in Los Angeles in the United States was 224 U.S. dollars . In 2016 , there were 5.6 million international overnight visitors to Los Angeles . That year , international visitor spending in Los Angeles reached 8.1 billion U.S. dollars , with most of the international overnight visitors coming from Shanghai , China .
