The distribution of coronavirus disease ( COVID-19 ) cases in Japan as of May 7 , 2020 , showed that the highest number of severely ill patients were aged 60 to 69 years old , with a total of 89 cases . The highest number of deaths could be seen among the patients aged 80 years and older at 228 cases .
