In the first ten days of December 2020 , 356 migrants arrived in Italy by sea . In 2020 , the number of migrants who stepped foot in Italy peaked in July at 7.1 thousand people . The largest number of people migrating to Italy were registered in the years 2016 and 2017 . In October 2016 , 27.4 thousand individuals reached the Italian coasts by crossing the Mediterranean Sea , the highest peak recorded in the past four years .
