This statistic depicts agrochemical company Monsanto 's net sales in the seed and genomics segment from 2011 to 2017 , by specialty . In 2017 , the corn seed and traits specialty came to approximately 6.27 billion U.S. dollars of net sales . Monsanto was an agricultural company specialized on genetically engineered seeds . The company was headquartered in St Louis , Missouri . In September 2016 , Bayer finalized a deal to take Monsanto over , which was completed on June 7 , 2018 .
