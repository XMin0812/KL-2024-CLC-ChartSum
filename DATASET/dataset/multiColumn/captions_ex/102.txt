The home ownership rate is the share of dwellings that were owner-occupied . In the presented European countries , the home ownership rate extended from 51.5 percent in Germany to as much as 96.4 percent in Romania . More developed European countries , such as France or Germany , tended to have a lower home ownership rate compared to the frontier countries , such as Lithuania or Slovakia . The share of house owners among the population of all 28 European countries has remained relatively stable over the past few years , amounting to approximately 69 percent .
