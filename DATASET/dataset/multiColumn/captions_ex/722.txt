This statistic shows the leading footwear retailers in the United Kingdom ( UK ) ranked by their market share in 2007 and 2012 . In both years shoe retailer C & J Clark , trading as `` Clarks '' , held the largest share of the UK footwear market . Compared to 2007 , Sports Direct gained market share , ranking them as the second biggest footwear retailer on the market in 2012 .
