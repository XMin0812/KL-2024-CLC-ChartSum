The distribution of coronavirus disease ( COVID-19 ) cases in Japan as of December 2 , 2020 , showed that the highest number of patients were aged 20 to 29 years old , with a total of around 36.6 thousand cases . The highest number of deaths could be seen among the patients aged 80 years and older at close to 1.2 thousand cases . The health ministry in the country announced on December 4 that there were a total of around 155.2 thousand confirmed cases of COVID-19 in Japan .
