In the English Premier League , the Golden Boot is awarded to the player ( s ) who scored the most league goals throughout the season . Since the 1888 - 89 season , the year of the first top flight season in English football , 108 different individuals have been named `` top goal scorer '' over 121 seasons . In 2019 - 20 , the Golden Boot was won by Leicester 's Jamie Vardy , who led for the vast majority of the season and fought off some stiff competition following the season 's restart .
