The breakdown by age in Estonia shows that the most vulnerable to COVID-19 infection are people in the age group from 30 to 54 years and above 65 years . . The first cases of coronavirus infection in Estonia were reported on 27 February . On November 20 , 2020 , Estonia reported 9,076 confirmed cases of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) .
