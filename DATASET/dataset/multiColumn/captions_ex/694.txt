As of October 2020 , Mexico 's gross domestic product ( GDP ) was forecasted to decrease by 10 percent during 2020 . These forecast places Mexico among the Latin American countries that will face the largest recession following the COVID-19 pandemic . Among the biggest economies in the region , Brazil was expected to face the lowest GDP decrease in 2020 , at -5.4 percent .
