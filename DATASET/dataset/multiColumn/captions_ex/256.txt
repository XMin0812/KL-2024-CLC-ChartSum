Being one of the largest automotive sectors , India had over 250 million registered vehicles since 2017 . It was the largest producer of two-wheelers across the globe in 2019 . The market within the country was dominated by this segment also . In 2020 , over 17 million units of two-wheelers were sold domestically across the south Asian country . A drop in the sales volume of two-wheelers was witnessed in the past year .
