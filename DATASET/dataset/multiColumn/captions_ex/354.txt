Burberry had a total of 465 stores in operation around the world as of March 28 , 2020 . Burberry is one of the most iconic British fashion brands in the world , and it is also among the most valuable luxury personal goods brands worldwide . Burberry offers high quality apparel , shoes , fragrances , and bags . Burberry products have a unique style , with the trademarked Burberry check a feature of many of their products .
