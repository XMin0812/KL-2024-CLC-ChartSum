The number of COVID-19 cases in Ukraine was approximately 919.7 thousand as of December 15 , 2020 , increasing by over 10.6 thousand over the past day . The highest number of cases was recorded in the capital Kyiv and the Kharkiv Oblast . The first case was reported in the country on February 29 , 2020 .
