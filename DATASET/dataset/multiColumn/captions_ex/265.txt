As of December 4 in 2020 , a total of around 3.68 million people in Japan underwent polymerase chain reaction ( PCR ) tests for coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) , of which about 3.3 million tests were for patients within the country . As of the same day , a total of approximately 155.2 thousand cases were confirmed positive with the virus . According to the source , number of PCR tests conducted in the national institute of infectious diseases and local institutes of health in the country amounted to 4,175,785 cases as of December 2 .
