The value of motor vehicle trading reached its peak in 2017 . That year , the United Kingdom exported 34.3 billion British pounds worth of such products , while imports valued at 40.8 million British pounds . The chasm between value of imports and exports closed again slightly that year . It was greatest in 2015 , when the difference amounted to nearly eleven million British pounds . The UK 's overall trade balance of goods was a negative 203.03 billion U.S. dollars in 2017 .
