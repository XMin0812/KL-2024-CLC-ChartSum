In 2019 , men aged 65 to 74 years had an average body mass index ( BMI ) of 29.3 kg/m2 and women aged 45 to 54 years had a BMI of 28.6 kg/m2 , the highest mean BMI among both genders across all the age groups . Apart from individuals aged 16 to 24 years , every demographic in England had an average BMI which is classified as overweight , indicating there is a problem with overweightness in England .
