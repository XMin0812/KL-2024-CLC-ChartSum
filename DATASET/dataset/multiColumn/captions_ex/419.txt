This statistic shows the global consumption of fertilizer by nutrient in 2013 , 2014 , and 2018 . In 2013 , the consumption of nitrogen fertilizers reached some 111.3 megatons . By 2018 , this amount is expected to increase up to nearly 120 megatons . Increasing crop prices lead to increased fertilizer demands and has been especially noted in recent years in South Asia . Favorable weather also increases demand for fertilizers in major agricultural regions .
