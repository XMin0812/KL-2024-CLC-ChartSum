This graph represents the usage of the four types of alternative fuel used in the UK in new cars registered in 2013 , 2014 , and 2015 . Significant growth can be seen between 2013 and 2015 in the pure electric and plug-in hybrid market segments , while the petrol/electric and diesel/electric sectors were shown to decline.With a share of 3.3 percent on the vehicle market , plug-in hybrids were fairly uncommon in 2013 . In 2015 , plug-in hybrid vehicle make for almost a quarter of the overall vehicle market in the UK . Plug-in-hybrids reached a share of 24.4 percent in 2015 .
