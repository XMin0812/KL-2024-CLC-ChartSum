This statistic presents the share of children who participated in netball in the last 4 weeks in England , according to annual surveys conducted between 2010/11 and 2017/18 . In the period 2010/11 , around 19 percent of children aged between 11 and 15 had played netball in the 4 weeks prior to the survey . This figure rose to almost 23 percent by 2013/14 .
