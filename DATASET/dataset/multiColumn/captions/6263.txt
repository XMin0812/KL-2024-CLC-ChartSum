As of week 47 , 2020 , the number of bankruptcies in Norway after the coronavirus outbreak were slightly lower compared to the weekly numbers of 2019 . The highest number of bankruptcies so far in 2020 was recorded in week seven , from the 10th to the 16th of February . By comparison , in week seven of 2019 , the total number of bankruptcies amounted to 81 in the Scandinavian country . The first case of COVID-19 in Norway was confirmed on February 26 , 2020 .
