As of April 20 , 2020 , the highest number of hospital-acquired infections ( HAI ) with the coronavirus disease ( COVID-19 ) was recorded in Tokyo Prefecture , in which 375 cases were confirmed across eight hospitals . The northernmost prefecture Hokkaido had the second highest number of hospital-acquired infections with 95 cases in seven hospitals .
