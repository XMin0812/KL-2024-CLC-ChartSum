As of April 17 in 2020 , the highest number of coronavirus disease ( COVID-19 ) patients in Japan was recorded among women aged 20 to 29 years old , at a share of approximately 20 percent . The biggest difference between gender could be seen in this age group as well as among patients aged 40 to 49 years old .
