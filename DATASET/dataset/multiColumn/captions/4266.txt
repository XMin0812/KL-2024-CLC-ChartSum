Despite the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak , the overnight stays of domestic tourists in Finland declined only by two percent in July 2020 and seven percent in August 2020 compared to 2019 . In September 2020 , there were around 1.15 million overnight stays recorded for domestic tourists , a decline of 12 percent from the previous year . By comparison , the overnight stays of international tourists dropped by 83 percent from 2019 , amounting to 87 thousand overnight stays .
