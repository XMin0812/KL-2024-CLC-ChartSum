This statistic displays the percentage of persons in the U.S. who had current , binge , and heavy alcohol use in the past month among persons aged 12 or older in the United States , by age group , in 2019 . During this year , 9.5 percent of persons between 26 and 29 year olds claimed to have heavy alcohol use . Heavy use refers to five or more drinks on the same occasion on each of five or more days in the last 30 days .
