This statistic depicts the brand value of the most valuable Spanish football teams from 2011 to 2018 . Real Madrid C.F . held the first place with a brand value of 1.57 billion U.S. dollars in 2018 ; followed by FC Barcelona and Club Atl√©tico de Madrid with 1.51 billion U.S. dollars and 0.43 billion U.S. dollars respectively . A brand is defined here as the trademark and associated intellectual property . Football clubs are made up of a mixture of fixed tangible assets ( stadium , training ground ) and disclosed intangible assets ( purchased players ) with brand value . To calculate brand values the Royalty Relief method was used . This approach assumes the company does n't own their brand and must license it from a theoretical third party . The method determines how much it would cost to do this .
