According to a survey carried out in Chile on March 27 2020 , approximately 68 percent of respondents were very concerned about getting infected with the novel coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) , up from a share of 60 percent registered in the previous week . As of March 30 , 2020 , Chile had the second largest number of coronavirus infections in Latin America .
