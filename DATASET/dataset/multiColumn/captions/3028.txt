This statistic shows supermarkets ' share of the fashion retail market in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from June 2004 to July 2014 , as value and volume share . Over this period , value and volume share has increased as supermarket fashion ranges become more common and popular among shoppers . In July 2014 , supermarkets had a 23.4 percent share of market volume compared to 16.7 percent around the same period in 2004 .
