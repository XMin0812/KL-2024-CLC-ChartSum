From March 23 to March 29 , 2020 , approximately 16 percent of users of personal finance app GuiaBolso in Brazil used food delivery apps , up from 10.5 percent in the first business week of the month . The share of GuiaBolso users who used ride-sharing apps decreased from over 23 percent to less than six percent in the same period . The changes were linked to the spread of COVID-19 in Brazil .
