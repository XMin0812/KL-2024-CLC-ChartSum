Based on projections made on December 17 , the number of deaths due to COVID-19 by the end of March 2021 could range from 2.5 million to 3.3 million worldwide , depending on the scenario . The best case scenario being 95 percent mask usage universally and the worst case being continued easing of social distancing mandates . This statistic shows the projected number of deaths due to COVID-19 worldwide from December 1 , 2020 to March 31 , 2021 based on three different scenarios , as of December 17 .
