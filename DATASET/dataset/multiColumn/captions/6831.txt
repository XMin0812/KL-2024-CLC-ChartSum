As of 5 March 2020 , the number of beds in infectious wards and hospitals fell in 13 voivodeships , and only in three voivodeships the count of such beds increased ( Dolnośląskie , Lubuskie , and Pomorskie ) . In the voivodships with the highest concentration of coronavirus patients , the number of beds mostly decreased . Hospitals and voivodeship offices declare that , if necessary , they will increase the number of beds in infectious wards , adjusting them to the needs . For further information about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , please visit our dedicated Facts and Figures page .
