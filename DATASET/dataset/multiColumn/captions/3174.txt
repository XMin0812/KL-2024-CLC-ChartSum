This statistic presents the wealth of high net worth individuals in the United Kingdom ( UK ) and in Europe from 2009 to 2019 . In 2009 the wealth of high net worth individuals in the UK amounted to more than 1.5 trillion U.S. dollars and it grew to over 2.1 trillion U.S. dollars by the end of 2017 . Overall , high-net worth individuals in Europe held approximately 16.7 trillion U.S. dollars in investable wealth in 2019 .
