Data showing how movies that received the 'Best Foreign Language Picture ' or 'Best International Feature Film ' Oscar at the Academy Awards performed at the box office revealed that most films fail to generate more than ten million U.S. dollars domestically . However , 2020 winner 'Parasite ' generated a total of 48.83 million U.S. dollars at box offices across North America , far outperforming other recent winners such as 'Ida ' _ , 'The Salesman ' _ , and ' A Fantastic Woman ' _ .
