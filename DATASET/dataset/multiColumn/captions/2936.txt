From 2004 to 2013 , the distribution of the French public opinion regarding the advantages and disadvantages of nuclear energy varied rather marginally . Still , the share of French people thinking that nuclear energy use had more disadvantages grew slightly , peaking at 46 percent in 2007 and at 45 percent in 2010 , before reaching 40 percent in 2013 . As of 2018 , the French public opinion appeared evenly polarized between favorable and opposed standings .
