In the view of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , thousands of companies were urged to implement home office for nearly their whole personnel , which boosted laptop sales worldwide , including Russia . To compare , over the tenth week of March , when the product realization growth relative to the previous week was measured at negative 18 percent , sales were already growing at10 percent rate . During the following week , both sales and realization levels grew at 27 and 50 percent relative to the week before , respectively .
