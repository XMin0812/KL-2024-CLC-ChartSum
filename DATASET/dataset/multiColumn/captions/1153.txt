After the outbreak of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Italy , many people died after contracting the infection . An in-depth study on 63.5 thousand coronavirus deaths revealed that the fatality rate is much higher for men . In fact , if the mortality rate for female patients was 2.8 percent , the corresponding figure for male patients was four percent . The chart shows how this gap was recorded among all age groups .
