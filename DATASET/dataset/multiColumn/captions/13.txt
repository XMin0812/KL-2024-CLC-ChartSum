Since 2015 , the highest average daily temperature in the United Kingdom was recorded in July 2018 , at 18.7 degrees Celsius . The summer of 2018 was the joint hottest since institutions began recording temperatures in 1910 . By comparison , the summer months of 2019 had a peak average temperature of 17.6 degrees Celsius as recorded in July . One noticeable anomaly during this period was in December 2015 , when the average daily temperature reached 9.5 degrees Celsius . This month also experienced the highest monthly rainfall in the UK since before 2014 , with England , Wales , and Scotland suffering widespread flooding .
