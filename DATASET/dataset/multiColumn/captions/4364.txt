Organic retail sales in 2018 came to approximately 37.4 billion euros in the European Union and 37.4 billion in Europe . In both cases an increase could be observed over the entire period . An annual increase was seen between 2004 and 2018 for both regions with a steep acceleration of growth , beginning around 2014 . Retail sales of organic food in Germany amounted to approximately 11 billion in 2018 . Second biggest market measured in retail sales was France .
