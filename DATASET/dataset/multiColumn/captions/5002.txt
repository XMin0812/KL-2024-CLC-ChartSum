Hydropower has been the major renewable source in the electricity generation mix within the 28 European Union members between 2013 and 2016 . Wind power has been the second major renewable source among those years , with shares increasing over the years . In 2017 and 2018 , wind power surpassed hydropower share in the electricity generation , accounting with 35.9 percent of the mix as of 2018 , while hydraulic power accounted with 33.3 percent .
