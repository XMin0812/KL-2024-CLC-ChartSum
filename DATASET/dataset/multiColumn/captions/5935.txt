In a survey conducted in September 2019 , around 53 percent of respondents in Mexico City agreed with the statement `` The law must allow women the right to abortion '' . In Oaxaca , state which decriminalized abortion in 2019 , only 27 percent of respondents were in favor of women 's right to abortion . Mexico City and Oaxaca are the only jurisdictions in Mexico where abortion is decriminalized within the first twelve weeks of pregnancy .
