Diesel and gasoline cost about one Danish kroner less per liter after coronavirus broke out . From the beginning of January to end of April , the prices for the fuel types GoEasy 95 E10 , GoEasy 95 Extra E5 , GoEasy Diesel as well as GoEasy Diesel Extra decreased overall . As of April 6 , 2020 , Diesel prices were between around eight and nine Danish kroner .
