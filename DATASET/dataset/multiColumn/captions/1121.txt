There have been several major E. coli outbreaks in the U.S. between 1993 and 2015 . The 1993 outbreak , which started at a Jack in the Box Hamburgers , resulted in 100 illnesses and 4 deaths . However , the 2006 outbreak , which stemmed from Dole baby spinach , resulted in 205 illnesses and 3 deaths . E. Coli is just one of many kinds of foodborne illnesses that affect residents of the U.S .
