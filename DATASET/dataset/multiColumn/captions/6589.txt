This statistic presents the Bank of Spain 's forecasts on the impact of the coronavirus ( SARS-Cov-2 ) on the national gross domestic product ( GDP ) from 2020 to 2022 , broken down by possible different scenarios . The forecasts of this institution are even more pessimistic than those presented by the Government itself ( a month earlier ) . Since in this worst case scenario , Spain 's GDP could recede by up to 15.1 percent .
