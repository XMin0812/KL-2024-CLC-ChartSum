This statistic shows positive and negative media coverage of nominees for the U.S. presidential election in April 2012 . In April 2 to 8 , 2012 , the percentage of positve media coverage for Mitt Romney was 35 percent . The analysis includes an analysis of more than 11,000 news websites around the United States and the full public feed of tweets on Twitter . In the data , tone of coverage is measured not by story but by the statements about a candidate and tracks how many are positive , negative or neutral in tone across stories . Neutral media coverage is not shown .
