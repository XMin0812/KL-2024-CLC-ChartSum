This timeline displays the revenue of the Italian winery Compagnia de ' Frescobaldi between 2010 and 2019 , by geographical area . As of 2019 , the revenues of the Italian winery Compagnia de ' Frescobaldi , which accounted for 122 million euros in total , were composed as follow : 45 million euros of sales in the Italian market and 76 million euros of sales in the foreign market .
