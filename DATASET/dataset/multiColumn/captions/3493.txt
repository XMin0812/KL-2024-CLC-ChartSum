Monthly temperature deviations in the UK from the long-term mean have varied greatly in recent years . In January 2020 , average temperatures were 2.1 degrees Celsius warmer than the long-term mean . In comparison , temperatures in Janaury 2019 were 0.3 degrees Celsius cooler . The most noteable deviation during this period was in December 2015 , when temperatures were more than four degrees warmer than normal .
