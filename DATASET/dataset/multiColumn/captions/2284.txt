In Denmark , the number of single mothers decreased slightly from 2015 to 2020 . After peaking at nearly 118.2 thousand single mothers in 2015 , the number amounted to approximately 114.1 thousand single mothers in 2020 . Among these women , most were mothers of one child , reaching a number of around 65.4 thousand . 37 Danish women were single mothers of seven children or even more .
