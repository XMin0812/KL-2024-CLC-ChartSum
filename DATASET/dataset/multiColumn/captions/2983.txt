When adjusted for inflation , the 2019 minimum wage in the United States is around 31 percent lower than the minimum wage in 1970 . Although the real dollar minimum wage in 1970 was only 1.60 U.S. dollars , when expressed in nominal 2018 dollars this increases to 10.35 U.S. dollars . The minimum wage in 2019 was 7.25 U.S. dollars , which decreases to 7.13 U.S. dollars when expressed in nominal 2018 dollars .
