How does the Danes rate their prime minister in light of the corona crisis ? According to a survey , the prime minister of Denmark , Mette Frederiksen , has gained popularity within the past month . While around 40 percent of the Danish population believed she did a good job in the last quarter of 2019 , Frederiksen 's popularity increased to nearly 80 percent as of April 2020 . Contrastingly , only five percent of the respondents found that she did a poor job . The first case of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Denmark was confirmed on February 27 , 2020 .
