This chart shows the iTunes chart rankings for Grammy performers and nominees at the beginning of the broadcast of the Grammy Awards in the Eastern time zone and after the end of the broadcast in the Western time zone , along with the total change in rank throughout the night . The chart used as a source had 1,500 total slots , and some songs which started the night unranked . Hunter Hayes 's `` Invisible , '' climed from place 271 to place 9 in that time .
