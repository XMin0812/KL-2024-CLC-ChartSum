The average under-23 player in the Serie A football league earns 1 million British pounds per year in basic pay . This average salary is seen to increase with age , with 23 - 29 and 30-plus year old players earning an average of 1.82 and 2.58 million British pounds respectively . Within each age group there is significant positional variability , with the exception of players under 23 , the forwards and midfielders typically earn more than goalkeepers and defenders .
