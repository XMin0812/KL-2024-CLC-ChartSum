The annual average temperatures in Helsinki and Sodankylä in Finland showed an upward trend in selected years from 1950 to 2019 . In Sodankylä , Northern Finland , the average annual temperature fell to -2 degrees Celsius in 1980 . It peaked in 2011 at 1.7 degrees Celsius . From 2013 onwards , the average temperature did not drop below 0 degree Celsius . In Helsinki , the capital of Finland , the average temperature remained above 4.6 degrees Celsius throughout the period under survey . In 2019 , the average temperature in Helsinki was measured at 7.4 degrees Celsius .
