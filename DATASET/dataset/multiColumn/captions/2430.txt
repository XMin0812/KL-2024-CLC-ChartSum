This statistic illustrates the market share of the main television providers in Italy in 2016 and provides a forecast for 2018 . The television company Sky had a third ( 33 percent ) of the Italian television market , while RAI and Mediaset had 30 percent in 2016 . According to the forecasts , while the market shares of Mediaset and RAI will both decrease by a percentage point , while the market share of Sky is expected to remain stable .
