In the final quarter of 2020 , The Walt Disney Company 's studio entertainment segment generated less than 1.6 billion U.S. dollars in revenue . The main reason for Disney 's lower revenue in both the majority of 2020 was the coronavirus outbreak . With theme parks closed and movie releases delayed or canceled , the company 's financial results were bound to suffer . Despite the ongoing success of Disney+ , the direct-to-consumer and media networks segments were also lower than expected , something the company noted was driven by the COVID-19 impact on live sports events which led to a drop in Disney 's advertising revenue .
