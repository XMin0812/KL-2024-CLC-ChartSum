Prince Harry was the most-liked member of the British Royal Family , according to a survey conducted just prior to his wedding to Meghan Markle in 2018 . With 42 percent of respondents in Great Britain saying they liked Harry , he was slightly more popular than his brother Prince William at 30 percent , and his sister-in-law Katie at 27 percent . Prince Charles , Harry 's father was only liked by nine percent of people in Great Britain , although his wife Meghan was liked by 14 percent of people .
