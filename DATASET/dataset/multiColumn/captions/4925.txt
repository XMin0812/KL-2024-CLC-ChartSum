This statistic shows the percentage of students who were in default on their students loans as of 2015 after attending different types of higher education institutions in the United States . Around 16.7 percent of student who went to public 2-year institutions were in default on their loans . The default rate for students in the FY 2015 cohort was 8.6 percent at 4-year degree-granting postsecondary institutions and 17.1 percent at 2-year degree-granting postsecondary institutions .
