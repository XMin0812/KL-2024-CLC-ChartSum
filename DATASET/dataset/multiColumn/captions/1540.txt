The average age of passenger cars on the road in the European Union was 11.1 years . Between 2013 and 2017 , the age of the continent 's fleet steadily progressed , with the oldest motor vehicles being heavy commercial units . As of 2017 , the European Union housed 264.2 million registered passenger cars . This was the peak since the beginning of the reporting period in 1990 , when figures where nearly one hundred million units lower .
