The United States is the world 's largest crude oil producer . In 2019 , it had an output of 17 million barrels worth of oil per day . This was nearly 10 million barrels more than ten years prior and largely a result of advances in unconventional tight oil production . Saudi Arabia and Russia ranked second and third , at 11.8 million and 11.5 million barrels , respectively . Oil production includes crude oil , shale oil , oil sands , and natural gas liquids .
