From 12 to week 26 in 2020 , the average daily number of deaths per week in Sweden was higher than the daily average in the years 2015 to 2019 . Several of the deaths in the first half of 2020 were related to the coronavirus epidemic . The highest number of coronavirus deaths were among individuals aged 70 years and older . Sweden was the Nordic country that reported the highest number of COVID-19 deaths in 2020 so far . All in all , the number of deaths in Sweden in 2020 amounted to about 74 thousand as of December 7 .
