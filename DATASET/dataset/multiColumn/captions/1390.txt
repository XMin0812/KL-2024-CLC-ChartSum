This statistic shows the revenue generated by their primary businesses of British Telecommunications ( BT ) from the first quarter of 2012/13 to the fourth quarter of 2019/20 . BT 's consumer segment generated the most sales in the most recent fiscal quarter , which amounted to 2.49 billion British pounds ( GBP ) . In addition , the total revenues of BT decreased compared to last year . British telecommunications is one of the leading telecommunications services company in the world . The headquarter of BT is located in the United Kingdom ( UK ) . BT provides many services in the UK and across the world including fixed-line services , broadband , mobile and TV products and services .
