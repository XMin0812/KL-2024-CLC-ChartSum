Beer was the most sold alcoholic beverage in Russia , with over 4.7 billion liters in retail sales from January to November 2019 . Sales of vodka fell in the last observed year compared to the previous equivalent period . Nonetheless , it was the second most sold alcoholic beverage countrywide . With over five percent increment in sales volume from one period to another , cognac accounted for over 102 million liters in sales from January to November 2019 .
