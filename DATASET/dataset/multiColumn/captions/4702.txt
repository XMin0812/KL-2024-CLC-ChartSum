This statistic shows the monthly average wind speed change in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2015 to 2020 . Monthly change rates varied during this period . In November 2019 the average wind speed dropped by 2.6 knots , while a month later it rose by 0.5 knots . The biggest deviation occurred in February 2020 , when winds increased by 4.7 knots compared to the average speed that month .
