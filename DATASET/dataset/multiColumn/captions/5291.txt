This statistic illustrates the sales volume distribution of the frozen foods market in Germany in 2017 to 2019 , broken down by product group . In 2019 , frozen baked goods accounted for 24.25 percent of frozen food sales - the highest share in this ranking . Frozen vegetables followed with 13.2 percent and frozen meals ( including stews and soups ) brought up the top 3 with 12.05 percent . The least popular product group based on sales volume were dairy products and desserts with 0.29 percent .
