This statistic gives the annual revenue of the Debenhams retail brand in the financial years 2014 to 2018 , broken down by country . In the year ending September 1st 2018 , Debenhams ' revenue in the United Kingdom reached 1.83 billion British pounds . Debenhams is a British retailer selling clothing , furniture and other household items in a department store format . The brand owns and operates stores in the UK , Ireland and Denmark as well as franchises in other international locations .
