In 2019 , Mexico was the Latin American country that showed the highest value of pharmaceutical exports , with more than 1.77 billion U.S. dollars . Meanwhile , Brazil was the country with the highest value of pharma imports that year , with nearly 7.3 billion U.S. dollars . The South American nation 's 2019 trade balance of pharmaceutical products amounted to a deficit of 6.11 billion U.S. dollars .
