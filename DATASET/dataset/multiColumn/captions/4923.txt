Less than 44 percent of internet users surveyed in all provinces of Peru except Lima said that they had accessed news websites from February 20 , 2020 to March 5 , 2020 . Once the first case of COVID-19 in the South American country was reported , on March 6 , 2020 , until March 21 , 2020 , almost 53 percent of the respondents in those regions stated that they had visited news websites . That represents an increase of nearly nine percentage points . Meanwhile , in Lima , there was an increase of 7.7 percentage points .
