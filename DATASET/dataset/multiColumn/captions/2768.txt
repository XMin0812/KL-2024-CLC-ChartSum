In October 2020 , the hiring rate in the United States was at 4.1 percent for total nonfarm . The seasonally adjusted total separations rate was at 3.6 percent in October 2020 . The data are seasonally adjusted . The separations figure includes voluntary quits , involuntary layoffs and discharges , and other separations , including retirements . Total separations is also referred to as turnover .
