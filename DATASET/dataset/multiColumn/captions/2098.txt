In 2020 , the total number of job losses in the retail sector in the United Kingdom ( UK ) reached 140,437 as of November 6 , 2020 . According to a forecast , excluding the temporary store closures caused by the impact of Coronavirus ( Covid-19 ) outbreak , there were over 15,000 retail store closures , 5,808 of which were through administration . Some 8,766 of these closures were a result of `` rationalisation '' processes , which the source defined as `` store closures or job losses occurring either as a regular aspect of business or a special cost-cutting programme . ''
