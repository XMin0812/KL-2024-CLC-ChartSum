In October 2019 , 55 percent of French people declared that they had a bad opinion of the President Emmanuel Macron . For a year , the share of French citizens having a bad image of the President remained stable . Emmanuel Macron was elected on May 7 , 2017 , winning the second round of the presidential election against the candidate of the National Front , Marine Le Pen . He became president on May 14 , 2017 . Aged 39 years , he was the youngest president in French history .
