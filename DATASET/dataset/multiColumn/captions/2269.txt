In the PISA test 2018 , Finnish students performed well above the OECD average in reading , mathematics , and science knowledge . Finnish students scored 520 points in reading , 507 points in mathematics , and 522 in science . OECD 's Program for International Student Assessment ( PISA ) is a worldwide study that evaluates educational systems in selected countries by assessing student performance in reading , mathematics , and science . It was conducted for the first time in 2000 and then repeated every three years . Over half a million 15-year-olds from 79 countries and economies took the PISA test in 2018 .
