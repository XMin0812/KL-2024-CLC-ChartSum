According to a survey carried out in the United States in March 2020 , almost 30 percent of respondents stated that they had increased the frequency of going shopping with the purpose to stock-up on products , due to the coronavirus crisis . In comparison , shopping trips for immediate consumption were being carried out more often by 19 percent of respondents .
