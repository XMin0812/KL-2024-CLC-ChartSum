Almost half of those aged 18 to 24 in Britain intend to make a New Year 's resolution in 2020 , the most of any age group . Younger age groups were far more likely to say they will make a resolution , with just 18 percent of over 55s stating they will make a resolution in 2020 . In the same year , women were slightly more likely than men to say they intended to make a resolution , although a majority of both genders said they had no plans to make a resolution in 2020 .
