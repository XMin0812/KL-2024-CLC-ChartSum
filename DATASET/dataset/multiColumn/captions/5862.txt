As of 2019 , there were approximately 6.87 million monthly phone plans with unlimited data transfer in Finland . The number of unlimited monthly data plans increased notably from 2011 to 2019 , while other types of plans decreased during this period . In 2019 , the number of phone plans with limited data transfer amounted to 1.7 million . All the major mobile operators ( Elisa , DNA , and Telia ) only sell unlimited data plans in Finland . The Finnish carriers focus on selling data plans with faster maximum speeds , instead of limiting data usage . In terms of monthly data consumption per capita , Finns clearly rank first among the Nordic countries and worldwide .
