The statistic shows gross domestic product ( GDP ) per capita in the Maghreb countries from 2008 to 2014 , with projections up until 2018 . GDP is the total value of all goods and services produced in a country in a year . It is considered to be a very important indicator of the economic strength of a country and a positive change is an indicator of economic growth . The Maghreb region in North Africa comprises Algeria , Libya , Mauritania , Morocco , and Tunisia . In 2018 , GDP per capita in Algeria amounted to around 4,080.91 U.S. dollars .
