Codelco , a leading global copper mining company from Chile , reported a revenue of approximately 12.5 billion U.S. dollars in 2019 , down from more than 14.3 billion dollars a year earlier . The company 's net profit has also decreased in the past year , from 190 million dollars in 2018 , to 14.5 million dollars in 2019 . Codelco 's copper production has followed a similar trend in recent years .
