This statistic illustrates the annual revenue of the Royal Bank of Scotland ( RBS ) from 2015 to 2019 , categorized by region of operation , in million British pounds . In 2019 the total revenue increased in all regions of operation but `` rest of the world '' . In the United Kingdom ( UK ) the total revenue in 2019 reached the highest value of all regions and all years , with a total amount of approximately 16.9 billion British pounds .
