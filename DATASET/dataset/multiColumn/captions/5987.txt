This statistic shows the active population in Belgium in 2018 , by sector and gender . In 2018 , approximately 1,1 million men and roughly 867 thousand women worked in the tertiary sector . On the other hand , in 2018 , 562 thousand men were working in the Belgian quarternary sector , whereas over 1,1 million women worked it this sector . The quaternary sector is a further delineation of the three-sector hypothesis of industry .
