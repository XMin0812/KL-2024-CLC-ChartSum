For an annual consumption of less than 100,000 gigajoule , the industry prices for natural gas peaked in 2012 at 4.09 euro cents per kilowatt hour , but decreased again in the following years , reaching 2.86 euro cents per kilowatt hour in 2018 . Since Italy relied on gas imports for over 90 percent of its demand , the industry price of gas was mostly dependent on the wholesale price rather than the domestic market .
