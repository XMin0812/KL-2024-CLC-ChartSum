This statistic displays the value of the import and export of goods in Sint Eustatius in the Caribbean Netherlands from 2012 to 2019 ( in 1,000 U.S. dollars ) . In 2012 , the value of goods imported to Sint Eustatius was valued at just over 33 million U.S. dollars . By 2019 , this had increased to 45 million U.S. dollars . In the same period , the value of goods exported from Sint Eustatius decreased from roughly 5.8 million U.S. dollars to just 1.3 million U.S. dollars .
