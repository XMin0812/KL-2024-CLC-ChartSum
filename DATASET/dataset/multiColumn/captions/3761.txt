According to a forecast conducted by the Danish institution , De Økonomiske Råd , unemployment in Denmark can face two different scenarios in 2020 and 2021 , as a consequence of the coronavirus outbreak . In the first , optimistic forecast , 150 thousand people are expected to be unemployed in 2020 , while fewer people , amounting to 116 thousand , are estimated to be unemployed in 2021 . In the second scenario , 232 thousand people are forecast to be unemployed in 2021 , and even more in 2021 . By comparison , 87 thousand individuals were unemployed in Denmark in 2019 .
