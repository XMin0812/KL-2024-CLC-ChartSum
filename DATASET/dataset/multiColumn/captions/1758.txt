This statistic presents the share of children that participated in dance activities in the last year in England , according to annual surveys conducted between 2008 and 2020 . The share of 11 - 15 year olds participating in 2019/20 was measured at 28.9 percent , down from 53 percent in 2009/10 . The share of 5 - 10 years olds participating in dance activities this year was slightly lower at 24.6 percent .
