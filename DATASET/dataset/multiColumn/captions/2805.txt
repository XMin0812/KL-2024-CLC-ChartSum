Roughly 3.5 million people in the Netherlands did some or all of their work on their main job at home in 2019 , an increase of half a million compared to 2018 . Telecommuting as a term does not exist in the Netherlands . Instead , the term more often referred to is thuiswerkers ( which roughly translates to `` home office workers '' in English ) . Employees made up roughly 2.6 million of the 3.5 million telecommuters .
