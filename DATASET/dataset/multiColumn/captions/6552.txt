This statistic illustrates the number of motorcyclists killed or seriously injured in road accidents , with engines of 50 cc or under , in Great Britain as of 2018 , categorized by gender and age group . In that year , there were 255 more male motorcyclists killed or seriously injured than female motorcyclists . People between 25 and 59 years old were by far the group age with the highest number of casualties , with 104 men and 17 women .
