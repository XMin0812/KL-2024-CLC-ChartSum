In 2019 , the average ticket price to see a movie at one of AMC Theatres ' cinemas stood at 9.54 U.S. dollars in the United States and 8.61 U.S. dollars internationally . Both figures were down from the previous year , particularly in cinemas outside the United States , and the lower ticket fees negatively impacted the company 's overall admissions revenue for the year , which fell from just over 3.38 billion U.S. dollars to 3.3 billion .
