Over the last week of March , when the Russian government implemented more restrictive measures in order to prevent the further spread of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) across the country , nearly one half of respondents in major Russian cities did not report significant changes in their usual lifestyle . The largest share of respondents sharing this view was recorded in Saint Petersburg at 51 percent .
