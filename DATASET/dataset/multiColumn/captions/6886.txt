As of April 2020 , a number of treatments and vaccines were in development worldwide to treat COVID-19 . This statistic shows the expected early result timelines for the most promising drugs , therapies and vaccines currently in development for COVID-19 ( coronavirus ) as of April 21 , 2020 . Early results are expected within the next three months for the mRNA 1273 vaccine by Moderna and NIAID , as well as for Novamax ' NVX-CoV2373 vaccine . The development of vaccines is of special interest as only a safe and effective vaccine is expected to stop the spread of the novel coronavirus permanently .
