The accommodation sector in Denmark has been heavily affected by the coronavirus outbreak . As of June 2020 , however , the number of overnight stays started increasing . While hotels in Denmark counted roughly two million overnight stays in August 2019 , the number amounted to around 1.2 million in August 2020 . However , the number of overnight stays in holiday houses exceeded those of the previous year . On March 11 , 2020 , Denmark officially shut down and closed all borders only a few days later . For further information about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , please visit our dedicated Facts and Figures page .
