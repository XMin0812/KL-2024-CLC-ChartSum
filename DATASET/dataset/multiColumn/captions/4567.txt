This statistic shows the migration numbers of Spanish people leaving or returning to Spain from 2010 to 2019 . During the period under review , more Spaniards left the country than returned to their homeland , peaking in 2015 at 94.6 thousand Spanish migrants . This trend however initiated a fall by 2016 , year in which the amount of Spaniards coming back to their country started to pick up . In 2018 , the amount of people originally from Spain that returned to their country outnumbered those who left for other places , with 86.7 thousand returnees .
