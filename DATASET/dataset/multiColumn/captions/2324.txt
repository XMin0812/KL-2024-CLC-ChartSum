This statistic depicts the usage of Fulfillment by Amazon ( FBA ) among top sellers across the Amazon marketplace as of December 2018 , sorted by country . During the most recently reported period , 73 percent of Amazon.com sellers in the U.S. were found to utilize FBA . In Australia , FBA was only launched at the end of February 2018 and already had a 33 percent adoption rate among top marketplace sellers at the end of the year .
