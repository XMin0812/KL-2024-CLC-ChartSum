This statistic shows the net revenue of Polo Ralph Lauren from 2007 to 2020 , by region . In FY2019 , the net revenue of Ralph Lauren 's European region amounted to about 1.63 billion U.S. dollars . The Ralph Lauren Corporation is an upscale American lifestyle company and fashion retailer that focuses on high-end clothes for men and women , as well as accessories , footwear , fragrances , home ( bedding , towels ) and house wares .
