This statistic presents the volume of soft and durum wheat produced in Spain from 2013 to 2018 . The production of common wheat has been fluctuating greatly over the analyzed period of time , peaking in 2016 with production figures reaching nearly seven million metric tones , and with its lowest point in 2017 , with approximately 4 million metric tons . The production of durum wheat , although much lower than common wheat , experienced an increase throughout the period under consideration , and reached over 1.3 million metric tons in 2018 .
