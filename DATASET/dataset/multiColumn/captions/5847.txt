This statistic presents the number of WESC retailers in spring 2011 and in the spring of 2012 , by country . The United States had the most amount of WESC retailers with a total of 740 in the spring of 2011 . WESC ( short for `` We are the Superlative Conspiracy '' ) is a Swedish clothing brand , based mainly on skateboarding influences and style . Founded in 2003 , its vision is to be the world 's best brand within the street fashion segment , by serving as a link between traditional fashion and streetwear .
