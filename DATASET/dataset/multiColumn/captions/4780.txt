The majority of Kenyan individuals reported fever ( 90.6 percent among men and 89.5 percent between women ) and dry cough ( 73.8 percent among men and 74.2 percent between women ) as the most known symptoms of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) . As of September 6 , 2020 , the virus caused 597 casualties in the country and the number of infections amounted to more than 35 thousand .
