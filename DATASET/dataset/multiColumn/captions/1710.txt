Based on data collected from the first quarter of 2009 to the second quarter of 2020 , this chart demonstrates a timeline of the number of advertising and market research industry jobs in the United Kingdom ( UK ) , broken down by employee gender and type of job . In the second quarter of 2020 , there were a total of 212 thousand individuals working in advertising and market research . During this time , the number of male and females in both full-time and self-employment were roughly equal .
