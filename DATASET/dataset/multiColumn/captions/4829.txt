This statistic shows the value of the healthcare IT market in Canada in 2008 and 2015 , with a forecast for 2022 , by category , in million U.S. dollars . In 2015 , the total value of the healthcare IT market in Canada was around 870 million dollars , with clinical IT systems accounting for the largest share of this total with around 474.5 million dollars . Revenue from clinical IT systems in expected to increase to over 721 million dollars by the year 2022 .
