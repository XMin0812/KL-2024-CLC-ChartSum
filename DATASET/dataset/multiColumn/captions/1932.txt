This statistic shows Syngenta AG 's sales worldwide from 2013 to 2019 , broken down by segment . Syngenta is one of the largest global agribusiness companies . It is based in Basel , Switzerland , and markets seeds and pesticides for crop production . In 2014 , the company 's crop protection sales amounted to around 11.38 billion U.S. dollars . In 2019 , that figure stood at 10.58 billion U.S. dollars .
