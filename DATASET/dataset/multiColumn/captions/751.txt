Harley-Davidson 's global mid-year revenue fell by around 40 percent between the first six months of 2020 , compared with the same period one year earlier . H-D generated around 2.16 billion U.S. dollars in revenue between January and June 2020 , some 394 million U.S. dollars of which was generated from financial services . In the first half of 2020 , revenue generated from motorcycle sales more than halved from the previous year to around 1.3 billion U.S. dollars . The U.S. was the most important regional market for Harley-Davidson in 2019 with almost 126,000 unit sales .
