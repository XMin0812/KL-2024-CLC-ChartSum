In 2019 , the average price of regular gasoline in Mexico reached 19.35 Mexican pesos per liter , up from 18.33 pesos per liter in the previous year . For premium gasoline , average prices increased from 19.88 to 20.78 pesos per liter in the same period . In the first three months of 2020 , the average prices of both regular and premium gasoline showed a decrease when compared to the previous year .
