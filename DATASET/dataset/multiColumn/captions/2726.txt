The statistic shows the global revenue of Burberry from 2015 to 2020 , by channel . In 2020 , Burberry 's retail channel generated a global revenue of approximately 2.11 billion GBP . Burberry is a British luxury fashion house founded in Basingstoke ( United Kingdom ) in 1856 , by Thomas Burberry . The company manufactures clothing , fragrances , and fashion accessories .
