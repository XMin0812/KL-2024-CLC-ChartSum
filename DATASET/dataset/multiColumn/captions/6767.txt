In 2018 , more than 42 thousand mobile phones were stolen in the Brazilian state of Rio de Janeiro . The number of mobile phones that were robbed by use of force or threat has been continuously increasing since 2012 , year in which 4.36 thousand phones were reportedly robbed in this state , reaching a historical record in 2018 with over 26.5 mobile phones stolen in that way . The number of petty thefts , which are defined by the lack of use of force , decreased between 2015 and 2017 .
