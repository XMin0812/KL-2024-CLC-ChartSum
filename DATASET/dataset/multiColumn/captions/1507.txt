This statistic illustrates the total assets development of the United Kingdom ( UK ) based Nationwide Building Society in the financial years ending April 2014 to April 2020 . It can be seen that the total assets increased year on year , reaching a value of over 248 billion British pounds for the group and over 245.7 billion British pounds for society as of 2020 . The smallest value of assets for the group was found in 2014 , when group assets of close to 190 billion British pounds were recorded .
