In the second quarter of 2020 , Poland 's seasonally adjusted GDP decreased by 8.2 percent compared to a year ago . Seasonally unadjusted GDP decreased by 7.9 percent compared to the same period last year . This estimate of GDP for 2020 takes into account the first effects of COVID-19 in Poland and the introduction of government measures to counteract the impact of the epidemic .
