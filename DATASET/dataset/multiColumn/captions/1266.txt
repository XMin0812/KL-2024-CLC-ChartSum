In 2019 , 97 percent of the 30 - 49 year-olds and 100 percent of 18 - 29 year olds used the internet at least occasionally . The United States have made large gains in online adoption over the last decade - in 2009 , only 76 percent of U.S. adults were online users . This share increased to 90 percent of the adult population in 2019 .
