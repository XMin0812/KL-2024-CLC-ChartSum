The GDP per capita in Sweden was forecast to have a negative growth rate of 5.5 percent in 2020 , due to the outbreak of the coronavirus , according to forecasts from September 21 , 2020 . This figure was more positive than what was forecast in July 2020 , when it was expected to decrease by minus 6.9 percent . According to the latter forecast , the GDP per capita was expected to have a positive growth rate the following years .
