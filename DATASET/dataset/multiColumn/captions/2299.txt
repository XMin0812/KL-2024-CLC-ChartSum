Using cash for payments in shops in Sweden became less popular over the years . For payments below 100 Swedish kronor , the share of respondents who used cash declined from 59 percent in 2010 down to 12 percent in 2020 . For payments above 500 Swedish kronor , the share decreased by two percentage point from a use of five percent in 2018 , to three percent in 2020 . During the same period , the use of debit cards for payments in shops increased for each period for payments below 100 Swedish kronor until 2018 , and then stayed at 71 percent in 2020 .
