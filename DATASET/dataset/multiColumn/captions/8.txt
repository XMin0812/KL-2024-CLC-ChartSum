Sadly , the trend of fatal police shootings in the United States seems to only be increasing , with a total 864 civilians having been shot , 192 of whom were Black , as of November 24 , 2020 . In 2018 , there were 996 fatal police shootings , and in 2019 this figure increased to 1,004 . Additionally , the rate of fatal police shootings among Black Americans was much higher than that for any other ethnicity , standing at 33 fatal shootings per million of the population as of November 2020 .
