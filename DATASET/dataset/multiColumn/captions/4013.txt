The statistic illustrates the distribution of main merger and acquisition ( M & A ) transactions , by the deal value , in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in 2018 and 2019 , broken down by region or main cities . It can be seen that London was the leading region in the UK , with deals worth approximately 138.3 billion British pounds in 2019 . The East of England followed by the South East of England completed the top three , with merger and acquisition transactions amounting to over 20 billion British pounds and 18.9 billion British pounds respectively in 2019 . The United Kingdom will see a significant drop in M & A activity between 2018 and 2019 .
