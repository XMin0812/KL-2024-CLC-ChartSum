As of March 2020 , it was estimated that among NAND flash products worldwide , enterprise solid states drives ( SSDs ) would experience the largest price increase in the second quarter of 2020 , with forecasts predicting price increases of between 10 to 15 percent . As suggested by the source , due to the rapidly growing share of enterprise SSDs in the NAND Flash market , their soaring cost will push overall NAND Flash prices up by between 5 and 10 percent .
