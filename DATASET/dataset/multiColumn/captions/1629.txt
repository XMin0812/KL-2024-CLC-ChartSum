In 2019 , the filmed entertainment segment of ViacomCBS generated 623 million U.S. dollars from its home entertainment operations , as well as 547 million in box office sales , referred to in the company 's reports as its theatrical section . ViacomCBS ' filmed entertainment segment is made up of Paramount Pictures , Paramount Players , Paramount Animation and Paramount Television Studios , and the majority of revenue is generated by licensing . It was noted in the 2019 report that the year-on-year 14 percent revenue growth was due to licensing of films to SVoD providers and recent releases to pay TV services .
