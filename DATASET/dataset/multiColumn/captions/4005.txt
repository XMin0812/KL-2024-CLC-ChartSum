In the second week of March 2020 , foot traffic in Sam 's Club stores increased by 66.9 percent when compared to the same period in 2019 . After weeks of increased foot traffic in their stores , Costco , Target , and Walmart Supercenter all experienced a decrease in foot traffic in the third week of March . This represented the first drop in traffic since the beginning of the pandemic in the United States .
