In January 2019 , Liguria was the region with the largest share of population ( 28.5 percent ) aged 65 years or older . By contrast , 18.8 percent of inhabitants in Campania belonged to this aged group , the lowest percentage nationwide . Data showing the age distribution by macro-region reveal that the North-West of Italy had the oldest population . By contrast , people living in the Southern regions of the country were the youngest nationwide .
