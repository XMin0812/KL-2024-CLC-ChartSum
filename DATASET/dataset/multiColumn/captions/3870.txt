This statistic shows the prospects of investment and development in the hotel property real estate market in Europe from 2018 to 2020 , according to industry experts . For both investment ( 4.03 ) and development ( 3.87 ) of hotel properties in Europe , the outlook according to industry experts improved between 2020 and the two previours years . The sectors with highest prospect scores in 2020 were retirement/assisted living , logistic facilities and co-living .
