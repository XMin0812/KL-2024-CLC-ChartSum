This statistic shows the size of the healthcare IT market in South Korea in 2008 and 2015 , with a forecast for 2022 , by category . In 2015 , the total value of the healthcare IT market in South Korea was around 355 million dollars , with clinical IT systems accounting for the largest share of this total with around 144.8 million dollars . Revenue from clinical IT systems is expected to increase to over 620 million dollars by the year 2022 .
