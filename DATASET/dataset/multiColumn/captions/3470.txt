As of April 17 , 2020 , the highest proportion of coronavirus disease ( COVID-19 ) cases per Japanese inhabitants was reported for women aged 20 to 29 , with approximately 13 cases per 100 thousand inhabitants of this age group and gender . The highest ratio for male patients was for the age group between 50 to 59 years , also 13 cases per 100 thousand inhabitants of this age group and gender .
