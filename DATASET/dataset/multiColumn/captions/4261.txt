This statistic describes the percentage of those who had current , binge , and heavy alcohol use in the past month among persons aged 12 to 20 in the United States , by gender , in 2019 . In that year , 10.4 percent of males between 12 to 20 years old claimed to have binged on alcohol within the past month . Heavy use refers to five or more drinks on the same occasion on each of five or more days in the last 30 days .
