First case of a person infected with the novel coronavirus in Peru was reported on March 6 , 2020 . On March 2 , 2020 , nearly 335 thousand children ( aged three to ten ) in urban Peru watched TV . Approximately two weeks and over 100 reported cases later , on March 17 , 2020 , the number increased to approximately 407 thousand .
