The average age at first marriage in Sweden increased among both men and women during the period from 2009 to 2019 . The average age at first marriage among women increased by 1.5 years during the ten-year period and was 33.9 years in 2018 . Among men , the age increase was not as large as for women . Over a ten-year period , the average age of first marriage among men had increased by 1.2 years , reaching an average age of 36.3 years .
