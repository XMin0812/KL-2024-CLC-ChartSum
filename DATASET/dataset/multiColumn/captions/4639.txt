In 2019 a survey was conducted to gauge the state of the conventions and meetings industry in the United States . The results of the survey show that 30 percent of respondents reported an increase in the number of exhibitors from 2018 to 2019 , and 33 percent expected another increase from 2019 to 2020 . However , it needs to be noted that the study was done long before the coronavirus outbreak which largely influenced the performance of this sector .
