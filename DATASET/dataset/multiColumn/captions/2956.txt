A recent poll shows that respondents from all countries are most likely to think the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak will last 2 - 3 months in their country , with 50 percent of those in France and 49 percent of those in Spain feeling this way . Only 2 percent of those in France and 3 percent of those in Spain think the outbreak will last longer than a year . This statistic shows the expected length of time respondents think the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak will last in their country , worldwide as of March 12 , 2020 , by country .
