This statistic shows the number of television ads aired in support of presidential candidates in the U.S. presidential election as of December , 2016 . Advertising by both the campaigns as well as the super PACs and other PACs supporting each candidate . The highest number of campaign advertisements came from the Hillary Clinton campaign with 404,704 .
