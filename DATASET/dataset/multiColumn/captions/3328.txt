The statistic shows the European net sales of Samsonite from 2010 to 2019 , by country . In 2019 , the net sales of Samsonite in Germany amounted to about 119.6 million U.S. dollars . Total European net sales of Samsonite amounted to about 792.2 million U.S. dollars in 2019 . Samsonite is a company that focuses on the manufacture and retail of luggage , with a large portfolio ranging from suitcases to small toiletry bags .
