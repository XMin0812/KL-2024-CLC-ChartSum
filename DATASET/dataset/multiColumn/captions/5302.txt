This statistic illustrates findings of a survey on the share of individuals who are current or past users of BDSMdate in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in June 2017 , by usage of dating portal and app . BDSMdate is an adult dating site that provides services to male and female users who seek short-term sexual encounters over the internet . During the survey period it was found , that 40 percent of responding current users of a dating portal and app reported that they were current users of BDSMdate . Furthermore , 30 percent within that survey group stated that they had never used it . Additionally , it was found that two percent of polled individuals stated that they knew BDSMdate at least by name .
