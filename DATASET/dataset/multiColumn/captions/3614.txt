The outbreak of coronavirus in Poland changed society 's hygiene habits . The largest share of Poles did not change their hand-washing habits . Both before and during the pandemic , 40 percent of the population washed their hands six to 10 times a day . However , a significant increase was recorded amoong the group of respondents washing their hands over 16 times a day .
