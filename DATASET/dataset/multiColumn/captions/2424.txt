Several Game of Thrones actors earned over 500 thousand U.S. dollars per episode according to data from early 2019 , including Emilia Clarke , who played Mother of Dragons Daenerys Targaryen , and Kit Harington who portrayed Jon Snow . On-screen brothers Peter Dinklage and Nikolaj Coster-Waldau had the joint highest estimated net worth at around 16 million U.S. dollars apiece .
