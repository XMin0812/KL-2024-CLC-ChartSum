This statistic displays the manufacture of opium alkaloids and their derivatives globally from 2014 to 2018 , by type . In 2014 , there were 463.6 kilograms of morphine manufactured worldwide . Global consumption of this drug has grown by almost a factor of six between 1991 and 2010 . Differences around the world in consumption levels of morphine for pain treatment are due to economic , knowledge , and regulatory factors .
