As of March 9 , 2020 , almost a quarter of U.S. consumers reported that they expected to spend either somewhat or a lot more on fresh and organic goods in the next six months . On the other end of the spectrum , 33 percent of U.S. consumers expected to either spend somewhat less or a lot less on tobacco and smoking products .
