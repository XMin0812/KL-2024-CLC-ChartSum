This statistic presents the share of generated revenue of WESC in 2010/11 and 2011/12 , broken down per market . In that year , the share of generated revenue by WESC 's German market was 10 percent . WESC ( short for `` We are the Superlative Conspiracy '' ) is a Swedish clothing brand , based mainly on skateboarding influences and style . Founded in 2003 , its vision is to be the world 's best brand within the street fashion segment , by serving as a link between traditional fashion and streetwear .
