This statistic shows the revenue streams of various private banking and wealth management companies , listed by firm type , in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in from 2013 to 2018 . Wealth management is a broad category of financial services , which includes management of client 's assets , and optimization of investment portfolio , according to client 's financial goals and objectives . Private banking provides services that might not include investments . It typically centers around high-net-worth-individuals ( HNWIs ) financial needs , offering personalized care of clients ' finances , either by public or private financial institution . As of 2018 , private banking subsector accounted for more than 3.2 billion British pounds in revenue , while investment managers accounted for a further 1.59 billion British pounds .
