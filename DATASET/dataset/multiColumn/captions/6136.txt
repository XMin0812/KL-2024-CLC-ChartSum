This statistic shows the number of recovered juveniles and arrested pimps in Operation Cross Country of the Innocence Lost Initiative in the United States on June 25 , 2012 . During the operation which was carried out by the FBI , 6 juveniles were recovered and 7 pimps were arrested in San Francisco . The number of recovered juveniles totaled to 79 , the number of arrested pimps totaled to 104 . For more information about the incident , please visit the official coverage of the FBI .
