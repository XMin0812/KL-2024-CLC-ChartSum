This statistic shows the number of Carrabba 's Italian Grill restaurants in the United States from 2011 to 2019 , by ownership type . In 2019 , there were 204 company-owned Carrabba 's Italian Grill restaurants in the United States . Carrabba 's Italian Grill is owned by Bloomin ' Brands , Inc. , a U.S.-based restaurant company .
