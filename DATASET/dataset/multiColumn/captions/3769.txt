In March 2020 , the everyday behavior of Swedes changed due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak . Within one week , Swedes reported to be washing their hands more often . While this was the case for 74 percent of the respondents surveyed between March 13 to 16 , the numbers increased to 85 percent one week later . The same tendency were seen for using hand sanitizer and shopping online . The first case of COVID-19 in Sweden was confirmed on February 4 , 2020 .
