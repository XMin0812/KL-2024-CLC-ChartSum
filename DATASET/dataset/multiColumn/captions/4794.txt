The largest portion of Russian users who watched videos on YouTube almost every day was observed among aged between 18 and 24 years , measuring at nearly 50 percent in 2019 . A significant difference in user behavior was observed during the shift from middle age ( 35 - 44 years ) to older population ( 45 - 59 years ) . In the latter age-group the share of respondents who did not use YouTube at all was 11 percent higher and the share of daily users was 12 percent lower than in the former .
