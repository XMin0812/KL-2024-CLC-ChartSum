Revenue generated from Sony 's game and network services amounted to 20.84 billion U.S. dollars in the company 's 2019 fiscal year , making it Sony 's largest business segment . Other major business segments include Music and Pictures services , which brought in 7.82 and 9.31 billion U.S. dollars respectively in the same fiscal year . Overall , Sony 's revenue reached around 76 billion U.S. dollars in that year , making it one of the 100 largest companies worldwide .
