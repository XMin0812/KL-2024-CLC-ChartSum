As of December 2019 , Cineworld Group plc had 9,500 screens worldwide across 787 sites . Whilst the majority of sites and screens are located in the United States as part of the Regal chain , prior to Cineworld 's acquisition of Regal in early 2018 , most of the company 's assets were based in the United Kingdom , where it operates Cineworld and Picturehouse cinemas .
