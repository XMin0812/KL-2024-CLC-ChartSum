The statistic above provides information on the percentage of pre-school enrollment in the United States in 2019 , by age group and type of program . In 2019 , about 34 percent of 4-year-olds were enrolled in state pre-kindergarten , while seven percent of 4-year-olds were enrolled in Head Start . Head Start is a federal program that promotes the school readiness of children ages birth to five years from low-income families .
