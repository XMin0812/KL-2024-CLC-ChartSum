In 2019 , life expectancy at birth in Belgium reached 81,83 years . However , life expectancy was subject to gender disparity . In 2019 , Belgian women had a life expectancy at birth of 84 years , whereas men had a life expectancy of 79,58 years . Life expectancy also differed from one country to another . For instance , in 2018 , a French man could at birth expect to live up to 79,5 years and a Dutchman up to 80,2 years . With that being said , the Belgian life expectancy at birth correlates with western European figures in 2019 .
