In England , the percentage of adults under 30 years of age in England who held a full car driving license within the last decade has fluctuated considerably . In this age group , the percentage of those holding a full car driving license generally decreased between 2009 and 2019 : with one percent fewer license holders aged 17 to 20 and two percent fewer license holders aged 21 to 29 . The percentage of driving license holders has been increasing almost every year for the age groups of 40 and above .
