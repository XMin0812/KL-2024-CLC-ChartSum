This statistic shows the selling space of the Metro Group worldwide from 2017/2018 to 2018/2019 . In that year , the Metro Group operated shops that had a combined 4.7 million square meters of selling space . The company discontinued it 's hypermarket business Real and Metro China in the 2018/2019 financial year . Those operations amounted to a separate 2.5 million square meters of selling space .
