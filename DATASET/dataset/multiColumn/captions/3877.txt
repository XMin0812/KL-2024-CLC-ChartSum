This statistic displays the results of a number of surveys on the voting intentions in the parliamentary elections in the Netherlands from November 2019 to February 2020 . During the survey period it was found that if elections were to take place in the Netherlands tomorrow , the VVD would likely remain the largest party in parliament . According to the most recent survey ( peil.nl ) the VVD would win 20 seats . This statistic shows the actual results of the last elections in March 2017 , which were won again by the VVD , with the PVV , CDA and D66 following up . Although the number of seats for the VVD decreased in comparison to the previous elections , with 33 seats the VVD remained the largest party in parliament .
