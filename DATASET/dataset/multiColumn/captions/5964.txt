The first case of a person infected with the novel coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Peru was reported on March 6 , 2020 . Between March 9 and 13 , an average Peruvian spent 136 minutes ( two hours and 16 minutes ) watching pay TV per day . A week later - between March 16 and 20 - the time increased to 154 minutes ( two hours and 34 minutes ) .
