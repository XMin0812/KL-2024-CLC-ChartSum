The statistic presents the annual revenue generated by CD Projekt Group from 2013 to 2018 . In 2018 , the company generated approximately 96.86 million U.S. dollars in revenue ( 362.9 million PLN ) . In 2015 , the Polish game developer and publisher generated approximately 205 million U.S. dollars in revenue , mostly owing to the release of the third installment of the Witcher series that became available on the market in May 2015 . In the previous year , the company reported revenues of 25 million U.S. dollars .
