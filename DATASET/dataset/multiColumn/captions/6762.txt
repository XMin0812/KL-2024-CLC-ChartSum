This statistic represents the age of the first teen pornographic TV viewing ( VOD , TV , DVD ) in France in 2017 . Thus , more than a third of the boys interviewed stated that they had watched pornographic content on a TV medium for the first time when they were between 13 and 14 years old . One in ten girls who have ever seen pornography on a TV set reported doing it for the first time before they were 11 years old .
