This statistic displays the share of individuals who have taken antibiotics orally such as tablets , powder or syrup in the last 12 months in Italy , in 2018 . Antibiotics are medications that can kill or hinder the growth of bacteria which cause infections in people and animals . In 2018 , 47 percent of respondents in Italy reported that they had taken oral antibiotics in the previous 12 months , this was above the European average at 32 percent . 53 percent of respondents in Italy reported that they had not taken oral antibiotics in the previous 12 months .
