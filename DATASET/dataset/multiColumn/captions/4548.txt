The coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic aggravated with a drastic drop in crude oil prices will cost a negative growth domestic product ( GDP ) growth in 2020 in Russia , according to every forecast scenario . If the effects of the crisis persist throughout the whole 2021 year ( a negative scenario ) , the GDP is expected to grow at a 1 - 1.5 percent rate in 2021 .
