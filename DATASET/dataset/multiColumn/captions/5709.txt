According to a survey conducted in Chile among company executives and directors , around 80 percent of respondents expected the country 's economy to be bad or very bad six months after the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak in March 2020 . Small and medium-sized companies were believed to have the worst prospects , as 91 percent of the businesspeople surveyed thought their economic situation would worsen after the COVID-19 crisis .
