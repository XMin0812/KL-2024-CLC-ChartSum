The statistic displays the results of a survey on the proportion of people within different age groups who gamble online on a monthly basis in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2016 to 2018 . As of June 2020 , it was found that 29.3 percent of respondents between the age of 35 and 44 years stated that they took part in a form of online gambling in the past four weeks . This age group had the highest proportion of people who gamble online . The age groups rank very similar to one another when taking a look at the age groups of gambling in general .
