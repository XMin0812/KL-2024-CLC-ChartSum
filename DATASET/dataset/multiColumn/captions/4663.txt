In April 2020 , a survey carried out in the United Kingdom ( UK ) , found that overall 19 percent of over 65 year olds reported they had been eating more unhealthy foods during the lockdown than before , while 40 percent said their consumption was the same as usual . On the other hand , 50 percent of those in the youngest age group said they had been eating more unhealthy food than before , the highest share across all age groups .
