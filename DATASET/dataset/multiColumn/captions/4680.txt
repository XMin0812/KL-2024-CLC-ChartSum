The number of tourists decreased in Denmark after the coronavirus outbreak . In April 2020 , nearly 89 thousand tourists visited Denmark , which was a significant drop , compared to the previous year . As of July and August 2020 , the number of tourists increased significantly , reaching approximately 1.3 million in August . On March 11 , 2020 , Denmark officially shut down and closed all borders only a few days later . For further information about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , please visit our dedicated Facts and Figures page .
