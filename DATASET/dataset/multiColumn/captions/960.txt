As of December 4 in 2020 , around 21.7 thousand patients in Japan with coronavirus disease ( COVID-19 ) were still being hospitalized or waiting to be hospitalized , while the total number of death reached 2,240 . On the same day , the total number of confirmed cases with the virus in the country amounted to around 155.2 thousand . Additionally , 712 passengers and crews who were on the cruise ship Diamond Princess docked in Yokohama , Kanagawa Prefecture , were diagnosed positive with the virus . All the passengers and crew had disembarked the ship by March 1 and the ship left Yokohama on May 16 .
