This statistic presents the share of adults donating money to sectors represented by the UK government 's Department of Culture , Media and Sport in England , in 2019/20 , by ethnicity . Sectors include the arts , heritage , museums , libraries , archives and sports . Over each survey period the proportion of white respondents giving to DCMS sector organisations tended to be higher than those of other ethnic backgrounds . In 2019/20 the group with white ethnical background had the share of donators at 35.6 percent .
