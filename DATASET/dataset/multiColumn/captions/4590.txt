This statistic displays the eating out behavior of consumers in England in the last seven days before interview in 2010 , 2012 and 2014 . Over the survey periods , the share of respondents who ate in a cafe or coffee shop increased , with 26 percent of respondents in 2014 compared to 24 percent in 2012 and 17 percent in 2010 . Those who had eaten in a pub also increased .
