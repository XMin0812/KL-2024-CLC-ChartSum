This statistic displays the number of new passenger car registrations in Spain from 2013 to 2019 , by autonomous community . Madrid appeared as the top region with most car registrations throughout the period of time under consideration , with a total number of registered units that amounted to over 474 thousand in 2019 . Catalonia ranked second on the list throughout the observed period , with car registration figures amounting to over 181.4 thousand units in this region of Spain in 2019 .
