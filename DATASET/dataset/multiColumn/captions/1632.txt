Which brand sells the most motorcycles in the Netherlands ? In 2018 , BMW sold around 2,100 newly registered motor bikes in the country , making it the market leader . Japanese brand Yamaha followed closely with approximately 2,050 bikes . One year before , Yamaha was in third place behind compatriot Kawasaki . Of the big motorcycle brands , the decline in Honda motorcycle sales in the Netherlands is noticeable . This was due to organizational reasons in the Benelux region .
