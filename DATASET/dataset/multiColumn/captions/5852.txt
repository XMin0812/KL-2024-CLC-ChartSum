This statistic shows the number of alternative fuel vehicles available in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in 2007 , 2010 and 2013 . There was an increase in the number alternative fuel vehicles over this period , especially in terms of petrol-electric hybrid vehicles which increased from five available models in 2007 to 24 in 2013 . The number of pure electric vehicles also increased to a total of 12 models in 2013 .
