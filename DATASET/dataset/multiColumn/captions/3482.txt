This statistic displays the group revenue of Debenhams from financial year 2013 to 2018 , split by UK and international revenues . In the year ending September 1st 2018 , revenue in the UK valued approximately 1.83 billion British pounds . International sales were much lower , at an estimate of 444 million British pounds . Debenhams is a British retailer selling clothing , furniture and other household items in a department store format . The brand owns and operates stores in the UK , Ireland and Denmark as well as franchises in other international locations .
