Before the coronavirus outbreak , in December 2019 , the U.S. ad market was projected to grow by 4.4 . percent , mostly owing to sales of advertising related to political and sport events planned for 2020 . However , as of march 2020 , revised forecasts indicate that most linear media will suffer declines in 2020 . Print and radio ad revenues are expected to drop by 25.4 and 14.1 percent , respectively - the highest results among traditional media .
