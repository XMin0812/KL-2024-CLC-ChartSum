As of March 2020 , during the COVID-19 outbreak in Brazil , around 15 percent of people who participated in a survey in that country and had voted for Jair Bolsonaro in the presidential election in 2018 said that they regretted having voted for him . Two weeks later , in the beginning of April 2020 , approximately 17 percent of respondents who had voted for Bolsonaro stated that they regretted it .
