In the second quarter of 2020 , the unemployment rate among young people between 20 and 24 years in Kenya increased to 22.8 percent . In the quarter before ( Q1 2020 ) , 8.6 percent of this age group were unemployed . On the other hand , between young people aged 15 to 19 years , the unemployment rate rose from 10.1 percent in Q1 2020 to 12.7 percent in Q2 2020 .
