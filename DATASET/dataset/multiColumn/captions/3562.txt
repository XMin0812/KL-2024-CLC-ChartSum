This statistic displays the estimated lifetime costs for those suffering a spinal cord injury , sorted by severity , as of 2019 . Lifetime costs for a 25 year old patient , suffering from paraplegia in the United States are estimated at around 2.5 million U.S. dollars . There are approximately 17,810 new cases of spinal cord injuries in the United States every year , with 43 years being the average age of injury . Lifetime costs are directly attributable to health care and living expenses and do not include loss of wages , fringe benefits and productivity .
