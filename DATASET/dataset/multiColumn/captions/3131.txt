On April 7 , 2020 , among all 27 federal units of Brazil , the Federal District — where the country 's capital , Brasília , is located — had the highest social isolation index at 56.5 percent . That means 56.5 percent of people geolocated through their mobile devices in the Federal District were self-isolating during the outbreak of COVID-19 in Brazil . By October 2020 , this share had dropped in all Brazilian states . At that point in time , Pernambuco and Rio de Janeiro led the ranking , with approximately 40.6 percent of people still self-isolating . An estimated 47 percent of the Brazilian population uses mobile internet .
