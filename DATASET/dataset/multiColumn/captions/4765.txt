In 2017 , there were over 124.6 thousand opposite-sex marriages in England and Wales among those aged between 25 to 29 , the most of any age group . Women under the age of 30 were more likely to get married than men , but in all the age groups over the age of 30 , there were more men who married than women . There were over 69.2 thousand women aged between 25 and 29 who were married in this year , the most of any demographic .
