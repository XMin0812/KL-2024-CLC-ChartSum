This statistic represents the McKesson Corporation 's revenues in the Distribution Solutions segment in fiscal years 2017 to 2020 . In FY 2020 , the total revenues in the medical-surgical solutions segment amounted to some 8.3 billion U.S. dollars . McKesson is a health corporation , headquartered in San Francisco , California . Its main business consists of pharmaceutical distribution , health information technology , medical supplies , and care management tools .
