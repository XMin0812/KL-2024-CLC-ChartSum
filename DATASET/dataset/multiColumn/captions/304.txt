Car demand in European markets shows signs of a recovery in the autumn of 2020 . And yet , the number of new car registrations in the European Union fell by 7.8 percent between October 2019 and 2020 . The drop came after the first increase of the year in September . Slovakia fared worst with a fall of almost 30 percent . Germany recorded the highest sales volume among all markets and yet , the market declined by almost four percent year-on-year . Across all European markets , EU , UK , and EFTA member states saw car sales decrease by around seven percent in October 2020 , compared with the same month one year earlier .
