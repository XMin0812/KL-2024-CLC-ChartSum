From February 12 to March 16 , 2020 , between 8.1 and 18.8 percent of COVID-19 cases in the U.S. among those aged 65 to 74 years required admission to an intensive care unit . This statistic shows the percentage of COVID-19 cases in the United States from February 12 to March 16 , 2020 that required intensive care unit ( ICU ) admission , by age group .
