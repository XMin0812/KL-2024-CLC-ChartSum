As of the second half of 2017 , tourists spent the most hotel nights in Amsterdam with approximately 8.64 million overnight stays . The number of hotel nights spent in Rotterdam amounted to roughly one million , whereas approximately 900,000 nights were spent in hotels in The Hague . The majority of the tourists that stayed over in hotel accommodations in the Netherlands were German tourists . As of the first half of 2018 , roughly 2.76 million hotel nights were registered of tourists from Germany , whereas over two million hotel nights were spent by individuals from the United Kingdom .
