From 2015 to 2019 , the number of tourist arrivals in Curaçao increased overall , passing from roughly one million to about 1.29 million . Over the period considered , most arrivals concerned cruise passengers rather than day-trippers or travelers who stayed overnight . As of 2019 , the number of cruise arrivals in Curaçao peaked at nearly 810 thousand . On the other hand , the island registered 463.6 thousand tourists arriving for overnight stays .
