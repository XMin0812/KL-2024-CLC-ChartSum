This statistic shows the gross domestic product ( GDP ) of the BRIC countries from 2009 to 2019 , with projections up until 2021 . The BRIC countries are the emerging countries Brazil , Russia , India and China . In 2019 , the GDP of all BRIC states amounted to approximately 46.22 billion international dollars . Gross domestic product in Russia was the second lowest of all four . See global GDP for comparison .
