The statistic shows the share of the total votes each political party received during the parliament elections on June 18 , 2015 and on June 5 , 2019 in Denmark . Socialdemokraterne received the biggest share of votes both years , with 26,3 percent in 2015 and 25,9 percent in 2019 . Dansk Folkeparti , ranking second in 2015 , lost 12.4 percent of the votes , and ranked third in 2019 with a share of 8.7 percent . Venstre , Denmark 's Liberal Party , went from ranking third in 2015 to ranking second in 2019 , with 23.4 percent of the votes in 2019 .
