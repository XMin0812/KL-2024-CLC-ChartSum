This statistic shows the brokerage of the top domiciled brokers in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in 2012 and in 2013 . In 2012 , AON was the broker leader with brokerage ( all business ) of 640 million British pounds ( GBP ) . In 2013 it was still ranked as first , with an increase in brokerage resulting in 726.5 million GBP in total .
