This timeline depicts the global net sales of Luxottica from 2010 to 2019 , broken down by channel . In 2019 , the global net sales of Luxottica 's retail division amounted to approximately 6.23 billion euros . Founded in 1961 in Agordo , Italy , the Luxottica Group S.p.A. is the world 's largest eyewear company . Its best known brands include Ray-Ban , Persol and Oakley , Inc . The company also makes sunglasses and frames for a multitude of designer brands such as Chanel and Prada .
