This statistic shows how supportive respondents to a climate change public attitude survey were of renewable energy in the United Kingdom ( UK ) between 2012 and 2020 . Levels of support for renewable energy for electricity , heat and fuel have remained relatively unchanged since 2012 . In 2012 , 79 percent of respondents reported being supportive of the use of renewable energy , while five percent were opposed . In 2020 , 82 percent of respondents reported being supportive .
