This statistic shows the share of adults who are likely to see the live-action version of the 'Mulan ' movie in theaters in the United States as of May 2019 , broken down by age group . The data reveals that 28 percent of 18 to 29-year-olds said that they were very likely to watch the new 'Mulan ' movie in cinemas , and 28 percent also said that they were somewhat likely to do so . Conversely , interest among older generations was far lower .
