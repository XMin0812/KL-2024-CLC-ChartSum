In 2018 , the average biking distance per person per day amounted to approximately 3.4 and 3 kilometers for men and women respectively . In terms of the average biking distance by age , children between 12 to 18 years old cycled 6.5 kilometers , the longest distance in 2018 . Furthermore , people aged 75 years and older had the lowest average kilometers with roughly two thousand meters a day .
