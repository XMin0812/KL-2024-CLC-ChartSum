The statistic illustrates the medal tally of the Italian team at the 2016 Olympic Summer Games , by sport . Italy competed at the 2016 Summer Olympic Games in Rio de Janeiro , Brazil , from 5 to 21 August 2016 . During the 2016 Summer Olympics , commonly known as Rio 2016 , Italian athletes won a total of 28 medals , including eight golds . Shooting was the most successful sport for the team , as Italy managed to win seven medals , of which four golds .
