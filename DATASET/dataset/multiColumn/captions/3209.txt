This statistic presents the total number of ivivva athletica stores worldwide from 2013 to 2017 , by country . In 2014 , ivivva athletica , which is owned by lululemon , operated a total of eleven corporate-owned stores throughout the United States.The athletic apparel producer is a lifestyle brand that is targeted primarily towards active females promoting a healthy , balanced and fun lifestyle . The company was founded in 1998 in Vancouver , British Columbia .
