Out of 361 deaths caused by the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Ukraine as of May 8 , 2020 , nearly 28 percent were in the age group between 60 and 69 years old . In total , the country had over 20 thousand COVID-19 cases as of May 22 , 2020 .
