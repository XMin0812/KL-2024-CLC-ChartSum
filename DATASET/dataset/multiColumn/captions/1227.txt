In 2018 , the rate of suicides among males was 17.2 per 100,000 population and among females it was 5.4 per 100,000 . The rate of suicide has slightly decreased for both genders since the beginning of given time period of the statistic , although the 2018 rate is an increase from the preceding few years . However , the rate of suicide for men has remained significantly higher than for women .
