Cosmetics brands in Brazil saw a decrease in sales during the second half of March 2020 , linked to the outbreak of COVID-19 in the country . In the week starting on March 22 , L'Oreal saw a ten percent decrease in sales in Brazil , after a nine percent increase registered in the previous week . Meanwhile , Coty saw a decrease in sales for two consecutive weeks , reaching a drop of 17 percent in the second week evaluated . The first death due to COVID-19 was reported in Brazil on March 18 .
