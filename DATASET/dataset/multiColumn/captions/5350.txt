This statistic shows a ranking of sororities in the United States , according to `` largest national footprint , most devoted sisters , and greatest philanthropic footprint '' . To measure this , the daily beast considered the number of active collegiate chapters , the number of Twitter followers , the number Facebook fans and the amount of money donated by the sorority to nonprofit organizations . In this graph the variables : number of Facebook fans and number twitter followers are depicted . Zeta Tau Alpha was ranked as the top sorority in 2012 with a massive 40,647 Facebook fans .
