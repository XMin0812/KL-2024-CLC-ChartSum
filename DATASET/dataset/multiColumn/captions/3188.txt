This statistic shows the carbon dioxide emissions of vehicles according to their fuel type in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in selected years from 2000 to 2017 . CO2 emissions have been falling for all the fuel types aside from a slight increase in petrol emissions in 2007 . In 2013 diesel vehicles emitted 0.4 g/km more than petrol vehicles and 33.7 g/km more than alternative fuel vehicles .
