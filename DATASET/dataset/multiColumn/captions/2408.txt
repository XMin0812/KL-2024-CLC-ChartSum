The statistic shows the mean household income for the lowest quintile and the top five percent of workers in the United States from 1990 to 2019 . Over this period , the mean household income for the low-paid workers in the lowest quintile went from 13,633 U.S. dollars in 1990 to 15,286 U.S. dollars in 2019 , while the mean income of the top five percent increased from 263,980 U.S. dollars to 451,122 U.S. dollars over the same period . The income for this period is based on the 2019 U.S. dollar value .
