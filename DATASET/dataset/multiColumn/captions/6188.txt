This statistic shows the annual death rate for prescription opioid overdose deaths in the United States in 2015 and 2016 , by ethnicity . According to the data , in 2016 American Indians and Alaskan Natives had a death rate from prescription opioids of 6.5 per 100,000 . Among this ethnicity , from 2015 to 2016 , the death rate from prescription opioids dropped from 7 to 6.5 .
