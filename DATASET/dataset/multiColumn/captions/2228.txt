Operating expenses for nuclear power plants run by major U.S. investor-owned electric totaled 28.33 mills per kilowatt hour . At that same year , figures for hydroelectric power plants amounted to 10.8 mills per kilowatt hour . According to the source , a mill is a monetary cost and billing unit equal to 1/1000 of the U.S. dollar ( equivalent to 1/10 of one cent ) .
