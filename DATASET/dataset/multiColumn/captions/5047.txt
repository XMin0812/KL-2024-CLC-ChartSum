The decree of the state of alarm in Spain on March 14 , 2020 banned any departure from the home that was non-essential . Going to the supermarket to get food and other products became almost the only possibility for people to go out . Proximity and product availability played a key role when deciding which supermarket chain to go to . Among all the existing chains in the country , Mercadona became the favorite to a large part of the Spanish population . In fact , throughout the period , the Valencian supermarket concentrated more than a fifth of national sales .
