During the observation period , rugby 's popularity has dropped among children between 11 and 15 years in England and has increased among children between five and ten years . In 2017/2018 , 14.6 percent of 11 to 15 year olds participated in rugby in the 4 years prior to the survey . Participation peaked in 2011/2012 , when 22.8 percent of the children in that age group played rugby .
