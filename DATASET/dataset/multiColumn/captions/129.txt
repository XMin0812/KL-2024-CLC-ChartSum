This statistic shows the shares of household income of quintiles in the United States from 1970 to 2019 . About 51.9 percent of the household income of private households in the U.S. were earned by the highest quintile in 2019 , which are the upper 20 percent of the workers . In contrast to that , in the same year , only 3.1 percent of the household income was earned by the lowest quintile . This relation between the quintiles illustrates the income inequality in the United States .
