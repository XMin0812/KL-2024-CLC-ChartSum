This statistic depicts the sales share of La-Z-Boy worldwide in 2012 to 2016 , by segment . In 2016 , the upholstery segment made up 71 percent of La-Z-Boy 's sales worldwide . In that year , fiscal sales amounted to approximately 1.53 billion U.S. dollars.La-Z-Boy is a furniture manufacturer based in Monroe , Michigan that makes home furniture , including upholstered recliners , sofas , stationary chairs , and sleeper sofas .
