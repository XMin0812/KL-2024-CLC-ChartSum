This statistic shows the share of American students in grades 9 to 12 who stated they have driven a car when intoxicated on one or more occasions in 2017 . The results were sorted by gender and ethnicity . The statistic only covers the occasions which took place 30 days before the survey was conducted . 3.8 percent of female student respondents with a white , non-Hispanic background stated they have been driving a car after they had been drinking alcohol during that time at least once .
