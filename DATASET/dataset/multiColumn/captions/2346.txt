Between 2014 and 2019 , the number of families and people living below the poverty line in Italy grew . In 2019 , 1.7 million families were living in poverty , roughly 350 thousand families more compared to 2014 . Moreover , the number of poor individuals in 2019 equaled to 4.6 million . However , between 2018 and 2019 , both figures experienced a decrease .
