The total tourism expenditure ( both inbound and outbound ) in the Netherlands amounted to a total of approximately 87 billion euros in 2018 . Domestic tourism had the highest expenditure with nearly 45.5 billion euros , whereas expenditures on inbound tourism in the Netherlands had a total of roughly 32.5 billion euros . Examining the holiday expenditures by different household types , families spend the most with an average expenditure of 2,770 euros . Couples with children spent the least money on holidays with 2,450 euros .
