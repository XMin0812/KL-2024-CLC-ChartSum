Wine production is one of the leading industries in Italy.This statistic displays the leading ten Italian wineries in Italy from 2014 to 2018 , broken down by turnover value . According to data , in 2018 the leading Italian winery was C.Riunite/CIV/GIV with 633 million euros of annual turnover , followed by GIV which registered a total annual turnover of 388 million euros .
