In September 2020 , it was found that Dutch-based online food delivery service Takeaway.com was projected to have the biggest revenue growth in the online marketplace sector with a 2019 to 2021 compound annual growth rate ( CAGR ) of 147 percent . According to GP Bullhound , Takeaway.com had a September 2020 market cap of 16.7 billion U.S. dollars .
