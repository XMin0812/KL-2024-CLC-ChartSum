This graph shows the share of children that were enrolled in either full-day or part-day pre-primary programs in the United States in 2018 , by race . In 2018 , about 50.42 percent of Hispanic children were enrolled in either nursery school or kindergarten . Of that 50.42 percent , 31.02 percent were enrolled in a part-day program , and 69.03 percent were enrolled in a full-day program .
