Greenland is the world 's largest island , located between the Arctic and Atlantic Oceans . Most of the country is covered by ice . It is the least densely populated country in the world . The population fluctuated slightly in the past few years . As of 2019 , Greenland had 56,992 inhabitants . Among these , more than 29.5 thousand were male and more than 26.4 thousand were female inhabitants .
