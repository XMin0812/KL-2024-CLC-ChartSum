This statistic displays the share of children who participated in music activities in the last year in England , according to annual surveys conducted between 2008/09 and 2019/20 . According to the most recent survey , of 5 - 10 year olds around 35 percent participate in music activities and over 56 percent of the older group ( 11 - 15 year olds ) . That is a significant decrease compared to the beginning of the period when the percentages per age group were 50 and 70 , respectively .
