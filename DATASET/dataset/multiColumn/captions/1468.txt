This statistic shows the net revenue of Polo Ralph Lauren worldwide from 2007 to 2016 , by business division . In 2013 , the Ralph Lauren Corporation 's net revenue from its retail division amounted to about 3.62 billion U.S. dollars . The Ralph Lauren Corporation is an upscale American lifestyle company and fashion retailer that focuses on high-end clothes for men and women , as well as accessories , footwear , fragrances , home ( bedding , towels ) and housewares .
