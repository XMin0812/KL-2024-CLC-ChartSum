This statistic shows the share of American students in grades 9 to 12 who stated they have been physically forced to have sexual intercourse when they did not want to at least once in their lives , by gender and state . The survey was conducted from August 2018 to June 2019 . According to the source , 16.9 percent of female student respondents from Arkansas stated they have been physically forced to have sexual intercourse against their will at least once .
