The statistic shows gross domestic product ( GDP ) in Timor-Leste from 2004 to 2018 , with projections up until 2021 . Gross domestic product ( GDP ) denotes the aggregate value of all services and goods produced within a country in any given year . GDP is an important indicator of a country 's economic power . In 2018 , Timor-Leste 's gross domestic product amounted to around 1.92 billion international dollars .
