Most IT enterprises and providers worldwide are concerned about IT infrastructure to cope with extra working at home , as well as the challenges of supporting the wellness of teams due to the wide effects of the global COVID-19 pandemic . The IT enterprises ' and providers ' biggest concern overall though was , if the local broadband infrastructure was good enough to sustain working from home during the crisis .
