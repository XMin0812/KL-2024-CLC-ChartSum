This statistic presents the driving factors that influence consumers in the United States to make a purchase on Amazon in 2017 , by age group . According to the findings , the primary factor that drove individuals across all age groups to make a purchase on Amazon was the price of an item . The outcome of this factor ranged from 71 percent for Generation Z , to 76 percent for Generation Y , to 78 percent for Generation X , and lastly 77 percent for Baby Boomers .
