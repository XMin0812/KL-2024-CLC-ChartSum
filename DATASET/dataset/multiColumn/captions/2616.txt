This statistic shows the results of a survey regarding the public opinion on which Disney princesses are the best role models for girls in Great Britain as of June 2018 . The survey found that 72 percent of British adults that had seen the movie 'Moana ' thought that this Disney princess was a good role model , while only two percent thought that she was not . Mulan and Merida were also perceived as good role models , at 68 percent and 66 percent respectively .
