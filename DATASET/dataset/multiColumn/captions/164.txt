In 2019 , Amazon 's net revenue from subscription services segment amounted to 19.21 billion U.S. dollars . Subscription services include Amazon Prime , for which Amazon reported 150 million paying members worldwide at the end of 2019 . The AWS category generated 35.03 billion U.S. dollars in annual sales . During the most recently reported fiscal year , the company 's net revenue amounted to 280.5 billion U.S. dollars .
