PSA is the leading commercial vehicle producer based in the United Kingdom . The manufacturer owns the Vauxhall brand which operates its commercial vehicle plant in Luton and produced some 55,000 vehicles in 2019 . This was a decrease of around 12 percent compared to the previous year . Since selling their first cars , Vauxhall established itself as a mainstay of the British automobile industry . Today it is the country 's oldest car brand still in production .
