This statistic shows the type of beer ( ale or lager ) adults prefer in Great Britain ( UK ) in 2014 , by gender . According to the results of the Yougov survey , 42 percent of male respondents said they prefer ale , compared to only 14 percent of female respondents . However an equal proportion of male and females chose as their preferred beer ( 39 percent ) , while 43 percent of females said they prefer neither .
