Between 2011 and 2016 , the sales of smartphones in France have continuously been increasing , from about 11.5 million units to almost 22 million in 2018 . On the other hand , the sales of laptops have been stagnating since 2012 , while tablet sales peaked at 6.2 million units in 2014 . Moreover , we can see that wearables have appeared on the French market for mobile devices since 2014 : these body-borne devices are small computing devices which extend the performances of computing technologies .
