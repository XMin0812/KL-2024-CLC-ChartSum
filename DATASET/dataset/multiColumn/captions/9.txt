Based on a comparison of coronavirus deaths in 193 countries relative to their population , Belgium had the most losses to COVID-19 up until December 16 , 2020 . As of the same date , the virus had infected over 73.5 million people worldwide , and the number of deaths had totaled more than 1.6 million . Note , however , that COVID-19 test rates can vary per country . Additionally , big differences show up between countries when combining the number of deaths against confirmed COVID-19 cases .
