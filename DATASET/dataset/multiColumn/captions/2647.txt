Among the most downloaded food & drink apps , grocery delivery apps in China experienced the largest increase in downloads due to the coronavirus epidemic in China in early 2020 . In the week of February 3 , when the epidemic was at its height , grocery delivery app downloads made up for more than half of the top 10 food & drink app downloads , in comparison to just 33 percent in the week of January 13 before the outbreak . Food delivery and recipe apps also saw considerable gain in the number of downloads , as people were mostly confined at home and had to come up with ways to feed their stomach .
