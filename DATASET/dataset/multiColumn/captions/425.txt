As of November 20 , 2020 , South Korea confirmed 30,017 cases of infection including 501 deaths after the first case of coronavirus in the country in January 2020 . After the first wave lasted till April , Seoul and the metropolitan areas were hit hard by a few group infections during the second wave in August . Currently , Korea is facing the third wave with 200 to 300 daily confirmed cases mainly in Seoul and its neighboring areas .
