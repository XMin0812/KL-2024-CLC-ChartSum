In March 2020 , it was found that the vast majority , 87 percent , of the British population feel that their fellow Brits are not taking the current coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic seriously enough . While 36 percent believe the government is not taking the outbreak seriously enough , 58 percent believe the government has got it about right . As of March 25 , 2020 , the United Kingdom had 9,529 confirmed cases of coronavirus .
