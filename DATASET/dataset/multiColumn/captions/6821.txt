The statistic displays the household recycling rates per region in England from 2010/11 to 2018/19 . It shows that recycling rates during this period were highest in the South West and East of England , with rates peaking at 50.1 percent in 2018/19 . The lowest recycling rates during this period were in London . Here , the household recycling rates declined between 2012/13 and 2015/16 , although they have increased again in recent years .
