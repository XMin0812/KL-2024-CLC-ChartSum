The most bankruptcies after the coronavirus outbreak in Sweden was recorded for companies in the construction industry as of November 2020 . With 15 bankruptcies , the number was higher than the previous month when the number amounted to 90 bankruptcies . The first case of the coronavirus in Sweden was confirmed on February 4 , 2020 .
