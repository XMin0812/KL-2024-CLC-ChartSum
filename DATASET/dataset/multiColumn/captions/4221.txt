According to data gathered on June 30 , 2020 , 72.34 percent of TV shows on Netflix were exclusively available on that platform . Netflix had a higher share of exclusive titles than any other service with the exception of Apple TV+ , though the latter had only recently launched as of the date of survey and featured only original content with no licensed shows or movies available . The services with the most disparity between the share of exclusive movies and TV shows were Hulu , Disney+ , and recently launched HBO Max .
