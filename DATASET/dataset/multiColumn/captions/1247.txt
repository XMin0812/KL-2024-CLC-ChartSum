The statistic represents the costs of electricity production in the United States between 2000 and 2014 , by source . In 2014 , the production cost of electricity generated from coal was 3.29 U.S. dollar cents per kilowatt hour . The average cost of electricity in the U.S. came to 9.43 U.S. dollar cents in 2015 .
