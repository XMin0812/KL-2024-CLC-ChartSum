In 2018 , municipal solid waste generation in the United States reached about 292 million short tons , of which , 146 million tons were considered discards to the landfill or were disposed of in another way . Of this landfilled waste , most of it is from food , yard trimmings , and inorganic waste . Plastic products and packaging are also one of the most commonly disposed waste materials in the United States and has been one of the main subjects of environmental pollution in recent years .
