On April 4 , 2020 , consumers across Russia bought 2.6 thousand metric tons of sugar , which was approximately five times less than the volume purchased on March 17 , 2020 , when the demand peaked due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak . Between March 31 and April 4 , 2020 , the demand did not increase for most product categories .
