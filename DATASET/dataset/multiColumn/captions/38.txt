In October 2020 , online search engine Bing accounted for 6.18 percent of the global search market , while market leader Google had a market share of 88.14 percent . Chinese search engine Baidu 's market share was 0.59 percent . Ever since the introduction of Google Search in 1997 , the worldwide market share of all search engines has been rather lopsided . Google has dominated the search engine market , maintaining an 86.86 percent market share as of July 2020 . The majority of Google revenues are generated through advertising . The company has also expanded its services to mail , productivity tools , enterprise products , mobile devices and other ventures . As a result , Google earned one of the highest tech company revenues in 2019 with roughly 160.74 billion U.S. dollars .
