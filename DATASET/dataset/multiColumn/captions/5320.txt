This statistic presents the number of paid dating subscribers registered to the Match Group as of the third quarter of 2020 , sorted by region . In the last reported quarter , the Match Group 's number of paid dating subscribers in North America amounted to over 5.1 million users . Match Group , formerly owned by IAC , owns and operates online dating platforms such as the eponymous Match.com , OkCupid , Tinder , PlentyofFish and others .
