Due to the COVID-19 outbreak , the Swedish economy was forecast to have a slower development than previously expected . GDP was expected to increase by 0.5 percent in 2020 and by 1.4 percent in 2021 . The COVID-19 outbreak has the highest impact on durable goods in Sweden . HUI Research revised their forecast growth from 3.2 percent in 2019 to 1.5 percent in 2020 . The first case of COVID-19 in Sweden was confirmed on January 31 , 2020 .
