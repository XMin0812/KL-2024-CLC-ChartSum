This statistic illustrates the share of satisfied retail banking customers ( customers with a positive experience ) of the Millenial ( Generation Y ) customers in Western and Central Europe from 2015 to 2016 . In the cases of both Western and Central European countries , Millenials reported lower levels of satisfaction in 2016 than in the previous year . Millenial satisfaction was slightly higher in Central Europe : 51.2 percent of Western European and 59.4 percent of Central European Generation Y customers were satisfied with their retail banking experience as of 2016 .
