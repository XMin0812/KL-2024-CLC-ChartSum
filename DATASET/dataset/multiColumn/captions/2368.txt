This statistic shows the number of Banco Santander branches in selected European countries as of the 31st of December 2018 and 2019 . At the end of 2019 , over 3.2 thousand bank branches operated in Spain . Additionally 616 were open for customers in the United Kingdom ( UK ) as of the end of 2019 . In total , over 1.4 thousand branches closed across Europe between 2018 and 2019 .
