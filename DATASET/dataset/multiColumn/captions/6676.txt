AGC Inc , formerly Asahi Glass Co. , Ltd. , is a Japan-based glass manufacturing company . AGC operates globally in not only the glass industry but also electronics , chemicals , ceramics , and more . The total net sales of AGC decreased from 1522.9 billion yen in 2018 to 1518 billion yen in 2019 . In 2019 , 742.9 billion yen in net sales are reported for the glass sector alone .
