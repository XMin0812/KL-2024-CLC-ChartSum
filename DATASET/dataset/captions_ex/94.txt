As of January 3 , 2021 , there were 26,399,030 confirmed cases of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) across the whole of Europe since the first confirmed case on January 25 . Montenegro has the highest incidence of coronavirus cases among its population in Europe at 7,907 per 100,000 people , followed by a rate of 7,643 in Luxembourg . Czechia has recorded the third highest rate of cases in Europe at 7,012 cases per 100,000 . With over 3.2 million confirmed cases , Russia has been the worst affected country in Europe , which translates into a rate of 2,234 cases per 100,000 population .
