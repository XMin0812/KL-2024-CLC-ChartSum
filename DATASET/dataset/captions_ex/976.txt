This statistic shows the most dangerous cities in the United States in 2019 , by violent crime rate . Four categories of violent crimes were used : murder and non-negligent manslaughter ; forcible rape ; robbery ; and aggravated assault . Only cities with a population of at least 200,000 were considered . In 2019 , around 1965.33 violent crimes per 100,000 residents were reported in Detroit , Michigan . This made Detroit the most dangerous city in the United States .
