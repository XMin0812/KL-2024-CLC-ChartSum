On January 7 , 2021 , there were 658,958 cases of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) reported in Romania . The spread of the virus had a relatively slow evolution in March , the highest daily increase being reported on November 18 . However , given than at that time the number of coronavirus cases was severely impacting Spain and Italy , the Romanian authorities reacted promptly . As a result , an emergency regime was decreed by the President of Romania , Klaus Iohannis , starting on March 16 . Since May 17 , the emergency regime has been replaced with a state of alert .
