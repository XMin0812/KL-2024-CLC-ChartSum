Manchester United are currently the record title winners in the English top flight having won the division a total of 20 times since 1889 . They have also won the world 's oldest domestic soccer competition , the FA Cup , 12 times , most recently in 2016 . This figure is surpassed only by Arsenal FC , who have lifted the cup 13 times . The London team pulled ahead of Manchester United after claiming their 13th title with a 2 - 1 victory against Chelsea in the 2016 - 17 season .
