India 's aviation sector had increasingly emerged as a fast-growing industry . The sector had established itself as an affordable and credible alternative to the tedious and long journeys via road or rail . With a visible growth trend , it was estimated that by 2034 , India would become one of the largest aviation markets in the world . As of 2019 , the passenger carrier IndiGo was the leader in the segment with around 43 percent in the market .
