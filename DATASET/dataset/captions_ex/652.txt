Video streaming giant Netflix had a total net income of over 1.86 billion U.S. dollars in 2019 , whilst the company 's annual revenue reached 20.15 billion U.S. dollars . The number of Netflix 's streaming subscribers worldwide has continued to grow in recent years , reaching 167 million in the fourth quarter of 2019 . Three years earlier , in the fourth quarter of 2015 , the figure stood at 74.76 million subscribers .
