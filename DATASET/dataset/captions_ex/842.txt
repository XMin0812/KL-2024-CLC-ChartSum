The average price of an acrylic fill in nail salons in the United States came at just under 30 U.S. dollars in 2019 . Many different types of manicures are offered at nail salons . One type of manicure is the acrylic nail . Acrylic nails are made by mixing liquid monomer a with powder polymer , which are shaped to the client 's nail . When applied to the client 's nails , they become hard and strong .
