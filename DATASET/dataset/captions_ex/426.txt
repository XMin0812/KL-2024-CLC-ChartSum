This statistics presents the unemployment rate in Australia from 2009 to 2019 , with projections up until 2021 . Unemployment occurs when people are without work , it is also known as joblessness . In order that the prevalence of unemployment can be measured , a calculation is made by the division of the number of unemployed individuals by all individuals currently in the labor force , this yields a percentage rate . In 2019 , about 5.18 percent of the Australian population had been unemployed .
