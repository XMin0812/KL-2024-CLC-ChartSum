California had the most automobile registrations in 2018 : More than 15 million such vehicles were registered in the most populous U.S. federal state . California also leads in terms of annual car sales with almost 887,000 sold in 2018 . Additionally , California residents purchased about 568,400 crossover and sport-utility vehicles ( CUVs and SUVs ) , some 313,600 pickups , and roughly 235,000 luxury and sports cars in 2018 .
