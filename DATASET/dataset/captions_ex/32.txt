How many points does LeBron James have ? In January 2020 , LeBron James passed Kobe Bryant on the NBA all-time scoring list , scoring 29 points in a regular season game against the Philadelphia 76ers . If James continues to produce at about his career average , he has a shot to move up even further and pass Karl Malone with about two more full seasons of games , and even catch Kareem Abdul-Jabbar , the NBA 's current all-time leading scorer . James ' current contract with the Los Angeles Lakers runs through the 2021/22 season .
