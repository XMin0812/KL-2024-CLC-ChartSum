As of January 5 , 2021 , the total number of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) cases in Italy surpassed 2.1 million , more than 569 thousand of which were active cases . Moreover , the number of people who recovered or were discharged from hospital after contracting the virus surpassed 1.5 million , while the number of deceased reached 76,329 .
