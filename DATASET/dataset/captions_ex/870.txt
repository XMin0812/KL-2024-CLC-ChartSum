As of November 2020 , the price of rubber amounted to 2.30 U.S. dollars per kilogram . The average annual price of natural rubber at the Singapore Commodity Exchange ( one of the key global commodity exchanges for rubber ) reached a high of 4.82 U.S. dollars per kilogram in 2011 . Since then , the price decreased to an annual average of 1.64 U.S. dollars per kilogram in 2019 .
