The Scottish Premiership is the highest football league in Scotland . In the past 20 years , the Celtic FC and the Rangers FC have been the only two teams to win the league , which was only established in 2013 . Beforehand the highest league was the Scottish Premier League . Since the new establishment , Celtic FC has been the only club to win the league . This is also reflected in the average annual player salary , which is highest at Celtic FC with roughly 896 thousand British pounds , followed by Rangers FC with approximately 650 British pounds .
