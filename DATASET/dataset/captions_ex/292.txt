Canada 's appeal as an immigration destination has been increasing over the past two decades , with a total of 284,387 people immigrating to the country Between July 1 , 2019 and June 30 , 2020 . This figure is a decrease from 2018 - 2019 , when about 313,601 immigrants came to Canada , but is much more than the estimated 199,170 immigrants arriving in 2002 - 2003 .
