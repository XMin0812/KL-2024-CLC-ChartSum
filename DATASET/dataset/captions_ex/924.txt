The statistic shows gross domestic product ( GDP ) in Brazil from 1984 to 2019 , with projections up until 2021 . Gross domestic product denotes the aggregate value of all services and goods produced within a country in any given year . GDP is an important indicator of a country 's economic power . In 2019 , Brazil 's gross domestic product amounted to around 3.48 trillion international dollars . In comparison to the GDP of the other BRIC countries India , Russia and China , Brazil was ranked second that year .
