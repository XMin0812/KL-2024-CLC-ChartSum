The number of cases of the coronavirus disease that originated in the Chinese city of Wuhan ( COVID-19 ) in Spain amounted to 1.98 million as of January 5 , 2020 . Since Spain confirmed its first case , the authorities have confirmed about 51.4 thousand deaths as a result of complications from coronavirus , most of them registered in the autonomous community of the Spanish capital Madrid with 11.88 thousand . The impact of the outbreak of this disease was such that the global stock markets were affected . The Spanish IBEX-35 was no exception to that , decreasing by 2500 stock market points during the first days of March .
