In 2020 , it was estimated that around 222 per 10,000 children in the United States had autism spectrum disorder , one of the highest prevalence rates in the world . Autism spectrum disorder ( ASD ) includes a variety of disorders . The `` spectrum '' denotes the range of symptoms as well as their severity . Symptoms can include difficulties with speech , social communication and interaction , repetitive behavior , sensory issues , and hypersensitivity to stimuli .
