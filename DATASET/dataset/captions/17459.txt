This statistic illustrates the share of smokers in the Italian population aged 14 years and older in Italy as of 2018 , broken down by region . According to data , the highest percentage of smokers in the Italian population was registered in Lazio , where roughly 23.9 people every 100 residents were smokers . The region with the lowest share of individuals smoking cigarettes was Calabria with around 16.3 percent of smokers in the population .
