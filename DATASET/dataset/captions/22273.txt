Sales from grocery delivery and pickup in the United States have surged in 2020 as a result of the COVID-19 outbreak . In August 2019 , sales were 1.2 billion U.S. dollars and grew to 4 billion dollars in March of 2020 , a 233 percent increase . Sales grew by an additional 80 percent from March to June before declining to 5.7 billion dollars in August .
