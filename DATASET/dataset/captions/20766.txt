As of March 2020 , the metropolitan region of Santiago is the one that have the largest number of hospitals in Chile , with 772 medical centers . It was followed by Los Lagos , with 383 hospitals . As of March 19 , 2020 , Chile was the Latin American country with the second largest number of confirmed cases of coronavirus disease ( COVID-19 ) , caused by the novel coronavirus ( SARS-CoV-2 ) .
