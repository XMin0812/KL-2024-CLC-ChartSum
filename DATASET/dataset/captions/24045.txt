In 2017 , there were 47 domestic mergers and acquisitions ( M & A ) deals in Belgium . A slowdown in M & A activity was forecast , however . By 2020 , domestic deals were expected to reach a number of 38 . Increasing market confidence , however , led to a higher-than-expected increase in the value of M & A deals in Belgium in 2018 . This confidence stemmed from growing business investment , an improving labor market and rising intra EU-trade , despite macro-economic uncertainty .
