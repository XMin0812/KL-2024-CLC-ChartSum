According to the Dutch business registry , the Netherlands gained roughly 1,300 new small and medium enterprises between early 2019 and early 2020 . The source defined SME ( or mkb , a Dutch abbreviation of midden- en kleinbedrijf ) as a company having between two to 249 employees , as well as companies with one employee that are not classified as a freelancer/self-employed person . SMEs were the Netherlands ' second largest type of business enterprise .
