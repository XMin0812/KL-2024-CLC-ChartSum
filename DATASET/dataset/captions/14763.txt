The statistic displays the average game attendance in the Football League Championship in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from season 2010/2011 to season 2019/2020 . During the 2013/2014 season , the average game attendance in the Football League Championship was 16,647 attendees . In recent years the average attendance has been increasing , reaching 20,181 in 2019 . The average attendance fell to 14,949 in 2020 due to the impact of the COVID-19 containment measures .
