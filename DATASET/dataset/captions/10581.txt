This statistic displays the average hourly wage in the Netherlands in 2018 , by working hours , in euros . As of 2018 , the average hourly wage in the Netherlands increased with weekly working hours . Employees who worked between one and 12 hours per week earned the lowest hourly wage , at roughly 13,50 on average , whereas employees working 35 hours or more received the highest hourly wage of approximately 24,50 euros per hour .
