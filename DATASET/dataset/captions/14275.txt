With a score of 82.8 , Tsinghua University in China secured the enviable top spot in the Asian university rankings in 2020 . This higher education institution beat the likes of Seoul National University and the University of Tokyo to the number one position . Interestingly , the overall scores of the leading universities in the Asia Pacific region have increased significantly throughout recent years , thus giving the region a reputation as being a home to some of the world 's most prestigious universities .
