This statistic shows the number of persons with a migration background in Germany in 2019 , by gender . That year , the number of men with a migration background living in Germany amounted to over 10.85 million . According to the source , a person is considered as having a migration background when they or at least one parent do not have German citizenship by law . This definition includes the following persons : 1 . Immigrated and non-immigrated foreigners . 2 . Immigrated and non-immigrated naturalized citizens . 3 . Late emigrants . 4 . Descendants born with German citizenship within the three groups named above .
