As of March 2020 , Sporting Clube de Portugal , or often referred to as Sporting Lisbon , is the European football club with the most appearances in the UEFA Europa League with a total of 33 appearances , followed by Belgian football Club Brugge with 30 appearances . When it comes to total matches in the Europa League however , Sporting Lisbon ranked third very close with Club Brugge , behind FC Inter Milan at 185 matches played .
