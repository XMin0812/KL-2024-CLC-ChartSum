The Philippines has a steadily growing economy , with a gross domestic product ( GDP ) that reached over 1,027.19 billion international dollars in 2019 . Gross domestic product ( GDP ) denotes the aggregate value of all services and goods produced within a country in any given year . GDP is an important indicator of a country 's economic power . The GDP of the Philippines is expected to increase substantially to over 1,140 billion U.S. dollars by 2021 .
