Because of the outbreak of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) , many countries around the world were put into lockdown . In February , average levels of PM2.5 pollution in the Chinese city of Wuhan were 35.1 micrograms per cubic meter . This was a reduction of approximately 44 percent when compared to the same period in 2019 .
