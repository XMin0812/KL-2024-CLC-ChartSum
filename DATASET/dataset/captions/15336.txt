In 2019 , Quebecor , a media and communications company based in Quebec , Canada , generated revenue of over 4.29 billion Canadian dollars . This marked an increase from the previous year , but the coronavirus is likely to impact revenue for 2020 . The outbreak led to a curtailment of some of the company 's operations in early 2020 , leading to temporary lay-offs of ten percent of Quebecor 's workforce .
