The statistic illustrates the total amount of investments of the private equity firms in Baltic companies from 2007 to 2016 . Private equity is the OTC provision of equity capital through private or institutional investments with the participation of companies in another company for a limited time in order to generate financial benefits . It can be seen that total private equity investments fluctuated overall during the period under observation , reaching a value of 73.79 million euros as of 2016 . The largest total value of private equity investments was found in 2007 , when total private equity investment of 78.54 million euros was recorded .
