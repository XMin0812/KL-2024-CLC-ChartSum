The statistic illustrates the value of ten British pound value banknotes issued by the Bank of England in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2003/2004 to 2018/2019 . It can be seen that the value of ten British pound banknotes issued fluctuated during the period under observation , reaching a total of over 3.5 billion British pounds of notes as of 2019 . The lowest value of ten British pound banknotes issued in one year was over 2.1 billion British pounds of notes , in 2013/14 .
