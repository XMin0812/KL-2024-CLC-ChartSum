The number of divorces in Sweden peaked in 2013 , when almost 27 thousand couples got divorced . Since then the divorce number decreased until 2018 , when it again increased and amounted to almost 25 thousand divorces . As of 2019 , the number of divorces amounted to 25,408 . A similar trend can be seen in the neighbor country Norway , where the number of divorces decreased for several years but increased again in 2017 .
