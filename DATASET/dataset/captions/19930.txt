The statistic shows trend-indicator-value of Australian arms imports in 2018 , by country . In 2018 , the TIV of Australian arms imports from the United States reached about 918 million . The TIV is based on the known unit production costs of a core set of weapons and is intended to represent the transfer of military resources rather than the financial value of the transfer . The depicted import value is only an indicator and does not correspond to the actual financial value of the transfers .
