This statistic shows the total annual turnover of the retail sale of spare parts and accessories for all types of vehicle and sales of bicycles in the United Kingdom from 2008 to 2018 . Over this period the retail turnover of such products fluctuated , peaking at approximately 1.6 billion British pounds in 2015 . The lowest point for the turnover of spare parts and accessories for all vehicle types was in 2009 , where turnover was only 924 million British pounds .
