Computer tomography ( CT ) scanners are vital medical technology used in the diagnosis and monitoring of various medical conditions . CT scanner utilize x-ray technology to make images of bones , vessels and other internal organs . As of 2019 , Japan had the largest number of CT scanners per one million population with 111.49 scanners per million people . The country with the second most scanners at that time was Australia with 70.25 scanners per million people .
