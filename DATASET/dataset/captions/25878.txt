HTK Lentes Oftálmicas , a Brazilian company headquartered in Rio de Janeiro that owns Haytek , an eyewear and contact lens brand , was one of the fastest growing companies in Latin America between 2015 and 2018 . In that period , HTK 's revenue grew 90.4 percent per year , based on its compound annual growth rate ( CAGR ) . Valemobi Consultoria Empresarial , a business services company , ranked second with a CAGR of 75.5 percent . The hydroelectric power producer and distributor Omega Geração came in third , with an annual revenue growth of 73 percent .
