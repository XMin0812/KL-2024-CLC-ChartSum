As of 2019 , household monthly expenditures on food and non-alcoholic beverages among young Italian consumers varied significantly according to the products considered . Indeed , it was found that consumers between 18 and 34 spent on average 49.05 euros per month on meat , while only 10.2 euros on coffee , tea , and cocoa . It was also found that a considerable amount of money was spent on bread and cereals ( 41.88 euros per person ) .
