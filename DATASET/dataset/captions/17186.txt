On January 8 , 2021 , 2,907 new cases of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) were reported in Hungary . Over the observed period , the highest number of new cases within a day was registered on November 29 . In total , there were 337,743 confirmed cases of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in the country .
