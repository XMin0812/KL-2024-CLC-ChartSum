This statistic shows the number of employees at Praxair between 2007 and 2017 . Praxair , Inc. is the largest industrial gases company in North and South America and one of the largest worldwide . In 2017 , the company had 26,461 employees . As of October 31 , 2018 , a merger of equals between Praxair and Linde was completed . The newly formed company is known as Linde plc .
