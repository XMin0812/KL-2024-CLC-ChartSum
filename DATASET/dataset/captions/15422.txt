As of September 25 , 2020 , 70 percent of Singaporean respondents stated that they had been avoiding public places during the COVID-19 outbreak , up from 44 percent on Feb 21 , 2020 . Singapore has since gradually opened up after lockdown measures were implemented to curb the spread of COVID-19 .
