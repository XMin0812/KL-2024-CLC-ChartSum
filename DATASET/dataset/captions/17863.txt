Land-based sports betting was legalized in the U.S. state of West Virginia as of March 9 , 2018 . This means it is now possible to play college sports betting , MLB sports betting , and NBA sports betting in the state . In September 2020 , West Virginia generated 4.6 million U.S. dollars from sports betting , up from the previous month 's total of 2.34 million U.S. dollars .
