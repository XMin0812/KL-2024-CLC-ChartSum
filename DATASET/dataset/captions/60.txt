The number of cases  of the coronavirus disease that originated in the Chinese city of Wuhan ( COVID-19 ) in Spain amounted to approximately 1.98 million as of January 5 , 2020 . The virus spread to all Spanish regions , with the community of Madrid suffering the highest number of cases with over400 thousand people . The impact in the regions of Catalonia and Andalusia was also significant , with 367 thousand and 270 thousand cases respectively , figures that stood far from those of the outbreak in Madrid .
