At a rate of 16.26 percent in the third quarter of 2020 , Spain was one of the countries with the highest unemployment rates in the European Union . As of the third quarter of 2005 , the unemployment rate in Spain was at roughly 8.4 percent , in the same quarter of 2012 it increased resulting in a rate of around 24.8 . As of the second quarter of 2017 the unemployment rate was 9.72 percent lower than its peak , the peak occurred in the first quarter of 2013 before fluctuating somewhat in a downward trend .
