This statistic charts the number of live births in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 1961 to 1990 . There were a number of fluctuations during this period . After Peaking in the first year , at 901,648 , the amount of live births decreased each year until hitting a low for this period in 1977 of 657,038 . This can perhaps be linked to the 1973 - 75 recession that the UK experienced . To see the same statistic for the period 1900 to 1930 , click here .
