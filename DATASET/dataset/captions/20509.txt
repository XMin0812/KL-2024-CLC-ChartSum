This statistic shows the total annual turnover of the retail sales of games , toys and hobbies ( including video games ) in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2008 to 2018 . Over this period turnover from the retail sale of such products has generally decreased , falling from 7.96 billion British pounds in 2008 to around 7 billion in 2013 . For 2018 the turnover increased greatly to 9.7 billion British pounds .
