In 2019 , China installed the largest share of the world 's new solar photovoltaic ( PV ) capacity , at 27 percent of the total capacity . This was a smaller share than it had in 2018 , when despite reduced feed-in-tariff ( FIT ) payments and certain policy changes , China was able to surpass its national solar capacity target for 2020 . In comparison , the United States and India installed 12 and nine percent each of the world 's 115 gigawatts of capacity additions . The United States added 13.3 gigawatt in that year .
