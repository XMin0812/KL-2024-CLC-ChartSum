This statistic illustrates the Common Equity Tier 1 ( CET1 ) ratio of the United Kingdom ( UK ) based Nationwide Building Society in their financial years from 2013 to 2020 . It can be seen that the Common Equity Tier 1 ( CET1 ) ratio steadily increased between 2013 and 2018 , reaching a value of 32.4 percent as of 2019 . The lowest Common Equity Tier 1 ratio was found in 2013 , when a ratio of 9.1 percent was recorded .
