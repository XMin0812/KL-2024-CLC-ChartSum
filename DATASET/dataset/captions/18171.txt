As of April 2020 , 70 percent of Finns considered the restrictive measures introduced by the government to be appropriate and timely . Less than one fifth of the respondents considered the measures too loose , while ten percent said they were unnecessarily strict . The first COVID-19 case in Finland was confirmed on January 29 , 2020 . For further information about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , please visit our dedicated Facts and Figures page .
