The highest-paid female entertainer in 2019 was Taylor Swift , with an annual income of an eyewatering 185 million U.S. dollars . Kylie Jenner ranked second with earnings of 170 million , followed by J.K. Rowling . Also on the ranking were music artists Katy Perry , Pink , and Rihanna , as well as Canadian singer Celine Dion . ' A Star Is Born ' actress and international popstar Lady Gaga also featured , with earnings of 39.5 million U.S. dollars .
