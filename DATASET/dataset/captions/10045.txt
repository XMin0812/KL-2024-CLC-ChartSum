On Sunday , January 3 , 2021 , the highest self-isolation index among Russian cities with over one million inhabitants was measured in Omsk at 3.6 points , indicating that most people were at home . In the capital Moscow , where most COVID-19 cases in Russia were recorded , the index reach 2.9 points . The non-working period in Russia ended on May 12 , 2020 .
