According to the results of a survey among Indians on the impact of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) and its consequent lockdown , a majority of respondents reported no change in their use of digital payments . However , 33 percent said they used digital payments more than before , while nine percent made online payments exclusively . India went into lockdown on March 25 , 2020 , the largest in the world , restricting 1.3 billion people , extended until May 3 , 2020 .
