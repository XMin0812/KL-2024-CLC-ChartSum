The operating revenue generated by the Japanese air transportation company ANA Holdings Inc. amounted to approximately 1.97 trillion Japanese yen as of March 31 , 2020 , down from its former peak of about 2.06 trillion in the previous fiscal year . The holding is the parent company of All Nippon Airways ( ANA ) , Japan 's largest airline , which operating revenues from air transportation decreased due to diminishing demand in light of COVID-19 .
