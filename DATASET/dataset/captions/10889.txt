Daimler 's figures for 2019 indicate that the company 's EBIT dropped by more than 60 percent to some 4.3 billion euros ( or about 4.8 billion U.S. dollars ) , compared with around 11 billion euros ( or about 12.4 billion U.S. dollars ) in 2018 . The 2019 figure was adjusted for additional expenses related to recalls of diesel engine-equipped Mercedes-Benz cars and vans in various markets . Daimler stated that such expenses were in the range of one to 1.5 billion euros . Due to differences in size and weight , the vans segment was affected more than the cars segment .
