The statistic illustrates the annual revenue of the fast food service industry in France from 2011 to 2016 . In 2016 , the annual revenue of the fast food service industry amounted to approximately 1.9 billion euros , which was roughly 100 million euros more than the year before . Furthermore , 2014 was the best performing year for the fast food service industry in the last four years . Revenue was the lowest in 2011 , with an approximate 1.1 billion euros of revenue .
