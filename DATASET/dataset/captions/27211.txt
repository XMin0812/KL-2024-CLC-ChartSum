This survey was aimed at assessing the favourite clothing retailers among shoppers in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in the year 2016 . M & S was the most popular clothing retailer , accumulating to a proportion of mentions of 13.7 percent , folllowed by Next , accounting for a share of mentions of 11.8 percent . Primark was ranked third , standing at 5.7 percent , whereas online warehouse Amazon took the tenth place with 2.4 percent .
