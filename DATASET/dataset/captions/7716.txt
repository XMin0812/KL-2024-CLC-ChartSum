A survey carried out in Great Britain in February 2020 found that 23 percent of respondents believe that the novel Coronavirus represents a major threat to public health in the country . A further 37 percent of respondents think that Coronavirus is a moderate threat to public health , while on the other hand 26 percent believe there is only a minimal risk from the virus . There has been widespread interest in the novel Coronavirus since the virus began to spread , and over 859 thousand cases have been confirmed worldwide as of April 1 , 2020 , with 1,789 deaths due to coronavirus occurring in the United Kingdom .
