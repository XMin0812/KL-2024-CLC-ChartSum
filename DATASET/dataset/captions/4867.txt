As of 2019 , the Dutch national men 's football team was positioned 12th on the FIFA World Ranking . This is an increase compared to the previous year , when the national team was ranked 14th . The best position was reached in 2010 and 2011 , when The Dutch Eleven reached a second place , after reaching the final of the World Cup 2010 in South Africa . The national women 's football team , on the other hand , ranked 3rd in 2019 , behind Germany and current World Champion United States . The Dutch women were very successful in the last two major tournaments with a European title in 2017 and a second place during the World Cup France in 2019 .
