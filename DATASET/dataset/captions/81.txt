How many iPhones have been sold in 2018 ? In the fourth quarter of 2018 alone , Apple sold 46.89 million iPhones worldwide , a figure which slightly outpaced its sales from the corresponding quarter of 2017 . In entire 2018 , 217.72 million iPhones were shipped worldwide . Apple typically releases at least one new version of its iPhone each year , a strategy which has helped the company consistently pull in quarterly sales figures in the tens of millions .
