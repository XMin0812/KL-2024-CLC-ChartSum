PetSmart , formerly known as PetFood Warehouse , is an American retail chain which focuses on the sale of pet products as well as services , such as grooming and training . PetSmart was the leading pet retailer in the United States in 2019 in terms of market share . Their revenue reached approximately 6.49 billion U.S. dollars in the United States in the 2018/19 fiscal year ending on January 31 .
