In 2019 , Hewlett Packard Enterprise ( HPE ) had 61.6 thousand employees worldwide . HPE is an American technology company which was formed on November 1 , 2015 , following the break-up of the IT giant , Hewlett-Packard Company ( HP Co ) . Initially producing a range of electronic test equipment , HP Co made its name in becoming one of the leading manufacturers of personal computers ( PCs ) worldwide , and a global manufacturer of servers , storage devices , and networking equipment , as well as delivering a variety of IT services for both consumers and enterprises . In 2015 along with the HPE spin off , the other company that emerged from the split was HP Inc .
