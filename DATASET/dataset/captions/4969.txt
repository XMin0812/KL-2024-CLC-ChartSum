The highest number of cases per 100 thousand inhabitants among Russian regions was observed in the Altai Republic , measuring at approximately 5.1 thousand as of November 30 , 2020 . The Yamalo-Nenets Autonomous Okrug had the second highest incidence rate , with 4.8 thousand infected per 100 thousand population .
