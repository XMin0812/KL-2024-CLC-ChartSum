JD Sports Fashion , the UK 's leading sports and footwear brand , closed the financial year 2019/2020 with an increased number of retail stores through new acquisitions . As highlighted in this statistic , from February 2020 , JD acted as parent company to 2,203 stores in total , including brands such as Perry Sport and Aktiesport in the Netherlands , Glue in Australia , and Hot-T in South Korea . With new retailers acquired worldwide , JD Sports expanded its retail space across its sports fashion and outdoors retail divisions in 2019 .
