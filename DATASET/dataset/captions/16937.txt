This statistic displays the share of generational groups who love to try new things as soon as possible in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in 2017 . When this statement was put to them , the generational group who agreed with it the most was the Gen Z group with 54 percent agreeing . The group who agreed with this the least was the Boomers group , with only 18 percent agreeing that they love to try new things as soon as possible .
