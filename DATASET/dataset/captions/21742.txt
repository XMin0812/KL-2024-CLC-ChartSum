This statistic illustrates the total assets of Case New Holland ( CNH ) from 2011 to 2019 . CNH is an industrial and agricultural machinery company , formed by a merger between Case and New Holland , with Fiat Industrial also later merging into CNH . The company also owns the Austrian tractor manufacturer Steyr and Italian industrial vehicle producer Iveco , among others . In 2019 , CNH held approximately 49.2 billion U.S. dollars worth of assets .
