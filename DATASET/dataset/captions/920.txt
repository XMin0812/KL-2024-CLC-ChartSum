The state of health in Spain is facing an arduous challenge with the emergence of the COVID-19 . The country does not seem to be able to flatten the curve of people infected , despite the government 's efforts to curb the spread by barring the population from all social interaction . The confirmed number of cases of the coronavirus disease that originated in the Chinese city of Wuhan ( COVID-19 ) in Spain amounted to 1.98 million as of January 5 , 2021 . The virus spread to all Spanish regions , with Madrid suffering the highest number of cases with over 400 thousand people .
