The effects of the coronavirus on inflation in the Netherlands are two-fold , as it increased in both March and April 2020 , but showed a small decline in May . This becomes apparent when looking at the CPI inflation on a month-to-month basis , and how much this changed during that time . May 2020 stands out when compared to the same month in previous years , as this month normally shows a small increase in CPI inflation . This changed in 2020 , however , as it become one of the few month , outside of December or January , with a decline .
