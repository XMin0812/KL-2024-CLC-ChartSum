This statistic shows the number of Mitsubishi L200 light goods vehicles registered in Great Britain between 2000 and 2019 . The figures represent a running cumulative total . The Mitsubishi L200 was one of the most popular LGV models in Britain by 2019 . The number of registered vehicles grew from around 11,600 in 2000 to some 105,300 registered vehicles by 2019 .
