The cruise industry is one of the worst-hit by the coronavirus pandemic . As of April 5 , 2020 , about three-quarters of the global cruise ship capacity was expected to resume operations by May 11 . According to cruise lines ' return plans , the global cruise capacity , which stands at around half a million passengers , will be restored in full by July 15 , 2020 .
