The retail industry encompasses the journey of a good or service . This typically starts with the manufacture of a product and ends with said product being purchased by a consumer from a retailer . As a result of globalization and various trade agreements between markets and countries , many retailers are capable of doing business on a global scale . As of 2018 , Wal-Mart was the world 's leading retailer with revenues of about 514 billion U.S. dollars .
