Italy 's unemployment rate almost reached 9.1 percent in the first quarter of 2020 , the lowest value since 2012 . The regions with the highest unemployment rates were situated in the South . Sicily , Calabria , and Campania registered rates from 15.6 percent to 19.2 percent , a large difference when compared to the Northern regions . For instance , four percent of people in Lombardy were unemployed , the lowest share nationwide .
