In 2019 , the per capita pork consumption in Indonesia was approximately one kilogram . In 2025 , this was forecasted to increase slightly , to 1.03 kilograms per capita . Indonesia 's pork consumption in 2019 was well below the OECD average of around 23.5 kilograms per capita . Fish and poultry were more commonly consumed meat types in Indonesia , where the majority of the population is Muslim , and for whom pig meat is forbidden .
