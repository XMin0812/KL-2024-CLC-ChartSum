This statistic displays the share of UK consumers spending more on clothing and footwear in the United Kingdom from 4th quarter 2011 to 4th quarter 2015 . According to Deloitte 's consumer tracker , consumer spending in this category increased in 2014 for the first time during the period in consideration . In quarter 4 of 2014 , 1 percent of consumers had spent more on clothing and footwear in the past three months .
