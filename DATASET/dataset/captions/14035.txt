The statistic shows gross domestic product ( GDP ) in Papua New Guinea from 2009 to 2015 , with projections up until 2021 . Gross domestic product ( GDP ) denotes the aggregate value of all services and goods produced within a country in any given year . GDP is an important indicator of a country 's economic power . In 2015 , Papua New Guinea 's gross domestic product amounted to around 28.75 billion international dollars .
