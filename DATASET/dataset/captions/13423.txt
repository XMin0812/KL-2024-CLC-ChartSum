In 2018 , current health expenditure in Ireland amounted to 22.5 billion euros . This figure has generally increased since 2011 when expenditure on health was 18.4 billion euros . When broken down by function , most of the spending in 2018 was taken up by the 12.54 billion euros spent on curative and rehabilitative care . An additional 4.82 billion euros was spent on long term healthcare and 3.1 billion euros on medical goods .
