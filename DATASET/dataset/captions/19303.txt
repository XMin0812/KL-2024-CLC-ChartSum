Roman Catholic was the most common religion affiliation in Bolivia in 2018 . In a survey carried out between July and August of 2018 , approximately 70 percent of Bolivian respondents claimed to be of catholic faith , whereas the second most chosen religion was evangelism , with 11.6 percent of the people interviewed . Nearly seven percent of respondents answered they did n't profess any religion and 0.8 percent defined themselves as atheists .
