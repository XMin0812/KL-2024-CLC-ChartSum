This statistic presents the total number of Macy 's Inc. stores worldwide from 2015 to 2019 . In 2019 , Macy 's Inc. had a total of 839 stores . Macy 's Inc. is an American company in the fashion and apparel industry , headquartered in Cincinnati , United States . It operates three branded stores : Macy 's , Bloomingdale 's , and bluemercury .
