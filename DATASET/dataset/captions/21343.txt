This statistic represents the net income of Dow Corning Corp. from 2009 to the first quarter of 2016 . In 2015 , the company 's net income stood at some 563 million U.S. dollars . Dow Corning Corporation is a company that deals with silicone and silicon-based technology . In 2003 , Dow Corning acquired Simcala Inc. , one of the largest U.S. silicon mining companies .
