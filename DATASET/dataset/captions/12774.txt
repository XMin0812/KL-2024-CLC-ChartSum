Since 2014 , the revenue of the fitness market in Germany has increased from roughly 4.7 billion to 5.51 billion euros . Germany has the highest revenue in Europe . Between 2018 and 2019 , the revenue of the German fitness increased by 180 million euros , an increase of 3.4 percent . This rate of growth is significantly greater than the rate of in GDP growth across the entire German economy , which over this same period exhibited only 0.57 percent growth .
