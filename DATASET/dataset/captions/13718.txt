The largest nuclear reactor worldwide based on gross capacity was Taishan , in China , with gross capacity of 1,750 megawatts as of December 31 , 2019 . Further , two out of three leading nuclear power reactors were located in France . Civaux and Chooz had a gross capacity of 1,561 and 1,560 megawatts respectively . At that time , there were 443 operational nuclear reactors and five nuclear reactors under construction in the world .
