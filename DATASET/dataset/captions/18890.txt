This statistic illustrates the construction output index of all work in Great Britain from 2000 to 2019 . This index measures the construction output of all work ( new housing , infrastructure , repair and maintenance ) within Great Britain . This index increased from 2000 until 2004 , fluctuating in the following years until it considerably dropped in 2009 . Since 2013 , the construction output index in Great Britain has been steadily increasing , peaking in 2019 at 108.4 index points .
