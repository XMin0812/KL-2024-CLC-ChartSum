In the 2020 fiscal year , HP Inc reached net earnings of 2.8 billion U.S. dollars , down from 5.33 billion U.S. dollars in 2018 , the highest net earnings in over five years . In 2012 , then HP Company 's net earning plummeted to negative 12.6 billion U.S. dollars , as the company began a drastic reshaping period whose results included a division into HP Inc and HP Enterprise Company .
