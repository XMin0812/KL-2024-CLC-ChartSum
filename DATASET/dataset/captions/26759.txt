Primary energy production from landfill gas has oscillated in Greece , peaking at 73 metric kilotons of oil equivalent in 2016 . Between 2013 and 2018 , figures decreased by three metric kilotons of oil equivalent . In 2018 , Greece 's primary energy production from landfill gas amounted to 65 metric kilotons of oil equivalent , while the 28 European Union members had a primary energy production of 2,429 metric kilotons of oil equivalent .
