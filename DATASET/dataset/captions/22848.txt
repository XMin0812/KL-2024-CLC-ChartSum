Due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , supermarket sales of hand sanitizers in Belgium increased by nearly 500 percent compared to the previous year . To a much lesser extent , sales of toilet paper also increased by 10.5 percent . On the other hand , food products such as pasta and rice also rose in the country . However , these figures were collected prior to the confinement of the Belgian population ( 18th of March 2020 ) , leaving the possibility of further increase .
