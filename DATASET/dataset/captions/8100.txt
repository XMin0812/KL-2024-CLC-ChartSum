The internet giant Google has made total donation of more than 900 million U.S. dollars to support businesses , organizations and healthcare workers to combat the novel coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) - most of the donation will come in form of ad grants and ad credits . The COVID-19 pandemic has had a global impact on many different industries . Over the past weeks , many of the leading technology companies have announced financial contributions in support of resolving the COVID-19 worldwide crisis .
