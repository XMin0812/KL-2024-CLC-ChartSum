This statistic shows the monthly trend in the volume of automotive fuel sold in Great Britain from January 2016 to June 2020 . The figures are seasonally adjusted estimates , measured using the Retail Sales Index ( RSI ) and published in index form with a reference year of 2016 equal to 100 . Over the time period observed , automotive fuel sales have generally increased , except in the months from March to June 2020 , where a low of 40.7 was reached in April 2020 .
