This statistic shows the total sales for H.Samuel in the United Kingdom between the fiscal years 2014 to 2019 . Since 2016 the company 's sales have decreased slightly , but with the 2018 fiscal year went up to 278 million British pounds . H.Samuel is one of the UK brands of the Signet Jeweler 's Group , one of the largest diamond jeweler retailers in the world . The company specializes in the retailing of jewelry , watches and associated services . Along with H.Samuel , the Signet Group also owns the Ernest Jones jewelry brand in the UK .
