This statistic shows Syngenta AG 's sales worldwide from 2013 to 2019 . Syngenta is one of the largest global agribusiness companies . It is based in Basel , Switzerland , and markets seeds and pesticides for crop production . In 2014 , the company 's global sales amounted to around 15.13 billion U.S. dollars . In 2019 , that figure stood at 13.58 billion U.S. dollars .
