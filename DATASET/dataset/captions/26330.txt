This statistic presents the Corruption Perception Index score obtained by Uruguay from 2012 to 2019 . This index is a composite indicator that includes data on the perception of corruption in areas such as : bribery of public officials , kickbacks in public procurement , embezzlement of state funds , and effectiveness of governments ' anti-corruption efforts . The highest possible score in perception of corruption is 0 , whereas a score of 100 indicates that no corruption is perceived in the respective country . In Uruguay , the corruption perception index score in 2019 was at 71 points , a one point increase from 2018 . Uruguay ranks as the country with the lowest corruption perception in Latin America .
