This statistic represents the number of COSCO Shipping container ships as of October 12 , 2020 . The shipping and logistics services supplier with headquarters in Beijing had a fleet of 499 ships carrying about 3 million TEUs . In July 2017 , COSCO put in a bid to buy Hong Kong based Orient Overseas Container Line ( OOCL ) as well as its parent company . The merger was completed in June 2018 .
