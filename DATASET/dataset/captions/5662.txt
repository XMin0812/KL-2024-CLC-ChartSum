As of 2019 , the Luxembourg national men 's football team was ranked 96th on the FIFA World Ranking and had an average position of 133 since the World Ranking creation . The best position the country held was in 2017 with an 83rd position , whereas the lowest ranking was in 2006 when the national team was placed 186th . The Luxembourg national women 's team was ranked 113th in 2019 . This is a decrease compared to the previous year , when the national team was placed 104th .
