Rocket League is a sports video game developed by Psyonix and released in July 2015 on Xbox One , Windows and PlayStation 4 . The game combines elements from both racing and soccer games - each player controls a vehicle which is used to strike a ball with the aim of scoring in the opposing goal . In the five years since its launch , five billion matches of Rocket League have been played , with gamers scoring a combined total of 29 billion goals .
