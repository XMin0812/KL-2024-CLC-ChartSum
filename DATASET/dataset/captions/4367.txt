As of 2020 , Robert Lewandowski is the all time leader in goals scored for the national football team of Poland with a total of 61 goals scored , followed by Wlodzimierz Lubanski with 44 goals . While Lubanski has long since retired from his career with the national team back in 1980 , Lewandowski actually played in the most recent 2018 World Cup and at the time had the highest market value of players in the team by far .
