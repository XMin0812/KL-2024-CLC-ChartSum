Due to the coronavirus ( Covid-19 ) , it has been estimated that global emissions will fall by 0.5 to 2.2 percent from 2019 levels , based on the economy recovering in Q3 and Q4 2020 . This projection is based on a range of scenarios that look into the economic impact of the pandemic .
