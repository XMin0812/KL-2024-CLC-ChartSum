What is the revenue of Bol.com ? In 2019 , net consumer sales on the online marketplace ( including both the Netherlands and Belgium ) were worth 2.8 billion euros , an increase of more than 30 percent to the previous year . Net consumer online sales are online sales including sales by third parties on the platform . Much like on Amazon , sales on the Dutch website can either come from the retailer itself , third parties or from a second-hand goods section . As of 2018 , 36 percent of survey respondents in the Netherlands reported to use this second-hand part of the website .
