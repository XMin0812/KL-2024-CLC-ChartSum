The weekly number of people tested for the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Sweden overall increased during the period . The number of tested individuals in week 49 ( November 30- December 6 ) amounted to 261 thousand . In total , around 357 thousand individuals had been confirmed infected with the virus in the country as of December 16 , 2020 .
