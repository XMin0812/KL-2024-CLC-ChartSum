This statistic shows the abortion rate per thousand women in Sweden from 2009 to 2019 . Within the given time period , the abortion rate remained relatively stable , with 20.9 abortions per thousand women as the highest value ( measured in 2010 , 2011 , and 2015 ) . The lowest rate was measured in 2018 and 2019 , and it amounted to 19.2 abortions per thousand women .
