The Community of Madrid is the epicenter of the COVID-19 crisis in Spain , with its increasing cases rapidly outnumbering those of the rest of the country . This has led to an overwhelming number of admissions due to coronavirus disease in Madrid hospitals , both in the capital and satellite cities . Among them , however , the La Paz/Carlos III /Canto Blanco hospital was the most affected , with 95 admitted under observation as of March 12 , 2020 .
