During the 2020 Chinese Spring Festival holiday , when the mojority of Chinese people were confined to their homes due to the COVID-19 epidemic , the use of mobile internet apps experienced a strong increase . The short video category , which includes apps like Douyin ( the Chinese version of TikTok ) and Kuaishou , saw its daily active user ( DAU ) number increase by around 82 million in comparison to the period prior to the epidemic .
