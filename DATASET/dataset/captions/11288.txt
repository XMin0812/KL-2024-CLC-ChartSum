This statistic shows the operating profit of the Debenhams retail brand from the financial years 2010 to 2018 . Within this period of time , operating profits of Debenhams have declined , reaching a low point of 43.4 million British pounds in the year ending September 1st 2018 . Debenhams is a British retailer selling clothing , furniture and other household items in a department store format . The brand owns and operates stores in the UK , Ireland and Denmark as well as franchises in other international locations .
