First case of a person infected with the novel coronavirus in Guatemala was reported on March 13 , 2020 ( Friday ) . One day before , the household rating of TV news programming in the Central American country amounted to 1.6 . Less than two weeks later , on March 22 , 2020 , the rating stood at 5.8 .
