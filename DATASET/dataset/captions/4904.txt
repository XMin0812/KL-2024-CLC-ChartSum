This statistic depicts the per capita consumption of fresh avocados in the United States from 2000 to 2019 . According to the report , the U.S. per capita consumption of fresh avocados amounted to approximately 7.81 pounds in 2019 . In 2019 , some 74 percent of U.S. grocery shoppers who participated in an annual consumer survey , reported the nutritional benefits associated with avocados as one important driver for the purchase decision .
