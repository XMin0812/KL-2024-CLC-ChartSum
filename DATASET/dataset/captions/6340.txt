In February 2020 , roughly half of surveyed Russians were of the opinion that there was a low probability of an outbreak of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in their country . Roughly one fifth stated in the survey that this was not possible . Russia temporarily restricted entry of Chinese nationals into the country , excluding official , business , humanitarian , and transit visits on February 19 , 2020 .
