This statistic gives information on the development of Facebook 's data use policy from 2005 to 2015 . As of November 2013 , the word count of the social network 's data use policy was 6,291 words , more than double the amount of words in 2007 . It remained unchanged as of November 2014 . In 2015 , Facebook restructured its privacy agreement with word count only amounting to 2,702 words .
