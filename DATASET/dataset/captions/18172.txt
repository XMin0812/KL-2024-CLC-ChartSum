Basketball is a commonly watched sport both in the United States and worldwide . The WNBA is an American professional women 's basketball league established in 1996 and owned by the NBA . The regular season takes place in summer from May to August and the play-offs in September . WNBA has gained significant popularity over the years which has increased its fan base and number of followers worldwide . In 2019 , the Los Angeles Sparks had around 103.14 thousand followers on Instagram worldwide .
