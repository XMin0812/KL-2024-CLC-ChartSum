This statistic shows the out-of-pocket price for albuterol in the U.S. from 2004 to 2017 , in U.S. dollars . In 2004 , albuterol cost about 11.5 U.S. dollars . As of 2017 , albuterol has increased to 14.84 U.S. dollars . Albuterol is primarily used to treat asthma including asthma attacks , exercise-induced bronchoconstriction , and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease ( COPD ) .
