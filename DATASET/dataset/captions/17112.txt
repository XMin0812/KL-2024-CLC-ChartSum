This statistic shows the net investments of Total S.A. , from 2016 to 2019 . In 2019 , Total S.A. had investments of some 17.45 billion U.S. dollars . Total S.A. is one of the world 's largest oil and gas companies . The French company , headquartered in Paris-Courbevoie , specializes on the entire oil and gas chain , as well as chemical manufacturing .
