The revenue of the Premier League primarily comes from broadcasting fees , followed by commercial and sponsorship deals . Matchday income accounts for an estimated 720 million British pounds during the 2019/20 season . Concessions is a part of that revenue and a prominent food being sold are pies . During the 2019/20 season , Chelsea sold the most expensive pie for 4.5 British pounds . The cheapest pie was sold by Liverpool for 2.9 British pounds .
