Due to the outbreak of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Poland , fitness facilities and clubs were closed down , and the practice of outdoor sports was limited . As a result , interest in specific categories of equipment increased by several hundred percent in March . Dumbbells enjoyed the most significant attention , but also training benches , stationary bikes , exercise mats . For further information about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , please visit our dedicated Facts and Figures page .
