In 2018 , the number of tourists surpassed the visitor volume of the previous years . A total of approximately 19 million tourists visited the Netherlands that year , an increase of approximately three million visitors in comparison to 2017 . Moreover , it is forecasted that in 2019 and 2020 inbound tourism will continue to grow to 19 and 21 million tourists respectively . Most of the individuals who travelled for holiday or business purposes to the Netherlands were from Germany , as roughly 5.24 million German tourists visited their neighboring country . Together with the Belgians and British they account for more than half of the total tourists visiting the Netherlands in 2017 .
