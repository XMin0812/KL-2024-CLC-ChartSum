A quick service restaurant drive thru is where a customer can place their order , pay and be served all while remaining in their vehicle . This typically takes place through speakers and windows , allowing for speedier customer service . In 2020 , 93 percent of respondents in the U.S. were highly or somewhat satisfied with the cleanliness and sanitation at Panera Bread drive thru service . Meanwhile , the service that respondents were least satisfied with was speed of service , with 89 percent being highly or somewhat satisfied .
