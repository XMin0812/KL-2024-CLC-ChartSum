As of January 6 , 2021 , the number of confirmed cases in Finland has risen to a total of 37,549 . The majority of the cases were found among 20 to 59 year-olds , with the most cases reported in the age group of 20 to 29 year-olds . The first coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) case in Finland was confirmed on January 29 , 2020 .
