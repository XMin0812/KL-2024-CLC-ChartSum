Škoda was the best-selling car brand in the Poland in 2019 . The Czech automobile manufacturer ( MMO 60894 ) had sold nearly 69,000 units in the Polish market that year , roughly six thousand units more than the second entry . Škoda cars have been produced since 1905 , with number of vehicles produced having grown significantly in the following decades . As of 2000 , it is owned by German automotive company Volkswagen Group .
