Mercadona held the largest share of the grocery retail market in Spain for the 12 weeks to December 29 , 2019 with 24.7 percent , followed by Carrefour with 8.6 percent . Although Mercadona has the largest share of the market , Carrefour 's net sales per store in Spain is the highest . On the back of the economic recession , the value and importance of discounters for Spanish cosumers have grown as these have had a smaller disposable income and reined in their spending .
