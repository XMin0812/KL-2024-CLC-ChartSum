This statistic illustrates the monthly number of customers who switched their current bank account provider in the United Kingdom ( UK ) between January 2016 to September 2020 . It can be seen that the number of switches fluctuated during the period under observation , reaching a total of approximately 62.5 thousand as of September 2020 . The largest number of switches in one month occurred in March 2016 , when more than 124.6 thousand switches were recorded . The number of switches recorded in the previous twelve months ( from the 1st of October 2019 to the 30th of September 2020 ) totaled more than three quarters of a million switches .
