Between 2010 and 2019 , the number of births in Italy decreased constantly . In 2010 , 549.8 thousand births were registered in the country , while in 2019 the figure dropped to 417.6 thousand . The largest number of births nationwide was registered in the North Italian region of Lombardy , with approximately 70 thousand infants born in 2019 . Indeed , Lombardy is the most populous region of the country .
