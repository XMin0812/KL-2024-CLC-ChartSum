Roman Catholic was the most common religion affiliation in Chile in 2018 . In a survey carried out between July and August of 2018 , 55.2 percent of Chilean respondents claimed to be of catholic faith , whereas the second most chosen religion was Evangelism , with 12.5 percent of the people interviewed . Almost 29 percent of respondents answered they did n't profess any religion and 0.4 percent defined themselves as atheists .
