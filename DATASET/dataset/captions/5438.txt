As more people stayed at home during the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , Pornhub made its premium content free and saw an increase in traffic in April 2020 . Russia saw the highest traffic growth among Central and Eastern European countries , measuring at over 20.8 percent as of April 28 , 2020 compared to an average day prior to the outbreak . However , the traffic growth rate saw a significant decline relative to the end of March 2020 .
