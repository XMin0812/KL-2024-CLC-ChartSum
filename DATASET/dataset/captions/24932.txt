From January 1st to August 20th of 2020 , a total of 74 thousand wildfires were registered in Brazil . The region most affected by them was the Amazon rainforest , which accounted for almost 48 percent of the fires reported . The Cerrado -a Brazilian biome equivalent to a tropical savanna- suffered over 27 percent of all the forest fires identified in this period , while the Atlantic forest represented 11 percent .
