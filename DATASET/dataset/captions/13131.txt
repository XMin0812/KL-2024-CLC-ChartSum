The statistic shows gross domestic product ( GDP ) per capita in Mongolia from 1995 to 2025 . GDP is the total value of all goods and services produced in a country in a year . It is considered to be a very important indicator of the economic strength of a country and a positive change is an indicator of economic growth . In 2020 , the estimated GDP per capita in Mongolia amounted to around 3,989.93 U.S. dollars .
