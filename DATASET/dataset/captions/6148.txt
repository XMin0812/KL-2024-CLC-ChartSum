As of December 15 , 2020 , over 1.2 million people have been tested positive for the coronavirus in the Benelux . Although Luxembourg was not left unscathed , Belgium and the Netherlands faced a higher number of confirmed cases . Since February 2020 , new COVID-19 cases are reported daily across these three countries . For instance , on December 14th , Belgium diagnosed 2,171 new infections and the Netherlands 3,879 .
