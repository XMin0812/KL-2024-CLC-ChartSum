This statistic shows the favorite beer brand in Italy as of May 2016 . As of the survey period , the share of respondents who considered Nastro Azzuro to be their favorite brand was approximately six percent . The most favored brand was Peroni ( 14 percent of the preferences ) , while , on the other hand , the least favored beer brands were both Edelmeister and Beck 's ( three percent ) . However , almost ten percent of the respondents did not have any favorite beer brand .
