This graph shows the total number of students enrolled in public elementary and secondary schools in Canada in the academic year 2018/19 , distinguished by province . In the academic year 2018/19 , about 2.04 million students were enrolled in public elementary and secondary schools in Ontario . At this time , there were a total of around 4.92 million students enrolled in public elementary or secondary schools in Canada , a slight increase from previous years . With a primary school enrollment rate of 99.5 percent in 2017 , Canada is one of the countries with the highest share of students in primary schools .
