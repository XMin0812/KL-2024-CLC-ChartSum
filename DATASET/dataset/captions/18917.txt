The statistic shows the result of the Survey Stili d'Italia conducted by Italiani.Coop on the frequency of Tinder usage among millennials in Italy from May to June 2018 . Only four percent of the people interviewed declared to use the platform everyday , while five percent claimed they use it only sometimes . On the other hand , almost half of the total stated that they do n't use it and it is not interested in it . Moreover , 22 percent does not even know the dating app .
