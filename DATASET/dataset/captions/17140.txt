From 2008 to 2018 , the value of gross written property insurance premiums in the United Kingdom ( UK ) saw a year-on-year increase , reaching its highest value of approximately 24.2 billion euros in 2018 . In comparison , in 2008 which was the year with the lowest value reported , the property insurance companies in the UK conducted almost 13.24 billion euros in gross written premiums , over ten billion less .
