This statistic displays the net migration rate of foreign nationals in Spain from 2010 to 2018 . During the analyzed period of time , the net migration rate resulted in negative figures , with its lowest point in 2013 , year in which the net migration rate reached approximately -202 thousand . This trend ended in 2015 , with the rate turning positive and reaching figures of approximately 359 thousand in 2018 . This is due to the change in the migration flow in 2015 , year that turned the tables towards a positive inflow of migrants into the country .
