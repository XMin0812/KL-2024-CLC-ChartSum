In 2019 , the highest homicide rate was registered in Venezuela , with over 60 murders committed per 100,000 inhabitants . Jamaica came in second , with a homicide rate of 47.4 and Honduras came in third place with a rate of 41.2 . In the same year , the lowest rate in Latin America and the Caribbean was in Chile , with a homicide rate of 2.6 per 100,000 inhabitants .
