This statistic presents the development of ATM numbers ( automated teller machines ) for self-operated cash withdrawals in European countries from 2005 to the first half of 2019 . In 2005 , there were approximately 325 thousand ATMs in Europe and the number grew up to more than 420 thousand as of 2016 . By the first half of 2019 , the number of ATMs in Europe had decreased to approximately 391.4 thousand .
