This statistic shows Volkswagen 's operating profit in the first quarter of 2015 and the first quarter of 2016In spite of the emissions scandal , Volkswagen produced operating profit of around 3.4 billion euros in the first quarter of 2016 . Between the first quarter of 2015 and the first quarter of 2016 , the company 's profit after tax fell from 2.9 billion euros to 2.37 billion euros .
