During a 2018 survey , around 38.2 percent of respondents interviewed in Brazil said they belonged to the lower middle class . Meanwhile , 29.1 percent of the interviewees defined their social class as `` low '' and 25.7 percent stated that they were part of the middle class.Furthermore , Brazil 's Gini coefficient , an indicator that measures wealth distribution , shows Brazil is one of the most unequal countries in the Latin American region .
