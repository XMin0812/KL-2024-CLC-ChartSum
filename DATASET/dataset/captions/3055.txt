Georges St-Pierre is leading the Ultimate Fighting Championship ( UFC ) in most takedowns landed as of March 2020 , followed by Gleison Tibau with 84 takedowns and Demetrious Johnson with 74 takedowns during UFC fights . At the same point in time Donald Cerrone holds the record for most knockdowns in the UFC with a total of 20 . Georges St-Pierre also holds the record for most strikes landed , with 2,591 landed strikes during UFC fights .
