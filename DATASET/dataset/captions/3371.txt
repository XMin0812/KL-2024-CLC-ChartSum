In December 2019 , singer Benjamin Lasnier was the most-followed of all Instagram influencers coming from Luxembourg . This according to data provided by InfluencerDB . The artist , born in 1999 in the Grand Duchy to a French father and Danish mother , had around 790,000 followers . Other entries in this ranking , such as Anna Grostina ( in second place ) or Coco Lili ( third ) , cover fashion and lifestyle . Compared to influencers from Belgium and the Netherlands , however , Luxembourgish Instagram-influencers did not have as many followers .
