This statistic illustrates the mortgage market share of the United Kingdom ( UK ) based Nationwide Building Society from 2011 to 2020 . It can be seen that Nationwide increased its share of the mortgage market overall during the period under observation , reaching a share of 11.4 percent as of the year ending April 2020 . The smallest mortgage market share for Nationwide was found in 2011 , when a market share of 10.4 percent was recorded .
