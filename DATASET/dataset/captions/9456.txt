Data on the box office revenue of Disney in the United States and Canada in 2019 revealed that the studio generated 3.8 billion U.S. dollars in revenue that year , up from just over three billion in the previous year . The year 2019 was a big one for The Walt Disney Company . Disney closed its acquisition of 21st Century , launched its very own streaming service , Disney Plus ( stylized as Disney+ ) , and released major movies 'The Lion King ' _ , 'Frozen 2 ' _ , and 'Toy Story 4 ' to name but a few .
