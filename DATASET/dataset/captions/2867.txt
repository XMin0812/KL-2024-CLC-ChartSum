South Africa is the fifth African country with the largest population , counting approximately 60 million individuals . In 2020 , the largest city of South Africa was Cape Town . The capital of Western Cape counted 3.4 million inhabitants , whereas South Africa 's second largest city was Durban ( eThekwini municipality ) , with 3.1 million inhabitants . Note that when observing the number of inhabitants by municipality , Johannesburg is counted as largest city/municipality of South Africa .
