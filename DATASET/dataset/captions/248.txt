This statistic shows the unadjusted annual inflation rate in the U.S. from 1990 to June 2020 . The data represents U.S. city averages . The base period was 1982 - 84 . In economics , the inflation rate is a measure of inflation , the rate of increase of a price index ( in the below case : consumer price index ) . It is the percentage rate of change in price level over time . The rate of decrease in the purchasing power of money is approximately equal . In the first half of 2020 , prices went up by 1.2 percent compared to the previous year . See the U.S. GDP growth rate and U.S. GDP per capita for more information .
