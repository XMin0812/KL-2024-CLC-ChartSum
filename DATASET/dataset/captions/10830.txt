This statistic shows the difference in number of stores of the leading 25 bakery retailers in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2015 to 2016 . The retailers are selected in order of those with the highest total number of outlets in 2016 , with the figures showing the difference in number of stores compared to 2015 . In 2016 , Subway opened 247 new outlets in the UK , the highest number of all the retailers selling baked products as the primary element of their food offering .
