According to a survey conducted to understand the impact of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) on Indian businesses , 63 percent of respondents who indicated disruption in their supply chain . Of these , over 47 percent of respondents had a delay of less than four weeks in sourcing products . Respondents included members belonging to Indian private and public corporate sectors and multinational companies .
