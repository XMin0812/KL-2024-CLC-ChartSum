Revenue from pay TV and television advertising will account for the majority of global media revenue in 2020 , with estimates showing that the medium could generate 400 billion U.S. dollars by the end of the year . Meanwhile , whilst the audiobook and podcast markets will collectively bring in less than five billion dollars , the two categories are by far the fastest-growing in the world . Predictions show that the audiobook market will bring in 3.5 billion dollars , marking a 25 percent increase from the previous year , whilst the podcast market is expected to grow by 30 percent and generate over one billion dollars for the first time .
