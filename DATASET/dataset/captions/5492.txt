With a market capitalization of over 35 billion euros , Intesa Sanpaolo was the leading bank in Italy listed on the Milan Stock Exchange as of August 2020 . Intesa Sanpaolo , together with its fierce competitor UniCredit , dominates the banking sector in the country . The two banking groups are not only the leaders in the domestic market , but also ranked among the 20 leading banks in Europe in terms of market capitalization in 2019 .
