This statistic shows the average internet connection speed in Germany from the first quarter of 2014 to the first quarter of 2017 ( in Mbit/s ) . The connection speed increased from 8.1 Mbit/s to 15.3 Mbit/s throughout this period . As the share of households with broadband internet access increases , so does the internet speed . With a higher demand for faster internet by consumers , the telecommunications industry must keep up .
