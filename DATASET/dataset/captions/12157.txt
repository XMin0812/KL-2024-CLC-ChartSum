As of March 2020 , the GDP growth outlook for Indonesia was downgraded to 4.7 percent from an initial forecast of 5.3 percent . This was mainly due to the outbreak of the novel coronavirus SARS-CoV-2 , and the impact this has had on the global economy . As of March 12 , Indonesia has reported 34 cases of COVID-19 . The low number of cases detected had prompted questions on Indonesia 's ability to detect and handle the virus outbreak .
