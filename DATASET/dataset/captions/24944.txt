As of March 7 , 2020 there was more than four thousand cases of influenza reported in Mexico , while the number of dengue fever occurences added up to almost two thousand . At that point in time , the number of cases of COVID-19 , caused by the novel coronavirus ( SARS-CoV-2 ) amounted to 7 . As of March 19 , 2020 , the number of coronavirus infections in the country had reached 93 .
