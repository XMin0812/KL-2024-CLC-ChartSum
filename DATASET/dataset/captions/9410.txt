The number of overseas arrivals to the United States experienced an increasing trend from 2011 to 2019 , peaking at 40.4 million . In 2020 , they were projected to drop to 7.2 million as a result of the travel restrictions due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic . As estimated , overseas arrivals will increase gradually during the following years , reaching 36.1 million by 2024 .
