This statistic shows the average healthcare costs per person under the Dutch Health Insurance Act ( Zvw ) from 2009 to 2018 ( in euros ) . The Dutch health insurance system as it is now was introduced in 2006 and combines elements of both public as well as private insurance . It is mandatory to have at least a basic health insurance ( in Dutch : basisverzekering ) when you work in the Netherlands , because healthcare is funded through taxation of income . In 2018 , Dutch health insurance companies paid an average of 2,549 euros in healthcare costs to every resident in the Netherlands . This is an increase when compared to the previous year .
