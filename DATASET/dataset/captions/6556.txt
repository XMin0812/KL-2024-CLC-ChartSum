Peru is the country with the highest mortality rate due to the coronavirus disease ( COVID-19 ) in Latin America . As of January 4 , 2021 , the country registered around 118.1 deaths per 100,000 inhabitants . It was followed by Mexico , with around 100.8 fatal cases per 100,000 population . In total , around 512 thousand people died due to COVID-19 in Latin America and the Caribbean .
