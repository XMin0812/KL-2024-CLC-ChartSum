This statistic displays the number of car sharing vehicles in selected countries in Europe in 2014 . Germany had by far the highest number of vehicles owned by car sharing companies , followed by France and the United Kingdom . In France there were 3,900 car sharing vehicles in 2014 and in the United Kingdom 3,000 in the same year . In the Netherlands there were 2,300 car sharing vehicles in 2014 .
