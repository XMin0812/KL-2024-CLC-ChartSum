This statistic illustrates the total number of counterfeit Bank of England banknotes that were removed from circulation in the United Kingdom ( UK ) as of 2019 , listed by the notional value of the banknote . It can be seen that approximately 383 thousand counterfeit 20 British pound notes were removed from circulation at that time . The second most common counterfeit note removed from circulation was the 50 pound banknote , with 42 thousand 50 pound notes being removed from circulation as of 2019 .
