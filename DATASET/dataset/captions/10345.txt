In the week ended February 29 , 2020 , the sales of health and hygiene products in the United States increased . After suspected local transmission of the coronavirus and Trump 's press conference , Americans started buying health products . In that week , the sales value of medical supplies rose by 85.3 percent compared to the same period of the previous year .
