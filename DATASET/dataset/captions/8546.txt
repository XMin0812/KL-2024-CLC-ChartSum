Roman Catholic is the most common religion affiliation in the Dominican Republic . In a survey carried out between July and August of 2018 , more than 44 percent of Dominican respondents claimed to be of catholic faith , whereas the second most chosen religion was Evangelism , with 9.5 percent of the people interviewed . More than 29 percent of respondents answered they did n't profess any religion at all , while only 0.2 percent defined themselves as atheists .
