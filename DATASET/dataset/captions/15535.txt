The statistic shows the annual growth rate of revenues in the managed security services market in North America , from 2012 to 2019 . In 2014 , the managed security services market grew 18.6 percent from the previous year , producing almost 3 billion U.S. dollars in revenue . Managed security services include security asset monitoring and management ; threat intelligence , research , detection , and remediation ; risk and compliance management ; and advanced and emerging managed security services .
