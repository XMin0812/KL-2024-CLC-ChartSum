This statistic shows the national debt of Djibouti from 2015 to 2019 in relation to gross domestic product ( GDP ) , with projections up until 2025 . The figures refer to the whole country and include the debts of the state , the communities , the municipalities and the social insurances . In 2019 , the national debt of Djibouti amounted to approximately 38.47 percent of GDP .
