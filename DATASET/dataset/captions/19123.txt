This statistic shows the tourism expenditure in Egypt from 2008 to 2017 . In 2017 , the expenditure was valued at around 8.64 million U.S. dollars , showing an increase compared to 3.31 million dollars in 2016 and a decline compared to 10.82 million dollars in 2010 . The crash of the Russian plane in 2015 , after its departure from Sharm El Sheikh International Airport , has affected negatively the number of tourist arrivals in 2016 , resulting in a major decline in tourism expenditure .
