This statistic displays the share of respondents reading newspapers or magazines regularly in the United Kingdom from 2005 to 2014 . The share of respondents reading newspapers and magazines on a regular basis declined notably in the years from 2005 to 2013 . In 2013 , 66 percent of all adults reported reading newspapers or magazines regularly . The Sun newspaper had the highest reach among selected UK newspapers , according to a survey carried out in 2013 .
