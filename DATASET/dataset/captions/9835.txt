From January to December 2018 , the number of migrants arrived in Pozzallo ( Sicily ) amounted to 3,818 , the largest value nationwide . Moreover , Lampedusa registered 3,468 arrivals , ranking second in the chart . In 2018 , about 23.4 thousand migrants landed in Italy . In that year , Tunisian and Eritrean were the most frequent nationalities of migrants . In particular , 5.2 thousand people came from Tunisia , whereas 3.3 thousands were Eritrean .
