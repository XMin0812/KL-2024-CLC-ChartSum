ByteDance , the Chinese parent company of the popular video-sharing social network TikTok , donated in total 436.8 million U.S. dollars for combating the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) epidemic , making it one of the biggest doners in this pandemic . Other tech companies such as Alibaba and Baidu have also made various amount of donations in combating the novel virus .
