Johnson & Johnson 's revenues have generally been increasing over the last several years . As of 2019 , Johnson & Johnson 's total net income was estimated to be about 15.12 billion U.S. dollars . This is a slight decrease from the previous year when net income was at about 15.3 billion U.S. dollars . Johnson & Johnson has been majorly impacted by tax laws that cost the company about 13.6 billion dollars in 2018 .
