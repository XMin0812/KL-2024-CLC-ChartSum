With an average of 16.84 million web searches per month , `` Avenger 's Endgame '' was the most searched film of 2019 . Avenger 's Endgame is a Marvel Cinematic Universe superhero film produced by Marvel Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures in April 2019 . The movie 's villain Thanos also held the record for being the most popular search query worldwide of 2019 .
