On June 25 , 2020 , 305 patients with COVID-19 were discharged from Singapore hospitals and self-isolation facilities . As of that date , around 42.7 thousand people had been infected with the novel coronavirus in Singapore , and around 36.6 thousand people have recovered . The country has since introduced a stimulus package worth 48 billion Singapore dollars to help the Singapore economy , which had been badly hit by the pandemic . Singapore is currently one out of more than 200 countries and territories battling the novel coronavirus .
