This statistic shows the total fertility rate ( TFR ) in the United Kingdom from 2000 to 2018 . Between 2002 and 2008 the total fertility rate increased from 1.63 to 1.96 , a peak for this period , before starting to decline . From 2012 onwards the total fertility rate has shown a year-on-year decrease , reaching 1.68 by 2018 , the most recent year in this statistic .
