The statistic shows the leading merger and acquisition ( M & A ) deals in Romania in 2019 , by transaction value . The largest deal amounted to 548.3 million euros ; this was the price paid for the acquisition of 100 percent stake of Central European Media Enterprises ( CME ) assets in Romania by PPF Group ( Czech Republic ) . The smallest deal in the top five amounted to 175 million euros by The Blackstone Group ( United States ) to acquire Superbet . All in all , the total value of M & A transactions in Romania in 2019 amounted to 2.82 billion euros , while in 2018 , M & A transactions peaked at 5.65 billion euros .
