This statistic illustrates the annual operating expenses of the Royal Bank of Scotland ( RBS ) group from 2012 to 2019 , in million British pounds . The operating costs rose briefly in 2013 , achieving the highest value of this period , with approximately 17.4 billion British pounds . Since 2016 the total operating expenditure has decreased , reaching the lowest value in 2019 , with a total of roughly 9.3 billion British pounds .
