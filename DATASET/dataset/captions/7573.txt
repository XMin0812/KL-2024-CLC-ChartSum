This statistic illustrates the number of full and part-time employees in the financial and insurance activities sector in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in 2019 , categorized by gender . In that year there were 521 thousand full time male employees in the financial and insurance activities sector in the United Kingdom ( UK ) , whereas there were 346 thousand female employees . On the other hand , in the same year and same activities sector there were 94 thousand part-time female employees and 13 thousand part-time male employees .
