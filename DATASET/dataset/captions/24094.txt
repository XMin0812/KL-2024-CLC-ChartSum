According to a survey carried out in Chile in March 2020 , 93 percent of respondents had started washing their hands more frequently as a measure of prevention against the novel coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) , while only 55 percent stopped using public transport . According to the same survey , 52 percent of Chilean respondents were very concerned about getting infected with COVID-19 .
