For the 2017/2018 League One football season in England , the most expensive season ticket was sold by Charlton for 500 British pounds , followed by Gillingham at 495 and AFC Wimbledon and Oxford , both at 475 British pounds . At the same time , Charlton also sold the second cheapest Leage One season ticket at 175 British pounds , giving Charlton 's season ticket the widest price range . The English League One is the third highest football league in England , behind the Championship and the Premier League .
