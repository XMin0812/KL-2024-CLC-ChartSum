In 2019 , the chemical company Dow generated some 33.6 percent of its total revenue in the United States . In September 2017 , the Dow Chemical Company and E. I. du Pont de Nemours and Company ( DuPont ) completed their merger , forming one of the largest chemical manufacturing companies in the world . In April 2019 , the DowDuPont conglomerate separated into three companies : Dow , dedicated to commodity chemicals , DuPont , dedicated to specialty chemicals , and Corteva , dedicated to agricultural chemicals and seeds . DowDuPont was headquartered in Midland , Michigan .
