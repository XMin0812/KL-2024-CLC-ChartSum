As a results of the coronavirus outbreak , one fifth of respondents who were surveyed on March 25 , 2020 in the United States stated that they now always sanitize carts and/or baskets before using them in-store . A further 15 percent of respondents claimed that a permanent change to their shopping behavior was keeping a distance from other shoppers in stores .
