As of November 20 , 2020 , around 52.8 percent of around 30 thousand confirmed coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) patients in South Korea were female . Additionally , people in their twenties accounted for the largest share in the total infection cases . South Korea confirmed 30,017 cases of infection including 501 deaths .
