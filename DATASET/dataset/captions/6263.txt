The greatest number of heating degree days ( HDD 's ) in the United Kingdom tended to be recorded in January and February . In 2019 , there were 11.3 HDD 's in January , which was an increase on the previous year . The most HDD 's in the period of consideration were seen in February 2018 , at 12.4 days . The following year , this figure only amounted to 8.6 HDD 's .
