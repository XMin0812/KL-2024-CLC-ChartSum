Household waste volumes per person in England declined slightly between 2010 and 2018 . Figures dropped to below 400 kilograms for the first time in 2018 , with 394 kilograms in household waste collected per person that year . The region which generated the largest volume of residual waste per household was the North East of England , where an average of 593 kilograms of waste was generated per person in 2018/19 .
