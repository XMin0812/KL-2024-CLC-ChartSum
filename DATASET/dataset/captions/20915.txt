The participation of the agricultural sector in Brazil 's total employment has been consistently decreasing since the beginning of the decade . In 2020 , the sector was estimated to represent around 9.1 percent of jobs in the country , down from a participation of more than 12.6 percent in 2010 . The value added by the agricultural sector represented 4.4 percent of Brazil 's gross domestic product ( GDP ) in 2019 .
