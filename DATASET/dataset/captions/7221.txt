Counter Strike is a series of first-person shooter video games in which two teams compete to perpetrate or prevent a terrorist act ( bomb attack , hostage taking , etc. ) . One of the latest games in the series , Counter Strike : Global Offensive , is popular with gamers and viewers alike . Counter Strike : Global Offensive events on the video streaming service Twitch were watched by a record average of over 84.53 thousand viewers in November 2020 .
