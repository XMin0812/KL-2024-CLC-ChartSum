The average height of the 45 men who have served ( or will serve ) as the President of the United States is approximately 180cm ( 5'11 '' ) ; this is roughly five centimeters ( two inches ) taller than the average U.S. male in 2020 . Abraham Lincoln has the distinction of being the tallest U.S. president in history , at 193cm ( 6'4 '' ) , while James Madison was the shortest ( and lightest ) U.S. president at 163cm ( 5'4 '' ) .
