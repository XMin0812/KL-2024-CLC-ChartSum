In 2019 , fossil fuels accounted for more than 71 percent of the primary energy consumption in Peru . Oil was the leading fuel consumed in the country that year , at 0.51 exajoules , followed by natural gas , which represented 0.3 exajoules of energy consumption . Hydroelectricity was the third largest source of energy consumption in the country , amounting to 0.28 exajoules .
