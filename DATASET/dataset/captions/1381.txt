The statistic depicts Russia 's gross domestic product ( GDP ) growth rate from 2015 to 2019 , with projections up until 2025 . GDP refers to the total market value of all goods and services that are produced within a country per year . It is an important indicator of the economic strength of a country . Real GDP is adjusted for price changes and is therefore regarded as a key indicator for economic growth . In 2019 , Russia 's real GDP grew by about 1.34 percent compared to the previous year .
