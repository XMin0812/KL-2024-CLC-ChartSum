Since entering the market , Huawei have enjoyed a prolonged period of growth . In Q1 2016 the company accounted for just 8.3 percent of all smartphones sold to end users . By the Q2 2020 , this figure climbed to 18.4 percent , more than they had ever had . However , in Q3 2020 , this market share declined by four percentage points to 14.1 percent . Huawei has not had a lower market share since Q3 2018 .
