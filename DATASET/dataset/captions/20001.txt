The military expenditure that Mexico reported for 2019 accounted for approximately 0.5 percent of the country 's Gross Domestic Product ( GDP ) , the same percentage as the previous year . The share of GDP for Mexico 's military expenditure has remained constant since 2009 , with the exception of 2017 when it dropped to 0.4 percent . That same year , Mexico had one of the lowest military spending in Latin America .
