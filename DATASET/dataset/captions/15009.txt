The statistic shows the leading merger and acquisition ( M & A ) deals in Bulgaria in 2019 , by transaction value . The largest deal amounted to approximately over 1.2 billion euros ; this was the price paid for the acquisition of 100 percent stake of Vivacom by BC Partners Holdings ( United Kingdom ) . The smallest deal in the top five amounted to 172.8 million euros by Advance Properties ( Bulgaria ) to acquire 95 percent stake of Nova Broadcasting Group .
