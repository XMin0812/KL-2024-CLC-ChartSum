The number of deaths in Spain experienced an upward trend over the 12-year period , presumably due to the aging population . Owing to this setup , the Spanish mortality rate went up from 8.33 in 2006 to 9.10 in 2018 . In Spain , diseases related to the circulatory system and neoplasms ( cancer ) ranked as the main causes of death , both with over 100 thousand cases in 2017 .
