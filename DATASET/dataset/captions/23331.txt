This statistic shows the rate of registrations of newly diagnosed cases of breast cancer per 100,000 population in England from 1995 to 2018 . Breast cancer occurs when cells in the breast begin to grown abnormally , these cells often form a tumor . In 2018 , 171.9 females per 100,000 population were registered as newly diagnosed with breast cancer . This was an increase on the previous year 's rate of registration .
