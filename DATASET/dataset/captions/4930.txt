In 2018 , the infant mortality rate in the Netherlands was 3.5 . This means that out of every 1,000 newborns , nearly four died before their first birthday . Infant mortality was lowest in 2015 , when the rate was 3.3 deaths per 1,000 live born children . In total , nearly 170,000 babies were born in 2018 . An infant mortality rate of 3.5 means that roughly 590 of these babies would not survive their first year .
