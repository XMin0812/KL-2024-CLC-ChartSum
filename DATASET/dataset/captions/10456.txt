This statistic shows the global revenue of LVMH Group 's wine and spirits segment from 2008 to 2019 . In 2019 , this segment generated a global revenue of approximately 5.6 billion euros . The LVMH Group is a French luxury goods corporation , which owns around 50 luxury brands worldwide , including Louis Vuitton and Bulgari . The fashion and leather goods segment generates the most revenue for LVMH .
