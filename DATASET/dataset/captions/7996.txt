In 2017 the number of moped or motorcycle crime offences committed soared to almost 24 thousand compared with just 1031 in 2014 . The surge in moped crime is one of the most widely reported aspects of increasing crime in London in recent years . This type of crime was reduced to just over 13.7 thousand in 2018 , with just 6.7 thousand offences recorded in the first three quarter of 2019 .
