Destiny 2 is a online multiplayer first-person shooter game which was first released for PlayStation 4 and Xbox One in September 2017 . Whilst the game was initially available on Windows the following month , it only became available on the online gaming platform , Steam , some two years later in October 2019 . In the first month of its release on Steam , the game peaked at over 292 thousand concurrent players worldwide . The game reached a peak of 93.74 thousand players gaming at the same time in October 2020 .
