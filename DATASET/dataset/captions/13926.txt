This statistic shows the quarterly trend in the volume of clothing retail sales ( quantity bought ) in Great Britain from 1st quarter 2015 to 2nd quarter 2020 . The figures are seasonally adjusted estimates , measured using the Retail Sales Index ( RSI ) and published in index form with a reference year of 2016 equal to 100 . Over the time period observed , sales reached their highest peak during the second quarter of 2019 , with the index measuring at 108.4 . Sales volume saw a sharp decline in the second quarter of 2020 with an index value of 50.3 .
