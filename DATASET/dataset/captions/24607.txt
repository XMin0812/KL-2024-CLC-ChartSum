In a survey of marketers and exhibitors in the United States gauging the state of the exhibition industry , respondents were asked about their level of confidence regarding the effectiveness of their trade show in 2020 . The results indicate that 66 percent of respondents were confident , while 17 percent were extremely confident of their future program 's success . However , the study was conducted before the global coronavirus outbreak , which largely impacted the exhibition industry in 2020 .
