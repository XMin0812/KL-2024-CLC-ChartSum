Electric vehicles contribute to reducing greenhouse gas emissions . As of 2008 , these types of vehicles saw a significant increase . In Belgium , ten electric vehicles were sold in 2008 and 2009 and this rose to 1,792 electric passenger cars in 2014 . As of 2020 , the volume amounted to 23,983 vehicles , approximately 13 times more than in 2014 . In terms of the number of charging stations for electric vehicles , Belgium had nearly 7,500 charging stations in 2020 , whereas neighboring countries the Netherlands and Luxembourg had roughly 60,000 and 1,000 charging stations respectively .
