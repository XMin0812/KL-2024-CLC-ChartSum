Which social media platforms are the most popular in Luxembourg ? When asked in 2017 which social network they used , roughly 80 percent of the respondents from the Grand Duchy answered they used Facebook . Picture apps Instagram and Snapchat followed , but were cited less often . Facebook had around 360,000 monthly active users from Luxembourg in 2018 . The share of the population in the Grand Duchy that used the platform in 2018 , however , was lower than in neighboring Belgium .
