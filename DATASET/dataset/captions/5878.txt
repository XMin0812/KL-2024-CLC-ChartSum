The financial crisis that burst out in 2008 , alongside with the consequential problems on the labor market and high unemployment rates , affected in particular the south of Italy . Despite an overall improvement of the country 's economic situation over the last years , some significant differences between the regions still can be observed . When it comes to the unemployment rate , in 2020 , Sicily , Calabria , and Campania were the three regions where the share of inhabitants without a job was the highest .
