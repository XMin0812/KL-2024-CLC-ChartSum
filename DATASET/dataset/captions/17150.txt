This statistic shows the number of cars sold by Hyundai in Czechia from 2008 to 2019 . Czech sales of Hyundai cars experienced continuous growth over the period till 2017 , rising from 7.4 thousand units sold in 2008 to over 21 thousand in 2017 . In 2019 , the sale of Hyundai cars in Czechia amounted to 19.3 thousand units .
