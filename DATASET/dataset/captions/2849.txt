As of 2019 , 127 thousand Italians were living in London . From 2015 to 2017 , the number of Italian citizens who lived in the English Capital increased steadily . However , the Italian population living in London fell between 2018 and 2019 . Nonetheless , the United Kingdom was the most popular destination country among Italian emigrants . During 2019 , about 19 percent of all Italians who moved abroad notably chose the United Kingdom .
