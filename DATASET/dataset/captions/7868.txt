As of 23rd January 2020 , Hakan Şükür is the all-time leader in goals scored for the national football team of Turkey with a total of 51 goals scored . Şükür retired from the national team in 2007 after playing in 112 international matches . He is trailing in goals in front of Burak Yılmaz with 24 goals , who is not yet retired from the team , and Tuncay Şanlı with 22 goals , who retired from the national team in 2010 .
