This statistic shows the construction output index of all work in Great Britain from January 2015 to August 2019 . The index was set at 100 in 2016 . This index has grown over the period in consideration , peaking in February 2019 at an index point of 109.9 and reached the lowest point in February 2015 at 95 . In August 2019 construction output index of all work in Great Britain was 108.9 .
