According to a survey completed in Great Britain in March 2020 , 25 percent of respondents rated their government as very good at communicating information about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak , while a further 48 percent rated the communication as fairly good . On the other hand , 24 percent overall gave a poor rating to the government for the information it was providing about the coronavirus situation .
