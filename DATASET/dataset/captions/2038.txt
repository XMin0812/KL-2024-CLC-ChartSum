This statistic depicts the total number of employees of L'Oréal worldwide from 2010 to 2019 . In 2019 , L'Oréal employed a total of 87,907 people throughout the world . L'Oréal is the world 's largest cosmetics and beauty company , concentrating on hair color , skin care , sun protection , make-up , perfumes , and hair care . The company is also the leading nano-technology patent-holder in the United States .
