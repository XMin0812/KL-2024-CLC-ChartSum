Production of yarn in India amounted to nearly five billion kilograms in fiscal year 2019 . The volume of yarn produced had consistently increased since fiscal year 2011 . Cotton yarn was the largest share of yarn variety at over 71 percent of market share , with a fabric production share of nearly 51.4 percent in fiscal year 2018 . That year , the WPI for cotton yarn was 113.3 , implying that it increased by over 13 percent from the base year of 2012 .
