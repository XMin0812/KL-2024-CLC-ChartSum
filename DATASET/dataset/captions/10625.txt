In 2018 , there were approximately 285 thousand British citizens living in Spain , with a further 145 thousand in France and almost 93 thousand in Germany . By comparison , there were only 283 British people living in Latvia , the fewest of any European Union member state . As a member of the European Union , British citizens currently have the right to live and work in any EU member state , although it is unclear what their legal status will be after Brexit is fulfilled and the UK leaves the European Union .
