This statistic shows how adults prefer their beer served in Great Britain ( UK ) in 2014 . According to the results of the Yougov survey , 21 percent of British adults prefer beer in a bottle format while only 3 percent prefer it served in a can . Almost a third said 'none of those ' _ , although it should be noted that the base of respondents included those claiming not to like beer at all ( 28 percent ) .
