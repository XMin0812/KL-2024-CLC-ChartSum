Land-based sports betting was legalized in the U.S. state of New Jersey as of June 11 , 2018 . This means it is now possible to play college sports betting , MLB sports betting , and NBA sports betting in the state . In September 2020 , New Jersey generated a total of 45.08 million U.S. dollars from sports betting , up from the previous month 's total of 39.51 million U.S. dollars .
