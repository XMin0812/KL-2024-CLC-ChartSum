This statistic shows the total turnover of wholesale trade businesses in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2008 to 2018 ( excluding motor vehicles and motorcycles ) . In 2008 , wholesale trade enterprises in the UK produced a turnover of over 777 billion British pounds . This figure fluctuated greatly over the ten year period , with turnover reaching a high point in 2013 at 982 billion British pounds . In 2018 , the turnover generated by UK wholesalers was at approximately 910.8 billion British pounds .
