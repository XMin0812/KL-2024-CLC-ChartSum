This graph depicts the franchise value of the Los Angeles Rams of the National Football League from 2002 to 2020 . In 2020 , the franchise value amounted to around four billion U.S. dollars . The Los Angeles Rams are owned by Stan Kroenke , who bought the franchise for 750 million U.S. dollars in 2010 . The franchise moved from St. Louis to Los Angeles prior to the 2016 season .
