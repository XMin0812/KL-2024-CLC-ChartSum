In 2018 , 92 people died as a result of MDMA/ecstasy use in England and Wales , this was by far the highest amount of recorded deaths in the 25-year period shown , while 78 deaths were recorded as a result of MDMA in 2019 . Since the turn of the century , the number of deaths has remained relatively high apart from a drastic fall in 2010 and 2011 before subsequently rising again .
