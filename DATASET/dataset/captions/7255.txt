As of April 17 , 2020 , Tokyo Prefecture recorded the highest density of people infected with the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) among all 47 prefectures in Japan , with approximately 20.1 infected people per 100 thousand inhabitants in the prefecture . Ishikawa Prefecture recorded the second highest density , with around 14.1 people per 100 thousand inhabitants in the prefecture being infected with the virus . There was an average of around 7.8 infected people per 100 thousand inhabitants in Japan during the measured time period . In terms of the total number of infection cases , Tokyo and Osaka Prefecture were the two prefectures with the highest number of patients of COVID-19 as of April 2020 .
