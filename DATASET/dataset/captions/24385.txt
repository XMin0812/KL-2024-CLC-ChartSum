This statistic illustrates how many Vauxhall Corsa passenger cars were registered for the first time annually in Great Britain , between 2001 and 2019 . A peak in sales of the Vauxhall Corsa was observed in 2003 , when over 106 thousand units were newly registered . In 2019 sales amounted to 53,942 units , a value which is almost half of the peak sales experienced in 2003
