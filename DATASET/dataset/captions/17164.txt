In 2019 , the average monthly consumption expenditure of Italian households amounted to 2,560 euros . The total annual consumption expenditure was 1.09 trillion euros . This figure represented an increase compared to the lowest level registered in 2013 . However , the average monthly consumption expenditure still did n't reach the pre-crisis level . In 2008 , for example , the monthly average was almost 100 euros higher compared to 2019 .
