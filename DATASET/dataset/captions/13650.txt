This statistic shows the daily capacities of oil refineries in Spain between 2005 and 2019 . The daily capacity increased over this period , to 1.55 million barrels from 2012 onwards . For 2019 , the daily oil refinery capacities of Spain made up 1.5 percent of the world 's total daily capacity , according to the source . In 2016 , the global refinery capacity for crude oil stood at approximately 97.4 million barrels per day .
