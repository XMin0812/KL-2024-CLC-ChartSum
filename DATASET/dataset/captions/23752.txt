In 2018 , the B2B e-commerce value of chemical manufacturing shipments in the United States amounted to 514.5 billion U.S. dollars , up from 506.5 billion U.S. dollars in the previous measured period . This represents a 1.6 percent year-over-year segment growth . In 2018 , e-commerce accounted for over 67 percent of total shipments in the chemical manufacturing sector .
