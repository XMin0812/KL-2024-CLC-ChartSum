The term 'community garden ' in the United States can mean a few different things . For example , they can function as gathering places for the community and/or neighbors , however , they can also resemble the allotment gardens , often found in Europe , used by individuals and families . Of course , some cities are home to more community gardens than others . In 2019 , both Portland , OR and St. Paul , MN had the largest number with 3.8 community gardens per 10,000 residents .
