This statistic shows gross domestic product ( GDP ) of the Mashriq countries in 2018 . The Mashriq , also Mashreq , is the region encompassing the eastern part of the Arab World , comprising the countries Bahrain , Egypt , Iraq , Jordan , Kuwait , Lebanon , Oman , Palestine , Qatar , Saudi Arabia , Sudan , Syria , United Arab Emirates , and Yemen . In 2018 , GDP of Saudi Arabia amounted to approximately 1,862.15 billion international dollars .
