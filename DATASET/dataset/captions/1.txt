How many users does Facebook have ? With over 2.7 billion monthly active users as of the second quarter of 2020 , Facebook is the biggest social network worldwide . In the third quarter of 2012 , the number of active Facebook users surpassed one billion , making it the first social network ever to do so . Active users are those which have logged in to Facebook during the last 30 days . During the last reported quarter , the company stated that 3.14 billion people were using at least one of the company 's core products ( Facebook , WhatsApp , Instagram , or Messenger ) each month .
