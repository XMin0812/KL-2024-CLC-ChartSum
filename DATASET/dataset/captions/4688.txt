According to a survey held in late October 2019 , the most popular 'Sesame Street ' character in the United States was the Cookie Monster , with 16 percent of responding adults citing the character as their favorite . Elmo and Big Bird ranked second and third respectively , followed by Oscar the Grouch , Grover , Count von Count , and Mr Snuffleapagus . 'Sesame Street ' first aired in November 1969 , and almost fifty years later the show remains popular among children and adults alike .
