This statistic illustrates the Index of Private Housing Rental Prices ( IPHRP ) in London from January 2016 to July 2020 . The Index of Private Housing Rental Prices measures the change in price of renting residential property from private landlords , based on an index value of 100 in January 2015 . The IPHRP increased gradually from January 2016 until October 2017 , oscillating on the following months , reaching a value of 106.3 index points as of January 2018 . As of July 2020 , the IPHRP stood at 108.3 index points .
