Breast cancer is one of the most frequently diagnosed diseases in Italy . As of 2016 , mortality rates due to this type of cancer changed according to the region . According to the source , Sardinia was the region with the highest incidence of breast cancer as cause of death , with 41.18 women out of 100,000 population , deceased from this type of tumor , in 2016 . Piedmont and Lombardy followed with mortality rates of 37.72 and 37.64 per 100,000 inhabitants , respectively .
