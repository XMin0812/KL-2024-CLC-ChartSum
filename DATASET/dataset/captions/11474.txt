This statistic shows the number of cars sold by Mercedes in Turkey between 2012 and 2019 . From 2012 to 2016 , Turkish sales of Mercedes cars increased steadily from 12.7 thousand units sold to over 32 thousand units sold . The sale of Mercedes cars declined in the following years , amounting to 9.9 thousand cars sold in 2019 , a decrease of roughly 70 percent in comparison with 2016 .
