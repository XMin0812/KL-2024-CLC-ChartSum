This statistic illustrates the monthly product cost index of cold drawn bars manufactured in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from July 2016 to July 2018 . Cold drawing refers to a process in which metal is formed in shape and size by being pulled through dies while cold , it is often used in the manufacture of wire , tubing and rods . This data shows the product cost index reached a peak in July 2018 at 111.4 points .
