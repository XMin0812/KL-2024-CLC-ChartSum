Roman Catholic is the most common religion affiliation in Colombia . In a survey carried out between July and August of 2018 , nearly 74 percent of Colombian respondents claimed to be of catholic faith , whereas the second most chosen religion was Protestantism , with almost 11 percent of the people interviewed . More than nine percent of respondents answered they had no religious affiliation , meanwhile a total of 0.3 percent defined themselves either as agnostic or atheist .
