The Manchester Metrolink recorded just over 44 million passenger journeys in 2019/20 . Since beginning its operation in April 1992 as the United Kingdom 's first modern tram system , the Metrolink has grown to become an integral part of public transportation within the city . The Metrolink is one of currently nine light rail systems in the country , next to those in London 's docklands , Croydon , Birmingham , Sheffield , Newcastle , Nottingham , Blackpool , and Edinburgh .
