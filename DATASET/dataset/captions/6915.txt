Waitrose sales have decreased by 1.9 percent in Great Britain over a 12-week period ending July 12 , 2019 compared to the same time period in 2018 . Waitrose has seen its sales grow during the last three and a half years . The second quarter of 2017 saw the highest growth , with sales going up over five percent . At the end of 2018 , the company experienced shrinking sales for the first time since mid-2015 .
