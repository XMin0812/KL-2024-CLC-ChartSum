This statistic shows the monthly trend in the volume of footwear and leather goods sales ( quantity bought ) in Great Britain from January 2016 to June 2020 . The figures are seasonally adjusted estimates , measured using the Retail Sales Index ( RSI ) and published in index form with a reference year of 2016 equal to 100 . In April 2020 sales reached an all-time low at 31 but has since increased in June 2020 to an index level of 68.8 .
