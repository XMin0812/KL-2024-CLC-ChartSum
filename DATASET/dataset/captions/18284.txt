This statistic shows the annual consumption of natural gas for energy use in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 1970 to 2019 . It shows that the consumption of natural gas for energy use increased significantly from 1970 to 2000 , going from 11.3 million metric tons of oil equivalent to 95.9 million metric tons of oil equivalent . In the most recent years however , the consumption decreased rapidly to 66.1 million metric tons of oil equivalent in 2014 . There has been a slight recovery in the following years .
