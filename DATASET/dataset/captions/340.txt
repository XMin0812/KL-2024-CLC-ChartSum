The Dallas Cowboys , one of the most iconic and popular teams in the National Football League , has had the most playoff appearances to its name with 33 . The team has made it to the Super Bowl eight times , winning five of those championship games . However , since the start of the new millennium another team , the New England Patriots , have been much more successful . As of the year 2000 , the Patriots have made the playoffs 17 times , winning the NFL championship game Super Bowl six times .
