This statistic shows the monthly amount of cars sold by Mitsubishi in the United Kingdom ( UK ) between July 2016 and December 2019 . Peaks in registration numbers were recorded in March and September of each year , which was due to the issuing of license plates by the Driver & Vehicle Licensing Agency ( DVLA ) in those months . In March 2019 , around 3,600 new Mitsubishi cars had been sold . Overall , 2019 saw sales down roughly 23.4 percent compared to 2018 .
