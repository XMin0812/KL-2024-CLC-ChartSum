This statistic displays the population of Belgium in 2020 , by age group . In 2020 , approximately 800 thousand inhabitants were aged between 55 and 59 , and roughly 93 thousand people were aged between 90 and 94 . The largest age group was formed by those between 55 and 59 years old , whereas those over 100 years old formed the smallest age group . In 2020 , approximately 1,794 people in Belgium were 100 years or older .
