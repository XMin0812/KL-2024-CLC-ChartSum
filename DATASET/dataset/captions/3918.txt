The statistic shows gross domestic product ( GDP ) per capita in Singapore from 1985 to 2025  . GDP is the total value of all goods and services produced in a country in a year . It is considered to be a very important indicator of the economic strength of a country and a positive change is an indicator of economic growth . In 2019 , the GDP per capita in Singapore amounted to around 65,233.88 U.S. dollars . For comparison , the country with the largest GDP per capita is currently Luxembourg with approximately 113 thousand dollars in 2019 .
