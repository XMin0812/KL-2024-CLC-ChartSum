The market capitalization of companies listed on the Alternative Investment Market on the Milan Stock Exchange ( also known as AIM ITALIA ) increased more than twofold over the period considered in this graph , before decreasing during the course of 2020 and reaching 5.5 billion euros as of September 30 , 2020 . Market capitalization represents the number of shares in a company multiplied by the price per share . Due to its success , the number of companies listed on AIM Italia , which operate in various sectors , increased steadily since it was established .
