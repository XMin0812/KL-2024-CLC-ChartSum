This statistic displays a comparison of predicted U.S. sand demand and capacity growth between 2013 and 2016 . In that period , demand is expected to increase some 96 percent . In the meantime , sand capacities are predicted to increase only 76 percent . According to some sources , the fracking industry could suffer from a frac sand supply shortage over the next years . In 2012 , about 2.6 billion U.S. dollars worth of industrial sand and gravel were used and sold in the United States . Most of the produced material was used as hydraulic fracturing sand and well-packing and cementing sand .
