This statistic presents the number of intermediaries cooperating with Mossack Fonseca as of April 2016 , by country . Mossack Fonseca , based in Panama , is one of the world 's largest offshore services providers . The data about the company and its clients was revealed in April 2016 as a result of a leak . Mossack Fonseca worked with various intermediaries , such as law firms , banks or accountants , which represented the interests of their clients . The company worked with 1,924 intermediaries from the United Kingdom as of April 2016 .
