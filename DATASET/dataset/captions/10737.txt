The statistic depicts the brand value of the most valuable Australian sports brands in 2013 . According to the ranking the Penrith Panthers of the National Rugby League ( NRL ) have a brand value of 46.2 million U.S. dollars in 2013 . A brand is defined here as the trademark and associated intellectual property . Football clubs are made up of a mixture of fixed tangible assets ( stadium , training ground ) and disclosed intangible assets ( purchased players ) with brand value.To calculate brand values the Royalty Relief method was used . This approach assumes the company does n't own their brand and must license it from a theoretical third party . The method determines how much it would cost to do this .
