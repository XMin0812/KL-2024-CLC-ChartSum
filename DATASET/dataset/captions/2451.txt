2019 saw the largest expenditures on Medicaid in U.S. history . At that time about 639.4 billion U.S. dollars were expended on the Medicaid public health insurance program that aims to provide affordable health care options to low income residents and people with disabilities . Medicaid was signed into law in 1965 . By 1966 just under 1 billion U.S. dollars were spent on the program .
