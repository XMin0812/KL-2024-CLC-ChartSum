Perhaps unsurprisingly , given the enormous popularity of Marvel movies across the globe , ‘ Avengers : Infinity War ' was the highest grossing movie in the world in 2018 , reaching box office revenue of almost 2.05 billion U.S. dollars . ‘ Black Panther ' followed with approximately 1.35 billion dollars in box office revenue , and ‘ Jurassic World : Fallen Kingdom ' ranked third . Fourth in the ranking was Brad Bird 's much anticipated ‘ Incredibles 2 ' with revenue of over 1.3 billion at the global box office , making the movie the most successful animated film on the list .
