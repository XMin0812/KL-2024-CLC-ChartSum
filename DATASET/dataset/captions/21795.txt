The recommendations of the Minister of Health concerning the order to work remotely , if allowed by the nature of the performed work , during the coronavirus pandemic in Poland were taken seriously by both employers and employees . However , not everyone preferred this style of work . For almost every fourth respondent , remote working was very burdensome . Every second person declared that the home office , as such is not cumbersome but creates some difficulties .
