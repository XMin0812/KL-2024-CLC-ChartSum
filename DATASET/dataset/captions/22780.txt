In the third quarter of 2020 , the share of unemployed individuals in Italy aged between 15 and 24 years stood at 30.7 percent . Between 2013 and 2020 , the youth unemployment rate recorded its highest figures in 2014 . In 2019 , the Italian regions registering the highest youth unemployment rate were the Southern regions of Sicily , Calabria , and Campania , with figures going from 47 percent to 51 percent .
