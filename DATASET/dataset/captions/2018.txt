As of May 3 , 2020 the number of deaths related to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) amounted to 28.9 thousand . Cerebrovascular disorders were estimated to have caused 20.4 thousand deaths . In fact , data on the number of deaths not caused by COVID-19 were estimated . In Italy , the northern regions registered the largest number of coronavirus deaths . Especially , Lombardy , Emilia-Romagna , and Piedmont recorded the highest number of deaths .
