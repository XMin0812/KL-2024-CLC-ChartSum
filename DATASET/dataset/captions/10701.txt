This statistic presents information on the percentage of mobile traffic to Zalando online properties from the third quarter of 2013 to the second quarter of 2020 . Zalando is a Berlin-based online fashion company . As of the second quarter of 2020 , 85.9 percent of traffic originated from mobile devices , including tablets . Over the period in consideration , the mobile share of traffic increased from 28.2 to 85.9 percent in the last reported quarter .
