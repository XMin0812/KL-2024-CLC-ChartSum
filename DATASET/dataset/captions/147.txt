On January 31 , 2020 , the first two cases of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in the United Kingdom ( UK ) were confirmed in Newcastle upon Tyne , a city in the Northeast of England . The number of cases in the UK has since risen to 2,654,779 , with 54,990 new cases reported on January 3 , 2021 . Since the beginning of September there have been a significant increase in the number of daily cases in the UK .
