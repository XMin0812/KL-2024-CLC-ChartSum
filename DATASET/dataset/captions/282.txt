This statistic shows the number of people infected , deceased and recovered from the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in France as of December 11 , 2020 . To this date , France has listed 2,405,049 cases of this novel coronavirus on its territory . 57,671 people had succumbed to the disease COVID-19 .
