This line graph shows the number of road traffic fatalities per year in Luxembourg between 2006 and 2015 . In 2010 , 32 individuals were killed in motor vehicle accidents . In 2015 , Luxembourg recorded 36 road traffic fatalities , a decrease of roughly 25 percent in comparison with 2009 .
