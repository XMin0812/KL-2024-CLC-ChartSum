It is estimated that , since the terrorist attacks of September 11 , 2001 , the global war on terror will cost the United States government just over 5.4 trillion U.S. dollars . This figure includes estimates of all budgetary spending related to the war on terror between FY 2001 and FY 2020 . This figure of 5.4 trillion does not include the ongoing medical and disability expenditure for veterans beyond FY 2020 , which is estimated to cost an additional one trillion U.S. dollars by FY 2059 .
