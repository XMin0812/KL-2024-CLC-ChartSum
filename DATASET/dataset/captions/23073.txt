The David di Donatello Award takes its name from DonatelloÂ´s David and is an Italian film award . The Awards follow the same criteria of the Oscars and were established in 1955 . As of 2006 , 24 categories were awarded . The ceremonies were hold in Rome in 1956 , at the Greek Theatre in Taormina between 1957 and 1980 , twice in Florence and finally in Rome again . In 2019 , the song for the movie Chiamami col tuo nome won the David di Donatello Award for Best Original Song with 338 votes .
