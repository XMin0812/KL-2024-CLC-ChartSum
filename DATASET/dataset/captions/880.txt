The statistic shows tobacco tax revenue in the United States from 2000 to 2019 , with an additional forecast from 2020 to 2025 . In 2019 , revenues from tobacco tax amounted to 12.46 billion U.S. dollars . The forecast predicts a decrease in tobacco tax revenues down to 11.55 billion U.S. dollars in 2025 . Total U.S. government revenue in 2018 was 3.5 trillion U.S. dollars .
