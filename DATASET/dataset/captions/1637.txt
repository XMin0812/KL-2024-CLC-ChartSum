As of January 1 , 2019 , there were approximately 3.8 million people younger than 20 years living in the Netherlands . The largest age group was formed by those between 40 and 65 years old . Together , this age group numbered nearly six million people . The retired population of the Netherlands consisted of approximately three million people . Considering this make-up of Dutch society , it is not surprising there are so many debates on increasing the retirement age . The ratio of the non-working population to the working population has decreased between 1950 and today . In 2019 , the green population pressure was 37.3 percent .
