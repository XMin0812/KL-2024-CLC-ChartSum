In the view of the coronavirus ( COVD-19 ) pandemic , Russians ' purchases of refrigerators and freezers skyrocketed over the second week of March 2020 . Namely , on good.ru , sales of such products grew by more than 16 times over the observed period relative to the yearly average in 2019 , while ozon.ru recorded a fourfold growth of refrigerator and freezer sales in March 2020 .
