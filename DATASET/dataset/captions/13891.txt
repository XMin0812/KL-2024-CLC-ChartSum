The statistic presents data on the most successful hip hop labels of all time in the United States as of November 2015 , based on their artists ' performance on Billboard 's Rap Chart . According to the source , artists signed to the Young Money record label have spent a cumulative 1322 weeks in the rap charts in the United States . The Young Money label was founded in 2005 by rapper Lil Wayne , and represents artists such as Drake and Nicki Minaj .
