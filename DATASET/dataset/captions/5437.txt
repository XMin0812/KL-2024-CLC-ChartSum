With more than 95 percent of the market share , Google was without a doubt the leader among search engines in Norway , as of October 2020 . Google was followed by bing and Yahoo ! , which accounted for market shares of roughly three percent and one percent respectively . The figures were similar for desktop search engines in the country . Google 's growth can be seen in the company 's revenue figures over the years , with a dramatical increase from 0.4 billion U.S. dollars in 2002 , to over 160 billion U.S. dollars in 2019 .
