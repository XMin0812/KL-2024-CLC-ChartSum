The number of people in employment in Sweden increased annually since 2009 , reaching around 5.1 million in 2019 . Of the employed population , around 505 thousand were self-employed , most common in the ages from 45 to 54 years where around 133.5 thousand were self-employed . This age group was followed by the age group from 55 to 64 years , where self-employed numbered nearly 126 thousand .
