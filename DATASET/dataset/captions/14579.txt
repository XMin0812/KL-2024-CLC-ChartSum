On February 29 , 2020 , the first case of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Ireland was confirmed in Dublin . As of January 7 , 2021 , the number of cases in Ireland has risen to 127,657 . There were over 26 million coronavirus cases in Europe as of January 3 , with Russia currently the worst affected country .
