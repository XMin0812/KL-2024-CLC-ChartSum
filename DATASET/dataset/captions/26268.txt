In Italy , the number of cars sold by Kia between 2011 and 2019 more than doubled , from 19,454 units in 2011 to 47,730 units in 2019 . The sales volume peaked in 2017 at nearly 48,000 units . However , sales figures dropped considerably in 2020 to just over 35,450 units , the lowest figure since 2015 .
