This statistic shows the number of visits to the Smithsonian National Museum of American History in the United States from 2001 to 2019 . Visits to the National Museum of American History amounted to approximately 2.8 million in 2019 , down from the previous year 's total of 4.1 million visits . The most visited Smithsonian museums in 2019 were the National Museum of Natural History and the National Air and Space Museum .
