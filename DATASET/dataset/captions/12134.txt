Germany and the United Kingdom remain the two largest national fitness markets in Europe with total revenues of about 5.51 billion euros each . Between 2017 and 2018 , the revenue of the UK fitness increased by 168 million euros , an increase of 3.3 percent . This rate of growth is significantly greater than the rate of GDP across the entire UK economy , which over this same period exhibited only 1.4 percent growth , the lowest growth rate seen since the financial crisis in 2008 .
