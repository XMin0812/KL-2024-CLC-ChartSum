The total contribution of the travel and tourism sector to employment in Cuba experienced some strong fluctuations during the past decade . In 2017 , the Caribbean country registered the peak in tourism employment , with over 561 thousand jobs related to that sector , while 2014 recorded a strong drop in tourism jobs , falling to the approximately 504 thousand . In 2018 and 2019 , travel and tourism in Cuba accounted for around 556 thousand jobs per year .
