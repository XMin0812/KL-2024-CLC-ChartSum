The statistic illustrates the Common Equity Tier 1 ratio ( CET1 ) for the Barclays PLC group from 2012 to 2019 . According to the source , capital requirements are part of the regulatory framework governing how banks and depository institutions are supervised . Capital ratios express a bank 's capital as a percentage of its risk-weighted assets ( RWAs ) . Barclays saw a CET1 ratio of 13.8 percent in 2019 .
