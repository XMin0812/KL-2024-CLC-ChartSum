On 4 March 2020 , the first case of coronavirus 2019-nCoV was confirmed in Poland . As of January 2021 , the number of patients infected with the coronavirus amounted to 1.3 million . According to the Minister of Health , more than 30 thousand infected patients died , and most of them had serious health problems before the infection .
