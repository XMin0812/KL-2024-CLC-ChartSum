In 2019 , Barnes & Noble had 627 stores in the United States , three fewer than in the previous year . The company had 726 locations all across the country back in 2008 , however this figure has been decreasing ever since , with the sharpest drop recorded between 2012 and 2013 . Barnes & Noble has had a patchy few years in terms of its profitability and reported a net loss of almost 125.5 million U.S. dollars in 2018 , followed by net income of just 3.77 million in 2019 . The company has also drastically reduced its number of employees , replacing full-time workers earning an annual salary with part-time staff on lower wages .
