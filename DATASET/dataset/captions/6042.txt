During the 2019/20 Scottish Premiership season , St Mirren sold the cheapest season ticket at 240 British pounds , followed by Ross County at 250 British pounds . Hamilton had their season ticket prices set at a fixed 300 British pounds , however the other team 's tickets ranged in pricing as for example , Celtic 's cheapest season ticket cost 456 British pounds , their most expensive season ticket cost 647 British pounds .
