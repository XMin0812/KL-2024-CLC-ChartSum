This statistic shows the membership figures of the political parties in Germany on December 31 , 2019 . At the end of 2019 , the CDU had 419,340 party members . There are currently five fractions in the German Parliament and six political parties represented : the SPD , the CDU and the CSU ( a fraction ) , die Linke ( The Left ) , das Bündnis 90/Die Grünen ( The Greens ) and the AfD ( Alternative for Germany ) .
