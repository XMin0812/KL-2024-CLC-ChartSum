According to a survey among members of the employers association , Dansk Erhverv , several industries in Denmark have experienced losses in April 2020 due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak . Compared to the previous year , especially the experience industry ( including travel , tourism and hospitality services ) seem to have suffered from the effects of the virus with 98 percent of the surveyed businesses experiencing a decrease in revenues . Similarly , 82 percent of businesses in the retailing industry stated having the same problem . The first case of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Denmark was confirmed on February 27 , 2020 .
