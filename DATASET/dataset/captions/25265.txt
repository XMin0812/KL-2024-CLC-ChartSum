Between the years of 2010 and 2013 , the internal travel and tourism consumption in the U.S. Virgin Islands was increasing , with the industry hitting its peak of the decade in 2013 at 3.24 billion U.S. dollars . However , the U.S. territory in the Caribbean islands experienced turbulence with its annual internal tourism consumption revenues in the years that followed . Nonetheless , the industry 's 2019 revenue of 2.59 billion U.S. dollars is up from the 2.53 billion reported in the U.S. Virgin Islands a year prior .
