This statistic illustrates the quarterly total amount of repayments of all secured loans on dwellings in the United Kingdom ( UK ) between the first quarter of 2012 and the third quarter of 2019 . Secured loans on dwellings includes all home purchase loans and remortgaging . It can be seen that the total value of repayments of secured loans on dwellings increased overall during this period with some fluctuation , reaching a total of nearly 56.5 billion British pounds as of the third quarter of 2019 .
