The citizens of Barbados had the most powerful passports in the Caribbean region as of July 2020 . At that moment in time , the Barbadian passport received a visa free score of 161 points . Passports from St. Kitts and Nevis and the Bahamas ranked second and third , scoring 156 and 155 as of July 2020 . Meanwhile , Chilean citizens were holders of the most powerful passport in Latin American region that same year .
