In 2019 , the Chinese idol boy band under Tencent , R1SE , sold over three million copies of their first music album `` Going To Powerfully Burst '' across the mainstream digital music platforms in China . Taylor Swift 's `` Lover '' ranked third and Ariana Grande 's `` Thank U , Next '' claimed the ninth spot . The music albums of Korean music groups - Blackpink , BTS , and EXO - also made to the top ten bestsellers .
