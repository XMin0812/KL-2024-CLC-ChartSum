This statistic shows the number of cars sold by Peugeot in Greece between 2008 and 2019 . Greek sales of Peugeot cars fell from over 10 thousand units sold in 2008 down to 1.4 thousand units by 2013 , although this was followed by growth in the following years . In 2019 , there were 9.8 thousand units of Peugeot cars sold in Greece .
