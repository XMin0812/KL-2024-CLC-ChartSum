The statistic shows the leading merger and acquisition ( M & A ) deals in Croatia in 2019 , by transaction value . The largest deal amounted to approximately over 220 million euros ; this was the price paid for the acquisition of 100 percent stake of Tele2 by BC Partners Holdings ( United Kingdom ) . The smallest deal in the top five amounted to 36 million euros by Mid Europa Partners ( United Kingdom ) to acquire 30 percent stake of Meritus ulaganja .
