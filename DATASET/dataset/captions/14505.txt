This statistic shows the results of a study comparing the price of Christmas grocery shopping at five leading grocery retailers in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in 2014 . The study calculated the prices of 33 items typically included on UK consumers ' Christmas shopping lists at various stores . Asda was found to be the cheapest grocery retailer , with a Christmas shop calculated at 119.96 British pounds ( GBP ) . This was almost 10 GBP cheaper than Tesco and almost 20 GBP cheaper than Sainsbury 's and Waitrose .
