It was estimated that around 5.6 percent of those aged 70 to 79 years who had COVID-19 in the United States from January 22 to May 30 , 2020 were admitted to the intensive care unit ( ICU ) . Hospitalizations due to COVID-19 are much higher among those with underlying health conditions such as cardiovascular disease , chronic lung disease , or diabetes . This statistic shows the percentage of people in the U.S. who had COVID-19 from January 22 to May 30 , 2020 who were admitted to the ICU , by age .
