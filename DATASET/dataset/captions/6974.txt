This statistic shows Hewlett Packard Enterprise 's ( previously a part of Hewlett-Packard Company ) expenditure on research and development for each fiscal year from 2013 to 2020 . In 2020 , HPE 's R & D expenditure came to 1.87 billion U.S. dollars . This represented a small portion of HPE 's net revenue , which reached 26.98 billion U.S. dollars .
