This statistic depicts the abortion rate per 1,000 U.S. females aged 15 - 19 years from 1973 to 2013 . According to the data , the abortion rate fell from 44 abortions per 1,000 U.S. females aged 15 - 19 in the years 1985 and 1988 to 10.6 abortions per 1,000 U.S. females aged 15 - 19 in the year 2013 .
