In January 2020 , the U.S. motion picture and sound recording industry employed over 456 thousand people , up from the 439.8 recorded in the previous year . Preliminary estimates for February and March of 2020 were similar to or higher than the figure reported for January , but in light of the coronavirus outbreak , the unfortunate reality is that a lot can change in a short period of time , and with many sectors at risk , employment will likely be affected in months to come .
