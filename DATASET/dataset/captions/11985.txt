In 2019 , approximately 4.3 thousand companies were estimated to be active in the footwear manufacturing sector in Italy . The number decreased gradually since 2014 , when there were 5 thousand companies operating in the sector . The decrease affected also the employment figures of the sector . In 2019 , 74.9 thousand individuals were employed in the footwear industry , compared to over 77 thousand in 2015 .
