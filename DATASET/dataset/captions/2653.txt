As of January 2020 , the superhero movies ‘ Avengers : Endgame ' _ , 'Spider-Man : Far From Home ' _ , and 'Joker ' among others collected a combined box office revenue of nearly 3.2 billion U.S. dollars . This figure includes the box office revenues generated by four 'Batman ' re-releases , as well as the 2019 revenues for 'Aquaman ' and 'Spider-Man : Into the Spider-Verse ' _ . Superhero movies have grown immensely popular in the last few years , not just in the United States but on a global level . DC 's ‘ Aquaman ' grossed almost 1.15 billion U.S. dollars worldwide in 2018 , and movies from the X-Men series continued to perform well in theaters across the world .
