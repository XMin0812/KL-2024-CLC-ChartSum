During the COVID-19 emergency in Italy , the support of the EU intervention was considered weak . A survey from March 2020 reveals that 41 percent of interviewees disliked the European Union 's attitude towards Italy . On the other hand , trust in the Italian Prime Minister Giuseppe Conte has been rising during the coronavirus emergency . In a survey from March 2020 , most Italian interviewees approved the government 's response to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic . Italy is one of the countries with the highest number of coronavirus cases worldwide . The Italian regions with the largest number of cases are Lombardy and Emilia-Romagna , located in the North .
