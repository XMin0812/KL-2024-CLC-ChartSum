The statistic shows gross domestic product ( GDP ) in Thailand from 1984 to 2018 , with projections up until 2021 . Gross domestic product ( GDP ) denotes the aggregate value of all services and goods produced within a country in any given year . GDP is an important indicator of a country 's economic power . In 2018 , Thailand 's gross domestic product amounted to around 1,322.56 billion international dollars .
