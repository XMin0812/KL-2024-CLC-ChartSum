About a third of respondents were happy with the current solution for funding TVP ( Telewizja Polska ) in 2020 - from advertising and RTV subscriptions . Over 40 percent thought it should be financed exclusively from advertising . The Polish government has been working on an amendment to the act on subscription fees , providing compensation to public media for lost subscription revenue . Currently , public media - including TVP - are financed primarily from advertising and RTV subscriptions . RTV subscription should be paid by owners of radio and television sets ; however , in reality , only about half of households pay this fee .
