Major League Baseball is a North American sports organization consisting of two leagues with a total of 30 teams : the National League and the American League with 15 teams each . The owning of an MLB team , as with any sports team , has come to be a status symbol among the mega rich . In 2020 , Ted Lerner , owner of the Washington Nationals , had a net worth of 4.8 billion U.S. dollars , making him the richest MLB team owner in the United States . Lerner , who made his money in real estate , ceded the role of managing principal owner of the Nationals to his son , Mark D. Lerner , in 2018 .
