Amazon UK Services Ltd. is responsible for Amazon 's logistics and fulfilment operations in the United Kingdom ( UK ) . As of 2018 financial year ended December 31 , the company employs over 20 thousand people throughout its warehousing and logistics workforce in the UK . Since 2010 , Amazon UK Services exponentially increased the number of employees , going from 2,265 to 22,073 people within the span of a decade .
