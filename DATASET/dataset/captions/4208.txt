This statistic depicts BP 's expenditure on research and development from 2010 to 2019 . In 2011 , the company 's R & D expenditure stood at approximately 636 million U.S. dollars . BP is a globally operating oil and gas company , and the seventh largest company generally worldwide as of 2018 , measured by revenue . It is headquartered in London , UK . Until 2019 , R & D spending decreased to some 364 million dollars .
