In the third quarter of 2020 , Intel 's Programmable Solutions Group 's revenue amounted to 411 million U.S. dollars , a decrease from the figure recorded for the same quarter of 2019 . The third quarter of 2020 also saw a fall in revenues generated by the Programmable Solutions Group segment when compared to the second quarter of 2020 where a figure of 501 million U.S. dollars was recorded .
