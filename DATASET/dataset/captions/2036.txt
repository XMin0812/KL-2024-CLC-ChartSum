The top selling album of all time in the United States based on certified unit sales is currently the Eagles ' Greatest Hits , with 38 million copies sold . Michael Jackson 's 1982 album , 'Thriller ' _ , ranked second with 33 million sales , and the Eagles also held third place in the ranking with 'Hotel California ' _ , which had 26 million certified unit sales as of September 2020 . Also featuring in the ranking were several world-famous rock bands : AC/DC , Led Zeppelin , Guns N ' Roses , Fleetwood Mac , and Boston .
