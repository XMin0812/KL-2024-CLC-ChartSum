In Denmark , the total number of reported criminal offences fluctuated in recent years . In 2019 , the number stood at close to 499 thousand crimes , and among them there were 52 homicides reported . According to the Danish Penal Code , the penalty for homicide ranges from a minimum of a five-year prison sentence to imprisonment for life . Imprisonment for life implies 12 years in prison , before the inmate can apply for probation .
