In the second round of the election , over 50 percent of respondents intend to vote for the incumbent President of Poland , Andrzej Duda . In the first round of the presidential election in Poland , which took place on June 28th , 2020 , the incumbent President won , gaining the support of 43.5 percent of the population . The candidate who receives more than half of all valid votes becomes President . Therefore , on July 12th , the second round of the election will occur , and Andrzej Duda 's rival will be the incumbent Warsaw Mayor , Rafal Trzaskowski .
