This statistic shows the quarterly market value of donation-based crowdfunding within the alternative finance sector in the United Kingdom ( UK ) , from the first quarter to the fourth quarter of 2014 and 2015 . Alternative finance refers to instruments and channels , which developed outside of the traditional and highly regulated finance system , such as banks and capital markets . The debt-based crowfunding market value was at the level of 0.4 million British pounds in the first quarter of 2014 . It increased to 5.53 million British pounds in the fourth quarter of 2015 .
