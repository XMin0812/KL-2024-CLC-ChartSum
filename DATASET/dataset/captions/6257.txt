The number of deaths in Denmark remained roughly the same during the period considered , peaking in 2018 , when around 55 thousand individuals died . The number of deaths was higher for males than for females until 2014 , when the death numbers for males started to be higher than for females . In 2018 , around 28 thousand men and around 27 thousand women died . In 2019 , there were 53,958 deaths in Denmark .
