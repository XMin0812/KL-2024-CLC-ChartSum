Over the past few years , the food delivery sector has started experiencing a tremendous growth . Changing consumer behaviors as well as new digital market developments enabled this industry to spread all over the Italian Peninsula . As a matter of fact , in 2019 the total value of the food delivery sector accounted for 3.2 billion euros . Although offline food delivery services were still predominant ( amounting to 82 percent of the whole industry ) , online services increased significantly , reaching 18 percent .
