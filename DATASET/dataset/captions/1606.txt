The statistic depicts Mexico 's real gross domestic product ( GDP ) growth rate from 2015 to 2019 , with projections up until 2025 . GDP refers to the total market value of all goods and services that are produced within a country per year . It is an important indicator of the economic strength of a country . Real GDP is adjusted for price changes and is therefore regarded as a key indicator for economic growth . In 2019 , Mexico 's real GDP fell by about 0.3 percent compared to the previous year .
