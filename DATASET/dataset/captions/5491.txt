Want to live somewhere quiet ? Then the Brussels-Capital Region maybe would not be the place for you . In a country where the population density was highly depended on the region , the Brussels-Capital Region far exceeded the others in terms of residents per square kilometer . Whereas in Brussels over 7,000 people lived per a square kilometer , in the Walloon Region this was only 214 . In total , roughly 1.2 million inhabitants lived in the Brussels-Capital Region in 2018 .
