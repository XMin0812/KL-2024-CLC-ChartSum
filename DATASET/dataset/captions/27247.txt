In the first half of 2020 , Democratic presidential candidate Elizabeth Warren spent the second largest amount on Facebook ads related to the topic of the Mueller report . Between January and May 2020 she devoted 479 thousand U.S. dollars to use social media to raise the issue of alleged Russian interference in the 2016 presidential elections . The largest ad expense belonged to Donald Trump 's campaign , reaching over 1.3 million U.S. dollars .
