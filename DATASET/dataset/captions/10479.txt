Based on their overall scores according to the Quacquarelli Symonds ( QS ) World University Rankings 2021 , some of the leading Latin American universities are located in Argentina , Mexico , Brazil , Chile , and Colombia . The Argentinian Universidad de Buenos Aires ( UBA ) ranked first with a score of 67.5 out of 100 , while the Universidad Autónoma de México ( UNAM ) ranked second , with 58.8 points .
