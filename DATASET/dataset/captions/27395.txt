This statistic displays the worldwide revenues of NuVasive from 2012 to 2019 , in million U.S. dollars . In 2014 , annual revenues were about 762 million U.S. dollars , while in 2019 , revenues were 1.17 billion dollars . NuVasive Inc. is a medical device company , headquartered in San Diego , California . The company is focused on spine surgery technology .
