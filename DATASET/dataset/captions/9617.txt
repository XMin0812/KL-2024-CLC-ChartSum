In 2016 , most deaths in Belgium were due to natural causes . In fact , only 6 percent of deaths were due to unnatural causes . Over the 108 thousand deaths reported in the country in 2016 , the predominance of natural causes also translates into numbers . Indeed , Belgium registered over 101 thousand natural casualties and 6.7 thousand unnatural deaths . A death by natural causes is due to an illness or an internal malfunction of the body . These casualties cover causes such as infections or heart attacks . Whereas unnatural causes are caused by external forces , such as homicides , suicides , and accidents .
