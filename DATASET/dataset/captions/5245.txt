This graph shows the ranking of the 25 richest people in France in 2019 , by amount of assets , in billions of euros . We can observe that in 2019 , the three richest people in France that year were Bernard Arnault , owner of the group LVMH , Françoise Bettencourt Meyers , L'Oréal 's main shareholder , and François Pinault , businessman and founder of the Kering group .
