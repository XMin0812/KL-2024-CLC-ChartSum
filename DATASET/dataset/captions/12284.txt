The statistic shows COSCO Container 's total TEU capacity as of October 12 , 2020 . The Chinese container company owned ships with a total capacity of around 1.55 million twenty-foot equivalent units . In July 2017 , COSCO put in a bid to buy Hong Kong based Orient Overseas Container Line ( OOCL ) as well as its parent company . The merger was completed in June 2018 .
