Since the state of emergency decreed in Chile on October 17th of 2019 , a total of 2,009 people were admitted to hospitals due to the injuries they suffered . Of them , 643 were reportedly wounded by shotguns . The crisis in Chile arose as a result of the decision of Sebastián Piñera 's government to increase the price of the metro fare . The announcement led to massive evasions in public transport , along with generalized street demonstrations .
