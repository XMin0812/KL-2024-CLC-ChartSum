This statistic provides key data on Beats Music as of March 2014 . The service 's March royalties paid to songwriters and publishers amounted to 22.97 thousand U.S. dollars , where the royalties paid to artists and labels amounted to 249.07 thousand U.S. dollars in the same time period . The source additionally estimated that the royalties per play amounted to 0.00012 and 0.00136 U.S. dollars , respectively .
