This statistic shows the share of responses of a survey on the main factors which could encourage people to buy electric cars or vans within Great Britain between in February 2016 . The main factor which could convince the respondents to buy an electric car or van was a good price followed by 19 percent of respondents saying the ability to drive further on a charged battery and convenience of recharging . However , 27 percent said nothing would encourage them to buy an electric car or van .
