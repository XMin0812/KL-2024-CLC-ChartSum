This statistic shows the share of French people avoiding crowded public places during the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic in 2020 . Whereas on March 10 , only 39 percent of French people said they avoided crowded public places , this share had risen to 82 percent by March 27 , ten days after the national lockdown announced by President Macron had started .
