The Dominican Republic registered a constant increase in inbound tourism spending during the past decade . From 2010 to 2019 , this type of expenditures grew around 75 percent , going up from over 4.5 billion U.S. dollars in 2010 to over 7.9 billion U.S. dollars in 2019 . That same year , international tourism accounted for 73 percent of the Caribbean country 's travel and tourism spending .
