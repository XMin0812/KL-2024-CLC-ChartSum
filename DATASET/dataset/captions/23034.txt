This statistic illustrates the average mix-adjusted house price in London , the United Kingdom ( UK ) from March 2012 to September 2020 , in quarterly intervals . During this period , the average mix-adjusted house price in London increased from roughly 290.3 thousand British pounds in the early part of 2012 to approximately 496.5 thousand British pounds in September 2020 . The recent fluctuation in the value of residential properties in London has also been observed by other measures , such as house price index . House price index is an important measure for the residential real estate market and is used to show changes in the the value of residential properties .
