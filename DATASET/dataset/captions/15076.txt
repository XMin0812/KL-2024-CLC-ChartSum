This statistic shows the most affordable cities for backpackers worldwide as of January 2020 . According to the source , Saigon ( Ho Chi Minh City ) , Viet Nam , was the city with the cheapest daily total with an average cost of 19.49 U.S. dollars per day - this includes : a dorm bed at a good and cheap hostel , three budget meals , two public transportation rides , one paid cultural attraction and three cheap beers ( as an `` entertainment fund '' ) .
