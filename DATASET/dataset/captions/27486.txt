In March 2020 , around 82 percent of the Finnish people had a positive opinion about the government 's efforts to tackle COVID-19 outbreak . Only four percent of the respondents thought that the government has handled the situation very poorly , while around 13 percent estimated that the government 's has acted somewhat poorly . The first COVID-19 case in Finland was confirmed on January 29 , 2020 . For further information about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , please visit our dedicated Facts and Figures page .
