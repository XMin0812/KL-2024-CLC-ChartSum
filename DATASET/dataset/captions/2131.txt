Released in March 2017 , PlayerUnknown 's Battlegrounds pits up to 100 players against each other in a battle royale fight to the death . Considering the size of each battle , it is unsurprising that the game topped the 2019 list for peak number of concurrent players on the online gaming platform , Steam . PlayerUnknown 's Battlegrounds reached almost 1.09 million players in a single hour in its peak , surpassing its closest rival , DOTA 2 .
