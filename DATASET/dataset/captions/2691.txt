Nairobi is the epicenter of the coronavirus pandemic in Kenya . As of January 4 , 2021 , the capital registered most of the confirmed COVID-19 cases in the country , 40,968 . The amount corresponded to nearly 42 percent of the total cases in Kenya , over 96 thousand on the same date . Mombasa , Kenya 's oldest and second largest city , had 8,842 cases , whereas in Kiambu , within the Nairobi Metropolitan Region , 6,375 infected people were registered . The number of deaths due to the coronavirus in the country exceeded 1,600 .
