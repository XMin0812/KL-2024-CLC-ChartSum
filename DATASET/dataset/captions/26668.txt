This statistic shows the Nuclear Materials Security Index ( NTI ) for countries with at least one kilogram of weapon-usable nuclear materials in 2020 . Nuclear materials are most secure in Australia , who scored 93 points on the Index . The five key factors the Index evaluated are ( 1 ) quantities and sites , ( 2 ) security and control measures , ( 3 ) global norms , ( 4 ) domestic commitments and capacity and ( 5 ) societal factors .
