In December 2019 , annual internet food sales of stores which predominantly sold food in the UK shrank in value by 6.8 percent . This was the lowest growth rate in the sector for the period in consideration . When we take a closer look on online sales growth of food stores in 2019 , we can see that during the first half of 2019 the average weekly sales growth slowed down in the first quarter and that sales in the second quarter shrank . Due to the coronavirus outbreak in early 2020 , internet sales exploded compared to a April 2019 .
