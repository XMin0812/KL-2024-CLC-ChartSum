The simple average house price for bungalow dwellings in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2000 to 2019 is illustrated in this statistic . It can be seen that the simple average house price for bungalow dwellings increased overall during the period under observation , reaching a value of 271 thousand British pounds as of 2019 . The smallest value for the simple average house price of bungalows since 2010 was found in both 2011 and 2012 , when an average price of bungalow dwellings of 221 thousand British pounds was recorded .
