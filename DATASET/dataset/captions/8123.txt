In 2019 , the Cuban travel and tourism sector contributed directly 2.97 billion U.S. dollars to the country 's gross domestic product ( GDP ) , showing a slight decrease in the upward trend registered since 2014 . In 2018 , Cuba recorded its highest direct tourism contribution to GDP of the decade , with over three billion U.S. dollars . According to a Statista forecast , this sector 's GDP share in the Caribbean country is expected to plunge in 2020 , showing a slow recovery in the following years .
