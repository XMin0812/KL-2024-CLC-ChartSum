Davinson Sánchez was the most valuable player in Colombia 's national soccer team as of November 2020 . At that specific time , the market value of the center back who plays for the English Tottenham Hotspur amounted to 49.5 million U.S. dollars . Duván Zapata and James Rodríguez , ranked as the second and third most valuable Colombian footballers in the national team , with market values of 44 million and 38.5 million U.S. dollars , respectively .
