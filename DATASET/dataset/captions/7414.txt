At the end of November 2020 , one U.S. dollar could buy approximately 20.04 Mexican pesos , approximately 1.26 pesos more than at the beggining of the year . Between March 2 and May 28 of 2020 , the Mexican peso lost approximately 11.4 percent of its value in comparison to the U.S. dollar , due to the economic and financial recession originated by the COVID-19 pandemic .
