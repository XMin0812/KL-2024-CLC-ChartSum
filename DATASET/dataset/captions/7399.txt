This statistic presents the values of S & P Case Shiller U.S. National Home Price Index from February 2017 to August 2020 . The index value was equal to 100 as of January 2000 , so if the index value is equal to 130 in a given month , for example , it means that the house prices increased by 30 percent since 2000 . The value of the S & P Case Shiller National Home Price Index amounted to 223.97 in August 2020 .
