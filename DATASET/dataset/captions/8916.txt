The statistic presents a ranking of Latin American goalkeepers participating in the 2018 FIFA World Cup in Russia , based on market value . As of June 2018 , Alisson was the most valuable Latin American keeper , with a market value of 60 million euros . The also Brazilian Ederson ranked second , with a value of 50 million euros . Among Latin American strikers at the 2018 World Cup , Messi and Neymar were the most valuable .
