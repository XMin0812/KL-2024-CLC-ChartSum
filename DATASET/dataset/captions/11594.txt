This statistic shows the number of cars sold by Skoda in Czechia from 2008 to 2019 . Czech sales of Skoda cars experienced net growth over the period , rising from 44.5 thousand units sold in 2008 and peaking in 2019.From 2014 to 2015 , Skoda sales in Czechia increased significantly from just over 58 thousand to almost 74 thousand . In 2019 , Czech sales figures of Skoda reached 85,895 units of Skoda cars sold .
