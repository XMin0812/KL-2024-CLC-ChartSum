The statistic illustrates the response to the survey question : `` Who do you expect to be champion of the Dutch Eredivisie in 2018 ? '' 31 percent of the respondents said they think PSV Eindhoven will be champion of the Dutch Eredivisie in season 2018/19 , whereas 30 percent indicated that Ajax will take the trophy by the end of the season . By the 16th of May it was known who could call themselves Champions of the Eredivisie in season 2018/19 . Ajax won from De Graafschap and took their 34th championship with three points ahead from PSV Eindhoven .
