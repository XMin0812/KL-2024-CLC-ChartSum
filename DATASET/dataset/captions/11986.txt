Nearly three million people used Twitter in the Netherlands in 2020 , an increase of over 300,000 users compared to the previous year . The online news service ranked among the lesser used social media platforms in the country , seeing the lowest user base since 2013 . 26 percent of 20- to 39-year-olds said they used Twitter in 2018 . Among respondents aged 18 - 21 years old , Twitter was used less often than Snapchat , Instagram , Facebook and WhatsApp .
