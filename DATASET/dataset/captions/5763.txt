As of June 2020 , the value of outstanding notes and coins in circulation in the United Kingdom reached approximately 85.9 billion British pounds . This was an increase of almost 4.7 billion British pounds as compared to January 2017 . When broken down by denomination , the twenty-pound note accounted for the highest share of notes in circulation . Twenty-pound notes also had the highest volume of banknotes in circulation in 2020 .
