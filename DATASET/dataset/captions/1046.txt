In 2019 , the Hindi film star Akshay Kumar was the highest-paid actor in India with his earnings valued at over 2900 million Indian rupees . Tamil superstar Rajinikanth and Bollywood legend Amitabh Bachchan were some of the other prominent names in the list . The list included numerous names among young Indians ' favorite actors . Akshay Kumar was also the only Indian actor to feature in the Forbes U.S. list of the world 's highest paid celebrities .
