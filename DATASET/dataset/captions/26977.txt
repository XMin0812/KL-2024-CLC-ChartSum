This statistic shows the percentage y-o-y change in the weekly retail sales value of alcoholic drinks , other beverages and tobacco in Great Britain ( GB ) from August 2015 to August 2020 . In August 2020 , the amount spent on alcohol , beverages and tobacco decreased by 5.9 percent compared to a year earlier . The biggest increase in sales during the period under consideration was in December 2019 with 64.4 percent .
