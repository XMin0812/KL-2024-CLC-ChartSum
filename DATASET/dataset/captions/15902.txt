This statistic displays the number of wage and salary workers in the United States paid hourly rates at the prevailing Federal minimum wage from 1979 to 2019 . In 1979 , about 3.99 million workers were paid hourly rates at the official minimum wage . In 2019 , about 392,000 workers were paid hourly rates at the prevailing minimum wage . The prevailing Federal minimum wage was 7.25 U.S. dollars per hour in 2019 .
