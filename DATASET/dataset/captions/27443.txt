The size of the Russian population decreased by approximately 36 thousand between 2019 and 2020 . Since the second half of the 20th century , the population steadily grew until 1995 . The population size saw an increase since 2009 , getting closer to its 1995 figures . As of January 1 , 2020 , according to a preliminary estimation , roughly 146.7 million inhabitants resided on the Russian territory .
