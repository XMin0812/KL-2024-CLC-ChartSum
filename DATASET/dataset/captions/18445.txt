How many private sector employees are working from home in Denmark because of the coronavirus ? According to a survey conducted by Dansk Industri , around one in three private sector employees worked from home in March , 2020 . In detail , 41 percent of the respondents were from the Capital Region of Denmark , while another 30 percent lived in the Central Denmark Region . Only 16 percent of private sector employees from the North Denmark Region worked from from home .
