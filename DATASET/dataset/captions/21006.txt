Due to the state of alarm implemented by the COVID-19 pandemic in mid-March 2020 , the arrival of tourists from abroad to Spain decreased by almost 64.3 percent compared to March 2019 . The most popular holiday regions , the Balearic Islands and Catalonia registered a drop of over 68 percent . The Canary Islands , with a drop of almost 66 percent in international arrivals , was also above the national average .
