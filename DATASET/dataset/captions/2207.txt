The revenue of chemical company DuPont generated some 21.5 billion U.S. dollars of revenue in 2019 . DuPont , with full name E. I. du Pont de Nemours and Company , is a U.S.-based chemical company , and one of the largest companies in this industry worldwide . It is headquartered in Wilmington , Delaware . On September 1 , 2017 , Dow Chemical and DuPont successfully completed their merger of equals . From that date until April 1 , 2019 , the single company was known as DowDuPont . At that point in time , the conglomerate split into three entities : Dow , DuPont , and Corteva .
