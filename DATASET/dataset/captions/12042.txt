In 2020 , 12,542 immigrants who reached Italy came from Tunisia . Moreover , 4,132 migrants were from Bangladesh , whereas 1,807 people originated from Ivory Coast . Tunisian represented 35 percent of the individuals arrived , whereas people from Bangladesh accounted for 13 percent of the total . As of December 2020 , the number of migrants who made it to Italy that year added up to 32.9 thousand .
