According to police figures , crimes that involve racism and xenophobia were far from uncommon in Italy . In 2019 , roughly 800 crimes were perpetrated for racist and xenophobic reasons . With over 200 cases , most of these crimes were classified as incitement to violence . However , many other forms of crimes were also filed . During that year , 152 grave desecrations and 104 physical assaults were notably perpetrated under racist or xenophobic pretenses .
