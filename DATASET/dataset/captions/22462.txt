When asked about when they expected their businesses ' revenue to recover from the effects of the coronavirus outbreak , 59 percent of the surveyed members of the Danish employers association , Dansk Erhverv , stated that it would take between one and six months . In comparison , 15 percent of the respondents expected it to take more than a year , while three percent thought that their business could not be saved . The first case of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Denmark was confirmed on February 27 , 2020 .
