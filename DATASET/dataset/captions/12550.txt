This statistic shows the per capita consumption of non-alcoholic beverages in the United Kingdom from 2012 to 2018 . Non-alcoholic beverages include soft drinks , packaged water , dilutables and juices and nectars . In 2017 , 200.8 liters of non-alcoholic drinks were consumed per person in the UK , which has increased to 207.3 liters per person in 2018 .
