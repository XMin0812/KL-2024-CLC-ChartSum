This statistic shows the gross domestic product ( GDP ) per capita in Greece from 1985 to 2019 , with projections up until 2025 . GDP refers to the total market value of all goods and services that are produced within a country per year . It is an important indicator of the economic strength of a country . In 2019 , GDP per capita in Greece amounted to around 19,458.53 U.S. dollars .
