In the view of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak and the social distancing period in Russia , Pornhub recorded a significant increased traffic since February and April 2020 . The most noteworthy traffic growth was recorded on March 24 , 2020 , at over 57 percent . Over the following months , the figures followed a positive trend , largely marking at over ten percent growth rate .
