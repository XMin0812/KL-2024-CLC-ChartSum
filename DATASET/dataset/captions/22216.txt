This statistic displays Gazprom 's share of the global natural gas production from 2009 to 2019 . In 2019 , Gazprom accounted for 11.7 percent of the world 's natural gas production . Gazprom is one of the largest natural gas extractors in the world . The company was created in 1989 after the Soviet Ministry of Gas Industry became a corporation . Its headquarters are located in Moscow .
