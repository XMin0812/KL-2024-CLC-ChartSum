As of March 2020 , the biggest share of the Danish respondents ( 47 percent ) had quite a lot of confidence that the national health care system can handle the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak in a good way . By comparison , only two percent had no trust in the health care system at all . The first case of COVID-19 in Denmark was confirmed on February 27 , 2020 .
