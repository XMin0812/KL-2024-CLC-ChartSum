The statistic shows the average monthly rental price for residential real estate in Italy as of August 2020 , by region . According to the data , the average price for properties in the Italian region of Lombardy was the highest , amounting to 15 euros per square meter . The regions of Tuscany and Lazio followed with an average price for properties for rent amounting to 12.7 and 12.6 euros per square meter respectively .
