In the U.S. state of Nevada , there were approximately 164.1 thousand slot machines and other mobile gaming devices in casinos in 2018 . Since 1965 , this figure had been steadily increasing up until the year 2000 in which it also reached its peak with around 213 thousand slot machines and other mobile gaming devices in casinos . From 2000 on , the number of casino gaming devices in Nevada has been going down continuously .
