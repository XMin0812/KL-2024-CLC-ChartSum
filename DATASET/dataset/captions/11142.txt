This statistic shows the FIFA World Ranking of the Swedish women 's national football team from 2003 to 2019 . The highest position ever reached was three in 2007 . Four years ago , when the team won silver in the 2003 FIFA Women 's World Cup , the rank was four . Rank 10 was the lowest result of the team , which was reached in 2017 . As of 2019 , Sweden 's position in the world ranking was 5 .
