The statistic presents the tier 1 capital ratio at Goldman Sachs from 2009 to 2019 . Tier 1 capital is the core measure of bank 's financial strength from the regulator 's point of view . It is the ratio of bank 's core equity capital to the total risk-weighted assets . The tier 1 capital ratio at Goldman Sachs amounted to 15.7 percent in 2019 .
