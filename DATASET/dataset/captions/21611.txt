The statistic illustrates the most expensive sales at auction by living artists worldwide between 2011 and 2014 . Between 2011 and 2014 , the American artist Jeff Koons had the most expensive sale at auction at Christie 's in New York in 2013 , with a lot that sold for 58.41 million U.S. dollars . Koons had four of the top ten most expensive auction lots worldwide between 2011 and 2014 .
