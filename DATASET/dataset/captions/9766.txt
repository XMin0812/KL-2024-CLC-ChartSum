In an annual survey , conducted with 12,790 adult respondents in 2018 , the most popular watersport activites in the United Kingdom ( UK ) were established . According to the survey , roughly 10 million people in the UK spent some leisure time at the beach in 2018 . The number of beach goers has significantly increased in recent years , by more than 30 percent in four years . The next most popular activities are coastal walking and outdoor swimming .
