A recent survey of U.S. adults showed that 37 percent believed that between 100 and 999 people will die in the U.S. over the next year due to coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) . This statistic shows responses to the question , `` If you had to guess , how many people do you think will die in the U.S. because of coronavirus over the next year ? '' as of March 11 , 2020 .
