This graph shows the results of the Happy Planet Index 's ranking of the unhappiest and least environmentally sustainable countries in the world . The Happy Planet Index measures life expectancy , experienced well-being , inequality of outcomes , and ecological footprint in order to determine the countries that are able to deliver the longest and happiest , but also most sustainable lives to their residents . The Index works to measure efficiency by ranking countries relative to how they offer their people long and happy lives , for each unit of environmental output . In 2016 , Chad was ranked as the `` unhappiest '' country with a HPI score of 12.8 .
