This statistic shows the gross domestic expenditure on research and development ( GERD ) as a percentage of GDP in Hungary from 2000 to 2018 . Over the period , the GERD share rose by a total of 0.74 percentage points . The peak was in 2018 , when gross expenditure on research and development made 1.53 percent of Hungary 's GDP . In 2015 , this ratio was 1.39 percent , much lower than in Austria but nevertheless , higher than in Italy .
