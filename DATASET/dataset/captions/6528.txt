Between 2008 and 2018 , roughly 15 percent of the Belgium population was at risk of poverty , defined by Statistics Belgium as 60 percent of the average disposable income in the country . People with an income lower than this are considered poor . In 2018 , this definition matched nearly 16 percent of the Belgians . Women were especially vulnerable , as the share of women living under the poverty threshold was two percent points higher than the share of men .
