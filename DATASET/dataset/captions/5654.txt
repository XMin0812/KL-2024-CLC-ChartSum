In 2019 there were approximately 696 thousand people who were aged 65 or over in the Republic of Ireland , compared with 1.2 million 45 to 64 year old 's , almost 1.4 million 25 to 44 year old 's , and 618 thousand 15 to 24 year old 's . Additionally there were just over a million children aged between 0 and 14 in the Republic of Ireland in 2019 .
