As of October 26 , 2020 , 87 percent of Malaysian respondents stated that they had been avoiding public places during the COVID-19 outbreak , up from 40 percent on Feb 24 , 2020 . Malaysia is currently experiencing a second wave of COVID-19 infections . To stop the spread of the disease , the government has imposed a partial lockdown until December 6 .
