In fiscal year 2019 , the value of deposits at Bandhan Bank in India was over 40 billion Indian rupees , marking a substantial increase in deposits from around 34 billion rupees in the previous fiscal year . Bandhan Bank was founded in 2015 but has been functional since 2001 as a not-for-profit enterprise that focused on the financial inclusion of underprivileged sections of the society through microfinancing .
