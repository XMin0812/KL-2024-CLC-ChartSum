The Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services estimates that prescription drug expenditure in the United States came to some 335 billion U.S. dollars in 2018 . This amount includes only retail drug spending , excluding nonretail . Estimations of drug spending can vary by investigating organization . For the U.S. , among the most relevant drug spending calculations are provided by CMS , ASPE ( Assistant Secretary for Planning and Evaluation ) , and pharmaceutical market researcher IQVIA .
