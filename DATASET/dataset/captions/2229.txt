In 2018 , natural gas accounted for 45 percent of the total electricity production in Italy . Among renewable sources , hydropower ranked first , covering 16 percent of the total production , followed by biomass and solar energy . Nowadays , climate change seems to be one of the most challenging issues that national governments should be tackling . Energy transition is imperative and yet considering how static the energy sector can be , it is easy to foresee a long way ahead before reaching a complete replacement of fossil fuels with renewable sources .
