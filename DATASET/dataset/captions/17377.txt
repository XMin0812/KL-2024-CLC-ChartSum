In 2019 , music in the hip-hop genre was most likely to contain samples , with 32 percent of hip-hop songs in the top 100 songs United States containing a sample in that year . R & B also had a significant proportion of songs including samples , with 24 percent of the most popular R & B records featuring a sample . Rock was the genre least likely to incorporate sampling , with only four percent of the top 100 rock tracks using a sample .
