In the third quarter of 2020 , The New York Times Company generated a total revenue of 426.90 million U.S. dollars , marking a small decrease from the corresponding quarter of 2019 . The fourth quarter of the year is generally the one in which the company makes the most revenue , and at the end of 2018 the figure surpassed 500 million for the first time in years . In 2019 this happened again , with Q4 revenues reaching almost 510 million dollars . The company also reported that digital revenues surpassed print for the first time in Q2 2020 .
