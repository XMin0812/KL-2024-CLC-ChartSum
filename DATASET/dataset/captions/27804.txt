Since 2012 , the value of gross written premiums by life insurance companies on the domestic market in Malta has seen a year-on-year increase , reaching the peak value of 415 million euros in 2018 . The lowest value registered was in 2012 at 170 million euros . The United Kingdom had the highest value of life insurance premiums among all European counties in 2018 , followed by France and Italy . Together , the top three countries accounted for more than 60 percent of the European market .
