At the end of November 2020 , one U.S. dollar could buy approximately 5.34 Brazilian reals , approximately 1.7 pesos more than at the beggining of 2019 . Between March 2 and May 28 of 2020 , the Brazilian real lost approximately 16 percent of its value in comparison to the U.S. dollar , due to the economic and financial recession originated by the COVID-19 pandemic .
