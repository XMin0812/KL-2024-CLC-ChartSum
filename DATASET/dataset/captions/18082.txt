This statistic shows the gross domestic expenditure on research and development ( GERD ) as a percentage of the gross domestic product ( GDP ) of Slovakia from 2000 to 2018 . Up until 2015 , the GERD share rose by a total of 0.54 percentage points with a rise of 0.3 points between 2014 and 2015 alone . In 2018 , the GERD declined to 0.84 percent .
