The statistic illustrates the distribution of total sales of the Connected Care and Health Informatics segment of the Philips Group in 2019 , by area of business . In that year , monitoring and analytics solutions generated over 40 percent the segment 's total sales . Connected Care and Health Informatics contributed roughly 4.7 billion euros to the Philips Group in 2019 . After spin-offs of other product segments , Koninklijke Philips N.V. today is a company focused on healthcare/medical technology .
