In Spain , the popularity of online shopping becomes evident when looking at the latest figures of B2C electronic commerce . This relatively new system to purchase products off the net has experienced a constant growth in recent years , with values reaching over 50 billion euros in 2019 up from only six billion in 2007 . In 2020 , about half of the Spanish population that bought items online used their debit or credit card as their preferred payment method for the virtual world .
