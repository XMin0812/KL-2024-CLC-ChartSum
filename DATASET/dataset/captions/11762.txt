This statistic shows the total sales of Ernest Jones from fiscal year 2014 to 2018 . Between 2017 and 2019 , the company saw a decline in sales , with the 2019 fiscal year ending at 186 million British pounds . Ernest Jones is one of the UK brands of the Signet Jeweler 's Group , one of the largest diamond jeweler retailers in the world . The brand specializes in the retailing of jewelry , watches and associated services . Along with Ernest Jones , the Signet Group also owns the H.Samuel jewelry brand in the UK .
