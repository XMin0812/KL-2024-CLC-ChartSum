In 2007 , the total amount of employees of Royal Bank of Scotland ( RBS ) almost doubled , reaching the highest of this time period , with 226,400 people employed . Since then , the number of people employed by the RBS has continuously decreased , reaching a total of under 63 thousand employees in 2019 . The figures presented in this statistic detail staff numbers as full-time equivalents of all positions as of the end of each year displayed . The Royal Bank of Scotland has one of the highest customer counts of any of the UK 's leading banks .
