FC Barcelona 's Lionel Messi topped the top scorers list of the Spanish football league 'La Liga ' in the 2019/2020 season . With a total of 25 goals , the Argentine won the so called `` Pichichi '' title . Real Madrid 's Karim Benzema won the second position on the list with 21 goals . During the season , the most common result was 1 - 1 , which was registered on 49 occasions .
