In the financial year 2019 , the average time before a cargo ship was berthed on a major port in India was around 1.1 days . This was the lowest pre-berthing time since the financial year 2010 . In simple words , pre-berthing time was the time the ship or the vessel needed to wait before it could enter in the berth . The port sector of the South Asian country handled over a billion metric tons of cargo in 2019 . A major portion of this cargo was handled by the major ports .
