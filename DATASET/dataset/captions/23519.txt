Spanish society is very divided over their views on the future on the monarchy in the country . As of August 2020 , a poll conducted by YouGov showed that 25 percent of the participants agreed that the Spanish monarchy should remain and the current King Philip VI should not be blamed for his father 's actions . Some other 25 percent of respondents agreed the people of Spain should be allowed to choose between a monarchy and a republic through a referendum . Juan Carlos I was involved in a series of scandals in the recent times , and therefore some 13 percent of respondents agree that his actions were unacceptable and monarchy should be automatically abolished .
