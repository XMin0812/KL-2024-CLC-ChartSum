This statistic shows the quarterly reach of the Sky 1 television channel in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from the first quarter of 2012 to the third quarter of 2019 . In the first quarter of 2016 , Sky 1 reached 24.45 million viewers , down from 25.14 million viewers in the previous quarter . In the second quarter of 2019 Sky 1 reached its lowest ever quarterly reach at roughly 19.8 million viewers .
