Due to the COVID-19 pandemic , the contribution of travel and tourism to the Caribbean gross domestic product ( GDP ) in 2020 could suffer losses of up to 44 billion U.S. dollars , in a worst case scenario . In a best case scenario , the losses in the tourism GDP of the region could amount to 27 billion U.S. dollars , if immediate actions were taken for the recovery of this sector during the second half of 2020 . In 2019 , the contribution of travel and tourism to the GDP in the Caribbean amounted to nearly 59 billion U.S. dollars .
