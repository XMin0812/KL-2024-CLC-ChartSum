This statistic displays an annual comparison of U.S. user 's satisfaction with online portals and search engines in 2020 , according to the American Customer Satisfaction Index ( ACSI ) . In 2020 , market leader Google was ranked first with 79 out of 100 ACSI index points . Google 's popularity is not only based on its leading position within the online search market but also related to the wide selection of online products offered by the company .
