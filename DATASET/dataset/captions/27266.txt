According to a survey among members of the Danish union for executives , Lederne , in 2020 most respondents were satisfied with the working from home situation during the coronavirus outbreak . In detail , 30 percent of the executives found that their employees were doing a very good job working from home , and another 53 percent stated that their employees were doing well . Only two percent of the executives reported that working from home was not working out .
