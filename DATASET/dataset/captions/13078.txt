This statistic shows the public sector net debt ( PSND ) of the United Kingdom ( UK ) as a percentage of gross domestic product ( GDP ) from fiscal year 2004/05 to fiscal year 2019/20 . PSND as a percentage of GDP rose continuously year on year between 2004/05 and 2009/10 , and reached a peak of 85.8 billion British Pounds in 2017/18 .
