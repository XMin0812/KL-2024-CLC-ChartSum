This statistic illustrates the number of cars sold by Audi in the United Kingdom ( UK ) between July 2014 and December 2019 . During the months here recorded , highest sales were recorded in the months of March and September of each year . This was due to these being the months of the year when the Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency issued new registration plates . Between 2016 and 2018 , December sales fell by 5,000 units . This number did bounce back , however , in December 2019 .
