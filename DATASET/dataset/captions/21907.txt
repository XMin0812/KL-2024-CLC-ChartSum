According to a survey conducted among Indonesian consumers in September 2019 , around 91 percent of respondents stated that groceries shopping contributed the most to their plastic waste . Indonesia was mentioned as the world 's second-largest plastic polluters of the ocean and furthermore deforestation of peatlands made Indonesia the world 's third largest emitter of greenhouse gases . But the rise of social media made Indonesians more aware of current environmental issues . Some of the plastic-related crisis have become trending topics in social media in Indonesia .
