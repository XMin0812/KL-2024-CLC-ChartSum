This statistic shows Express Scripts ' EBITDA from continuing operations between 2006 and 2017 . Express Scripts is one of the largest pharmacy benefit managers in the United States . They provide services to manage drug plans for government agencies , corporations , and unions . In 2008 , the company generated EBITDA of approximately 1.37 billion U.S. dollars . In 2018 , Express Scripts was acquired by U.S. health services company Cigna .
