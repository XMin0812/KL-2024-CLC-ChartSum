This statistic illustrates the amount of journeys on the Glasgow Underground in Scotland , in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2000/01 to 2019/20 . In the period under consideration , the number of journeys fluctuated . In 2007/08 , Glasgow Underground recorded the highest number of journeys , at 14.5 million . In 2019/20 , there were a total of 12.7 million passenger journeys recorded .
