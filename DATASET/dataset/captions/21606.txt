This statistic describes worldwide imports of selected major types of fertilizers into the U.S. in 2012 . Potassium muriate , a straight potash fertilizer , imports totaled 9.2 million tons in 2012 . The United States depends heavily on nitrogen and potash imports and is a net importer of these products . Trinidad and Canada supply a large amount of the United States ' supply of nitrogen fertilizers , while Canada supplies a majority of potash fertilizers .
