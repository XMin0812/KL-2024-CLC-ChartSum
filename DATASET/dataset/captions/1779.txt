This statistic shows the development of the Real Gross Domestic Product of the United States of America from 1990 to 2019 in annual numbers chained to the U.S. dollar value of the year 2012 . In 2019 , the United States generated about 19.09 trillion U.S. dollars ( 2012 chained ) . See the U.S. GDP for further information . Real Gross domestic product is an inflation-adjusted measure that reflects the value of all goods and services produced in a given year , expressed in base-year prices .
