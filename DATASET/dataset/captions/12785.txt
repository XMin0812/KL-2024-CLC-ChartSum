This statistic shows the average usual weekly hours worked on the main job in Spain from 2000 to 2019 . Over this period , the weekly average fell by a total of 2.4 hours . The overall trend is one of decline as the last peak was in 2001 at 38.8 hours per week . In the recent years people in Spain worked on average between 36.4 and 36.9 hours per week .
