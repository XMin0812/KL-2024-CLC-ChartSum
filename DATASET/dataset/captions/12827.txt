The unemployment rate in Spain was highest in the southern regions as of the third quarter of 2020 . During the period under consideration , the autonomous city of Ceuta registered Spain 's highest unemployment rate at almost 27.14 percent . Extremadura , a landlocked southern region bordering Portugal , ranked fifth at 20.88 percent . On the flip side , the northern regions , such as the Basque Country or Navarre featured a much lower unemployment rate at an average of approximately 10 percent .
