This statistic illustrates the total money invested in maritime port infrastructure in Turkey from 2004 to 2018 , in million euros . In the period of consideration , maritime port infrastructure investments oscillated . In 2017 , investments in this sector reached its peak at approximately 91 million euros . The lowest amount of investments in sea port infrastructure was recorded in 2015 , at a total of 8.4 million euros . No data was recorded for 2018 .
