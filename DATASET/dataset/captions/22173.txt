In 2018 , approximately 59 thousand people participated in kitesurfing in the United Kingdom ( UK ) . The kitesurfing participation peaked in 2016 with 139 thousand kitesurfing participants . In 2018 , approximately 31.8 percent of people in the UK participated in any watersport activitiy . More information about water sports in the UK can be found in the Dossier : Water sports in the United Kingdom ( UK ) .
