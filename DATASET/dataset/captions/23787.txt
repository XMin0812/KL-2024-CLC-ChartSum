This statistic shows the annual sales value of wool produced in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in 2007 , 2012 and 2013 . Since 2007 , sales of British wool have increased from 180 million to 300 million British pounds in 2013 . The source reported that the wool industry as a whole , including clothing , carpets , accessories and transport upholstery , is worth around 3 billion British pounds .
