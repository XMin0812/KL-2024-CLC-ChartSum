In 2017 , the Italian fashion company Ermenegildo Zegna Holditalia S.p.A. , which controls the brands Ermengildo Zegna , Z.Zegna , Agnona and Thom Brown , reported an annual turnover of approximately 97 million euros . This figure represents an overall increase of about ten million euros compared to 2012 , the first year considered in this graph . Moreover , with a net profit of almost 45 million euros , the company is one of the most successful among Italian enterprises in the luxury sector .
