The usually booming flower market in the Netherlands was hit hard by the coronavirus crisis in 2020 . As flower markets were forced to close due to the lockdown in the spring of 2020 and the expected international demand for cut flowers was not met , flower auctions suffered from record-low sales figures and prices . In March and April of 2020 , the average price of cut flowers sold in auctions in the Netherlands dropped below 20 eurocents , which was the lowest price since July 2013 .
