Since 2010 , the household price of electricity in Poland has seen little change , increasing from 13.41 euro cents per kilowatt-hour to 14.75 euro cents in the first semester of 2020 . Looking at the figures , it can be seen that on average prices were cheaper in the second half of each year . A noticeable exception was in the second half of 2012 , when prices increased by 1.11 euro cents compared the first half of the year . This was the peak price during the period of consideration .
