The number of employees in Denmark decreased slightly from 2008 to 2013 but grew again in the years that followed . In 2018 , the Danish labor market counted roughly 2.3 million people on a full-time basis . Among the 20 biggest companies based in Denmark , measured by the number of employees , A.P . Møller – Mærsk A/S ranked first , employing nearly 76 thousand people as of July 2020 .
