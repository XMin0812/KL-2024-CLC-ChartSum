Since the spread of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Italy , started in February 2020 , the number of cases has increased daily . However , the vast majority of people who contracted the virus have recovered . As of January 5 , 2021 , the number of individuals who recovered from coronavirus in Italy reached 1,536,129 . On the other hand , the number of deaths also increased , surpassing 76 thousand . When looking at the regional level , the region with the highest number of recoveries was Lombardy . The region , however , registered the highest number of coronavirus cases in the country .
