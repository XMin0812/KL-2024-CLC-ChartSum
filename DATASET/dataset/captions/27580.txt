This statistic depicts the share of respondents from 2008 to 2019 who agreed that gambling in the United Kingdom was conducted fairly and could be trusted . In 2009 , approximately 50 percent of the respondents agreed . By 2019 , the share of respondents who agreed gambling was fairly conducted decreased to 29 percent . Further information on UK betting industry can be found in the Dossier : Betting industry of the United Kingdom ( UK ) .
