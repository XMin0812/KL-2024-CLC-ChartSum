This statistic shows the development of the revenue of Dutch biotech company Qiagen from 2012 to 2019 . In 2019 , net sales of this company stood at 1.53 billion U.S. dollars . Qiagen N.V. is headquartered in the Netherlands and in Germany , and is operating in many countries around the world . The company is specialized on sample and assay technologies for molecular diagnostics , applied testing , academic and pharmaceutical research .
