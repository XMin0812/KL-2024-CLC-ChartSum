This statistic shows wearables as component of wellness and prevention internet of health things ( IoHT ) solutions for payers in U.S. , according to a survey conducted among health care providers and payers between 29th January 2016 and 16th February 2016 . Almost 21 percent of the payers said yes , the members are being provided with wearables and 45 percent said that members are being provided with wearables at subsidized costs .
