This statistic shows the percentage of people who identified with a political party in Great Britain ( GB ) in 2012 , by age . 61 percent of 18 to 24 year olds identified with a party . This was , however , the lowest age group total . Political identification increased up to 71 percent for the 35 to 44 year olds . Despite a dip for 45 to 54 year olds , the figure rises to 83 percent for the oldest age group of 65+ . It is not clear from this statistic whether political identification rises with age or if it is a generational factor creating the difference .
