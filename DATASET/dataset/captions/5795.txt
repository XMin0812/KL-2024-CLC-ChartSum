Around 28 percent of U.S. LGBTQ youth who had experienced conversion therapy had attempted suicide within the previous 12 months as of 2020 , compared to 12 percent of LGBTQ youth who had not experienced conversion therapy . The statistic illustrates the share of LGBTQ youth in the U.S. who attempted suicide within the past 12 months as of 2020 , by experience with conversion therapy .
