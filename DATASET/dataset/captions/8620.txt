Chocolate has inspired uncountable desserts in many countries across the globe and throughout history . This delicacy originating in Mesoamerica is the favorite sweet of many places including Spain , which imported this delight to Europe during the country 's explorations in the Americas . Spain 's sweet tooth has been on the rise in recent years , with a peak in chocolate and cocoa product consumption amounting to 165.5 million kilograms in 2013 . The consumption of this delight decreased slightly in 2019 , standing at approximately 162.2 million kilograms that year .
