This statistic shows U.S. charities ( out of the 100 largest ) with the highest donor dependency rates as of 2019 . Americares Foundation had the highest rate of dependency on its donors with a score of 127 percent . A score of 100 percent would signify that the charity had broken even in its budget . A score higher than 100 percent signifies that the charity ran a deficit as donations were not high enough to cover all costs .
