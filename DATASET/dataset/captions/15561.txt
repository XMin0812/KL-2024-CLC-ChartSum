This graph presents information on the sales revenue generated by selected game titles within their opening weeks as of march 2016 . Between 2013 and 2016 , Grand Theft Auto V , developed by Rockstar , was reported to have generated one billion U.S. dollars in revenue within the first week after launch . In the period of six months , between its release in November 2015 until April 2016 , Call of Duty : Black Ops III sold 22.5 million units worldwide .
