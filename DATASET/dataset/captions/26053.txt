The approval rate of Uruguayan presidents has been decreasing recently . During the last years of Tabaré Vázquez 's administration , from 2016 to 2018 , the approval rate fluctuated between 43 percent and 41 percent . This represents a steep decrease from a 75 percent approval rate registered in 2010 , when José `` Pepe '' Mujica assumed office.The ratio of government expenditure to gross domestic product ( GDP ) in Uruguay is predicted to remain at approximately 33 percent within the next years .
