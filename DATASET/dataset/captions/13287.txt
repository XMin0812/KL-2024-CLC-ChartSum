This statistic illustrates how many Vauxhall Insignia passenger cars were registered for the first time annually in Great Britain , between 2009 and 2019 , in thousand units . A peak in sales of the Vauxhall Insignia was observed in 2011 , when over 45,000 units were newly registered . By 2019 , sales volume declined and amounted to roughly 8,205 units .
