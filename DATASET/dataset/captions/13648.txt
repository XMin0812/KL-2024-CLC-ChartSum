This statistic depicts the number of retail chains by sector in Norway in the year 2016 . Amounting to a total of 150 retail chains , fashion and clothing was the leading sector , accumulating to 22 chains , followed by consumer electronics , accounting for 17 retail chains . Toys and games retail followed , standing at 16 chains , while jewellery and watches as well as baby ware had the least amount at 1 chain .
