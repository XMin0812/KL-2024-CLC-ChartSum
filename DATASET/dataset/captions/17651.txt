In 2018 , the B2B e-commerce value of furniture and related product manufacturing shipments in the United States amounted to 43.2 billion U.S. dollars , up from 41.8 billion U.S. dollars in the previous measured period . This represents a 3.4 percent year-over-year segment growth . In 2018 , e-commerce accounted for 55.5 percent of total shipments in the furniture manufacturing sector .
