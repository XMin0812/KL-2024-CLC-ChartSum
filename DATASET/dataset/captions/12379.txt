The statistic depicts the Traveler Sentiment Index from 2007 to 2013 . In July 2011 , the index score was 88.3.The Traveler Sentiment Index tracks changes in travel-related factors over time through the travelhorizons survey . The Index is tied to a March 2007 base . A value above 100 means that a higher percentage of respondents hold more positive perceptions about a particular attitude toward travel than those who feel negatively ; a score below 100 reflects higher negatie attitude than positive.The index is based on six indicators including : Overall interest in travel , demands on time available for travel , personal finances available for travel , affordability of travel , quality of service received and safety of travel in the United States .
