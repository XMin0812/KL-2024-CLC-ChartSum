According to a survey conducted in Indonesia in February 2020 , 36 percent of the respondents who had not bought tickets for overseas travel stated they would still stick with their traveling plan and buy the ticket . Indonesia 's President , Joko `` Jokowi '' Widodo confirmed the first two confirmed cases of the disease in the country on March 2 , 2020 . Before the announcement , Indonesia had no confirmed cases of COVID-19 , raising concerns about the country 's ability to detect the virus .
