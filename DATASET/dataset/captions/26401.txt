This statistic depicts the percentage of those who had driven under the influence of alcohol in the past year among persons aged 16 or older in the United States , by age group , in 2015 . In that year , some 14 percent of people aged 30 to 34 years claimed to have driven under the influence of alcohol in the past year . Alcoholism refers to problems with alcohol and often refers to alcohol addiction . Alcohol has the potential to cause damage to all organs of the body .
