As of 2019 , 95 percent of Danish households had internet access . This percentage was a slight increase from the previous year , when the internet penetration rate among households amounted to 93 percent . The highest share of Danish households with internet access in the period from 2007 to 2019 was in 2017 , at 97 percent . How often was the internet used in Denmark ? 95 percent of people reported to have used been online once a week as of 2018 . There were only two percent of the respondents who had used the internet less often than once a week that year .
