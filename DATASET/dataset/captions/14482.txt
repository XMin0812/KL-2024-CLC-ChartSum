In 2019 , Gazprom produced over 501 billion cubic meters of natural and associated gas in Russia , which was the highest volume recorded since 2012 . Between 2018 and 2019 , the production increased by approximately 0.5 percent . Gazprom is one of the largest natural gas extractors in the world . The company was created in 1989 after the Soviet Ministry of Gas Industry became a corporation . Its headquarters are located in Moscow .
