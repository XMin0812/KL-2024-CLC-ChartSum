During a March 2020 survey , it was found that Brazilians trusted scientists most to tell the truth about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) and its progression . As many as 91 percent of responding Brazilians named scientists among their top four most trusted institutions . Respondents ' own doctor ( s ) and WHO officials ranked second and third , with 86 and 82 percent , respectively .
