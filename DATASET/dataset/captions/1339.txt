The Louvre in Paris is the most visited museum in Europe , recording an attendance of approximately 9.6 million visitors in 2019 . As the largest art museum in the world , it is known across the globe for exhibiting some of the world 's most famous artworks including the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo . It ranks not only as the most visited art museum in Europe , but also worldwide . The Vatican Museums followed with approximately 6.9 million visitors .
