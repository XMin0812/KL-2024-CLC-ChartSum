The number of Harley Davidson branded motorcycles registered in Great Britain from 2000 to 2019 has been in continuous fluctuation . With an initial 15,409 motorcycles in 2000 , which was the lowest number of motorcycles registered in this period , the number gradually increased to a peak of 41,395 units before declining . The number of registered motorcycles then remained relatively constant before increasing , reaching 52,446 units in 2019 , the highest number of registered motorcycles in this period .
