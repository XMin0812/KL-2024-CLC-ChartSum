This statistic depicts a ranking of sororities in the United States , according to `` largest national footprint , most devoted sisters , and greatest philanthropic footprint '' . The daily beast considered the number of active collegiate chapters , the number of Twitter followers , the number Facebook fans and the amount of money donated by the sorority to nonprofit organizations . In this graph the variable of funds donated is depicted . Although not ranked as the top sorority over all , Delta Gamma raised the most funds at 792,499 U.S. dollars .
