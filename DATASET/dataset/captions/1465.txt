This statistic shows the number of shops of the Lush Cosmetics Limited group worldwide in the year ending June 30th 2019 , by county . The chart shows that there are just over 100 shops in the United Kingdom and another 84 shops in Japan . Bulgaria , Portugal , Czechia , and Estonia each had two Lush Cosmetics shops in the country . Lush Cosmetics Limited is a cosmetics retailer , with headquarters in Dorset , United Kingdom , which was founded in 1995 . The company is most known for their hand-made beauty care products , including soaps , face and body care , bath and shower cosmetics , hair styling products and perfumes .
