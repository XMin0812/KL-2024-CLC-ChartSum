In an annual survey by Sport England , the the sports governing body for England , about the number of people who play tennis , it was found that as of 2020 , roughly 739,900 people play tennis at least twice a month , considering any intensity and duration . The survey was conducted online and the latest update on it was conducted for the time period between May 2019 and May 2020 with a total of 190,401 thousand respondents .
