Due to the crisis of the coronavirus COVID-19 , in March 2020 there were a large number of cancellations of hotel reservations in Spain for the Easter holidays of that year . In the Canary Islands , the most affected region , the month-on-month variation rate for the first 17 days of March compared to February 2020 was close to 130 % . Across the country , the increase in hotel reservation cancellations compared to the previous month was 40 % .
