The statistic shows the growth rate of the real gross domestic product ( GDP ) in the United States from 2015 to 2019 , with projections up until 2025 . GDP refers to the total market value of all goods and services that are produced within a country per year . It is an important indicator of the economic strength of a country . Real GDP is adjusted for price changes and is therefore regarded as a key indicator for economic growth . In 2019 , the growth of the real gross domestic product in the United States was around 2.16 percent compared to the previous year . See U.S. GDP per capita and the US GDP for more information .
