Due to the coronavirus outbreak , many summer holiday plans of Dutch citizens needed to be cancelled , rebooked or postponed . According to a survey on the holiday plans for the summer in 2020 , nearly 60 percent of the respondents indicated to have postponed holidays to a later stage , whereas 46 percent mentioned to doubt at all to go on holiday due to the coronavirus measurements taken by some countries . As of April 1 , 2020 , the total number of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) cases reported by the Dutch authorities amounted to 14,697 .
