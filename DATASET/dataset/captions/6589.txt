The number of upper secondary students in Denmark amounted to over 145 thousand students in 2019 , which was the lowest number in eight years . Since 2009 , however , the number of students in upper secondary education increased significantly from around 123 thousand in 2009 to over 150 thousand students in 2017 . In Denmark , upper secondary education is divided into general upper secondary programs , which primarily prepare for higher education , and vocational upper secondary education and training programs , focusing on careers in a specific trade or industry .
