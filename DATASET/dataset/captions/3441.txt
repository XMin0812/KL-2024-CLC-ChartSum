Hewlett-Packard 's revenue from printers and copiers reached about 17.64 billion U.S. dollars in the fiscal year 2020 . The printing segment is one of two main business segments of HP Inc . Personal systems is HP Inc 's second business segment , which in 2020 generated around 39 billion U.S. dollars in revenue , bringing the company 's overall revenue to more than 56 billion U.S. dollars in that year .
