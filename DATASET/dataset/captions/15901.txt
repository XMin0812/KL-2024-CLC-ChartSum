In 2019 , there were 1,817 crimes per 100 thousand people in Romania . Despite a decreasing trend between 2015 and 2017 , 2019 saw an increase in the number of crimes , reaching the highest crime rate in Romania in the last five years . In spite of that , the figures show that Romania 's crime index has been slowly decreasing since 2018 , reaching the value of 27.64 by 2020 .
