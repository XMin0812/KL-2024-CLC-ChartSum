In total there are about 61 active dentists per every 100,000 people living in the United States , though there is significant variability in the number of dentists available depending on the state . As of 2017 , California , Texas and New York had the largest number of active dentists working . California was ranked first with over 30 thousand active dentists . Despite having the largest number of dentists working , these states were not always the states with the highest number of dentists per population .
