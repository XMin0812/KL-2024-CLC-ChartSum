The subscriber churn rate of Spotify worldwide amounted to 4.8 percent in the final quarter of 2019 , down from 5.2 percent in the corresponding quarter of the previous year . Cmulative figures for 2019 are not available , but in the annual churn rate amounted to 19.8 percent , which the source noted was partially due to promotional trials . Whilst such trials draw in new subscribers , many of whom tend to convert , Spotify counts paid trialists in its subscriber numbers , meaning that those who do not convert then become churned subscribers .
