This statistic shows the number of legal abortions performed in Brazil from 2009 to 2017 . Figures only include abortions that were authorized according to the following decriminalized causes : if the pregnancy is a result of rape , if performing an abortion is the only way to save the woman 's life , or if the fetus suffers from anencephaly . In 2017 , the number of legal abortions in Brazil amounted to 1,636 , down from 1,680 legal abortions registered in 2016 .
