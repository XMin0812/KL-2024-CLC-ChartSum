The first death case related to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Sweden was reported on March 11 , 2020 . The number of deaths has since increased to a total of 7,893 . The number of people who were or had been confirmed infected by the virus in the country had reached a total of 357,466 as of December 17 , 2020 . For further information about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , please visit our dedicated Facts and Figures page .
