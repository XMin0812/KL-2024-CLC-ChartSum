Household electricity prices in Czechia average 18.41 euro cents per kilowatt-hour ( kWh ) in the first half of 2020 . This was the highest price during the reporting period . Prices dropped noticeably between 2014 and the first half of 2015 , when households payed less than 13 euro cents per kWh . Czechia has one of the most expensive electricity prices in Central and Eastern Europe , however , it still ranks below the average household electricity price in the EU .
