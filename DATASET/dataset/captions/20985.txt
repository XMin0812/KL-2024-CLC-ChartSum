How well is the Danish women 's national football team performing worldwide ? This statistic shows the FIFA World Ranking of the Danish women 's national football team from 2003 to 2020 . The highest position ever reached was seven in 2004 and 2008 . Rank 16 was the lowest result of the team , which was reached in 2014 . The past two years , Denmark 's women 's position in the world ranking remained at 16 .
