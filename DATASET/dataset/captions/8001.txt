As of July 2020 , the oldest living Academy Award winner of all time was Walter Mirisch , who won an Oscar for his work as producer of `` In the Heat of the Night '' . Previously leading the list was Olivia de Havilland , who in her 53-year Hollywood career won two Best Actress awards . Olivia de Havilland sadly passed away in July 2020 at the age of 104 years old .
