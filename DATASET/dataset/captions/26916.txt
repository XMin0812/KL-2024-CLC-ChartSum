As of 2019 corporate data , ASML generated a net sales revenue of 11.82 billion euros , which is an increase compared to the previous year and the highest revenue reached in the period between 2014 to 2019 . ASML is a Dutch international company and globally the largest supplier for photolithography systems for the semiconductor industry . Looking at the operating income of ASML , this amounted to approximately 2.72 billion euros in 2019 .
