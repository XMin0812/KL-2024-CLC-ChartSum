In 2018 , the largest number of mafia crimes were registered in the Italian region of Calabria . In this region located in the South of the country , 896 criminal reports were recorded in that year . Other three Italy 's southern regions followed in the ranking : Sicily , Campania , and Apulia . In 1991 , an investigation body was founded in Italy with the aim to enhance the fight against mafia , the Anti-Mafia Investigation Directorate ( Direzione Investigativa Antimafia or DIA ) .
