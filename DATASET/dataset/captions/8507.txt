In 2018 , approximately 30 percent of Hondurans were living on less than 3.20 U.S. dollars per day , down from 31.6 percent of the country 's population a year earlier . Throughout the indicated period , the poverty headcount ratio reached its lowest level in 2010 , when 29.4 percent of the population were estimated to survive with less than 3.20 U.S. dollars a day .
