Year over year net sales of Walmart U.S. grew by 2.9 percent in fiscal year 2020 when compared to the previous period . Walmart U.S. operates retail stores in Walmart 's domestic market of the United States . Walmart , formerly known as Wal-Mart Stores , Inc. , is one of the most well-known and valuable brands in the world . Walmart deals in a wide variety of products , such as groceries , apparel , furniture , home appliances , and electronics .
