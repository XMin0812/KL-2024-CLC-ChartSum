According to experts , the Russian banking system would record revenues in 2020 and 2021 only if the critical state of the crisis caused by the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic and sinking oil prices had its negative effect only during 2020 . In less optimistic cases , the banking industry was expected to incur losses over the indicated period .
