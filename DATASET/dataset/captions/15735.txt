This statistic shows British public opinion on September 24 to 25 , 2014 , on whether the RAF should join the US-led air strike operations against Islamic State/ISIS in Iraq and/or Syria . Whereas nearly half of the 1,972 respondents approved of a RAF campaign in both countries , only 6 percent approved of an Iraq only campaign , and even fewer for a Syria only campaign . The complexity of the situation was also highlighted , with almost a quarter saying that they did n't know .
