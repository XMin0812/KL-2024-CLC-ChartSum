Household electricity prices in the Netherlands averaged 14.27 euro cents in the first half of 2020 . Between the first half of 2010 and the first half of 2015 , electricity prices for households in the Netherlands saw a relatively steady increase in prices , rising to 19.86 kilowatt hours . This was despite prices in 2014 dropping . In the years following 2015 , prices began to decline , but by the second half of 2018 had increased again to 17.07 cents per kilowatt-hour , before reaching a peak of 20.55 cents per kilowatt-hour in the second half of 2019 .
