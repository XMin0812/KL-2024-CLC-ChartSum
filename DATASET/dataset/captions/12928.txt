This statistic shows the number of insurance company employees in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2004 to 2018 . From 2004 the trend was that of fluctuation , leading to approximately 104.7 thousand people being employed as direct employees of insurance companies on the British market in 2013 before seeing slight rise as of 2016 where the number of insurance company employees in the UK reached 105 thousand . The number of insurance company employees in the UK in 2018 was 94200 .
