This statistic presents the Corruption Perception Index score obtained by El Salvador from 2012 to 2019 . This index is a composite indicator that includes data on the perception of corruption in areas such as : bribery of public officials , kickbacks in public procurement , embezzlement of state funds , and effectiveness of governments ' anti-corruption efforts . The highest possible score in perception of corruption is 0 , whereas a score of 100 indicates that no corruption is perceived in the respective country . In El Salvador , the corruption perception index score declined from 35 points in 2018 to 34 points in 2019 .
