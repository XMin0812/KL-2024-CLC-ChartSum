This statistic shows the annual production of concrete roofing tiles in Great Britain ( GB ) from 2007 to 2018 , in thousand meters of roof covered . There was a noticeable decline in production of these goods in 2009 , fluctuating between 2009 and 2013 . However the sector has been recovering since 2014 , presenting in 2018 a total production of approximately 90 % higher compared to 2009 .
