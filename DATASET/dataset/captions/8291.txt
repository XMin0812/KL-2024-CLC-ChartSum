Awareness towards healthy eating has changed significantly throughout the years , and more and people in Germany are nowadays trying to follow balanced diet programs . From a 2019 study conducted by Kitchen Stories among United States ( U.S. ) individuals , it emerged that only 2.73 percent of the respondents never followed a balanced diet during the period considered . By contrast , more than 36 percent of U.S. individuals had healthy food very often during 2019 .
