The 2020 budget of the epidemiological surveillance of the Secretariat of Health in Mexico amounted to 533 million Mexican pesos ( approximately 22.7 million U.S. dollars at March 2020 exchange rates ) . It is the lowest amount in the most recent 11 years . During the swine flue epidemic of 2009 , the budget amounted to 2.16 billion pesos and the epidemic was partly combated with the Catastrophic Expenditures Fund of the Public Insurance . In 2018 , Mexico invested approximately 5.5 percent of its GDP in healthcare .
