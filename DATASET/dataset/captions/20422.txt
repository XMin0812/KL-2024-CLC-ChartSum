This statistic shows the number of employed full-time managers and proprietors in hospitality and leisure services in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from April 2018 to March 2019 , by occupation . In this period there were 135 thousand full-time restaurant and catering establishment managers and proprietors employed in the UK . This was more than those employed full-time as hotel and accommodation managers and proprietors , which amounted to roughly 55 thousand .
