There was a total of eight active coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) infections in Iceland as of June 17 , 2020 . There was one infection per age group in the groups from 13 to 17 years , and 40 to 49 years . There were three infections each in the age groups from 18 to 29 , and 30 to 39 years . The first case of the coronavirus in Iceland was confirmed on February 28 , 2020 . The number of cases rose to a total of 1,815 on June 16 , 2020 , and did not increase since .
