In Mexico , passenger car sales have been oscillating recently . It is estimated that , in 2018 , over 883 thousand passenger cars were sold in the country , down from more than 984 thousand observed a year earlier . The source defines passenger cars as motor vehicles with at least four wheels , used for the transport of passengers , and comprising no more than eight seats in addition to the driver 's seat . Furthermore , the number of commercial vehicles sold in Mexico has also decreased lately .
