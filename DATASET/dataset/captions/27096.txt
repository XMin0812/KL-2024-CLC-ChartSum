From January to August 2020 , a total of 163 femicides were recorded in Honduras . February and March were the months with the highest number of femicide victims with 30 and 29 women murdered on account of their gender , respectively . Since the COVID-19 outbreak , the government of Honduras imposed a nationwide lockdown in order to halt the spread of the virus . Since quarantine began , 88 women have been victims of femicide . During nationwide lockdowns , domestic violence and sexual abuse reports grew in many Latin American countries .
