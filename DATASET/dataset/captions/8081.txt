The statistic shows the gross domestic product ( GDP ) in the Czech Republic from 2004 to 2019 , with projections up until 2021 . Gross domestic product ( GDP ) denotes the aggregate value of all services and goods produced within a country in any given year . GDP is an important indicator of a country 's economic power . In 2019 , the Czech Republic 's gross domestic product amounted to around 412.98 billion international dollars .
