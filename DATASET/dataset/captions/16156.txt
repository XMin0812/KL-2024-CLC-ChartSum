The statistic represents the U.S. Medicare outlays from 2000 to 2019 with an additional forecast from 2020 to 2030 as a percentage of the Gross Domestic Product . Medicare outlays amounted to 775 billion U.S. dollars in 2018 , which was about 3.6 percent of the U.S. GDP . The forecast predicts an increase in Medicare outlays up to 1,611 billion U.S. dollars in 2030 , which would be about 5.2 percent of the projected U.S. GDP .
