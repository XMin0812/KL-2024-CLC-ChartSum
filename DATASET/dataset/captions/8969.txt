The statistic shows the U.S. federal revenue from 2000 to 2019 with an additional forecast from 2020 to 2030 as a percentage of the Gross Domestic Product . Federal revenue amounted to 3.46 trillion U.S. dollars in 2019 , which was about 16.3 percent of the U.S. GDP . The forecast predicts an increase in federal revenue up to 5.75 trillion U.S. dollars in 2030 , which would be about 18 percent of the respective U.S. GDP .
