The COVID-19 pandemic that spread across the world at the beginning of 2020 was not only a big threat to public health , but also to the entire sports industry . Several professional sports leagues , including the NBA and NHL , as well as most professional soccer leagues in Europe , took the decision to postpone or suspend their seasons in order to mitigate the spread of the disease . This unprecedented move was covered extensively in the media with ramifications for both players and fans alike . In a March 2020 survey , 13 percent of respondents strongly agreed with the sentiment that the suspension of live sports had a significant impact on their daily life .
