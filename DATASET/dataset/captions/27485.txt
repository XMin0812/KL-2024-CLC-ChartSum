In March 2020 , a decrease in the overall economic index among the main sectors of the economy was recorded in comparison to March 2019 . The sharpest deterioration was observed in the industrial processing and services sector in Poland . The industry most vulnerable to losses caused by the coronavirus pandemic is the hotel and catering industry , where a downturn of 22 percent year-on-year was recorded .
