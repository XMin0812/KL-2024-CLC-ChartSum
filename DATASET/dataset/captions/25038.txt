This statistic depicts the crude birth rate in Brunei Darussalam from 2008 to 2018 . According to the source , the `` crude birth rate indicates the number of live births occurring during the year , per 1,000 population estimated at midyear . '' In 2018 , the crude birth rate in Brunei Darussalam amounted to 14.9 live births per 1,000 inhabitants .
