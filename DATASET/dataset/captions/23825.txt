This statistic shows the vote share of political parties in the general election in the United Kingdom in 2017 . The Conservative Party had gained the greatest vote share , with 42.2 percent of all votes for the party under leadership of Theresa May . By comparison , the Labour Party had won a similarly large share of votes , with a further 40 percent of all votes for Jeremy Corbyn 's party . Together , the Conservatives and the Labour Party made up 82.2 percent of all votes , with a further 7.4 percent of votes for the Liberal Democrats .
