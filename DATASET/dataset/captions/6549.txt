Siemens Gamesa Renewable Energy is the leading offshore wind turbine manufacturer in Europe . In 2019 , the Spanish based company accounted for 68.1 percent of all cumulative installations , operating 3,438 turbines which generate 15 gigawatts of electricity . Siemens and Gamesa merged their wind business in April 2017 and generated over ten billion euros in revenues in their 2019 financial year .
