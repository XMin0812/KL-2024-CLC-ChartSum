The statistic illustrates the distribution of total sales of Diagnosis and Treatment by Philips Group in 2019 , by type . In that year , diagnostic imaging had with 40 percent the largest share in sales of Diagnosis and Treatment . The total sales of the Diagnosis and Treatment segment was approximately 8.49 billion euros . After spin-offs of other product segments , Koninklijke Philips N.V. today is a company focused on healthcare/medical technology .
