In financial year 2020 , the highest proportion of production land was used to cultivate fruits in the Indian state of Arunachal Pradesh , which amounted to around 48 thousand hectares . In comparison , the size of croplands utilized for plantations , aromatic and medicinal crops accounted for less than 0.5 percent of the total area of cultivation in this state . The total area of production in Arunachal Pradesh was about 69 thousand hectares in the financial year 2020 .
