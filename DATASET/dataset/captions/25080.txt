According to a survey , 57 percent of Norwegians strongly agreed with the Norwegian government 's decision on banning people from going to their holiday homes over Easter this year due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak . Only 13 percent of the respondents either partially or strongly disagreed with the government 's prohibiton . The first case of COVID-19 in Norway was confirmed on February 26 , 2020 .
