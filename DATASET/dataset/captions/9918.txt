At the end of fiscal year 2020 , the production volume of dyes and pigments across the country was around 191 thousand metric tons . Some of the commonly manufactured products under this category were disperse dyes , optical whitening agents , and reactive dyes . The Indian chemical industry is highly diversified . With a coverage of over 80 thousand products , the south Asian country was the sixth largest producer of chemicals in the world and the fourth largest in Asia .
