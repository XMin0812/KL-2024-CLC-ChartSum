The statistic shows Switzerland 's share in the global gross domestic product ( GDP ) adjusted for Purchasing Power Parity ( PPP ) from 2015 to 2025 . GDP refers to the total market value of all goods and services that are produced within a country per year . It is an important indicator of the economic strength of a country . In 2019 , Switzerland 's share in the global GDP adjusted for PPP was approximately 0.46 percent .
