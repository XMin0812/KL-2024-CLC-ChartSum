This statistic shows British public opinion by age on September 24 to 25 , 2014 , on whether the RAF should join the US-led air strike operations against Islamic State/ISIS in Iraq and/or Syria . A total of 48 percent of participants approved of campaigns in both Iraq and Syria , and 24 percent of people were unsure . People were more supportive of an Iraq only campaign than a Syria only campaign , although both were vey unpopular .
