As of the third quarter of 2020 , CBS All Access , Showtime OTT , and other ViacomCBS branded services had a combined userbase of 17.9 million , up from 10.4 million in the corresponding quarter of the previous year . Meanwhile , ViacomCBS service Pluto TV had over 28 million MAU ( monthly active users ) in Q3 2020 , marking a jump of over ten million from Q3 2019 figure .
