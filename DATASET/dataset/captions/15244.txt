The amount of municipal waste that was incinerated in Italy remained quite steady over time . In 2018 , approximately 5.6 million tons of waste were incinerated . Incineration is the process of getting rid of waste through combustion . Most of the times , the outcome is energy production . This process improved throughout the years , becoming less harmful for the environment due to the deployment of new technologies , which make it possible to eliminate pollutant agents .
