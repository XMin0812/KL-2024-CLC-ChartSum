As of 2018 , South Africa 's number of households increased and counted approximately 16.67 million in total , of which the majority of it lived in Gauteng , Kwazulu-Natal and Western-Eastern Cape . Gauteng ( includes Johannesburg ) is the smallest province of South Africa , though highly urbanized with nearly five million households according to the estimates . Region Kwazulu-Natal had roughly three million households , whereas Western Cape counted 1.87 million .
