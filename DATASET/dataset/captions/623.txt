The statistic shows the gross domestic product ( GDP ) in Japan from 1984 to 2019 , with projections up until 2021 . In 2019 , the gross domestic product of Japan amounted to around 5.71 trillion international dollars . This makes Japan third in the world GDP ranking . For comparison , the GDP of China had amounted to 8.39 trillion U.S. dollars in 2012 , marking the second-largest economy in the world .
