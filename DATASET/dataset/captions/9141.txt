The statistic depicts the brand value of the most valuable European football leagues in 2012 . According to the ranking , the Barclays Premier League has a brand value of 4.17 billion U.S. dollars in 2012.A brand is defined here as the trademark and associated intellectual property . Football clubs are made up of a mixture of fixed tangible assets ( stadium , training ground ) and disclosed intangible assets ( purchased players ) with brand value.To calculate brand values the Royalty Relief method was used . This approach assumes the company does n't own their brand and must license it from a theoretical third party . The method determines how much it would cost to do this .
