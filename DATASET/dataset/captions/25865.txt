In the fiscal year ended March 31 , 2020 , net profits of Isuzu Motors Limited plunged to 81.23 billion Japanese yen , down from around 113.44 billion yen in the previous fiscal year . These numbers need to be assessed in the context of Isuzu 's operating income in this period . Isuzu Motors is a Japan-based company specializing in the manufacturing of commercial vehicles and vehicle engines .
