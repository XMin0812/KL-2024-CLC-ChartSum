This statistic displays the gross domestic product ( GDP ) of Saba in the Caribbean Netherlands from 2012 to 2017 ( in million U.S. dollars ) . Between 2012 and 2016 , GDP of Saba increased year on year . In 2012 , GDP was valued at roughly 42 million U.S. dollars . By 2016 , this had grown to 48 million U.S. dollars . Only in 2017 Saba 's GDP decreased slightly , to 47 million dollars .
