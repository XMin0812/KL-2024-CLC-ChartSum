As of April 2020 , Cameroon international André Onana had the highest market ( transfer ) value in the Dutch Eredivisie . The 24-year-old Ajax goalkeeper saw his market value amount to 36 million euros . Brazilian international David Neres had a value of 36 million euros as well , whereas Nicolás Tagliafico saw its market value amount to 28 million euros . Note that the market values decreased significantly due to COVID-19 pandemic and the stop of competitions . Nevertheless , Ajax dominates the top three and is well represented with 12 players in the top 25 , representing approximately 242 million euros of the total of 283.25 million euros market value of Ajax Amsterdam .
