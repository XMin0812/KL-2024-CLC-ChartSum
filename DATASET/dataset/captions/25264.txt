In 2019 , the travel and tourism sector in Antigua and Barbuda contributed to nearly 91 percent of the country 's total employment . This Caribbean country was by far the most tourism-dependent island in the region in terms of employment that year . Aruba ranked second in the list , with a contribution share of 84.3 percent , while Saint Lucia was third , with 78.1 percent . That same year , the total contribution of travel and tourism to employment in the whole Caribbean region was 15.2 percent .
