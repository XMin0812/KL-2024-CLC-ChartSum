As of November 17 , 2020 the number of COVID-19 cases in the Australian state of Victoria was at 205.9 people per 100,000 of the population . From March and most of April 2020 , New South Wales held the highest proportion of cases . Tasmania overtook New South Wales in late April , however in late June a rapid increase in recorded cases in Victoria saw the south eastern state overtake the other states and territories in recorded cases .
