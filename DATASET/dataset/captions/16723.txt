This statistic shows the national debt of Brunei Darussalam from 2015 to 2019 in relation to the gross domestic product ( GDP ) , with projections up until 2025 . The figures refer to the whole country and include the debts of the state , the communities , the municipalities and the social insurances . In 2019 , the national debt of Brunei Darussalam amounted to approximately 2.58 percent of the GDP .
