The coverage ratio of gross non-performing exposures of the Italian banking Group UniCredit improved significantly from 2014 to 2019 . According to data , this ratio grew from 51.2 percent in 2014 to over 65 percent in 2019 . The coverage ratio measures a bank 's ability to absorb potential losses from its non-performing loans and is calculated as follows : ( Loans - Reserve balance ) / Total amount of non-performing loans .
