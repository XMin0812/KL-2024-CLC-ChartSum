This statistic shows the number of cars sold by Nissan in the United Kingdom ( UK ) between July 2014 and December 2019 . The spikes seen in March and September are due to the issuing of new registration plates by the Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency . In December 2019 , Nissan sold 5,309 cars in the UK , briging annual sales to 92,372 units , a ten percent drop from 2018 .
