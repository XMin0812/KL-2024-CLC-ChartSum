This survey carried out regularly between March and May 2020 reveals the level of trust of French people have in their government to deal effectively with the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) situation in 2020 . It shows that 39 percent of French people had trust in the ability of the government to confront the virus on April 22 . On March 20 , the majority still trusted their political leaders . By May 15 , trust levels were back to 44 percent . For further information about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , please visit our dedicated Facts and Figures page .
