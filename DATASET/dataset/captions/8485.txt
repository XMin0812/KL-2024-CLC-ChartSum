The highest grossing slasher movies of all time in North America is 'Halloween ' _ , which was released in October 2018 and featured Jamie Lee Curtis . The movie marked the eleventh installment of the 'Halloween ' franchise and generated 159.34 million U.S. dollars in box office revenue . Wes Craven 's 1996 movie 'Scream ' was second in the ranking , and remakes of well-known slasher movies including 'Friday the 13th ' and ' A Nightmare on Elm Street ' also featured in the list .
