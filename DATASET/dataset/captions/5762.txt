The New York Times Media Group 's circulation revenue amounted to 300.95 million U.S. dollars in the third quarter of 2020 , up from 267.3 million in the corresponding quarter of 2019 . The Times ' subscription revenue generally grows steadily over the course of each year . However , for the most part , the company sees a small revenue decrease in the third quarter , which has been an ongoing and consistent trend for the company over the last few years .
