This statistic illustrates the average ( mean ) weekly rent of social renters in England from 2008 to 2018 . It can be seen that the average ( mean ) weekly rent for social renters in England increased between 2008/2009 and 2018/2019 , from a value of 71 British pounds per week to a value of 102 British pounds per week . An increase of 31 British pounds over the ten year period . In contrast , as of 2018/2019 , the average ( mean ) weekly rent for private renters in England ( not including London ) stood at a value of 95 British pounds per week .
