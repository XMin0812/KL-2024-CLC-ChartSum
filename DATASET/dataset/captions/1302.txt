The 122 murders recorded by the London Metropolitan Police in 2019/20 occurred following an 18 percent increase from the previous year , when there were 122 murders reported . Although this as still fewer than the 163 recorded in 2017/18 , it was far higher than in any year between 2011/12 and 2016/17 , when the number of murders never exceeded 103 and was as low as 93 in 2014/15 . In the last five years , the worst quarter for murders was the second quarter of 2017 , when there were 51 murders , followed by the first quarter of 2018 when there were 46 .
