Data on the gross median hourly pay for employees in the United Kingdom ( UK ) for 2020 , by age group , shows that the age group which earned the highest hourly pay were those between the ages of 40 and 49 years , at 15.86 British pounds per hour . The age group that earned the lowest hourly pay , excluding 16 and 17 year olds , were those between the ages of 18 and 21 years , at 9 British pounds per hour .
