In 2019 , many respondents in Poland perceived Halloween as a purely entertaining event , but the flaws of the holiday were also noticed . For most Poles , Halloween was a strictly commercial day . Respondents noticed that this was another opportunity for shops and brands to increase their sales . Some Poles were of the opinion Halloween should not be celebrated . According to them , celebrating domestic traditions connected with All Saints ' Day and All Souls ' Day should be much more crucial .
