The Indian sports industry has grown dramatically over the last few years . The industry became one of the fastest growing with a margin of around ten percent over the global average . The total revenue of the industry was projected to be around 1.9 million U.S. dollars in 2015 . The Indian Premier League , Hockey India League , Indian Badminton League , Pro Kabbadi and the Indian Sports League are some of the initiatives giving a major boost to the industry and also encouraging the younger generations to take up other sports in a country where cricket has dominated the sports culture since the British rule .
