This statistic shows wearables as component of wellness and prevention internet of health things ( IoHT ) solutions by providers in the U.S. , according to a survey conducted among health care payers and health care providers between 29th January 2016 and 16th February 2016 . Almost 42 percent of the providers said yes , they are providing wearables to patients and 28 percent said , yes , they are providing wearables at a subsidized cost for patients .
