As of January 06 , 2021 , the number of confirmed COVID-19 cases in Africa amounted to 2,929,778 which represented around 3.43 percent of the infections around the world . By the same date , coronavirus cases globally were over 87.6 million , causing nearly 1.89 million deaths , while approximately 63.1 million people recovered from the disease . In the African continent , South Africa was the most drastically affected country , with more than 1.15 million infections .
