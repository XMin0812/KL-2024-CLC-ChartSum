According to a survey from March 2020 , almost 90 percent of the Finnish people had a positive opinion about the public broadcasting company 's ( Yle ) response to COVID-19 situation . Around 39 percent of respondents evaluated that Yle had done a very good job , with only six percent saying that Yle had handled the coronavirus situation somewhat or very poorly . The first COVID-19 case in Finland was confirmed on January 29 , 2020 . For further information about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , please visit our dedicated Facts and Figures page .
