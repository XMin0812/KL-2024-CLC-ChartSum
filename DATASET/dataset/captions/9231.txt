This statistic depicts a pharmaceutical company ranking based on R & D for developing countries in 2018 , according to the Access to Medicine Index 2018 . In that year , the leading company concerning research and development for these markets was GlaxoSmithKline with an indicator score of four points . The Access to Medicine Index is an independent initiative that ranks the world 's 20 largest companies according to their efforts to make their products more available , affordable and accessible in developing countries , highlighting policy and practice that either facilitate or hinder access to medicine .
