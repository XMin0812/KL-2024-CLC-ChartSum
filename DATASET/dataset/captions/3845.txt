The United Kingdom 's economy grew by a record 15.5 percent in the third quarter of 2020 , right after the record 19.8 percent contraction in the second quarter of 2020 . During that quarter , the UK was officially in a technical recession , as the first quarter of 2020 also saw a growth rate of negative 2.5 percent . Following the global outbreak of Coronavirus , this sharp contraction in GDP was anticipated , but it remains to be seen how long it will take for the economy to recover .
