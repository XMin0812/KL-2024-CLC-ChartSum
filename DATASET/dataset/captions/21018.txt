This statistic illustrates the total assets of the McKesson Corporation in fiscal years 2007 to 2020 . A steady growth can be observed over the whole period , reaching its climax in FY 2020 , with total assets of more than 61 billion U.S. dollars . McKesson is a health corporation , headquartered in San Francisco , California . Its main business consists of pharmaceutical distribution , health information technology , medical supplies , and care management tools .
