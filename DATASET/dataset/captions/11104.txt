In 2019 , 6,460 patients necessitated a kidney transplant . This was by far the organ that was most commonly needed by patients on an organ donation waiting list . In the same year , on hold for a liver transplant were 1,031 patients , whereas 670 ill individuals were waiting for a new heart . Lastly , 363 patients on the waiting list needed a lung transplant and 253 of them a new pancreas . For the third year in a row , the total number of patients on an organ donation waiting list decreased . The steady decline of patients on a kidney waiting list that occurred in the last few years impacted the total number considerably .
