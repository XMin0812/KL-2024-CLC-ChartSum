This statistic displays the number of psychiatric hospitals in the United States as of 2019 , sorted by facility operation type . There were 708 such facilities in total , of which 325 were identified as private for-profit hospitals . The United States is expected to increase its expenditures for mental health services from 87 billion U.S. dollars in 2001 to nearly 240 billion U.S. dollars in 2020 . The prevalence of different mental disorders can vary based on age group , ethnicity , and many other factors .
