As of June 2018 , the most popular YouTube comedy channel was that of Cyprien , accounting for more than 12 million subscribers . In second and third places came Norman fait des vidéos ( 9.5 million ) and Rémi Gaillard ( 6.4 million ) . Moreover , in a ranking of the most popular YouTube channels in France in general , Cyprien and Norman fait des vidéos also ranked as first youtubers the same year .
