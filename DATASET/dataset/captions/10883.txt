This statistic illustrates the number of Toyota and Lexus ( the luxury branch of Toyota ) hybrid cars sold in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in 2018 . The Toyota Yaris Hybrid was the most successful of the Toyota hybrid cars , selling over 17 thousand units in 2018 . The Yaris Hybrid first appeared in 2011 , but was based on a previous Yaris model . The most successful Lexus hybrid was the NX , with 4,835 sold cars in 2018 , which first debuted in 2014 .
