As of March 2020 , most Finns ( 56 percent ) had the opinion that the government 's way of handeling the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak was quite good or very good in Finland . By comparison , 11 percent thought that it was very badly handeled . The first case of COVID-19 in Finland was confirmed on January 30 , 2020 .
