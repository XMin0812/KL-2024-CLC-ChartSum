This statistic displays the monthly exchange rate ( as of the end of each month ) of the euro to the British pound sterling ( EUR GBP ) , according to data from the European Central Bank , from January 2014 to November 2020 . The concrete , or point in time , measure shows the exchange rate as of the last day of each month , which is different to the standardized measure ; calculation of average based on observations throughout the period in question . On the last trading day of November 2020 , one euro was worth 0.9 British pounds .
