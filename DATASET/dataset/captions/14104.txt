In February 2020 , it was found that 60 percent of individuals surveyed in Italy and Great Britain thought that the Coronavirus represented a threat to public health in their countries , the countries with the highest feelings of threat from the virus in Europe . On the other hand , only 30 percent of respondents in Denmark believe that Coronavirus is a public health threat in their country .
