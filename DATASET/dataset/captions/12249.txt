This graph depicts the revenue of the sporting goods company Cybex from 2005 to 2011 . In 2006 , the revenue of Cybex was at 126.9 million U.S. dollars . Cybex is a fitness equipment manufacturer for commercial and consumer use from the U.S. Cybex mainly produces cardiovascular and strength equipment such as arc trainers , treadmills , stationary bicycles and steppers .
