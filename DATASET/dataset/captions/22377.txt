Data on the global revenue generated by French media conglomerate Vivendi in different geographic regions in 2019 showed that the company generated most of its revenue in Europe and the Americas , though revenue from Africa increased from just over 600 million in 2018 to almost 700 million in 2019 . The company 's operations in Asia and Oceania also brought in more revenue in 2019 than in the previous year , but has yet to reach two billion euros .
