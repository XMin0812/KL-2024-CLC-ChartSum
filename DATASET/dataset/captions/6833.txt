In the European Union , the number of companies in the business of textile and clothing manufacturing has ebbed and flowed over the period of time between 2009 and 2018 . In 2018 , the total number of textile and clothing manufacturers was recorded as 171,072 in EU28 countries , marking a loss of about 17,000 of them since 2009 . In contrast , the turnover figures of the industry demonstrated a consistently positive trend before peaking at 181 billion euros in 2017 .
