In 2019 , the CPI in Romania was 103.83 with an inflation rate of 3.8 percent . The index of consumer prices measures the development of the total prices of purchased products and services that have been used by the population in the current year compared to the previous year . The highest CPI in the last ten years was registered in 2010 when it reached 106.09 with a yearly rate of inflation of 6.1 percent compared to the previous year . The lowest index of consumer prices in Romania was recorded in 2016 when it reached 98.54 .
