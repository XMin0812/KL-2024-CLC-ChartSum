This statistic shows the price distribution of a pack of 20 cigarettes in Denmark in 2017 . At the time of the survey , the total price for a pack of 20 cigarettes in Denmark was 5.41 euros . Out of the total amount , tobacco tax made up for 3.23 euros . After the deduction of the value-added tax , the net price of a cigarette pack was 1.10 euros .
