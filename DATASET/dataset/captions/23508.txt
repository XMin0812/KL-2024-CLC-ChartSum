The Ford Fiesta was the most common passenger car to be found on roads in the United Kingdom at the end of 2018 . It began production in the 1970s and is currently in its seventh generation . Since then , the number of registered models has grown to over 1.5 million units . The Fiesta was followed by another Ford model , the 1998 released Focus . The U.S. based automobile manufacturer began selling vehicles to the UK from the early twentieth century onwards and has since become one of the most beloved car brands in the country .
