This statistic shows the average annual output price of potatoes in the United Kingdom from 1994 to 2018 in an index format . Between 1994 and 2018 , total output prices have generally risen reaching a peak in 2013 with a price index of 147.7 . In 2014 , the index had dropped to 98.1 points . In 2018 , the output price index for potatoes stood at 121.1 points . ( 2015 = 100 index points )
