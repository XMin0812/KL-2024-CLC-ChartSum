This statistic gives information on the mobile data traffic in Central and Eastern Europe in 2016 with projections until 2021 . In 2016 , mobile data traffic in Central and Eastern Europe amounted to 923,803 terabytes per month . In 2021 , Central and Eastern European traffic from mobile devices such as smartphones , laptops , tables , M2M connections and non-smartphones is projected to reach 5,252,334 terabytes per month . The projected compound annual growth rate ( CAGR ) amounts to 42 percent .
