This statistic shows the revenue of Arch Coal between 2009 and 2019 , in millions of U.S. dollars . Arch Coal is a U.S. company that mines , processes , and markets bituminous and sub-bituminous coal with low sulfur content . It is headquartered in St. Louis , Missouri . In 2019 , the company generated some 2.29 billion U.S. dollars of revenue .
