This survey shows the opinion of U.S. Americans towards the U.S. withdrawal from Afghanistan prior to the end of 2014 in March 2012 . In the poll from March 21 to 25 , 2012 ; 44 percent of the respondents thought that the United States should withdraw their troops sooner .
