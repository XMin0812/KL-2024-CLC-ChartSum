In light of the coronavirus outbreak , a decrease in services in the hotel and restaurant industry in Norway is expected to have negative consequences for the growth of the Norwegian gross domestic product ( GDP ) . If hotel and restaurant services in Norway experience losses of ten billion Norwegian kroner , the GDP is expected to decrease by 8.7 billion kroner , according to a recent forecast from April 2020 . Moreover , hotel and restaurant services are expected to decrease by 4.1 billion Norwegian kroner .
