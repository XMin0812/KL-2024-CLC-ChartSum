This statistic presents the total number of mentions worldwide of Netflix series Black Mirror season four on Twitter and Reddit from December 2017 to January 2018 , by episode . The episode , USS Callister reported in the highest number of mentions over both Twitter and the Black Mirror subreddit with a total of 46,000 mentions . Tied in second place , are the episodes Arkangel and Hang the DJ , which both respectively recorded a total of 38,000 mentions each .
