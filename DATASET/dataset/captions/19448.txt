The approval rate of Mauricio Macri , the president of Argentina since December 2015 , has decreased from 39 percent in his first year of government ( 2016 ) to only 23 percent in 2018 . After reaching a 86 percent peak in 2003 , year in which Eduardo Duhalde was succeeded by Néstor Kirchner , the approval rate decreased to 25 percent in 2009 , when Cristina Fernández de Kirchner was in office .
