This statistic presents the losses incurred as a result of automated teller machines - related attacks or incidents reported in selected European countries from 2010 to 2019 . According to data on the number of fraud incidents such as transaction reversal fraud , card skimming and physical attacks , the number of ATM attacks increased between 2018 and 2019 . During the period displayed , the value of losses incurred through payment terminal crimes fluctuated . In 2019 , the value of reported losses amounted to just under 250 million euros .
