This statistic shows the poverty rate for families in the U.S. from 1990 to 2019 . In 2019 , 7.8 percent of the families in the U.S. lived in poverty . Poverty is the state of one who lacks a certain amount of material possessions or money . Absolute poverty or destitution is inability to afford basic human needs , which commonly includes clean and fresh water , nutrition , health care , education , clothing , and shelter .
