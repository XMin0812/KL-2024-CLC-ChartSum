According to a survey held in September 2019 , 43 percent of adults in the U.S. stated that they had not seen , read , or heard anything about Apple 's upcoming Apple TV Plus subscription video-on-demand platform . Although the service is not widely know about as of yet , Apple 's attempt to enter the streaming market is assisted by the popularity of the company 's consumer electronics devices , many of which come preloaded with a range of Apple apps .
