This statistic shows the monthly trend in the volume of textile sales ( quantity bought ) in Great Britain from January 2016 to July 2020 . The figures are seasonally adjusted estimates , measured using the Retail Sales Index ( RSI ) and published in index form with a reference year of 2016 equal to 100 . In July 2020 , textiles sales dropped to an index level of 95 , after declining to 33.3 in May 2020 .
