In 2019 , the highest earning podcast/podcaster in the world was 'The Joe Rogan Experience ' by Joe Rogan , which made 50 million U.S. dollars that year and reportedly had almost 200 million downloads per month . In second place was popular true crime podcast 'My Favorite Murder ' by Karen Kilgariff and Georgia Hardstark , which generated 15 million dollars in 2019 .
