The statistic depicts Mexico 's gross domestic product ( GDP ) per capita from 1985 to 2019 , with projections up until 2025 . The GDP refers to the total market value of all goods and services that are produced within a country per year . It is an important indicator for the economic strength of a country . In 2019 , Mexico 's GDP per capita amounted to approximately 9,862.44 U.S. dollars . For further information , see Mexico 's GDP .
