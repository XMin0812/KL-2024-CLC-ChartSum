In 2018 , the B2B e-commerce value of apparel manufacturing shipments in the United States amounted to almost 5.1 billion U.S. dollars , down from 5.3 billion U.S. dollars in the previous measured period . This represents an 3.6 percent year-over-year segment decline . In 2018 , e-commerce accounted for over 51 percent of total shipments in the apparel manufacturing sector .
