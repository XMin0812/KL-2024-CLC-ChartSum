First developed by Markus Persson , Minecraft it is a sandbox video game which officially released in November 2011 . The game immerses players in a dynamically created world composed of blocks representing different materials such as earth , sand , stone , water , lava or minerals forming various structures and animals or monsters . Minecraft events on the video streaming service Twitch were watched by an average of 106.29 thousand viewers in November 2020 .
