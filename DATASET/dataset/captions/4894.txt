In 2019 , a total of 42 Ultimate Fighting Championship ( UFC ) events were hosted around the world featuring 516 fights . The highest live attendance in 2018 was at UFC Fight Night 136 – Hunt vs Oleinik with 22,603 ringside spectators and the highest attendance to date was at UFC 193 – Rousey vs Holm in 2015 with 56,214 attendees .
