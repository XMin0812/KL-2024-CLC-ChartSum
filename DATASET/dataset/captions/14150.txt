This statistic depicts the distribution of young Italians aged between 18 and 34 years who live with their parents ( or one parent ) in Italy as of January 2018 , broken down by age group . According to data , 81 percent of the respondents from the age group between 18 and 24 years admitted to live at their parents ' place , whereas the value for individuals aged from 30 to 34 years reached 34 percent .
