Since 2000 , the average annual wages in France have been increasing . In 2000 , the average annual wages in France amounted to 31,247 euros . Since then the average wage in France has been increasing , despite a slight period of stagnation between 2006 and 2008 . While the average annual wages were of 33,974 euros in 2008 , they reached 39,099 euros in 2019 . In 2019 , most of French citizens earned between 1,000 and 2,999 euros a month .
