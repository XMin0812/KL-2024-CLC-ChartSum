This statistic shows the percentage of methamphetamine potency in the U.S. from HY1 2012 to HY2 2018 . According to the data , potency has increased from 85.4 percent in early 2012 to 96.9 percent in the first half of 2017 . Potency is defined as the measure of drug activity in terms of the dosage required to exert an effect on the body and is measured by the amount of the highly potent d-isomer present in the drug substance .
