The statistic shows the leading toothpaste vendors in the United States in 2020 , based on sales . For the 52 weeks ended October 4 , 2020 , Procter & Gamble was the leading U.S. toothpaste vendor with sales of over one billion U.S. dollars . Total sales of U.S. toothpaste amounted to about 3.11 billion U.S. dollars in that period .
