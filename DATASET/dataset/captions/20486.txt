In the United States , the number of drivers involved in property-damage-only crashes in U.S. road traffic fluctuated considerably between 1995 and 2018 . In 2018 over 8.4 million drivers were involved in such crashes on U.S. roads ; this is the highest figure recorded since 1995 . Motor vehicle crashes are the leading cause of death among those under the age of 55 in the United States .
