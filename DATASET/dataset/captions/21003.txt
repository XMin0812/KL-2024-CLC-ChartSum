Due to the rapid spread of COVID-19 in Spain , the Government to was compelled to declare the state of health alarm on March 14 , 2020 . The impact of the coronavirus outbreak in Spain was significant in many industries , including advertising.During the state of imposed isolation , the media became one of the population 's main allies , especially streaming platforms and television . Due to this , the consumption of television advertising also increased . In the case of the open generalist channels , a Renault commercial broadcast by Telecinco the night of March 19 , 2020 ranked as the most viewed so far during the state of alarm .
