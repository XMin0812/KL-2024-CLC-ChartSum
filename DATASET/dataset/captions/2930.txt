Hyundai 's share of new car registrations in the EU between January 2019 and November 2020 fluctuated between 3.1 and 4.3 percent . In November 2020 , Hyundai 's share of new car registrations in the EU stood at 3.2 percent . The Hyundai Motor Company , a South Korean car manufacturer belonging to the Hyundai Kia Automotive Group , was founded in 1967 by Chung Ju-Yung .
