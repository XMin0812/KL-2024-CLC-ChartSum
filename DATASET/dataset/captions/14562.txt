Capital ratios express a bank 's capital as a percentage of its risk-weighted assets ( RWAs ) . Capital requirements for European banks were raised after the Basel III accord and phased in on the 1st of January 2015 , with a new minimum requirement of CET1 ratio of 4.5 percent . The United Kingdom 's largest banks have all continued to meet the minimum requirement up to the 4th quarter of 2019 .
