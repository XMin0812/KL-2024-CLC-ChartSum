Telenor ASA ranked first as the leading telecom company in Norway , by turnover , as of June 2020 . Its turnover was over 110 billion Norwegian kroner . Telenor Norge AS and Telia Norge AS came second and third in the ranking , with revenues of roughly 24 billion and ten billion Norwegian kroner . Telenor ASA was also first measured by net profit , which was nearly 22 billion Norwegian kroner . ( ID 554665 ) .
