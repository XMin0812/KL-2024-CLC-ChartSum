This statistic shows the total number of office buildings in the Netherlands from the first quarter of 2012 to the second quarter of 2020 . In the second quarter of 2020 , there were roughly 96,000 office buildings in the Netherlands . This is an increase when compared to the previous quarter . The source defines office buildings as : `` a building , or part of a building , where a consultancy firm , an accounting or law firm , an insurance company or city hall is located '' .
