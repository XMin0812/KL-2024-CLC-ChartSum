The statistic illustrates the total amount of investments of private equity companies based in Poland from 2007 to 2016 . Private equity is the OTC provision of equity capital through private or institutional investments with the participation of companies in another company for a limited time in order to generate financial benefits . It can be seen that total private equity investments fluctuated overall during the period under observation , reaching a value of almost 660 million euros as of 2016 . The largest total value of private equity investments was found in 2015 , when total private equity investment of close to 808.7 million euros was recorded .
