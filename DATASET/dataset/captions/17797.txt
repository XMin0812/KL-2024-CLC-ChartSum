This statistic shows the number of cars sold by Peugeot in Turkey between 2012 and 2019 . In the period of consideration , Turkish sales of Peugeot cars presented a trend of growth , despite some fluctuation . Peugeot sales peaked at 30 thousand units sold in 2017 . In 2019 , Peugeot sold approximately 23.1 thousand cars , a decrease of roughly seven percent in comparison with the previous year .
