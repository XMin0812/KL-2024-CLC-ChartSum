In 2019 , the national gross income per capita in Guatemala amounted to around 4.6 thousand U.S. dollars , up from 4.3 thousand U.S. dollars per person in the previous year . Panama and Costa Rica were the Central American countries with the highest national income per capita that year . Gross national income ( GNI ) is the aggregated sum of the value added by residents in an economy , plus net taxes ( minus subsidies ) and net receipts of primary income from abroad .
