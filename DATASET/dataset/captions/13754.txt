Along with other Latin American stock exchange markets , Chile 's IPSA stock index plummeted since the coronavirus outbreak in late February 2020 . On March 18 , 2020 the IPSA index closed at 2,876 Chilean pesos , down from 4,519 pesos on February 19 . The S & P/CLX IPSA index shows the average performance of a portfolio composed by 40 stocks with the highest trading value in the Santiago Stock Exchange , located in the Chilean capital .
