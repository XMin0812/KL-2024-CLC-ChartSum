Due to the coronavirus pandemic , some 85 percent of retailers estimate that food safety and sanitization methods will be heightened in foodservice operations in convenience stores in the long-term . In addition , more than half of the sample ( 53 percent ) believe that online ordering and delivery will become more common than in-store purchasing as a result of social distancing measures .
