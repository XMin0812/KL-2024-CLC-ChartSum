Germany is among the leading destinations for skiing in Europe . In 2020 , it offered 498 ski areas for ski enthusiasts . It was also the European country with the highest number of skiing participants , with 14.6 million Germans taking part in the sport . When considering the total population , the share of people who ski in European countries was the highest in Liechtenstein and Austria as of 2019 , with 36 percent of the population in either country , which comes to a much higher number in Austria due to population size .
