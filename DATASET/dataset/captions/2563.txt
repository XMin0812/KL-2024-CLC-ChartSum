In the 2019 Japanese Football League ( J1 ) season , the average annual player salary for the first team squad of Vissel Kobe amounted to approximately 1.57 million U.S. dollars . The team is headquartered in Kobe , a city in central Japan located in Hyogo prefecture , and plays its home games from the Kobe City Misaki Park Stadium ( also known as Noevir Stadium Kobe ) . Vissel Kobe was the best-paid team in Japan 's first football division ( J-1 ) by a considerable amount in the 2019 season .
