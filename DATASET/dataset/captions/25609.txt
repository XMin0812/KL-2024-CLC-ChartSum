This statistic represents the market share of electronic sell through video sales in the United Kingdom in 2018 . The market was dominated by Sky Store with 42 percent of the market share . It was followed by iTunes with 28.1 percent . Amazon Video held 17.6 percent of the market , while Google Play held 7.1 percent . Electronic sell through means that the consumer pays for every separate content bought and does not have a subscription service .
