The number of bullfighting events and bull festivals in Spain has been experiencing a significant decrease in the last decade , with 2,422 events registered in 2010 down to 1,425 in 2019 . The most common of these activities is Spanish-style bullfights or corridas , which make up about 25 percent of these events . Other festivals involving cattle , such as the widely known San Fermin celebrations in Pamplona , account for approximately 14 percent of bull events .
