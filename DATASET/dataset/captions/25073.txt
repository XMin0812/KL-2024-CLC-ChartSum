In recent weeks , all sports events in Poland were canceled due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic . This had a massive effect on TV sports channels . Overall , sports channels in Poland saw a 68.82 decrease in viewership between February and March 2020 . Canal+ Sport4 and Eleven Sport 2 noted the sharpest decline of 100 percent . For further information about the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , please visit our dedicated Facts and Figures page .
