In the first half of 2020 , the foodservice industry contributed with nearly 401 billion Mexican pesos to the country 's gross domestic product ( GDP ) , which represents around 38.2 percent of the industry 's GDP registered in the previous year . During the 2010s , the foodservice contribution to Mexico 's GDP grew by 65.8 percent . As of October 2020 , Mexico had more than 673.3 thousand foodservice establishments .
