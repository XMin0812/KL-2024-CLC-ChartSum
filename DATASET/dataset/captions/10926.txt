This statistic shows the gross domestic expenditure on research and development ( GERD ) as a percentage of GDP in Italy from 2000 to 2017 . Over the period , the GERD share rose by a total of 0.34 percentage points . The peak was in 2014 when 1.38 percent of Italy 's GDP was dedicated to research and development . This ratio is lower than in other European countries such as Germany and Austria .
