The load factor for electricity generation from anaerobic digestion in the United Kingdom has fluctuated since 2010 . In 2019 , the load factor of energy from anaerobic digestion increased from the previous year to 62.6 percent . In the period of consideration , load factor of energy from waste was at an average 59.6 percent . Load factors are the ratio of how much electricity was produced as a share of the total generating capacity .
