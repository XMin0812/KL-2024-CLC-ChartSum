In 2019/20 the planned budget for the United Kingdom 's Ministry of Justice was 8.05 billion British pounds , a slight increase on the 8.04 billion pounds budgeted in 2018/19 . During the provided time period , the budget for the Ministry of Justice peaked in 2009/10 at 9.1 billion pounds , before the coalition government of 2010 adopted a fiscal policy of austerity , which led to spending cuts in most governmental departments . Between 2010/11 and 2015/16 the Ministry of Justice 's budget shrank from 9.08 billion pounds to 7.35 billion pounds , approximately 1.75 billion pounds of cuts overall .
