This statistic displays the population of Saba in the Caribbean Netherlands in 2020 , by age group . It shows that most people living on Saba were between 25 and 30 years old . In 2020 , roughly 220 people fell in this age group . The group 95 to 100 years was the smallest . In 2020 , one person living on Saba was between 95 and 100 years old .
