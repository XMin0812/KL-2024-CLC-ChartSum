This statistic shows the U.S. Consumer Sentiment Index from December 2019 to December 2020 . The index is normalized to have a value of 100 in December 1964 and based on a monthly survey of consumers , conducted in the continental United States . It consists of about 50 core questions which cover consumers ' assessments of their personal financial situation , their buying attitudes and overall economic conditions . The Consumer Sentiment Index stood at 80.7 in December 2020 .
