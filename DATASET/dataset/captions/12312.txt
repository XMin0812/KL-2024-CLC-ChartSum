As of March 2020 , the biggest share of the Swedish respondents ( 33 percent ) had the opinion that the government 's way of handeling the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) outbreak was quite good . By comparison , only nine percent thought that it was very well handeled . The first case of COVID-19 in Sweden was confirmed on January 31 , 2020 .
