As of February 2020 , the South Korean film Parasite 's sales in its home country exceeded 87 billion South Korean won and the cumulative audience amounted to over 10 million . Parasite won the best picture at the 92nd Academy Awards , the first non-English language film to do so , and it is filmed by South Korean director Bong Jun-ho.South Korea 's film industry has grown significantly over the last decade , with theater audiences reaching over 220 million for the first time in 2019 , and box office revenue reaching a record 1.19 trillion South Korean won .
