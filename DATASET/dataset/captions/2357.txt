The statistic shows the price of potassium chloride from 2014 to 2019 , including a forecast until 2030 . The price per metric ton was 297 U.S. dollars in 2014 , and in the following year the price per metric ton was lowered by 296 U.S. dollars . In 2019 , the price of potassium chloride decreased to 256 U.S. dollars per metric ton , with an decrease in price down to 245 U.S. dollars per metric ton forecast for 2020 . It is expected that by 2030 the price will increase to 320 U.S. dollars per metric ton .
