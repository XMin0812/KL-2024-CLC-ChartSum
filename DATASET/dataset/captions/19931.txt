This statistic shows the number of Internet of Things ( IoT ) active connections in the tracking sector in the European Union ( EU ) in 2016 , 2019 , 2020 and 2025 . The number of IoT active connections in the tracking sector was expected to increase through the years . It was at 13.26 million connections in 2016 , and it was expected that it will reach 124.80 million connections by 2025 .
