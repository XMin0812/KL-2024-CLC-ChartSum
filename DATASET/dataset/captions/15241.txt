This statistic illustrates the total annual value of gross mortgage lending in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2007 to the first quarter of 2020 . It can be seen that the value of gross mortgage lending fluctuated overall during the period under observation , reaching a total value of approximately 276 billion British pounds in 2019 . The value of gross mortgage lending was highest prior to the global recession , with a total of more than 371.7 billion British pounds being recorded in 2007 . The smallest value of gross mortgage lending in the United Kingdom ( UK ) was found in 2010 , when just over 146 billion British pounds of gross mortgage lending was recorded .
