The statistic shows gross domestic product ( GDP ) per capita in Panama from 1985 to 2025  . GDP is the total value of all goods and services produced in a country in a year . It is considered to be a very important indicator of the economic strength of a country and a positive change is an indicator of economic growth . In 2019 , GDP per capita in Panama amounted to about 15,834.04 U.S. dollars .
