Between January and early December 2020 , the European country registering the largest number of migrants ' arrivals by sea was Spain . As of December 3 , 2020 , nearly 36.3 thousand immigrants reached the Spanish shores . Furthermore , Italy recorded around 32.7 thousand arrivals by sea , while the number of migrants who arrived on the Greek coasts amounted to approximately nine thousand . Lastly , Malta reported almost 2.3 thousand individuals .
