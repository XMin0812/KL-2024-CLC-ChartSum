This statistic shows the quarterly trend in the volume of second-hand goods retail sales ( quantity bought ) in Great Britain from 1st quarter 2015 to 2nd quarter 2020 . The figures are seasonally adjusted estimates , measured using the Retail Sales Index ( RSI ) and published in index form with a reference year of 2016 equal to 100 . In spite of some fluctuations , sales volumes of second hand goods have generally increased over the time period observed , reaching a peak in the first quarter of 2019 , at 145.2 .
