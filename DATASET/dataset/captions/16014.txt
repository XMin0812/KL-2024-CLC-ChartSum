This statistic illustrates the per capita consumption of non-alcoholic beverages in the European Union ( EU ) from 2010 to 2019 . Non-alcoholic beverages include soft drinks , packaged water and juices and nectars . In 2010 , 238.2 liters of non-alcoholic drinks were consumed per person in the EU , which has increased to 243.9 liters per person by 2019 .
