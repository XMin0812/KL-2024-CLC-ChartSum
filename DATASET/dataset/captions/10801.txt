Three major cruise lines dominated the European cruise market in 2019 : Carnival , MSC Cruises and TUI Group . Carnival Corporation had the largest combined passenger capacity , including its brands Costa , AIDA , P & O and Cunard . MSC Cruises followed as the biggest single brand with a capacity of 2.2 million passengers . The third major player , TUI Group , operates TUI cruises in Germany , Hapag Lloyd and Marella in the UK .
