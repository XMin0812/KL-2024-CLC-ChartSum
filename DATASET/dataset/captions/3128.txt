This statistic represents the leading container ship operators worldwide as of October 12 , 2020 , based on TEU capacity . In October 2020 , APM-Maersk 's ships had a capacity of roughly 4.2 million twenty-foot equivalent units ( TEUs ) , topping the rankings as the carrier with the largest capacity . They were followed by Mediterranean Shg Co who had a capacity of about 3.8 million TEUs .
