The outbreak of COVID-19 , also known as the novel coronavirus , has led to revised growth expectations for enterprise software spending in 2020 . A survey of software buyers and users revealed that on the whole , organizations will spend less on software , yet certain software solutions , particularly remote work solutions , are expected to witness spending increase . As of April , 67 percent of respondents reported increases in spending on web conferencing software .
