This statistic shows the evolution of the money supply ( measurement M1 ) in the United Kingdom ( UK ) economy from January 2016 to June 2020 . The money supply is the amount of monetary assets available in an economy . M1 , called the `` narrow money '' , includes currency in circulation ( banknotes and coins ) and overnight deposits . It depicts the value of the most liquid components of the money supply . In the latest month displayed , the value of total money supply ( M1 ) reached over two trillion British pounds ( GBP ) .
