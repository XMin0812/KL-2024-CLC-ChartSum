The statistic shows gross domestic product ( GDP ) per capita in current U.S. dollars in Cuba from 2008 to 2018 . GDP is the total value of all goods and services produced in a country in a year . It is considered to be a very important indicator of the economic strength of a country and a positive change is an indicator of economic growth . In 2018 , the estimated GDP per capita in Cuba amounted to around 8,821.82 U.S. dollars .
