This statistic displays the population of Sint Eustatius in the Caribbean Netherlands from 2011 to 2020 . Between 2011 and 2020 , the population of Sint Eustatius decreased from roughly 3,600 to approximately 3,300 . In 2020 , 3,139 people lived on Sint Eustatius . Approximately 15 percent of the total number of inhabitants of the Caribbean Netherlands live on Sint Eustatius .
