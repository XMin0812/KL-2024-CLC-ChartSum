In April 2020 , due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) crisis the majority of production and manufacturing companies in Norway experienced a decrease in revenues in the last month . 48 percent of the surveyed companies reported a decrease in revenues of up to 25 percent . Whereas 29 percent of the companies experienced a loss between 26 and 50 percent , seven percent experienced a decrease of more than 76 percent . Companies who experienced an increase in revenues in April 2020 were amongst others companies operating in the pharmaceutical industry and the aquaculture sector .
