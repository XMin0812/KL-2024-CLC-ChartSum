Primary energy production from biogas presented a trend of continuous growth over the years in the 28 members of the European Union . Between 2013 and 2018 , figures increased by 2,892 metric kilotons of oil equivalent . In 2018 , the EU-28 had a primary energy production of 16,839 metric kilotons of oil equivalent from biogas . The country with the largest energy production was Germany , followed by the United Kingdom and Italy .
