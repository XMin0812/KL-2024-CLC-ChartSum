In 2019 , Santander joined a growing number of the world 's largest banks in making a financial commitment to sustainable financing and investments to help projects , technologies , and clean energies in delivery of the Paris Agreement and the UN Sustainable Development Goals . Banco Santander 's goal is to facilitate and mobilze 120 billion euros of green finance between 2019 and 2025 . Between 2019 and 2030 the bank wants the level of green finance to have reached 220 billion euros . During 2019 , Banco Santander reached 18.6 billion in green finance , with the majority in project finance lending and financing .
