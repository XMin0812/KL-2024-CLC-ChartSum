This statistic shows the population in place names associated with the Christmas season in 2010 . In 2010 , 2,481 inhabitants lived in Santa Claus , Indiana . Remarkably less people lived in the town of the same name in Georgia - only 165 people . While there are several towns named Holly in the United States , Holly Springs in Mississippi made the list with 7,699 inhabitants , as well as Mount Holly , North Carolina with 13,656 people living there . Some towns are even named after Santa Claus ' reindeer : Rudolph , Wisconsin ( 439 inhabitants ) , Dasher , Georgia ( 912 inhabitants ) , or Snowflake in Arizona ( 5,590 inhabitants ) .
