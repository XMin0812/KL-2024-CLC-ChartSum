A higher number of women than men had tested positive for the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Sweden as of December 182020 . The number of men who were or had been confirmed infected was 142,001 and the number of women was 162,701 . The first case of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Sweden was confirmed on February 4 , 2020 . The number of cases has since risen to a total of 304,793 .
