The number of students enrolled at Spanish universities maintained steadily above 1.4 million over the past decade . The number of university students fluctuated over the period under review , with figures registering the lowest point during the 2008/2009 academic year at over 1.4 million students and peaking during the 2018/2019 academic year at almost 1.6 million . Most of the university students in Spain opted for public institutions to complete their studies , with approximately 1.3 million enrolled during the 2018/2019 academic year .
