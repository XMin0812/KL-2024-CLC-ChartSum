Indian public sector banks collectively owed approximately 6.8 trillion Indian rupees as non-performing assets at the end of fiscal year 2020 . This value was much higher at around 7.5 trillion rupees in the previous fiscal year , indicating a slow but slight relief for India 's economy in terms of non-paying assets at public banks . Public sector banks in India consist of 19 nationalized banks , the State Bank of India and other associate banks of the State Bank of India . At the same time , the government of India is the major stakeholder in all these banks .
