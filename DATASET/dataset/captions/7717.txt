Sixty percent of surveyed CIOs and IT executives worldwide expect a decline in their IT budget for 2020 as a result of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic . In contrast , only 18 percent expect their IT budget in 2020 to increase , while 22 percent forecast no change in the IT budget . The midpoint average for the impact of COVI-19 on IT budgets worldwide is a decline of 7.7 percent .
