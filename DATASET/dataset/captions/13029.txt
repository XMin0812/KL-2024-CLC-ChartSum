The Faster Payment Scheme ( FPS ) is a payment service that enables the quick and secure transaction of mobile , internet , telephone and standing order payments . This statistic illustrates the total volume of payments made though the scheme in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from September 2017 to December 2019 . During this period , the volume of payments increased from over 137.7 million in September 2017 , to approximately 227.7 million in December 2019 , reaching a value of nearly 171 billion British Pounds in FPS payments . More in-depth information could be found in the Statista dossier on payment instruments in the UK and Ireland .
