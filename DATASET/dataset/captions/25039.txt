This statistic displays the total passenger movements at Princess Juliana International Airport from 2012 to 2016 . With the exception of a slight decrease in 2013 , the number of passenger movements at Princess Juliana International Airport increased annually from 2012 to 2016 . In 2012 , approximately 1.7 million passengers passed through Juliana International Airport , whereas by 2016 this figure had increased to over 1.8 million passengers .
