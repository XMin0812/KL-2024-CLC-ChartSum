The statistic depicts the brand value of the top tier football leagues in Europe in 2020 , by country . In 2020 , the top tier football league in England , the Premier League , had a brand value of approximately 8.6 billion euros . A brand is defined here as the trademark and associated intellectual property . Football clubs are made up of a mixture of fixed tangible assets ( stadium , training ground ) and disclosed intangible assets ( purchased players ) with brand value . Of the clubs within the five most valuable European football leagues , the Big Five , Real Madrid , from the Spanish top tier football league La Liga , had the highest brand value out of any football club worldwide with a total of roughly 1.85 billion euros .
