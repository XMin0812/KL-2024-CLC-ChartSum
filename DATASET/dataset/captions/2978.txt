The coronavirus pandemic reminded us that infectious diseases are an integral part of humanity . The presented data show the number of people infected with particular infectious diseases in Warsaw in 1920 . The most dangerous illness of that period was scarlet fever , from which over three thousand people suffered . One hundred years later , precisely on 4 March 2020 , the first cases of COVID-19 infection were detected in Poland . On 20 November , over 45 thousand patients with coronavirus were registered in Warsaw .
