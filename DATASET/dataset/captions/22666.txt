This statistic displays the value of German exports of automotive parts and accessories to the United States of America ( USA ) from 2015 to 2018 , in thousand U.S. dollars . From 2015 to 2017 , exports of German automotive parts declined from approximately 6 billion U.S. dollars worth of exports in 2015 to 5.49 billion U.S. dollars in 2017 . In 2018 the total exports value increased , reaching roughly 5.93 billion U.S. dollars .
