This statistic shows the take up of London West End ( UK ) office space properties in the first quarter of 2020 , by the business sector of companies leasing space . Take up space refers to the sum of lettings and owner occupied office space . According to a report conducted by BNP Paribas , in the first quarter of 2020 , the top three sectors with strongest presence on the London West End office space market were media tech , association and serviced office , accounting for a total of 53 percent of take up space .
