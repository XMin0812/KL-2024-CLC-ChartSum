As of 2020 , Valeri Popovitsh lead the score board of the Finnish Football League ( Veikkausliiga ) with 155 scored goals . The former Russian footballer played in the Finnish top-division football team FC Haka . The second on the top scorer list is Rafael Vieira with 134 goals , who was a member of the Helsinki football club HJK around the year 2000 . Football is a beloved hobby sport in Finland , but it falls behind ice hockey in popularity as professional spectator sport .
