This statistic shows the share of American students in grades 9 to 12 who stated they have been threatened or injured with a weapon at school on one or more occasions during the 12 months before the survey . The results have been sorted by ethnicity and sexual identity . The survey was conducted from August 2018 to June 2019 . According to the source , 12.9 percent of black student respondents stated they have been threatened or injured with a weapon on school property at least once during the 12 months before the survey .
