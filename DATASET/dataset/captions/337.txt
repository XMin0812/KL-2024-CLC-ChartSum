On January 31 , 2020 , the first two cases of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in the United Kingdom ( UK ) were confirmed in a facility Newcastle upon Tyne in the Northeast of England , after two members of the same family became unwell in York . The number of cases in the UK increased significantly at the beginning of March and again at the end of 2020 . On January 3 , 2021 , the number of confirmed cases in the UK amounted to 2,654,779 .
