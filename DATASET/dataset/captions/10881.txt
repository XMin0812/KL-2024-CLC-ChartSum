Among the African countries with the highest GDP growth rates , Ethiopia ranked first with an annual percentage change by nine percent in 2018 over 2017 . Ethiopia 's GDP reached 94 billion U.S. dollars in 2019 , ranking also among the 20 countries with the highest GDP in Africa . Moreover , Rwanda followed with a growth rate by over eight percent in 2019 compared to the previous year . The country 's GDP equaled to roughly 9.8 billion U.S. dollars in 2019 .
