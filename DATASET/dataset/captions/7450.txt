This timeline depicts the global revenue of Essilor from 2008 to 2019 . In 2019 , the global revenue of Essilor amounted to approximately 7.9 billion euros.Founded in 1972 , Essilor International S.A. is a French company that produces ophthalmic lenses along with ophthalmic optical equipment . The company is responsible for creating Varilux , the world 's first progressive lens which corrects presbyopia and allows clear near , intermediate and far vision . In 2018 , EssilorLuxottica , based in Paris , was born from the incorporation of the Italian Luxottica by the French Essilor .
