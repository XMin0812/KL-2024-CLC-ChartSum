This statistic displays the fatality rates West African Ebola compared to major global infectious diseases as of March 30 , 2016 . Polio has a death rate of around 10 percent while Ebola 's is four times higher . The Ebola virus causes extremely severe hemorrhagic fever and is considered a Risk Group 4 Pathogen by the World Health Organization ( WHO ) . The health sector will focus on cross-border regions to strengthen treatment , testing , and contact tracing .
