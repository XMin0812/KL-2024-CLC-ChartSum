The popularity of Facebook in Spain can not be denied , with the number of users in the country fluctuating above 20 million in the last few years , namely 22 million users in 2019 . Considering that Spain 's population stood at approximately 47 million inhabitants in 2019 , this figure translates into a penetration of almost 46 percent of the country . As of 2020 , the country that featured the highest number of Facebook users was India , with 290 million people on the social network .
