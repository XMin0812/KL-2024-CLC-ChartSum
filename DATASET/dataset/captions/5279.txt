In the period of consideration , the number of road traffic fatalities in Romania peaked in 2008 , totaling 3,065 deaths . Despite minor oscillations , the number of victims recorded a declining trend . Thus , by 2019 road traffic fatalities reached 1,864 . This represented a decrease of roughly 40 percent compared to 2008 . By contrast , the number of non-fatal road traffic injuries increased to almost 40 thousand in 2019 .
