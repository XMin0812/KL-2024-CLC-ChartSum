The first case of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Finland was confirmed on January 31 , 2020 . The number of cases in Finland has since risen to a total of 37,549 . From September 2020 onwards , the number of new cases has been on the rise again in Finland . There were 599 new cases confirmed on November 25 , which was the highest number of cases recorded in a single day . The worldwide number of confirmed cases of coronavirus was roughly 86.9 million on January 6 , 2021 .
