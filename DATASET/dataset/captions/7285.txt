During a survey conducted in Brazil from October 2019 to March 2020 – therefore partially impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic – around 97 percent of respondents aged between 16 and 24 years had accessed the internet . Among Brazilians aged 60 and over , the internet penetration rate stood at 38 percent . Overall , it has been forecast that 67 percent of the population of Brazil was online in 2020 .
