Over the last few years , the total attendance at regular season games of the National Football League has consistently been at more than 16 million per season . The NFL is composed of 32 teams and each team plays eight home games during the regular season for a total of 256 games per season . In 2019 , the NFL came out on top of the list with the highest number of attendees per game - an average of 67.1 thousand fans watched each NFL game in 2019 .
