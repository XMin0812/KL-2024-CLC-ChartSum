This statistic shows the five leading periods of growth in the United Kingdom automotive market since 1959 . The earliest period of extended car market growth was between August 1962 and April 1964 , a period of 21 months . The most recent period started in March 2012 , and in September 2015 , 43 months later , the market was still growing . Growth in the car market ended in October 2015 , when a decline in passenger vehicle sales was observed .
