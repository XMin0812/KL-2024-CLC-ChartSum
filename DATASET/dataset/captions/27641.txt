Displayed are the total sales revenue from the organic food sector in France between 2010 and 2019 , combining all sales channels . The annual turnover for the market was of 3.7 billion euros in 2010 , and it has been increasing ever since . Between 2018 and 2019 alone , the consumption of organic products in France grew by more than 1.4 billion euros per year , reaching 11.93 billion euros , an increase of + 13.5 % compared to 2018 .
