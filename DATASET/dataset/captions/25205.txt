This statistic shows the quarterly expenditure on food and non-alcoholic beverage purchases in the United Kingdom from 1st quarter 2013 to 4th quarter 2019 , based on volume . During the fourth quarter of 2018 , UK households purchased approximately 25.23 billion British pounds worth of food and non-alcoholic drinks . By the third fourth of 2019 this had dropped to 25.21 billion British pounds .
