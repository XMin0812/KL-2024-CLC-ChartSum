The Seychelles was the number one supplier of tuna into the UK , exporting 18 thousand metric tons in 2017 . In the year prior , Ghana provided a larger share of UK imports than the Seychelles , with approximately 23 thousand tons of tuna . Imports from the country dropped the following year , seeing Ghana drop down into the UK 's second biggest supplier in 2017 . In order of volume , the next biggest suppliers were Ecuador , Mauritius and the Philippines . Total imports amounted to 113,457 metric tons of Tuna . The UK Tuna trade in detail
