The revenue index of the hospitality industry in the Netherlands in the first quarter of 2019 was 105.6 . Compared to the same quarter in 2018 , this was an increase of roughly 5 percent . In the second quarter of 2019 , the revenue index of the Dutch hospitality industry increased to approximately 136 . Since the first quarter of 2020 , the revenue index of hospitality industry went continually down due to the Covid19 pandemic . For the second quarter , the revenue decreased to 55.4 .
