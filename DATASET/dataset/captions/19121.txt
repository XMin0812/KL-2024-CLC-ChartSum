According to an early estimation by the Association of British Insurers ( ABI ) , the association member insurance companies expect to pay approximately 1.2 billion British pounds in compensation following the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic . The largest share of the claims , 75 percent or 900 million British pounds , are for business interruption . For reference , in 2018 , the UK non-life insurance sector paid a total of approximately 65 billion euros in claims .
