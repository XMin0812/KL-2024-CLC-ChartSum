This statistic shows the quarterly net income of the Inditex Group worldwide from 2012 to 2020 , in million euros . In the third quarter of 2019 , the net income of the Inditex Group amounted to over 1.17 billion euros worldwide , which represented the highest figure of this study . Due to the coronavirus pandemic in the first months of 2020 , the company suffered its first ever recorded net income loss , with results revealing -409 million euros during the first quarter of 2020 .
