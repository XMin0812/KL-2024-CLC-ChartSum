Gross domestic product ( GDP ) growth rate in Finland fluctuated fairly strongly between 5.3 and -8.1 percent in the period from 2005 to 2019 . According to preliminary data , the GPD growth rate was 1.1 percent in 2019 . The Finnish economy contracted almost nine percent from 2008 to 2009 , as a result of the global financial crisis . After 2009 , GDP growth rates did not reach the pre-crisis levels , although the GDP slowly resumed its growth .
