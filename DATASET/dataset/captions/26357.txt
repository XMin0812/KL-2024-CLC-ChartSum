This statistic presents the year-end closing values of the Dow Jones Transportation Index from 2000 to 2020 . The value of Dow Jones Transportation Index amounted to 12,506.93 points in 2020 . Even with the economic effects of the global coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , this is the highest point of the index in the past two decades . Statista offers a dedicated , freely accessible website with information on COVID-19 around the world .
