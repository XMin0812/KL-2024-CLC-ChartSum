The statistic shows the percent of households in the United Kingdom that have a home computer based on their household composition . The survey was conducted in 2018 . In this year , only 45 percent of households consisting of 'one adult , retired mainly dependent on state pensions ' had a home computer . In the category , 'Two adults , four or more children ' 100 percent of respondents had a home computer . It is important to note that this does not include laptop computers .
