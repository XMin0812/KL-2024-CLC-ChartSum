This statistic displays the age-standardized death rate in Canada , from 2000 to 2018 , for diseases of the appendix . In 2018 , around 0.1 out of 100,000 Canadians died from diseases of the appendix . In 2000 , the death rate also stood at about 0.1 per 100,000 . Death rates for diseases of the appendix have remained fairly stable over the last decade .
