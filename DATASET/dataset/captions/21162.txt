According to a survey from March 2020 , 37 percent of respondents in Italy believed that their online spending on Amazon was going to increase in the next month . In March 9 , 2020 , the Italian government imposed a national lockdown . Italian inhabitants were allowed to leave their houses only for strict necessities such as work or health circumstances .
