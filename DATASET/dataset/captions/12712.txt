In 2019 , Cineworld Group 's global revenue amounted to over 4.36 billion U.S. dollars , the highest figure recorded since the company 's inception . The company 's box office revenues make up the majority of the total figure and were also up year on year , although the coronavirus pandemic will very likely mean that the chain will struggle throughout 2020 as closures and consumer reticence to visit theaters continue to impact admissions .
