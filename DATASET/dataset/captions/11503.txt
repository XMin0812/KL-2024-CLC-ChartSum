Since the schoolyear 2009/10 , the number of teachers in primary school in Sweden increased . During this schoolyear , there were 73,255 teachers ; a number that had increased to around 92,890 in the schoolyear 2019/20 . Since the schoolyear 2010/11 , the number of pupils in primary school increased as well , reaching around 1.09 million pupils during 2019/20 .
