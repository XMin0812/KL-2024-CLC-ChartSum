This statistic shows the mobile subscription revenues of Virgin Media Inc. from their operations in the United Kingdom ( UK ) and Ireland from 2012 to 2019 . The mobile subscription revenues of Virgin Media has decreased in recent years , falling from 479.9 million British pounds ( GBP ) in 2014 to 361.4 million British pounds in 2019 . While revenues from mobile subscriptions decreased , revenues from cable subscriptions have increased . Despite this , the company has not been profiting in recent years , accumulating a losses in net income between 2014 and 2017 .
