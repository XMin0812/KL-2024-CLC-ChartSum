This statistic displays the monthly consumer price index ( CPI ) in Belgium from January 2018 to September 2020 . The consumer price index in September 2020 was 109.42 . For a more detailed overview of the development of the CPI , this statistic shows the monthly consumer price index for different expenditure categories . This shows that the prices for different goods and services can sometimes increase much more or less than other goods and products . For example , the consumer prices of non-food products increased much less in the last available month than the prices for services .
