Heart disease is currently the leading cause of death in the United States , accounting for 23 percent of all deaths . Other leading causes of death include cancer , accidents , chronic lower respiratory diseases and cerebrovascular diseases . The leading causes of death worldwide are like those in the U.S . However , lung cancer , road injury , diarrheal diseases and tuberculosis are all major causes of death worldwide , but are not among the leading causes in the U.S . Instead , accidents , influenza and pneumonia , intentional self-harm and kidney diseases have a larger impact in the U.S .
