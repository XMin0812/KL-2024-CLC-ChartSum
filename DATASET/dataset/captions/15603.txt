During last days of March 2020 , respondents were most worried about the necessity of isolation due to the outbreak of the coronavirus pandemic ( COVID-19 ) in Poland . Only half of the Poles noted any positive side of the current situation , while every fourth participant believed that the pandemic-related situation has a good influence on the time spent with the family .
