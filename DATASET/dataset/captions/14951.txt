In 2020 , the largest shares of Estée Lauder 's net sales were generated in Europe , the Middle East , and Africa . After almost a decade of constant growth , the company reported a slight drop in sales figures in 2020 . That year , Estée Lauder 's net sales amounted to 14.29 billion U.S. dollars , down from 14.86 billion U.S dollars in 2019 .
