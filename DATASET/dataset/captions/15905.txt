In the United States , nonfarm business sector unit labor costs decreased by 8.9 percent in the third quarter of 2020 compared to the previous quarter . The data are seasonally adjusted at annual rates . Unit labor costs describe the relationship between compensation per hour and productivity , or real output per hour , and can be used as an indicator of inflationary pressure on producers . Increases in hourly compensation increase unit labor costs ; labor productivity increases offset compensation increases and lower unit labor costs .
