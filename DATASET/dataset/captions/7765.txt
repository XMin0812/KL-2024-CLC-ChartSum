Imports of dried fruit to the United Kingdom ( UK ) were valued at approximately 142 million British pounds in2019 . Import values in 2019 were down compared to the previous year and their peak of 175 million pounds seen in 2016 . Export values roughly mirrored import trends with growth culminating in a peak in 2016 and a drop the following years . However , the value of dried fruit exports in 2019 was only a small fraction of 2019 imports .
