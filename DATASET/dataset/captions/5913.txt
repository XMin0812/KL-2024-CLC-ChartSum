As of January 06 , 2021 , the overall deaths due to coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Africa reached 69,960 . South Africa faced the highest number of casualties . With 31,368 deaths , the country accounted for roughly 44.8 percent of the total . Egypt was the second most affected in the continent , as the virus made 7,975 victims in the nation , nearly 11.4 percent of the overall deaths in Africa . Morocco accounted for around 10.9 percent of the casualties in the continent , with 7,618 victims . By the same date , Africa recorded more than 2.92 million cases of COVID-19 .
