Venice had around 3,550 floods vetween 1966 and 2019 . The majority were only minor floods , however : the water level remained under 90 cm above sea level in 60 percent of the cases over the considered period . Water levels higher than 170 cm were recorded only once : during the 1966 tide , when it peaked at 194 cm above sea level . In November 2019 , Venice experienced another record tide , with a water level of 187 cm .
