This statistic shows the average weekday individually paid print circulation of select newspapers in the United States as of September 2015 . On an average weekday , Newsday , published by Cablevision Systems Corporation , sold 217 thousand copies . The leading newspaper on this list was the Wall Street Journal , which had a weekday paid print circulation of over 1.06 million . The New York Times Company , whose main newspaper was second on the list , generated circulation revenue of over 880 million U.S. dollars in 2016 .
