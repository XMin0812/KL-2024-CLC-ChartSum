Kobe Bryant is widely regarded as one of the best basketball players of all time . He sits third on the list of NBA all-time scoring leaders , having amassed 33,643 points across his 20-season career . Bryant featured in all 82 games of the LA Lakers ' regular season on four occasions , most recently in 2010 - 11 , when he scored 2,078 points at an average of 25.3 points per game .
