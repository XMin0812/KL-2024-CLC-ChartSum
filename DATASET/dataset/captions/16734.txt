This statistic illustrated the added value generated by automotive industry suppliers each year in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2013 to 2018 , in billion British pounds . In the period of consideration , the lowest value was recorded in 2014 , when GVA by suppliers dropped to 4.3 billion British pounds . In the other years , this value remained relatively stable , amounting to 4.9 billion British pounds in 2018 .
