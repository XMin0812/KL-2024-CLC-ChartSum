This statistic displays the prevalence of men that are overweight or obese in selected Asian countries as of 2013 . In South Korea , 36.9 percent of men are considered overweight or obese as of 2013 . In many Asian countries , weight gain as well as higher rates of diabetes , hypertension , and weight-related disabilities have been observed in recent years . Using body mass index ( BMI ) , a person with a BMI of 30 or more is considered obese . A BMI over 24 is considered overweight .
