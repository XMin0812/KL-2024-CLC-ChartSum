In 2019 , internal travel and tourism consumption in Puerto Rico amounted to roughly 5.51 billion U.S. dollars , which is an increase of over a half of a billion U.S. dollars from the year prior . Nonetheless , this annual revenue is less than the amount generated at the beginning of the decade . Puerto Rico 's internal tourism consumption reached its peak of the last ten years in 2016 , with 6.66 billion U.S. dollars .
