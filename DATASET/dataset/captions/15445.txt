This statistic displays the results of a survey on internet usage in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in 2019 , by age group . In 2019 , internet usage was highest among younger individuals , with a hundred percent of respondents aged between 25 and 34 years using any device , connection type , at any location to access the internet . Internet usage appeared to decline among the older age groups , with only 70 percent of those aged 55 and above stating that they go online .
