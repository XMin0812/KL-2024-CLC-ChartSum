In 2019 , the population of Groningen ( including the smaller towns Haren , Hoogkerk , Ten Boer and Glimmen ) amounted to just over 230,000 , making it the largest city in the north of the country and the eighth-largest city in the Netherlands . In the last ten years , Groningen 's population grew slowly but steadily , from roughly 184,200 in 2009 to approximately 231,300 today .
