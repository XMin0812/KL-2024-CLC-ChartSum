This statistic , based on data provided by Parrot Analytics , shows a ranking of the most popular platforms for digital original content in South Africa . Netflix titles accounted for 67.9 percent of expressed demand in the first quarter of 2019 , followed by Amazon Prime Video and Hulu digital originals with 9.2 percent each . Netflix is even more in-demand in South Africa than in the United States , as well as on a global level .
