This statistic shows the total amount of passenger cargo uplifted by Virgin Atlantic Airways Ltd in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2008 to 2019 . Virgin Atlantic Airways Ltd is part of the Virgin Group and is based at Heathrow airport . The amount of cargo uplifted by Virgin Atlantic fluctuated during this period . The peak was recorded in 2008 at nearly 215,000 tons . In 2019 , Virgin Atlantic uplifted approximately 177,338 metric tons .
