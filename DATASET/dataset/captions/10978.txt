This statistic shows the average expenditure in billion U.S. dollars on Federal Pell Grant Awards in the United States from 1979/1980 to 2019/2020 . In the academic year 1979/80 , expenditure on Federal Pell grants totaled to 8.3 billion 2019 U.S. dollars . By the academic year 2019/20 , expenditure was up to 27.8 billion 2019 U.S. dollars .
