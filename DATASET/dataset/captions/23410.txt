The majority of European nations experienced government imposed lockdowns to halt the spread of the novel coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) . This in turn had a major impact on the electricity industry , with demand dropping across the continent . Between March 28 and April 30 2020 , Italy saw electricity demand drop 23 percent when compared to the same period in 2019 .
