The timeline shows the per capita consumption of frozen yogurt in the United States from 2000 to 2019 . The U.S. per capita consumption of frozen yogurt amounted to 1.2 pounds in 2017 . Frozen yogurt is a frozen , low-calorie dessert and it is often served in a large variety of flavors . Some frozen yogurt shops offer their customers the option to add various toppings for their frozen specialty .
