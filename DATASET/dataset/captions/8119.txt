The statistic illustrates the amount of private equity funds raised by European companies from 2015 to 2019 . Private equity is the OTC provision of equity capital through private or institutional investments with the participation of companies in another company for a limited time in order to generate financial benefits . It can be seen that the total amount of private equity funds raised in Europe increased during the period under observation , reaching a value of approximately 109.3 billion euros in 2019 . This was the highest single year for private equity funds raised during the period displayed .
