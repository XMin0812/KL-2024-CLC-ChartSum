This statistic illustrates the number of new specialist sports car registrations in the United Kingdom ( UK ) in 2019 , by model type . In that year , there were in total 44,122 new specialist sports vehicles registered . The leading five models accounted for around 37 percent of the segment share . Porsche held two places , which combined accounted for 12 percent of total sales in that segment . The Porsche model 911 sold 2,913 units , 521 more than the Porsche 718 . The Audi TT ranked first in this segment , accounting for around 10.7 percent of registrations .
