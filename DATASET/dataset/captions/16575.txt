The severe drop-off in global smartphone shipments from February 2019 to February 2020 gives some indication as to the impact of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic . The volume of shipments fell 38 percent year-on-year . This , according to the source , is the largest fall in the history of the global smartphone market .
