In June 2020 , the domain tools.usps.com was the most popular government domain with more than 314 visits in the preceding 30 days . In contrast , the CDC 's website recorded 107.66 million visits in the preceding 30 day period , down from over 432 million digital visits during the same timespan in March 2020 . The CDC is the most trusted source of information for the U.S. public regarding the current COVID-19 outbreak , followed by other government and public health websites .
