From 2015 to 2018 , the number of people who participated in windsurfing in the United Kingdom ( UK ) has increased . Whereas in 2015 , the number of peope who windsurfed was 109 thousand , in 2018 , it was 123 thousand . In 2018 , approximately 31.8 percent of people in the UK participated in any watersport activitiy . More information about water sports in the UK can be found in the Dossier : Water sports in the United Kingdom ( UK ) .
