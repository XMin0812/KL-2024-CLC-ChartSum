Gross advances of residential loans refers to the amount of new mortgage loans to borrowers . It can be seen that the value of gross mortgage lending reached an estimated value of approximately 73.5 billion British pounds as of the fourth quarter of 2019 . In the beginning of 2020 , mortgage lending plunged . This was largely to be attributed to the unclear outcomes of the coronavirus ( covid-19 ) crisis and people 's confidence in the economic environment decreasing .
