This statistic shows the annual average Consumer Price Index ( CPI ) of personal care products and appliances in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from 2008 to 2019 , where 2015 equals 100 . In 2019 , the price index value of personal care products and appliances was measured at 98 , displaying an increase by 1.2 percentage points on the previous year 's average of 96.8 .
