This statistic presents the annual expenditure on outbound travel in Norway from 2012 to 2018 , with a forecast for 2028 . Outbound travel spending increased from 106.4 billion Norwegian kroner in 2012 to 142.1 billion in 2016 . Expenditure in 2017 dropped back to 138.9 billion kroner . However , the figure is expected to increase to 142.5 billion kroner in 2018 and rise in the next ten years .
