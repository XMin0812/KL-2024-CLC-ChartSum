This statistic displays the share of small and medium enterprises ( SME ) in the wholesale and retail sector with growth plans for the next 12 months in the United Kingdom ( UK ) from fourth quarter 2011 to second quarter 2019 . The peak of this period was in the second quarter of 2014 and third quarter of 2015 , when 60 percent of wholesale and retail sector SMEs stated that they planned to grow in the following 12 months .
