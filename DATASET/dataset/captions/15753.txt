As the global demand for N95 masks has skyrocketed in 2020 due to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic , producers have scrambled to increase production to meet that demand . The market value of N95 masks amounted to more than 802 million U.S. dollars in 2019 . By 2027 , it is forecast that this value will increase by more than double , to nearly 1.90 billion U.S. dollars . It is estimated that production of the highly protective N95 face masks in the United States alone will increase to 180 million units per month by the end/winter months of 2020 .
