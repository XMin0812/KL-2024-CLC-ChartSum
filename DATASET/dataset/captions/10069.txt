This statistic shows the global revenue of LVMH Group 's selective retailing segment from 2008 to 2019 . In 2019 , this segment generated revenues of 14.8 billion euros . The LVMH Group is a French luxury goods corporation , which owns around 50 luxury brands worldwide , including Louis Vuitton and Bulgari . The fashion and leather goods segment generates the most revenue for LVMH .
