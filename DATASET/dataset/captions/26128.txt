This statistic shows the quarterly trend in the volume of retail sales ( quantity bought ) in non-specialized , predominantly non-food stores ( i.e . department stores ) in Great Britain from 1st quarter 2015 to 2nd quarter 2020 . The figures are seasonally adjusted estimates , measured using the Retail Sales Index ( RSI ) and published in index form with a reference year of 2016 equal to 100 . Department store sales reached a peak in the third quarter of 2018 , when the index measured at 104.5 and then saw a gradual decline throughout 2019 .
