This statistic displays the number of masjids or other premises in the United Kingdom , by their theme in 2017 . In this year there were 797 masjids in the United Kingdom that were the assocaited with Deobandi . This was by far the most common type of masjid in the United Kingdom . It was followed by Barelvi which accounted for 459 masjids in the United Kingdom . There was only one masjid in the United Kingdom associated with Ibadi in this year .
