This statistic shows the share of American students in grades 9 to 12 who stated they have been bullied at school during the twelve months before the survey . The results have been sorted by gender , ethnicity , and sexual identity . The survey was conducted from August 2018 to June 2019 . According to the source , 23.6 percent of female student respondents stated they have been bullied on school property during the previous twelve months .
