In 2020 , a national governmental annual survey found out that between November 2018 and November 2019 , approximately 6.8 million people in England went running at least twice within 28 days . Running at any intensity and any duration was considered in the survey . Other than jogging or sprinting , there are different running styles . For example , the same study found that 280,600 people participated in fell running and 93,600 people took part in parkour or free running . Running is the most popular sport in England , followed by fitness class .
