This statistic shows the Flemish Parliament election results of the regional elections of May 26 , 2019 in Belgium . The Flemish nationalist party , N-VA , won the majority of the votes with 24.83 percent ( 35 seats won ) . Vlaams Belang and the Flemish Christian democrats , CD & V , managed to obtain respectively 18.50 percent and 15.40 percent of the votes ( 23 and 19 seats won ) .
