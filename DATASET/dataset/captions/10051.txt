In the 2019 Canadian federal election , the Conservative Party received a total of nearly 6.16 million votes , compared to around 5.92 million votes for the Liberal Party . However , the Liberal Party leads with 157 electoral districts , while the the Conservative Party only secured 121 due to Canada 's first-past-the-post electoral system . These figures are as of October 22 , 2019 , with 99.67 percent of the polls reporting .
