As of June 2020 , Brico io boasted 107 retail stores in Italy . This figure made Brico io the do-it-yourself brand with the highest number of stores in the country . Due to its widespread presence , the company was able to reach a turnover of over 181 million euros in 2018 , which represented an increase of approximately six million euros compared to the previous year . Second in the ranking of the leading retailers in terms of number of stores came Brico Ok with 103 stores , followed by Bricofer with 87 stores .
