Looking for sexual partners online was more common among men than women in Denmark according to a survey from 2017 and 2018 . Over 22 percent of the male respondents stated to have searched for sexual partners on the internet , whereas the corresponding figure for women was under nine percent . Denmark led among the other Nordic countries , by share of adults who had tried dating apps . 38 percent of the Danes stated to have tried such apps as of 2018 .
