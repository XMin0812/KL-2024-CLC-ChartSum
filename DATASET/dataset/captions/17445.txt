This statistic provides the results of a survey , asking British adults which DIY tasks they have attempted in the last twelve months as of April 2014 . The most common tasks carried out were changing a lightbulb ( 80 percent ) and hanging pictures ( 56 percent ) . Tiling a room was the least common activity attempted by adults in their home , with only 6 percent of respondents having tried in the last 12 months .
