This statistic shows the net income of Virgin Media Inc. from their operations in the United Kingdom ( UK ) and Ireland from 2008 till 2019 . Virgin Media 's losses increased year after year between 2014 and 2016 . Virgin Media 's losses increased from 37 million British pounds ( GBP ) in 2018 to 337.3 million British pounds ( GBP ) in 2019 . Virgin Media is a telecommunications company located in the United Kingdom ( UK ) . Its revenues have been decreasing throughout the years in all areas . In the United Kingdom , Virgin Media 's revenues from access and calls of fixed-line networks , residential networks , and business networks were steadily decreasing throughout the years .
