In the third quarter of 2020 , Intel 's Internet of Things Group ( IOTG ) generated revenues of 677 million U.S. dollars , a tremendous reduction from the figure recorded in the same quarter of 2019 . However , the IOTG revenues in the third quarter of 2020 are a slight increase from the 670 million U.S. dollars that the company posted in the second quarter of 2020 .
