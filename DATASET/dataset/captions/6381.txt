Fast fashion industry has been under the environmental spotlight in recent years , due to a variety of reasons including the growing amount of household textile waste in Europe . One of the main reasons why fast fashion is a usual suspect in the issue is the large quantities of clothing and apparel items sold by brands usually at cheap prices . As displayed in this statistic , in 2019 , leading Europe-based fast fashion brands sold as many as almost 3 billion units , as in the case of Zara . Following the Spanish fashion group Inditex 's Zara , H & M ranked in second place , having sold around 2.2 billion clothing and apparel items in the year 2018/19 .
