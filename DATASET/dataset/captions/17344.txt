What kind of support would businesses in the tourism industry in Denmark appreciate in light of the coronavirus ? According to a survey from April 16 , 2020 , 55 percent of businesses would prefer economic support to assure liquidity . Furthermore , 40 percent could use data and forecasts to help planning in the future . The first case of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Denmark was confirmed on February 27 , 2020 .
