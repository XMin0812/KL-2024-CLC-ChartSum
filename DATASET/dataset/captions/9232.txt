This statistic shows the oil production in the United States from 1998 to 2019 . In 2019 , the United States produced around 746.7 million metric tons of oil . According to the source , oil production includes crude oil , shale oil , oil sands , and NGL ( the liquid content of natural gas , where this is recovered separately ) . It excludes liquid fuels from other sources such as biomass and coal derivatives .
