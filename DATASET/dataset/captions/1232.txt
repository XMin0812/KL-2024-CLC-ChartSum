The statistic represents the U.S. defense outlays from 2000 to 2019 with an additional forecast from 2020 to 2030 , as a percentage of the Gross Domestic Product . Defense outlays amounted to 676 billion U.S. dollars in 2019 , which was about 3.2 percent of the U.S. GDP . The forecast predicts an increase in defense outlays up to 888 billion U.S. dollars in 2030 , which would be about 2.9 percent of the U.S. GDP .
