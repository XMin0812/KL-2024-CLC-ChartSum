With an audience market share of roughly 26 percent , the first channel of the Swedish public service television company Sveriges Television AB – svt1 – was the second most viewed television channel in Sweden in week 52 of 2020 . The private station TV4 ranked first . The results almost reflect the annually ranking of television channels market shares : svt1 and TV4 came first and second , reaching percentages of over 20 percent each in 2019 .
