Based on a selection of 63 tech-related conferences scheduled to take place in 2020 , 44 events have either been modified to take place entirely online , or postponed to a later date in response to the outbreak of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) . The majority of events that claim to be proceeding as planned are scheduled for August or later . The SAP Sapphire Now conference , currently scheduled to occur from May 12 to May 14 in Orlando , Florida , is the earliest event not to have changed their statust . Several big-name conferences are among the cancellations , including the Mobile World Congress Barcelona , Facebook F8 and Google I/O .
