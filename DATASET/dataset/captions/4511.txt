This statistic shows the car models with the highest number of new registrations in Germany in 2019 . Approximately 204.6 thousand units of the VW Golf model had been registered , making it the most popular model in that year . From the ten leading car models half were produced by Volkswagen . However , in terms of amount of units sold , the German vehicle manufacturer held approximately 63 percent of the leading models sold in Germany as of 2019 .
