It is estimated that 2020 will see plug-in electric light vehicle ( PEV ) sales of around 2.3 million units . After having emerged as one of the leading markets for plug-in light vehicle sales , China has entered a phase of slowdown : During the first six months of 2020 , Chinese plug-in electric vehicle sales declined by 40 percent year-on-year . Germany could become the largest market for plug-in electric vehicles in 2020 due to a combination of regulations and incentive changes .
