The number of recreational visitors to the Grand Canyon National Park ( GCNP ) in the U.S. reached a total of 5.97 million in 2019 . This shows a drop when compared to previous year 's total of 6.38 million visitors . The number of visitors has increased by about 2 million people per year in the last seven years . Discussions and plans within the park and surrounding cities have begun to implement better infrastructure for the increase in tourism with conservation and climate change action plans in mind .
