The statistic shows the leading holding companies in the United States in 2015 , ranked by total revenue . A holding company is a company or firm that owns other companies ' outstanding stock . The holding company usually does not produces good or services itself and its purpose is to own the shares of other companies . In 2015 , Berkshire Hathaway Inc topped the list of holding companies in the United States in terms of revenue , with revenue amounting to over 210 billion U.S. dollars .
