This statistic shows the Consumer Price Index for urban consumers for selected cities and metropolitan areas in the United States of America in 2019 . In 2019 , the CPI in U.S. cities averaged at 255.66 . However , the CPI for the New York-Newark-Jersey City metropolitan area amounted to about 278.16 .
