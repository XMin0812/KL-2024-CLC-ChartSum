Since 2007 , the gross minimum wage in Romania constantly increased . As of 2020 , the gross minimum wage peaked at 2,230 Romanian lei . The growth rate of the gross minimum wage variated over the years . It recorded an increase of 31.03 percent in 2018 compared to 2017 , one of the highest salary increases since 2016 . The growth rate for 2020 was only of 7.21 percent .
