Some of the most well-known sports clothing brands battle it out on a yearly basis to have the world 's best tennis players wear their clothes on and off the court . While Roger Federer is kitted out by Uniqlo in a deal worth hundreds of millions of U.S. dollars , Serene Williams has a bumper sponsorship deal with Nike . Overall , there were an estimated 69 sponsorship deals between clothing and accessories brands and professional tennis players .
