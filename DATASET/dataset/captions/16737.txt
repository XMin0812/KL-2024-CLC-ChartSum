The statistic shows the results of a survey about the share of people having an outstanding housing loan in Singapore in 2017 , by type of population . In the period surveyed , a very high share of respondents with an outstanding housing loan was among people at the age of 25 years or over , with around 23 percent , while only about five percent of surveyed respondents at the age 15 to 24 years had an outstanding house loan .
