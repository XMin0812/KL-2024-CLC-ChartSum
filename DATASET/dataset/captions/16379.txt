This statistic presents the total income generated by arts and cultural organizations regularly funded by Arts Council England ( ACE ) from financial year 2009/10 to financial year 2018/19 . In the latest financial year , ACE funded arts organizations generated a total revenue of 2.03 billion British pounds , of which around half was earned through the organizations ' own trade and activities.Arts Council England is the national development agency providing government and lottery funding for the arts in England .
