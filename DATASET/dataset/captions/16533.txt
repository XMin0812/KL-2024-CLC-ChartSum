This statistic presents the most-tweeted football moments during the FIFA World Cup 2014 final between Germany and Argentina on July 13 , 2014 , ranked by number of tweets . Mario Götze 's match-winning goal , assisted by André Schürrle , generated more than 556,499 tweets per minute . Overall , more than 32.1 million tweets were sent during the match , making it one of the most-tweeted sporting event of all time .
