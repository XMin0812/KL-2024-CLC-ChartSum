This statistic shows the gross domestic expenditure on research and development ( GERD ) as a percentage of the gross domestic product ( GDP ) of Portugal from 2000 to 2017 . Over the period , the GERD to GDP ratio experienced an overall increase , peaking in 2009 at 1.58 percent . In 2017 , an estimate of 1.32 percent of Portugal 's GDP was spent on research and development .
