This statistic shows the median age of the population in Swaziland , officially the Kingdom of Eswatini , from 1950 to 2050 . The median age is the age that divides a population into two numerically equal groups ; that is , half the people are younger than this age and half are older . It is a single index that summarizes the age distribution of a population . In 2015 , the median age of the population of Swaziland was 19.7 years .
