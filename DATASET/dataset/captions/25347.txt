This statistic represents the reasons why people became car sharing club members in London ( UK ) from December 2017 to November 2018 . 93 percent of the members stated that the reason for joining a car club was to have access to a car without the hassle of ownership . 58 percent stated that the reason for joining was to have another travel option available to them . 28 percent joined to be environmentally friendly and 4 percent wanted to try an electric car .
