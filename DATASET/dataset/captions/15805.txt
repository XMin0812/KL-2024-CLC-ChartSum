There were approximately 7.24 nurses per 1,000 South Koreans in 2018 . This is slightly increased from the preceding year . The nurse-to-people ratio has increased continuously since 2000 , with sharper increases in the past several years . For example , from 2015 to 2016 , there was the sharpest increase from 5.94 to 6.8 nurses per 1,000 people .
