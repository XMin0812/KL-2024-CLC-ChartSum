In 2019 , approximately 71.68 million passengers utilized Amsterdam Airport Schiphol to fly to and from their destination . This is an increase compared to 2018 , when passenger traffic amounted to just under 71 million . Furthermore , the majority of the passengers flew with KLM , easyJet and Transavia at Amsterdam Airport in 2019 . KLM transported roughly 35 million passengers , whereas over 11.3 million passengers flew with easyJet and Transavia via Schiphol airport .
