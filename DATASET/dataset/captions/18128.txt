This statistic represents the leading countries based on new solar PV capacity additions in 2019 . That year the United States increased its solar photovoltaic capacity by 13.3 gigawatts in 2019 . The global solar PV cumulative capacity has increased massively over the past 20 years . In the year 2000 , the capacity amounted to just over one gigawatt and by 2018 had surpassed 500 gigawatts . Solar capacity is expected to continue to rise as renewables increase their role in the global energy mix .
