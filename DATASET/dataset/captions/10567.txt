The graph shows the price composition of a grande latte at Starbucks China as of September 4 , 2013 . Labor costs represent nine percent of the total price of a grande latte at Starbucks China . According to the source , Starbucks customers paid almost one US dollar more for their latte in China than in the US , a fact that is related to higher government taxes and import tariffs , but may also refer to the Chinese middle classes ' willingness to pay more on imported goods .
