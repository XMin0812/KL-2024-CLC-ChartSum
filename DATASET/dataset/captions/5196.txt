Tamil Nadu confirmed around 823 thousand cases of the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) as of January 7 , 2021 , with over 12 thousand fatalities and about 804 thousand recoveries . India reported more than 10.4 million confirmed cases , including this state that same day . The country went into lockdown on March 25 , the largest in the world , restricting 1.3 billion people and extended until the end of May . As per new guidelines , districts were divided into red , orange and green zones . Red zones were hotspots and did not see any relaxations in comparison to the areas considered less prone to the spread of the virus .
