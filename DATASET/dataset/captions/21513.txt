This statistic illustrates the share of offices that are pre-let among the total office space under development for the real estate market in London 's West End , United Kingdom ( UK ) , between 2021 and 2024 . It can be seen that approximately 61 percent of all office space being developed in 2021 is already pre-let , with a further 25 percent of office space being developed for 2022 being pre-let .
