In 2019 , the value of digital music albums downloaded in the U.S. was 394.5 million U.S. dollars , down from 499.7 billion a year earlier . The year 2011 to 2015 saw the figure reach and remain above one billion U.S. dollars , but annual decreases of around 150 to 200 million per year for the last few years have seen the value drop back down to a similar level as in 2007 .
