This statistic shows the total annual number of azithromycin prescriptions in the U.S. from 2004 to 2017 , in millions . In 2004 , azithromycin was prescribed almost 27 million times . As of 2017 , azithromycin prescriptions had dropped from to just over 12 million . Azithromycin is an antibiotic and primarily used for the treatment of a number of bacterial infections . This includes middle ear infections , strep throat , pneumonia , traveler 's diarrhea , and certain other intestinal infections . It may also be used to treat sexually transmitted infections including chlamydia and gonorrhea infections .
