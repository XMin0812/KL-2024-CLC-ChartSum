This statistic presents the share of consumers in the United Kingdom ( UK ) that use different authentication methods when logging in to their online bank in 2017 . It is observed that the most popular method used for logging in to online banking was with a username and password with 69 percent . The least popular method ( other than 'other method ' at 3 percent ) was the use of Call ( automated phone call from the bank with a one-time code ) which was used by 7 percent of consumers surveyed .
