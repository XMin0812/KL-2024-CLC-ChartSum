The Russian tourism industry was projected to lose under 100 million U.S. dollars due to the outbreak of coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in China in January 2020 . According to the Association of Tour Operators of Russia ( ATOR ) , approximately 45 thousand organized tourists from China were expected to travel to Russia in March 2020 . Due to the infection spread , Russia closed its Far Eastern border with China , resulting in suspension of tours purchased by Chinese travelers .
