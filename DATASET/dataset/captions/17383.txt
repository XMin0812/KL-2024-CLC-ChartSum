This statistic shows the total amount in student grants that was offered by different federal , institutional , private and state sources to students in the United States from 1999/00 to 2019/20 . In the1999/00 academic year , universities offered a total of 52 billion U.S. dollars ( in constant 2019 dollars ) to students across the United States in the form of grants . By 2019/20 , total grants had increased to 140.9 billion U.S. dollars .
