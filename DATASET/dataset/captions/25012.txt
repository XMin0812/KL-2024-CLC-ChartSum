This statistic shows the number of mandates each political party received during the parliament elections on June 5 , 2019 in Denmark . Socialdemokraterne was the biggest party in the parliament with 48 mandates . The second biggest party was Venstre , Denmark 's Liberal Party , with 43 mandates . Dansk Folkeparti and Radikale Venstre won 16 mandates each in the election . Nye Borgerlige received four mandates and was thence represented in the Danish Parliament for the first time in 2019 .
